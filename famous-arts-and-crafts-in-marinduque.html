13. ARTS AND CRAFTS IN MINDORO, MARINDUQUE, ROMBLON AND PALAWAN?


MIMAROPA or the Mindoro, Marinduque, Romblon, and Palawan areas are known for their arts and crafts, particularly those arts and crafts that were made by the indigenous peoples living in MIMAROPA.

 

Some of the arts and crafts are the following:

1.  Mangyan Bracelets

2.  Romblon Basket

3.  Iarayas Basket

4.  Hanunoo Basket

5.  Manungul Jar

6.  Salugin

7.  Ambalad

 

And many others.
