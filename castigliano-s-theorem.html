7. Which of the following concepts will best prove and justify the following statement? If points R, S and T are on the same line and RS + ST = RT, then S is between R and T. A. Supplement Theorem B. Segment Addition Postulate C. Linear Pair Postulate D. Right Angle Theorem


The concept that best proves and justifies the statement is the Segment Addition Postulate (option B).

The Segment Addition Postulate states that if three points A, B, and C are collinear and B is between A and C, then AB + BC = AC. In this case, the given statement RS + ST = RT follows the same format as the Segment Addition Postulate, indicating that point S is between R and T.

Therefore, the correct answer is option B, Segment Addition Postulate.
