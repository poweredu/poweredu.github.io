2. - Pinagmulan ng word na fantastic - Kahulugan ng word na fantastic - Advantages at disadvantage ng word na fantastic -Tapos halimbawa ng word na fantastic Sana po masaagot kailan Lang sa school ​


Answer:

Ang salitang "fantastic" ay nagmula sa salitang Latin na "fantasticus" na nangangahulugang "imaginative" o "fantastical." Sa kasalukuyang panahon, ang salitang "fantastic" ay karaniwang ginagamit upang magpahayag ng kahanga-hanga, kamangha-mangha, o kahindik-hindik na mga pangyayari, konsepto, o bagay.

Ang mga advantages ng paggamit ng salitang "fantastic" ay kasama ang pagbibigay ng pambihirang o masayahing kahulugan sa isang bagay o pangyayari. Ito rin ay maaaring magdagdag ng tibay ng damdamin o malikhaing halimbawa sa mga akda o pagpapahayag ng mga ideya.

Ang mga disadvantage ng paggamit ng salitang "fantastic" ay maaaring magbigay ng hindi malinaw na kahulugan, at maaaring magdulot ng sobrang-pagpapahalaga sa mga bagay o pangyayari. Dahil dito, maaaring magdulot ito ng hindi tamang pagpapahalaga sa mga bagay o pangyayari na hindi naman talaga dapat ipinagmamalaki o pinapahalagahan.

Halimbawa ng paggamit ng salitang "fantastic" ay:

•"The performance of the artist was fantastic!"

•"The view from the top of the mountain was absolutely fantastic!"

•"The new gadget is fantastic, it's so advanced and user-friendly!"

23. clyde,carlo and claire are siblings clyde is 5years old than claire carlo is twice as old as claire if the total of age is 45years how old is clyde​


AGE PROBLEMAnswer with Step-by-step explanation:

Problem:

Clyde, Carlo and Claire are siblings. Clyde is 5 years old than Claire. Carlo is twice as old as Claire. If the total of age is 45 years old, how old is Clyde?

Solution:

Let x be the age of Claire.

x+5 be the age of Clyde since he is 5 years older than Claire.

2x be the age of Carlo since he is twice as old as Claire.

The sum of their ages is 45 so:

age of Claire plus age of Clyde plus age of Carlo equals 45 which can be expressed in an algebraic equation as:

x + (x+5) + 2x = 45

We can combine like terms and do algebraic manipulations to solve for the value of x.

x + x + 2x = 45 – 5

4x = 40

4x/4 = 40/4

x = 10

Hence, the value of x is 10. However, x is only for Claire's age. We are looking for the age of Clyde which is: x + 5. By direct substitution:

Clyde's age = x + 5 = 10 + 5 = 15

Therefore, Clyde's age is 15.

Now let us continue solving the age of Carlo which is equal to 2x = 2(10) = 20.

So Carlo's age is 20 years old.

To check, their sum should equate to 45, so:

10 + 15 + 20 = 45

45 = 45

Therefore, our answer is correct.

24. captain ali is the captain of the ship. the captain of the ship is captain ali. what is the name of the ship?


WHAT.

WHAT is the name of the ship.  

Or

The name of the ship is WHAT.

There is no other name mentioned in the riddle except for Ali and What. Ali is the captain and his name can’t be the same name of the ship. This riddle is usually recited orally as to confuse the guesser of what is really the answer since one can play with the intonation of the last line making it ambiguously asking or revealing. Since this is a riddle, the person being told must make a guess.  

In writing, you may intentionally put a period at the end, or just simply write it as a question. Either way is acceptable though some would argue once the answer is revealed.

Some also calls this as a catchy or catching question because it’s goal is to challenge someone to catch the hidden message.
