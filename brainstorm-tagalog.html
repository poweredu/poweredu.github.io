There is no structure in brainstorming, and no idea is considered wrong. All ideas are noted during the brainstorming sessions, and some can even be clubbed together.

2. What are the steps in brainstorming?

Six steps in the brainstorming process.

1. Create the environment. For group brainstorming, try to limit yourself to under 10 participants.

2. Identify the problem. Once you have gathered participants, outline the goal of the brainstorming session.

3. Generate ideas.

4. Share ideas.

5. Narrow the list of ideas.

6. Make an action plan.

7. Create an infographic that presents the definition of brainstorming and 7 easy ways to brainstorm ​


Answer:

Definition of brainstorming: Brainstorming is a technique used to generate creative ideas and solutions to problems by encouraging the free flow of thoughts and opinions among a group of people.

7 easy ways to brainstorm:

Mind mapping: Draw a central idea in the middle of a page and branch out to related ideas.

SWOT analysis: Identify the strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats of a particular situation.

Random word association: Choose a random word and brainstorm related ideas and associations.

Role-playing: Assume different perspectives and brainstorm ideas based on each perspective.

Group discussion: Encourage open and honest discussion among a group of people to generate ideas.

Reverse brainstorming: Identify the negative aspects of a situation and brainstorm ways to avoid or overcome them.

SCAMPER technique: Use the acronym SCAMPER (Substitute, Combine, Adapt, Modify, Put to another use, Eliminate, and Reverse) to brainstorm ideas based on these categories.

29. rules of brainstorming


The Four Rules of Brainstorming. They are intended to reduce the social inhibitions that occur in groups and stimulate the generation of new ideas. The expected result is a dynamic synergy that will dramatically increase the creativity of the group.

The four rules of brainstorming are:
Focus on quantity
NO criticism
Encourage wild ideas
Combine and improve ideas
