28. explain the tourism area life cycle analysis as applied to tourism product development.​


Answer:

The model recognizes six stages in the tourism product life cycle: exploration, investment, development, consolidation, stagnation and followed, after stagnation, by decline or revitalization of the product. These six stages can in turn be regrouped into four main stages.It suggests that the tourist destination life cycle goes through six stages; exploration, involvement, development, consolidation, stagnation and decline or rejuvenation (Bulter, 1980).


29. 1. What are the importance of of tourism product development? 2. In your own understanding, how do you develop tourism product?​


1.Tourism product development is important because it helps to create new and innovative products that can attract tourists and boost the tourism industry. It can also help to improve the quality of existing products and services, making them more appealing to tourists.

2.There is no single answer to this question as product development involves many different elements, all of which must be considered in order to create a successful tourism product. However, some key considerations in developing a tourism product include understanding the target market, understanding what they are looking for in a tourism product, and creating a product that meets their needs and expectations. Other important factors to consider include the unique selling points of the product, the need for the product in the market, and the feasibility of developing and delivering the product.
