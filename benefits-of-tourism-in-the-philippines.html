5. What are the benefits of sustainable tourism in the tourism and hospitality industry? To explain each.150 words or less​


Sustainable tourism can have many benefits for the tourism and hospitality industry. Here are some of the main benefits:

Economic benefits: Sustainable tourism can help to generate income, employment, and business opportunities for local communities, which can in turn help to reduce poverty and promote economic development.

Environmental benefits: By promoting sustainable practices, such as responsible waste management, energy efficiency, and conservation of natural resources, the tourism and hospitality industry can help to protect and preserve the environment.

Social benefits: Sustainable tourism can foster a greater sense of community, cultural exchange, and mutual understanding among local residents and visitors. This can help to promote social cohesion, tolerance, and inclusion.

Quality of life: By prioritizing sustainability, the tourism and hospitality industry can help to create more livable, healthy, and enjoyable destinations for both residents and visitors.

Reputation and competitiveness: By adopting sustainable practices, the tourism and hospitality industry can enhance its reputation and competitiveness, as well as improve its long-term prospects and resilience.

Overall, sustainable tourism can help to create a more positive, sustainable, and equitable future for the tourism and hospitality industry, and for the world as a whole.

13. Explain how to protect the environment for the benefit of tourism ​


Answer:

In the case of tourism, sustainability means the wise use, development and protection of the natural, cultural and built environmental elements, as a result of which the interests and expectations of the participants in tourism are met at the same time, they benefit from the economic benefits at the same time. Sustainability in the field of tourism can be promoted by building a Regional Tourism Destination Management (TDM) system. This bottom-up management, created with the help of the public and private sectors, with its own resources and trained professionals, can manage the whole process of tourism. Understanding the destination management model is the key to creating sustainable tourism. However, without maintaining the environment at the heart of the model at the municipal level, the entire system may operate uncertainly, so it is necessary to establish a local destination management for environmental protection.
