23. John Michael's lunch box measure 5incher wide and 10 inches long.what will be its perimeter ​


The width is 5 inches and the length is 10 inches.

Therefore, the perimeter of the lunch box will be:

P = 2(length + width)

P = 2(10 inches + 5 inches)

P = 2(15 inches)

P = 30 inches

Therefore, the perimeter of John Michael's lunch box is 30 inches.
