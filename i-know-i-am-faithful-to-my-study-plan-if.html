1. I know that my study habits work well if - I consistently do it.

2. I know that my study habits do not work well if - I never practice for this study habit.

3. I know I am faithful to my study plan if - I do self-punishment when breaking it.

4. My goal for making my study plan is - to reach my dream to be a successful entrepreneur.

5. For my study habits to work, I need to - be controlled by my parents.

EXPLANATION:

1. I know that my study habits work well if I consistently do it

To achieve success, not only by building new good habits, but also being able to be consistent in doing so. New habits such as studying hard do take time. Some argue that if you want to grow a new habit, that person should spend about 2 hours a day. There is also a suggestion to do for 10,000 hours. I don't know, because according to one of the well-known American psychologists, James Clear, everyone has a different way. In this case, consistent means done continuously every day. The methods vary from making plans, practicing patience, and starting from something simple.

2. I know that my study habits do not work well if I never practice for this study habit

If I want to develop a new habit of studying, I have to practice more often. I then saw satisfactory results and experienced great development regarding this study habit. So if I never practice, it takes me a long time to make a new thing become a new habit.

3. I know I am faithful to my study plan if I do self-punishment when breaking it.

Something has to control me if I break  my rule of the study habits rules that I make. No need for severe punishment. For 1 mistake I can give alms to the needy or just feed the workers on the road.

4. My goal for making my study plan is to reach my dream to be a successful entrepreneur.

The study habits plan will make me more focused and focused when doing it. That way, my goal of achieving my dream will hopefully be achieved according to the targets that have been set. My dreams can develop well. The next use of the study habits plan is to predict my future.

5. For my study habits to work, I need to be controlled by my parents.

The participation of my parents in the success of my education is in various ways, they always give attention, affection, motivation, and also facilities, especially attention to my learning activities at home. Parents' attention has a great influence on my learning activities.
