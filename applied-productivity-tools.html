26. How can you apply productivity tools effectively?​


Preventive Maintenance

Explanation:

It is the systematic care and protection of tools,equipment and machines in order to keep tools in safe,usable condition,limit downtime and extend productivity.We must always be aware the maintenance tasks themselves are potentially hazardous and can result an injury.

An effective maintenance program is:

1.Well-organized and scheduled;

2.Controls hazards;

3.Defines operational procedures;and

4.Trains key personnel.
