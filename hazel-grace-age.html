22. Maylene is 20 yrs older than her daughter Hazel. In ten years' time she will be twice as old as her daughter. Find their present ages.​


Answer:

Hazel = 30 years oldHazel's Daughter = 10 years old

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x be Hazel's daughter's age

Present age

Hazel = x + 20

Hazel's daughter = x

In 10 years

Hazel = (x + 20) + 10

Hazel's daughter = x + 10

Since it is stated in the problem that in 10 years time, Hazel will be twice as old as her daughter, we can formulate an equation...

2(Daughter's age in 10 yrs) = (Hazel's age 10 in yrs)

Plug in their ages and then combine like terms...

2(x + 10) = (x + 20) + 10

2x + 20 = x + 30

x = 10

Substitute:

Hazel's daughter = x = 10 years oldHazel = x + 20 = 10 + 20 = 30 years old

Check:

2(10 + 10) = 30 + 10
