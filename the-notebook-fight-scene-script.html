23. Is a spontaneous style of theater through which scenes are created without advance rehearsal or a script.


Improvisation, or improv, is a spontaneous style of theater in which scenes are created without advance rehearsal or a script. This is a type of acting live in which the dialogues and plot are made up at the moment. Stand-up comedies or dramas can both use improv, or even in music, this can be used too.  

Improvisation helps an artist to think creatively, can improve confidence, and develop social and emotional skills. These are just some of the benefits and advantages of using improv.
