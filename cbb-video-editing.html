13. Mabel spends 4 hours to edit a 3 minute long video. She edits at a constant rate. How long does Mabel spend to edit a 9 minute long video?


Answer:

Mabel spends 12 hours to edit a 9-minute long video.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let us analyze the given problem.

What is asked?

How long does Mabel spend to edit a 9 minute long video?

What are the given?

length of a video - 3 minutes

amount of time spent in a 3-minute video - 4 hours

length of the other video - 9 minutes

There are two steps to know how long Mabel can edit a 9-minute video.

Find how many 3-minute videos there are in a 9-minute video.Multiply the answer by the amount of time spent in every 3-minute video.

What is the number sentence?

1. Find how many 3-minute videos there are in a 9-minute video.

9 ÷ 3 = N

2. Multiply the answer by the amount of time spent in every 3-minute video, which is 4.

number of 3-minute video × 4 = N

Solution:

Step #1

9 ÷ 3 = N

9 ÷ 3 = 3

There are 3 (three) 3-minute videos in a 9-minute long video.

Step #2

number of 3-minute video × 4 = N

3 × 4 = 12

Final Answer:

Mabel spends 12 hours to edit a 9 minute long video.
