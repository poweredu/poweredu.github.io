5. WHAT ARE THE 7 HABITS OF A WISE SAVER?


Here are seven habits of a wise saver

 

1) A wise saver works hard to earn money.

2) The wise saver practices frugality, he refuses to buy on impulse and may purchase something important after a week of thinking hard.

3) They invest their money in one way or another. They make their money work for them.

4) They usually live a simple life. They are not out to impress other people with the material things they have acquired.  

5) They make sure all debts are paid.  

6) They would rather pay with cash, the thought of owing somebody money makes them uneasy.

7) They continually think of how to acquire more money and appears to have little interest for other things since money is the only reason for existing.  

The title of being a wise saver is really a blanket term when referring to a person that does not spend very much. It hides the fact that the individual may be a Scrooge, a miser, or even just plain greedy. It depends on the person but they are generally comfortable with being secure financially and won’t have it any other way. A good person who happens to be a wise saver won’t think twice about helping out when someone he knows is in dire need of financial assistance.

14. ano ang 7 habits of a wise saver


1. Know your bank Know the owners of your bank -- the people behind it and the people who manage it. Find out and ask about your bank's finances and its strengths and weaknesses. PDIC, BSP, SEC, and your bank's websites, newspapers, magazines, television and radio will provide most of the information you need. 2. Know your bank products Understand where you place your cash. Do not confuse investments with regular deposits. Read and understand the fine print and don't hesitate to clarify with bank personnel terms and conditions that are not clear. 3. Know your bank's services and fees Choose the right bank for you by knowing your needs and matching these with your bank's services. Be aware of bank charges and fees. 4. Keep your bank records safe and updated Secure your passbook, ATM, certificate of time deposit (CTD), checkbook and other bank records at all times. Have your passbook and CTDs updated every time you do a transaction. Inform your bank whenever there are changes in your contact details to avoid bank mails with sensitive information getting into the wrong hands. 5. Transact only inside the bank with authorized bank personnel Do not hesitate to ask personnel to present an ID and always ask for proof of your transaction. 6. Be informed about PDIC deposit insurance PDIC guarantees deposits up to P500,000.00 per depositor. Investment products, fraudulent accounts, laundered money and deposit products from unsafe and unsound banking practices are not covered by insurance. 7. Be cautious Simply walk away from offers that are too good to be true. Generally, excessively high interest rates carry more risks.

29. Magtala ng magandang epekto ng pagiging isang wise saver


Answer:

1. Mayroon kang pag-unawa sa kahalagahan ng pamamahala ng iyong pera.

2. Mayroon kang mas mahusay na kakayahang magplano para sa iyong hinaharap.

3. Mayroon kang mas malaking pag-iimpok para sa iyong hinaharap.

4. Mayroon kang mas mahusay na kakayahang pamahalaan ang iyong mga gastusin.

5. Mayroon kang mas mahusay na kakayahang maiwasan ang pagkalugi.

6. Mayroon kang mas malaking pananagutan sa iyong sarili at sa iyong pamilya.

7. Mayroon kang mas mahusay na kakayahang maging responsable sa iyong pera.

8. Mayroon kang mas mahusay na kakayahang maging matipid sa iyong pera.

9. Mayroon kang mas mahusay na kakayahang makapag-ipon para sa mas malaking mga proyekto.

10. Mayroon kang mas malaking katatagan sa buhay.

Answer:

Mayroong maraming magandang epekto ng pagiging isang wise saver. Narito ang ilan sa mga ito:

1. Nakakatipid ng pera - Sa pagiging wise saver, natututo tayong magtipid ng pera at magplano ng ating mga gastusin. Dahil dito, mas nakakapag-ipon tayo ng pera para sa mga pangangailangan at pangarap natin sa buhay.

2. Nakakapagbigay ng financial security - Sa pagkakaroon ng savings, mas mayroon tayong financial security dahil mayroon tayong pambayad sa mga hindi inaasahang gastusin tulad ng emergency expenses.

3. Nakakapag-invest - Dahil sa pagiging wise saver, mayroon tayong extra money na pwede nating gamitin sa pag-iinvest. Sa pamamagitan ng pag-iinvest, mas lumalaki ang ating pera at mas nakakatulong ito sa ating financial goals.

4. Nakakapagbigay ng peace of mind - Sa pagkakaroon ng savings, mas nababawasan ang ating stress at anxiety tungkol sa pera. Mas nakakapag-focus tayo sa ibang mga bagay tulad ng ating trabaho, pamilya, at iba pang mga personal na bagay.

5. Nakakatulong sa pagpapalago ng ekonomiya - Sa pagiging wise saver, nakakatulong tayo sa pagpapalago ng ekonomiya dahil mas maraming pera ang naiikot sa ating mga komunidad. Ito ay dahil mas maraming tao ang mayroong pera na pwedeng gastusin sa mga lokal na negosyo at serbisyo.

Sa kabuuan, ang pagiging wise saver ay mayroong maraming magandang epekto hindi lamang sa ating personal na buhay kundi pati na rin sa ating komunidad at ekonomiya.
