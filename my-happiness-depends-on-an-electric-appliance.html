15. my happiness depends on an electric appliance and i do not mind giving it so much credit what is the predominant feeling of the speaker in these lines? why does he feel that way?


Answer:

Thankful.

The speaker feels positive and strongly about electric appliances. He is certain that his happiness depends on electric appliance that he went so far as adding that he would not mind giving it much credit.

Explanation:

It could be said that the person is living too dependent on modern technology or electric appliances. Here we see a person who would not be content in living without electricity because he was probably born into the comforts of these appliances. If this person were to be immersed in a far-flung barangay with no electricity, he would not have stayed for days.

How could we have made an interpretation from these short lines? We interpret the gravity or weight of what a person says. For instance, if he were simply stating a fact he would say that electric appliances is helpful to his life. However, he feels that he is thankful with electric appliances because his happiness depends on it and that he would give it that much credit.

Our choice of words and expressions often reveal our feelings. Hence, we should always be careful with what we say and how we say it.
