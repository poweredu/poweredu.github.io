4. what is the summary belief of Christianity?


The Beliefs Of Christianity: Capsulized

 

Summarizing The Beliefs of Christianity

Christianity is a religion that is monotheistic, meaning, the Christians, the adherents of this religion, believes in only one God. The beliefs of Christianity can be summarized in the following:

Believes only in one God

Christians believes that there is only one God whom they call as Father as taught by Jesus

Believes also in the Holy Trinity (The Father, The Son, The Holy Spirit)

The Holy Trinity is also one of the center of the beliefs of Christianity. The Christians believes in the existence of the Holy Trinity, God as the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit

Jesus is the Messiah, the anointed one by God as a savior of the humanity

Christians believes that Jesus was the Son of God who was sent to fulfill  God's promise, the salvation.

Belief in the justification by faith

Faith in the beliefs of Christianity is very important. Through the belief in Jesus as the Son of God, and in his death and resurrection, Christians believe that they can have a right relationship with God whose forgiveness was made once and for all through the death of Jesus Christ.

Believes that there is a life after earthly death.

The Christians believes that there is life after death.
