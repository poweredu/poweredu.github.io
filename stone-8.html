6. A stone weighing 2 kilograms is perched on a castle wall 8 meters above the ground. What is the gravitational potential energy of this stone?​


Answer:

156.96 Joules

Explanation:

P.E. = mgh, where m is the mass in kilograms, g is the acceleration due to gravity and h is the height in meters.

P.E. = [tex](2kg)(9.81m/s^2)(8m)[/tex]

P.E. = 156.96 Joules
