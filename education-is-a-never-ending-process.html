Human beings though scientifically are considered as belong to the animal Kingdom, are fundamentally different from them in at least two aspects. The first is the capacity to think of abstract ideas (intellect, abstract thinking). When most animals are driven by instincts, humans can be driven by both instinct and abstract concepts. Secondly, humans can use language. While animals also use some form of communication, the are not as complex as the one we use when communicating with other human beings.
