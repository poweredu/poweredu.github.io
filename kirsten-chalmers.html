Answer:

Thomas Chalmers FRSE, was a Scottish minister, professor of theology, political economist, and a leader of both the Church of Scotland and of the Free Church of Scotland. He has been called "Scotland's greatest nineteenth-century churchman". He served as Vice-president of the Royal Society of Edinburgh from 1835–42.

The total amount whoul kristen have to buy

Total Amount = Php 87.65

The total amount Kirsten would have to pay is Php 87.65.
