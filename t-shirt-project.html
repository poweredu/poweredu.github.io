6. 1/3 of the class wear red t-shirt while 1/4 wear blue t-shirts and the rest of the class wear pink t-shirts. What part of the class wear pink t-shirt? ​


In the given problem, apply your knowledge in algebra specially in solving fraction and it's application in real life situation.

The problem is an application of division of parts using fraction.

Given

1/3 of the class wear red t-shirt

1/4 wear blue t-shirts

The rest of the class wear pink t-shirts.

Problem

What part of the class wear pink t-shirt?

Solution

Remember, the whole portion of a fraction is 1, and if you add all the fraction parts, the total is 1.

Thus, 1/3 + 1/4 + x = 1, where x represent the fraction part of the class wear pink t-shirt.

1/3 + 1/4 = 7/12

1 - 7/12 = 5/12

Answer: 5/12

23. 4. Which of the following would absorb the most sunlight? A. A white t-shirt B. An orange t-shirtC. A yellow t-shirt D. A black t-shirt ​


Answer:

A black t-shirt would absorb the most sunlight. Darker colors tend to absorb more light than lighter colors, and black is the darkest color. The absorbed light energy is converted into heat energy, which is why black clothing can become much hotter in the sun than lighter colored clothing.
