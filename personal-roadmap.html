2. what are the interview roadmap ​


Answer:

An interview roadmap is a set of at least 10 core questions that you bring into the interview. Inject appropriate questions into your natural conversation flow during the interview. Avoid waiting for the end of the interview, when the interviewer typically asks: “So, do you have any questions for us?”

How do you answer roadmap interview questions?

Highlight Assumptions and Revisit Questions

Validate your assumptions surrounding elements like inputs, resources, or time taken to complete. Product roadmap interview questions are no exception. This section also enables the interviewer to ask any questions they have about your product roadmap.

What do you mean by roadmap?

A roadmap is a visual representation of your strategic plans. It ties together your strategy (the "why"), the work you will need to do to achieve your goals (the "what"), and a timeline for completion (the "when").
