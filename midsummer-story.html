1. the midsummer story what is/are the conflict in the story?​


A Midsummer Night's Dream is a play written by William Shakespeare. Most of Shakespeares' plays revolve around love and the complications that surround it.

The main conflict of the story is centered on Hermia's refusal to marry the man her father, the Duke of Athens, has chosen for her. This refusal could mean death for Hermia.

Throughout the story, several other conflicts arise with the same theme: love at first sight as a result of a love potion and the rejection of said love.

26. the midsummer story who are the main characters and give a short descriptions​


Answer:

Hermia - is a feisty, confident young woman from Athens. She is in love with a man named Lysander, but her father, Egeus, commands her to marry Demetrius instead.

Helena -is a young woman from Athens and a friend of Hermia. She was betrothed to Demetrius until he left her for Hermia, and she remains desperately in love with him.

Lysander is a young man from Athens who is in love with Hermia at the start of the play. Egeus, Hermia's father, accuses Lysander of “bewitching the bosom of [his] child” and ignoring that Hermia is betrothed to another man.

Demetrius- a young man from Athens, was previously betrothed to Helena but abandoned her in order to pursue Hermia.

Puck -is Oberon’s mischievous and merry jester. Technically, he is Oberon’s servant, but he is both unable and unwilling to obey his master.

Oberon- is the king of the fairies. After witnessing Demetrius’ poor treatment of Helena, Oberon orders Puck to repair the situation through the use of a love potion.

Titania -is the queen of the fairies. She recently returned from a trip to India, where she adopted a young changeling boy whose mother died in childbirth.

Theseus - is the king of Athens and a force of order and justice. At the beginning of the play, Theseus recalls his defeat of the Amazons, a society of warlike women who traditionally represent a threat to patriarchal society.

Hippolyta - is the queen of the Amazons and Theseus’ bride. The Amazons are a powerful tribe led by fearsome women warriors, and as their queen, Hippolyta represents a threat to the patriarchal society of Athens.

Egeus - is Hermia’s father. At the start of the play, Egeus is enraged that his daughter will not obey his wishes to marry Demetrius.

Bottom-perhaps the most foolish of the players, Nick Bottom gets wrapped up in the drama between Oberon and Titania.

Players-the group of traveling players includes Peter Quince, Nick Bottom, Francis Flute, Robin Starveling, Tom Snout, and Snug.
