Explicit Signals

In the song "Let It Go", the explicit signals used by the singer to highlight significant points of the song are the following:

A kingdom of isolationConceal, don't feelBe the good girlLet it GoThe cold never bothered me anywayI'm free

A kingdom of isolation means that she is alone in a place.Conceal, don't feel means do not get affected, do not mind and care. Be the good girl means stay good and kind just like before .Let It go means do not hold back, release it. The cold never bothered me anyway means she is not afraid of the cold because the power in her hands are way colder. I'm free means nobody gets to tell what is right or wrong, you can do whatever you want .

Implicit Signals

In the song "Let It Go", the implicit signals used by the singer to highlight significant points of the song are the following:

Looks like I'm the QueenSwirling storm insideLet the storm rage onI am one with the wind and skyMy soul is spiraling in frozen fractals

Looks like I'm the Queen means that she can release her true powers, she is in control in a place she chose to stay .Swirling storm inside means that there are lots of questions, worries and confusions deep inside her. Let the storm rage on means let all the bad things happen, let all the worst come all at once .I am one with the wind and sky means she has powerful allies and friends. My soul is spiraling in frozen fractals means her soul is finally free to move anywhere, feeling carefree.
