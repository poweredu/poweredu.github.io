14. who Sun Yat-sen? cite three things that made him notable person in history and politics​


Answer:

Sun Yat-sen was a Chinese revolutionary and politician who is widely recognized as the founding father of the Republic of China. Here are three notable things that made him a significant person in history and politics:

1.) He led the Xinhai Revolution which overthrew the Qing Dynasty in 1911, ending 2,000 years of imperial rule in China.

2.) He founded the Kuomintang (KMT) or the Nationalist Party of China, which played a major role in Chinese politics for much of the 20th century.

3.) He advocated for Chinese nationalism, modernization, and economic and social reforms to improve the livelihood of the Chinese people and promote social justice.

16. it represent the sun's apparent path across the sky as earth revolves around the sun​


Sun's apparent motion

The ecliptic is the apparent path of the Sun throughout the course of a year. Because Earth takes one year to orbit the Sun, the apparent position of the Sun takes one year to make a complete circuit of the ecliptic.

Answer: Ecliptic

Explanation: The ecliptic is the apparent path of the Sun throughout the course of a year. The actual speed with which Earth orbits the Sun varies slightly during the year, so the speed with which the Sun seems to move along the ecliptic also varies.
