Have you ever wondered why planes do not collide in the air? There are traffic rules for the planes in the air as there are traffic rules for the cars in the road. There are airways or roads in the sky through which planes must travel. All planes are guided by air-traffic controls. They must fly at designated heights so that planes coming from opposite directions fly at different heights or air-roads. Every plane is fully equipped with instruments, and, unless these instruments become out of order, air collision is not possible. 1. Write the word that tells where airplanes land or leave.​


Answer:

Airfield              

Explanation:  
An airfield is a place where airplanes land and take off. It is a designated area of land or water that is used for the operation of aircraft. Airfields can vary in size and can be used for different types of aircraft, such as commercial airlines, private planes, and military aircraft. They usually have a runway, which is a strip of pavement or concrete where planes can take off and land, as well as other infrastructure such as hangars, terminal buildings, and control towers. Airfields can be found at airports, military bases, and other locations. They are an important part of the aviation industry, allowing planes to safely and efficiently travel to and from different destinations.

30. Have you ever traveled outside the Regions? If so, describe one cultural difference you remember in other region in the Philipines that you visited​


Answer:

I've Visited Bicol

The Bicol are largely an agricultural and rural people, producing rice, coconuts, and hemp. Nearly all of them are Roman Catholics. Their language is closely related to others of the central Philippines, all of which belong to the Austronesian (Malayo-Polynesian) family of languages.Some persisting traditional practices are the pamalay, pantomina and tigsikan. Beliefs on god, the soul and life after death are strongly held by the people. Related to these, there are annual rituals like the pabasa, tanggal, fiestas and flores de mayo
