Solutions:

Given that Mary is 24 years old then,

M = 24

where M represents Mary's age.

Given also that Mary is twice as old as Ann when Mary was as old as Ann then,

M = 2A

M = A

where M represent's Mary's age and A represents Ann's age.

Given that M = 24 then,

24 = 2A

24 = A

by addition it will give

48 = 3A
