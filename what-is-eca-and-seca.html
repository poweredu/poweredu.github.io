5. 1.if CA = 12cm what is the measure of ER?2.if diagonal CR = 14cm. how long is SR?3.If m<ARE=75° what is m<CAR4.If m<ECA=100°, what is m<ERA?5. if ES=5.5 cm, how long is diagonal AE?​


[tex]\underline{\mathcal{SOLUTION}}:[/tex]

1. if CA = 12cm what is the measure of ER?

opposite sides of parallelogram are equalCA = ER

[tex]\underline{\mathcal{ANSWER}}:[/tex]

[tex]\begin{gathered}\begin{gathered}\boxed{\begin{array}{l}\boxed{\sf ER = 12cm}\end{array}}\end{gathered}\end{gathered}[/tex]

2. if diagonal CR = 14cm. how long is SR?

Diagonals of parallelogram bisect equally

[tex]\underline{\mathcal{ANSWER}}:[/tex]

[tex]\begin{gathered}\begin{gathered}\boxed{\begin{array}{l}\sf SR = \frac{CR}{2} \\ \\ \sf SR = \frac{14}{2} \\ \\ \boxed{\sf SR = 7cm}\end{array}}\end{gathered}\end{gathered}[/tex]

3. If m∠ARE = 75° what is m∠CAR?

adjacent sum of interior angles of a parellogram is 180°

[tex]\underline{\mathcal{ANSWER}}:[/tex]

[tex]\begin{gathered}\begin{gathered}\boxed{\begin{array}{l}\sf m∠ARE + m∠CAR = 180° \\ \\ \sf m∠CAR = 180° - 75° \\ \\ \boxed{\sf m∠CAR = 105°}\end{array}}\end{gathered}\end{gathered}[/tex]

4. If m∠ECA = 100°, what is m∠ERA?

opposite angles of parallelogramm∠ECA = m∠ERA

[tex]\underline{\mathcal{ANSWER}}:[/tex]

[tex]\begin{gathered}\begin{gathered}\boxed{\begin{array}{l}\boxed{\sf m∠ERA = 100°}\end{array}}\end{gathered}\end{gathered}[/tex]

5. if ES = 5.5 cm, how long is diagonal AE?

Diagonals bisect equally

[tex]\underline{\mathcal{ANSWER}}:[/tex]

[tex]\begin{gathered}\begin{gathered}\boxed{\begin{array}{l}\sf AE = 2 × ES \\ \\ \sf AE = 5.5 × 2 \\ \\ \boxed{\sf AE = 11cm}\end{array}}\end{gathered}\end{gathered}[/tex]
