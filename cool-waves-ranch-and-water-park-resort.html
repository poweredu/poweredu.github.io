18. An Artist who resort to traditional media like water color etc.​


Answer:

Watercolour paintings are instantly recognisable and have a glow and vividness that no other medium can match. You will end up with paintings that express dark and light as only watercolour can. The paints are very unpredictable as you are actually adding colour to water and working with the water. At times you can control it, and at others you can't. But if you learn to accept that unpredictability, and use it to your advantage, each painting will be unique and vibrant.

Watercolour lends itself to landscapes, seascapes and light effects. You can be very detailed with buildings or loose with the sky and sea.
