17. The Ark of the Covenant is described as a chest of acacia wood 2.5 cubits in length and 1.5 cubits in width and height. Given that a cubit is equivalent to 17.7 in, find the volume of the arc in cubic feet (ft3).​


Explanation:

Length = 2.5 cubits x 17.7 inches/cubit = 44.25 inches

Width = 1.5 cubits x 17.7 inches/cubit = 26.55 inches

Height = 1.5 cubits x 17.7 inches/cubit = 26.55 inches

The volume of the Ark of the Covenant is then given by:

Volume = Length x Width x Height

Volume = 44.25 inches x 26.55 inches x 26.55 inches

Volume = 31,083.97 cubic inches

To convert cubic inches to cubic feet, we divide by the conversion factor (12 inches/1 foot)^3:

Volume = 31,083.97 cubic inches / (12 inches/1 foot)^3

Volume = 18.07 cubic feet (rounded to two decimal places)

Therefore, the volume of the Ark of the Covenant in cubic feet is approximately 18.07 ft3.
