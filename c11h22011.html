8. begin:16. Find the molar mass of C12H22011?​


Solution:

Since the molar masses of C, H, and O are 12.01 g/mol, 1.008 g/mol, and 16.00 g/mol, respectively, the molar mass of C₁₂H₂₂O₁₁ can be written as

molar mass = (12.01 g/mol × 12) + (1.008 g/mol × 22) + (16.00 g/mol × 11)

[tex]\boxed{\text{molar mass = 342.3 g/mol}}[/tex]
