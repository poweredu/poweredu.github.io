The Pasig River Light was the first light station in the Philippines when it was established in 1642.

Answer:

The Pasig River Light was the first light station in the Philippines when it was established in 1642. The first lighthouse tower in the country was erected in the station and first lit in 1846. It was then located on the north jetty at the mouth of Pasig River in San Nicolas, Manila, marking the entrance of the river for vessels cruising Manila Bay, looking to dock on the inland Port of Manila that was then located along the banks of the Pasig River in Binondo and Intramuros.

22. Ilarawan ang parola binondo compound. ​


Answer:

Ang Parola Binondo Compound ay isang lugar sa Binondo, Maynila, Pilipinas. Ito ay matatagpuan sa gilid ng Pasig River at malapit sa kahabaan ng Daang Bakal, na dating kinakailangan upang makabangka patungo sa mga karatig-pook. Ang lugar na ito ay tinatawag na "Parola" dahil dito dati nakatayo ang isang parola o lighthouse na ginagamit upang magbigay ng liwanag para sa mga naglalayag na mga barko.

Ngayon, ang Parola Binondo Compound ay tahanan ng ilang mga kahalintulad na negosyo tulad ng mga talyer ng mga barko at mga kumpanya na nagbibigay ng serbisyo sa mga naglalayag. Sa lugar din na ito matatagpuan ang kilalang seafood restaurant na may mga siglong nagsisidatingan para tikman ang mga masasarap na pagkain.

Bukod sa mga negosyo, ang Parola Binondo Compound ay isa rin sa mga pangunahing access point ng Maynila sa Pasig River. Naglalayong paigtingin ang turismo sa lungsod, itinatag ang "Manila Riverwalk Project" sa lugar na ito na nag-aalok ng mga pampublikong pasyalan at gawain sa tabi ng ilog.

Parola Park

HomeThings to DoHistory & HeritageParola Park

Also known as Presing Park named after the wife of former Mayor Loreto Urieta during his term. This Mountain Park was served as the setting of a historic watchtower and cannon built in 1861 against Muslim pirates and intruders ho used to raid the area. Round the clock watchers and defenders stationed there at signal or warn the people to hide while launching volleys of cannon fires to defend the town. Today, a lighthouse and an old cannon could still be found there. The park offers the best sunset view in town.
