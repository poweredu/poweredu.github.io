13. analyze the poem "who knew" by viva andrada o flynn a filipina poet who recently won the covid them poetry competition using historical and biographical approach​


The poem created by Viva Andrada O'Flynn entitled "who knew" gave us all the motivation to stand firm against the risks brought by the pandemic. She mentioned lots of things that changed but she never expressed it negatively. Vigor still came out of her piece as she mentioned lots of fun activities we now enjoy while staying at home and being heroes to everyone and for everyone.

The poem also serves as a motivation, reminder, and appreciation. It motivates us to keep thinking of others who are more vulnerable to the virus. It is a reminder that heroes do not always wear capes, some just stay home and prevent the spread. And it appreciates the frontliners' efforts in doing their best amidst the chaos.
