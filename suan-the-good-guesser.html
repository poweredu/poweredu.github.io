1. suan the good guesser


Suan the good guesser is a story about a sharp-witted and clever boy. At a young age, instead of going to school he instead observed his mother at the tree tops. When his mother came home he told her what he had observed and his mother had concluded that he was a diviner.


The story goes on about how Suan's luck and skills help him solve problems handed to him. The first was finding the Datu's ring which he did by pointing at each soldier and saying that the ring was with them and no one else. The guilty soldier went to Suan that night and confessed that he will give him the ring for safe passage.


There are other tests that the Datu has provided Suan and with each he used his own ingenuity to prove himself. Suan's not a diviner but Suan's the wise guesser!
