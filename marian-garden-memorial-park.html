29. How did dao ming si regain his memory meteor garden 2?


Meteor Garden 2 is the famous sequel to the Taiwanese drama that we all loved, Meteor Garden 1. In this part, the story focused more on the love stories of the characters.

  After graduating from the University, Shan Cai and Dao Ming Si (Ah Si for short), went on a trip to Barcelona, Spain. The latter was about to propose to his beloved when he had an accident prompting him to lose his memory. He met Ye Sha, who took care of him. When Shan Cai and the other F4 members learned about this, they tried their hardest to help him regain his memory.
