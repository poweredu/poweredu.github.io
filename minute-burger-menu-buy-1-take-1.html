24. Mhel loves cheese burgers. He enters a 24 hour cheese burger eating contest. During the contest Mhel eats a cheese burger every 15 minutes. How many cheese burgers will he eat during the entire contest? 32 cheese burgers 36 cheese burgers 54 cheese burgers 96 cheese burgers


Convert 24 hour into minutes
24 hours(60mins/1hour) = 1,440 minutes

Mhel eaten cheese burger = 1,440/15
Mhel eaten cheese burger = 96 cheeseburgers

Mhel will eat 96 cheese burgers during the entire contest.
