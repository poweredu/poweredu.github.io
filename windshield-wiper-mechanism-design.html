7. after a trade discount of 30%, a garage is able to purchase windshielded wiper blades for a net price of Php 1,946. what is the list price of the blades?


Answer:

Let's assume the list price of the windshield wiper blades is x.

According to the question, after a trade discount of 30%, the garage can purchase the blades for a net price of Php 1,946. This means that the garage pays only 70% of the list price after the discount.

So we can write:

0.7x = 1,946

To find the list price x, we can solve for x by dividing both sides of the equation by 0.7:

x = 1,946 ÷ 0.7

x = 2,780

Therefore, the list price of the windshield wiper blades is Php 2,780.

25. The windshield wipers of a car is 24 inches long. How many inches will the tip of the wiper trace out in 1/4 revolution.​


✒️CIRCLE

[tex]••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••[/tex]

[tex] \large\underline{\mathbb{PROBLEM}:} [/tex]

The windshield wipers of a car is 24 inches long. How many inches will the tip of the wiper trace out in 1/4 revolution.

[tex]••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••[/tex]

[tex] \large\underline{\mathbb{ANSWER}:} [/tex]

[tex] \qquad \Large \rm{\approx 37.68 \, inches} [/tex]

[tex]••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••[/tex]

[tex] \large\underline{\mathbb{SOLUTION}:} [/tex]

» Measuring 1/4 revolution is just 1/4 of the circle's circumference. Let's imagine the wiper as the radius measuring 24in. Let us find the circumference with the radius of 24in.

[tex] \begin{align} & \bold{Formula:} \\ & \boxed{\rm C = 2\pi r} \end{align} [/tex]

[tex] C = 2\pi (24\,in) [/tex]

[tex] C = (\pi) 48\,in [/tex]

» Determine the fourths of its circumference that it traces the 1/4 of a revolution.

[tex] = \frac{\,(\pi)48\,in\,}{4} \\ [/tex]

[tex] = (\pi)12\,in [/tex]

» Let 3.14 be the approximate value of pi.

[tex] \approx (3.14)12\,in [/tex]

[tex] \approx 37.68\,in [/tex]

[tex] \therefore [/tex] The measure of the tip of the wiper that traced out 1/4 of the revolution is about 37.68in.

30. A jeepney has a windshield wiper on the driver’s side that has total arm and blade 10 inches long and rotates back and forth through an angle of 95°. The shaded region in the figure is the portion of the windshield cleaned by the 7-inch wiper blade. What is the area of the region cleaned?


Step-by-step explanation:

The area of the region cleaned would be the area of the sector subtracted from the area of the circle.

Area of sector = (1/2)r2θ = (1/2) x (7/2)2 x 95° = 190.625

Area of circle = πr2 = π x (7/2)2 = 61.57

Area of region cleaned = 190.625 - 61.57 = 129.055
