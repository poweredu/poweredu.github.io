17. What is uses of taiko instrument​?What is uses of Sheng instrument?What is uses of piri instrument?


Answer:

TAIKO

A taiko is a drum made from an open-ended wooden barrel with animal skin stretched over both ends. It is played with two bachi (wooden sticks). There are two distinct methods of fitting the animal skin to the drums. Those that have skins tacked directly onto both ends are collectively called byodome-daiko.

SHENG

sheng, Chinese free reed wind instrument consisting of usually 17 bamboo pipes set in a small wind-chest into which a musician blows through a mouthpiece. Each pipe has a free reed, made of metal (or formerly of bamboo or reed), that vibrates to produce sound when a finger hole on the pipe is covered.

PIRI

The piri is a cylindrical double-reed bamboo oboe with eight finger holes, one in the back for the thumb and seven in the front. Its large reed and cylindrical bore gives it a mellower sound than that of many other types of oboe.
