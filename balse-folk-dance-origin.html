What is folk dance and it's benefits?

What is folk dance?

- A folk dance is a dance developed by people that reflect the life of the people of a certain country or region.Ritual dances are usually called "Religious dances" because of their purpose. The terms "ethnic" and "traditional" are used when it is required to emphasize the cultural roots of the dance.

Benifits of folk dance:

- Folk dance helps to preserve the cultural unity of the people. Importance. People think folk dances are important because they help keep a culture alive. People have been doing folk dances for hundreds of years, and there is value keeping that tradition alive.

Describe the dance Balse Marikina.

- Balse is a popular Tagalog dance which originated from Marikina, Rizal during the Spanish time. The word balse means "waltz". PURPOSE OF THE DANCE. The purpose of this dance is to bring rural and barrio communities together during celebrations.

-This famous dance of grace and balance originated from Lubang Island, Mindoro. Balse was a popular dance in Marikina, Rizal during the Spanish times.
