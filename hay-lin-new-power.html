Menstruation is a common thing experienced by all women. Menstruation is a normal monthly cycle in women which is marked by blood coming out of the vagina. During a woman's menstrual cycle (women cycle), the body is preparing for pregnancy when fertilization occurs. Generally, normal menstrual cycles in women on average occur every 28 days. However, every woman can have a different menstrual cycle. 30. Lin walked one and five-eighths miles to the park and then five-sixths of a mile to the smoothie shop. Estimate how far Lin walked in total.


Answer:

To estimate how far Lin walked in total, we can round the distances she walked to the nearest half mile:

One and five-eighths miles is closer to 1.5 miles than to 1.75 miles.

Five-sixths of a mile is closer to 1 mile than to 0.5 miles.

So, rounding each distance to the nearest half mile, Lin walked approximately:

1.5 miles to the park

1 mile to the smoothie shop

Therefore, Lin walked approximately 2.5 miles in total.
