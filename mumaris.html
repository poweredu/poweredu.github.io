12. sumary of El filibusterismo tagalog​


Answer:

Ang nobelang El Filibusterismo ay isinulat ng ating magiting na bayaning si Dr. José Rizal na buong pusong inalay sa tatlong paring martir, na lalong kilala sa bansag na GOMBURZA - Mariano Gómez, José Apolonio Burgos, and Jacinto Zamora.

Tulad ng Noli Me Tangere, ang may-akda ay dumanas ng hirap habang isinusulat ito.  Sinimulan niyang isulat ito sa London, Inglatera noong 1890 at ang malaking bahagi nito ay naisulat niya sa Bruselas, Belgica.  Natapos ang kanyang akda noong Marso 29, 1891.  Isang nagngangalang Valentin Ventura na isa niyang kaibigan ang nagpahiram ng pera sa kanya upang maipalimbag ang aklat noong Setyembre 22, 1891.        

Ang nasabing nobela ay pampulitika na nagpapadama, nagpapahiwatig at nagpapagising pang lalo sa maalab na hangaring makapagtamo ng tunay na kalayaan at karapatan ang bayan.

Sa kasalukuyan, ang El Filibusterismo ay isa sa mahahalagang akda ng Panitikang Filipino na pinag-aaralan ngayon sa mataas na paaralan (sa ikaapat na taon), alinsunod sa kurikulum na itinakda ng Kagawaran ng Edukasyon.

20. sumary of clean water and sanitation​


Answer:

Clean water and sanitation refers to the provision and access to safe drinking water and adequate sanitation facilities. It is considered a basic human right and is essential for good health and well-being.

Access to clean water can reduce the incidence of waterborne diseases such as cholera, typhoid and diarrhea, which are major causes of death, particularly in developing countries. Adequate sanitation facilities, including toilets and latrines, can also help prevent the spread of diseases and improve overall public health.

However, access to clean water and sanitation is not universal and many people around the world still lack these basic necessities. In many developing countries, clean water is often scarce and difficult to access, especially in rural areas. Sanitation facilities are also often inadequate or non-existent, leading to poor hygiene and health conditions.

Efforts to improve access to clean water and sanitation include the construction of wells, boreholes, and other water sources, as well as the installation of water treatment systems and the building of latrines and toilets. Education and awareness campaigns on the importance of hygiene and sanitation can also play a crucial role in promoting clean water and sanitation.

Overall, clean water and sanitation is a critical issue for public health and well-being and addressing it remains a major challenge for many countries and communities
