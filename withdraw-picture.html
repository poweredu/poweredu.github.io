7. what is withdrawal slip​


Answer:

A withdrawal slip is a written instruction to the bank to pay the said amount to the account holder. The funds are debited from the account number mentioned. Much like the deposit slip, a bank withdrawal slip is a record of your banking transaction. It helps the bank keep track of your withdrawals.

27. Sixto has a bank account of 43 200 and plans to withdraw 5 400. what percent will he withdraw? . . . with solution pls


Answer:

12.5%

Step-by-step explanation:

1. We assume, that the number 43200 is 100% - because it's the output value of the task.

2. We assume, that x is the value we are looking for.

3. If 43200 is 100%, so we can write it down as 43200=100%.

4. We know, that x is 12.5% of the output value, so we can write it down as x=12.5%.

5. Now we have two simple equations:

1) 43200=100%

2) x=12.5%

where left sides of both of them have the same units, and both right sides have the same units, so we can do something like that:

43200/x=100%/12.5%

6. Now we just have to solve the simple equation, and we will get the solution we are looking for.

7. Solution for what is 12.5% of 43200

43200/x=100/12.5

(43200/x)*x=(100/12.5)*x - we multiply both sides of the equation by x

43200=8*x - we divide both sides of the equation by (8) to get x

43200/8=x

5400=x

x=5400

now we have:

12.5% of 43200=5400
