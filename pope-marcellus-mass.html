27. what is Pope Marcellus Mass​


Answer:

Missa Papae Marcelli, or Pope Marcellus Mass, is a mass sine nomine by Giovanni Pierluigi da Palestrina. It is his best-known mass,[1][2] and is frequently taught in university courses on music[citation needed]. It was sung at the Papal Coronation Masses (the last being the coronation of Paul VI in 1963).
