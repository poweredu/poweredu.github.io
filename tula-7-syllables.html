Isa sa pinaka-masayang bahagi ng buhay ay ang pagkakaroon ng kaibigan. Yung sa hirap at saya, kalokohan at tawanan ay lagi mo siyang kasama. Text Tula

Text tula is a poetry genre that is from the Philippines. The poems are primarily written and can be read as text on our mobile phones. And in this genre, poetry's elements are still present. It is usually a short formal type of verse.

At tiyak and gantimpala

"Family"

Family is everything

I need them no matter what

They are my friends forever

Supports me through ups and downs
