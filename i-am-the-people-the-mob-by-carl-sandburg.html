13. Carl Sandburg’s poem “Fog” is an example of ____________. Select one: a. Limerick b. Ballad c. Free Verse d. Haiku


Answer:

C.) free verse

C.) free verse

• Question : Carl Sandburg’s poem “Fog” is an example of ____________.

• ANSWER :

❥︎ LETTER C. (FREE VERSE)

•Explanation :

• "Fog" is a short poem, six lines long, split into two stanzas. It is a FREE VERSE poem, having no regular rhyme or set meter (metre in British English). The poem is an extended metaphor, the poet seeing the fog as a cat that comes on tiny, silent feet, as cats do when they are stalking for example.

Answer:

YES.

Explanation:

The poem is showing irony because it is talking about the piles of bodies from war and how they are being covered by grass and forgotten, but the grass is personified and speaks of how it is covering all of these bodies and the atrocities of war. The grass is saying that it will cover all of the bodies, no matter how many there are, and that it will continue to grow despite the violence and death. The literal meaning of the poem is about the aftermath of war and how nature continues on despite the human suffering.
