1. what is the passive voice of1. Mariah sings a song 2. Mariah sang a song 3. Mariah will sing a song4. They sing a song​


Answer:

A passive voice construction is a grammatical voice construction that is found in many languages. In a clause with passive voice, the grammatical subject expresses the theme or patient of the main verb – that is, the person or thing that undergoes the action or has its state changed

Answer:

2kgs

Step-by-step explanation:

Marlon = 4kgs

Mariah = 2kgs

Let x be the the number of kgs Marlon harvested than Mariah

x = Marlon - Mariah

x = 4kgs - 2kgs = 2kgs

19. Mariah wants to measure 3cups of flour, what are the steps she needs to do?


Answer:

1. Use a spoon to fluff up the flour within the container.

2. Use a spoon to scoop the flour into the measuring cup.

3. Use a knife or other straight edged utensil to level the flour across the measuring cup. I have a plastic straight edge that I keep in my flour bin at all times for just this purpose.
