1. summary sTory of manila zoo​


Answer:

The Manila Zoo, officially known as the Manila Zoological and Botanical Garden, is a zoo located in the heart of Manila, Philippines. It was established in 1959 and has since been a popular tourist attraction, showcasing a variety of animals such as tigers, elephants, monkeys, and birds. In recent years, the zoo has faced criticism for its inadequate living conditions for the animals, leading to calls for its renovation and improvement. Despite this, the Manila Zoo remains a beloved and well-visited destination for locals and tourists alike.
