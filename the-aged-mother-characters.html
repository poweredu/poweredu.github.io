is the loving and patient son of the Aged Mother. He loved his mother so much that he chose to hide her in his home after not bearing to abandon her in Obatsuyama or the mountain where “the aged are abandoned” and leaves them to die. He represents the “youth”

The Aged MotherThe titular character in the story. She is the mother of Shinano. She represents “wisdom”, giving proof that the ruler abolished his policy in the end
