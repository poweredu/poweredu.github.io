4. marcus, sally and sammy decided to share 20 sweets .marcus took 8 sweets and sally took three times as many as sammy.how many sweets did sammy recieve


Answer:

[tex]3x + x + 8 = 20[/tex]

[tex]4x = 20 - 8[/tex]

[tex]4x = 12[/tex]

[tex] \frac{4x}{4} = \frac{12}{4} [/tex]

[tex]x = 3[/tex]

Check to verify :

[tex]3(3) + (3) + 8 = 20 \\ 9 + 3 + 8 = 20 \\ 12 + 8 = 20 \\ 20 = 20[/tex]

#AnswerForTrees

#BrainlyBookSmart

ANSWER: 3 SWEETS

Given: 8 Sweets for Marcus

n sweets for Sammy

3n sweets for Sally

Solution:

3n + n + 8 = 20

4n + 8 = 20

4n = 20 - 8

4n/4 = 12/4

n = 3

Checking:

3(3) + (3) + 8 = 20

9 + 3 + 8 = 20

12 + 8 = 20

Marcus with 8 sweets

Sally with 9 sweets (thrice of Sammy's)

Sammy with 3 sweets

Total: 20 sweets
