5. In a group, 25 persons like tea or coffee, of these 15 like tea and 6 like both coffee and tea. How many like coffee? *


Answer:

There are 4 people who like coffee.

Step-by-step explanation:

This is one of the problems where we can apply the concepts of set operations. Furthermore, we can make use of the Ven Diagram to illustrate the situation.

Venn Diagram

It is a graphical representation that shows the relationships among things or finite groups of sets.

Sets Operations

1. Union (or)

2. Intersection (and)

3. Complement

4. Subset

For us to be able to understand the situation, let's illustrate the situation through a Venn diagram. Take note that there are 25 persons who like tea or coffee, this means to say that the sum of people who like coffee or tea is 25.

Check out the figure below.

As you notice, we placed 6 on the intersection of the two circles since this is the event when people like both coffee and tea. We also placed 15 on the tea side only, since this is the number of people who like tea. So, basically, we just need to know how many people like coffee only.

                                     

                                        [tex]25-15-6=4[/tex]

Thus, we have 4 people who like coffee only.

6. in a group, 30 persons were asked if they like coffee or tea, of these:18 like coffee15 like tea 11 like both coffee and tea1. create a venn diagram to model the information.2. how many people like coffee or tea?3. how many like coffee but not tea?4. how many people don't like either?​​


Answer:

In a group of 50 person, 30 like tea, 25 like coffee and 16 like both. How many like either tea or coffee?

Using a venn diagram.

T = Tea

C = Coffee

n means number

n(T) = 30

n(C) = 20

Universal = 50

n(T U C)' = 6 , T union C complement (people who like to drink neither)

Let n(T Π C) i.e. T intersection C (People who like to drink both) = x

n(T) + n(C) - n(T Π C) + n(T U C)' = 50

30 + 20 - x + 6 = 50

56 - x = 50

- x = 50 - 56

-x = -6

x = 6

Therefore people who like to drink BOTH Tea and Coffee are 6.

People who drink coffee = 20

Thus, People who ONLY drink coffee = 20 - 6 = 14 people

21. A pound of coffee beans makes 50 cups of coffee when brewed according to the instructions (4cups = 1 qt). How many liters of coffee can one kilogram of coffee beans produce?


KILOGRAM OF COFFEE BEANS

Since one pound of coffee beans makes 50 cups of coffee, we can calculate how many cups of coffee can be made from one kilogram of coffee beans as follows:

1 pound = 0.453592 kilograms

50 cups = 12.5 quarts = 11.785 liters

So, one pound of coffee beans can make 11.785 liters of coffee.

To find out how many liters of coffee can be made from one kilogram of coffee beans, we can use the following conversion:

1 kilogram = 2.20462 pounds

Therefore, one kilogram of coffee beans can make:

(1 kilogram / 2.20462 pounds) * 11.785 liters = 5.3456 liters

So, one kilogram of coffee beans can make approximately 5.3456 liters of coffee when brewed according to the instructions.

ANOTHER WAY TO SOLVE:

One pound of coffee beans produces 50 cups of coffee when brewed according to the instructions. We can use this information to determine how many cups of coffee one kilogram of coffee beans can produce.

First, we need to convert the weight of the coffee beans from pounds to kilograms. There are approximately 0.45 kilograms in one pound, so one kilogram of coffee beans is equivalent to approximately 2.2 pounds of coffee beans.

Now, we can use the ratio of 50 cups of coffee per pound of coffee beans to determine how many cups of coffee one kilogram of coffee beans can produce.

50 cups of coffee / 2.2 pounds of coffee beans = 22.73 cups of coffee per pound of coffee beans

Next, we need to convert cups to liters. According to the instructions, 4 cups of coffee is equivalent to 1 quart, and 1 quart is equivalent to 0.946 liters.

22.73 cups of coffee / 4 cups per quart * 0.946 liters per quart = 12.91 liters of coffee

Therefore, one kilogram of coffee beans can produce approximately 12.91 liters of coffee when brewed according to the instructions.
