10. Christy is six years older than Kitkat. Intwo years, Christy will be twice as old asKitkat. Find their ages five years fromnow.​


Answer:

Christy: 15yrs. old

Kitkat: 9yrs. old

Step-by-step explanation:

Make an algebraic expression of their ages:

x+6 Christy's age (present)

x Kitkat's age (present)

Make an algebraic expression and equation of their ages in two years:

x+6+8 Christy's age

x+2 Kitkat's age

(*We can simply denote Christy's age by x+8)

x+8=2(x+2)

x+8=2x+4

8-4=4=x

Substitute value of x to their present ages

10 Christy's age

4 Kitkat's age

Since we are to find their ages five years from their present age, we simply add 5

15 and 9
