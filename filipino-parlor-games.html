1. What does parlor game mean?​


Answer:

A parlour or parlor game is a group game played indoors. They were often played in a parlour. These games were extremely popular among the upper and middle classes in Great Britain and in the United States during the Victorian era. The Victorian age is sometimes considered the "Golden Age" of the parlour game.

7. A. Don Juan has 30 prizes for parlor games. He decided to give 2/3 to the winnersof the relay game. How many of these prizes are for the other games?​


Answer:

Ten (10) prizes are reserved for the other games.

Step-by-step explanation:

Don Juan has 30 prizes for parlor games. He decided to give 2/3 to the winners  of the relay game. How many of these prizes are for the other games?

If [tex]\frac{2}{3}[/tex] of the prizes are for the winners of the relay game, then the remaining [tex]\frac{1}{3}[/tex] are for the other games. Find the [tex]\frac{1}{3}[/tex] of 30.

[tex]30 \times \frac{1}{3} = \frac{30}{3} = \boxed{10}[/tex]

Therefore, 10 prizes are for the other games.

You can also solve this in another way.

Find the [tex]\frac{2}{3}[/tex] of 30 to determine the number of prizes for the winners of the relay game.

[tex]30 \times \frac{2}{3}=\frac{60}{3}\\=20[/tex]

Subtract the result, 20, from 30 to determine the number of prizes there are for the other games.

[tex]Total\ number\ of\ prizes\ - Prizes\ for\ the\ relay\ game = Prizes\ for\ the\ other\ games\\30 - 20 = \Large \boxed{10}[/tex]

13. Bakit makabuluhan ang mga tradisyonal na laro at parlor games?


Answer:

Traditional games and parlor games are important because they promote socialization, cooperation, problem-solving, and communication, and promote overall mental health. Additionally, they bring people together, providing a safe, fun way for people to interact that does not involve the use of electronic devices. Traditional games and parlor games offer a sense of nostalgia for adults and are great for teaching children important social and strategic skills.
