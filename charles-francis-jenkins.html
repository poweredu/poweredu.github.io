Answer:

Francis is 16 years old and Glen is 24 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let's start by assigning variables to the ages we're trying to find. Let's say that Francis' age is "F" and Glen's age is "G".

From the first sentence, we can write an equation:

"Five less than twice Francis' age is 3 more than Glen's age"

2F - 5 = G + 3

We can simplify this equation by adding 5 to both sides:

2F = G + 8

Now let's look at the second sentence:

"8 years ago, Glen was as old as Francis is now."

We can represent this with another equation:

G - 8 = F

Now we have two equations with two variables, which we can solve using substitution or elimination. Let's use substitution:

We can substitute the expression we found for G in the first equation into the second equation:

G = 2F - 5 - 3

G = 2F - 8

Now we can substitute this expression for G into the second equation:

(2F - 8) - 8 = F

2F - 16 = F

F = 16

Now we can use either equation to find G:

G = 2F - 8

G = 2(16) - 8

G = 24

So Francis is 16 years old and Glen is 24 years old.


17. 1. Explain Charles's Law.2. What are the components used is Charles's law? 3. What are variable quantities in Charles's Law? 3. Cite an example wherein Charles's Law is seen.​


1. Explain Charles's Law.
1. Charles's law is an experimental gas law that describes how gases tend to expand when heated. A modern statement of Charles's law is: When the pressure on a sample of a dry gas is held constant, the Kelvin temperature and the volume will be in direct proportion.
2. What are the components used is Charles's law?
Charles's law—named for J. -A. -C. Charles (1746–1823)—states that, at constant pressure, the volume V of a gas is directly proportional to its absolute (Kelvin) temperature T, or V/T = k.
3. What are variable quantities in Charles's Law? Solution : Variables, Volume (V) and absolute temperature (T)
and Constants: Mass (m) and pressure (P) of the gas.
3. Cite an example wherein Charles's Law is seen.
Here are a few examples of circumstances where Charles' Law is in effect: When you take a basketball outside on a chilly day, the ball shrinks slightly as the temperature drops. This is true of any inflated object, which is why checking your car's tire pressure as the temperature drops is a smart idea.

28. 1.      Ano ang Transmedia ayon kay Henry Jenkins ? Magbigay ng midyum na maaaring gamitin sa pagbuo ng Transmedia . ​


Answer:

Ayon kay Henry Jenkins, isang sikat na propesor, manunulat, at media theorist, ang transmedia ay tumutukoy sa pagsasama-sama ng iba't ibang midya (media) upang makabuo ng isang cohesive at immersive na karanasan sa pagkonsumo ng media.

Explanation:

1.) Kahulugan ng Transmedia

- Ang Transmedia ay isang proseso ng paglikha ng mga kuwento at karanasan sa pamamagitan ng paggamit ng iba't ibang midyum at platform upang maabot ang iba't ibang uri ng mga manonood o mambabasa.

2.) Transmedia batay kay Henry Jenkins

- Ayon kay Henry Jenkins, ito ay isang paraan upang magamit ang mga teknolohiya upang maging mas immersive ang karanasan ng mga manonood at mambabasa.

3.) Halimbawa ng medium sa pagbuo ng Transmedia

- Maaaring gamitin sa pagbuo ng Transmedia ang iba't ibang midyum tulad ng pelikula, TV shows, komiks, musika, video games, social media, at iba pa. Ang mga midyum na ito ay maaaring magtulungan upang mapalawak at mapalalim ang kwento o karanasan ng mga manonood o mambabasa.

4.) Sa paanong paraan magagamit ang Transmedium

a.) Ang mga midyum ng Transmedia ay maaaring magtulungan upang mapalawak at mapalalim ang kwento o karanasan sa pamamagitan ng pagbibigay ng mga karagdagang impormasyon at detalye tungkol sa mga karakter, lugar, at pangyayari sa kwento.

Halimbawa:

- maaaring magkaroon ng isang pelikula at isang video game na may parehong kwento at mga karakter, ngunit mayroong karagdagang detalye at karanasan na maibibigay ang video game dahil sa interaktibong kalikasan nito.

b.) Ang paggamit ng iba't ibang midyum ng Transmedia ay maaari ring magbigay ng iba't ibang uri ng karanasan sa mga manonood o mambabasa.

Halimbawa:

- maaaring magkaroon ng isang online na komunidad kung saan ang mga manonood ay maaaring makipag-ugnayan sa mga karakter sa kwento, o maaaring magkaroon ng isang konsiyerto na may mga kanta na nagmumula sa soundtrack ng pelikula.

CONCLUSION:

Sa pamamagitan ng mga ganitong uri ng mga midyum at platform, maaaring maging mas immersive at nakakatugon sa iba't ibang uri ng mga manonood o mambabasa ang mga karanasan at kwento ng Transmedia.
