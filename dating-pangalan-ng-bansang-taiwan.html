1. dating pangalan ng taiwan


In the early times of discovery, it was named Formosa, next is Taiwan, People's Republic of China - Taiwan and finally at present time Taiwan.

It was first discover by a Portuguese sailor, govern under China. Since China's defeat by the Japanese Taiwan was colonized by Japan.

During the World War II, China won over Japan and recovered Taiwan as part of mainland China.

In mainland China, the Communist fought with the Nationalist and they won. It was then that the Nationalist people of China opted to move in Taiwan and established their own government and named it People's Republic of China - Taiwan. 

In our present time, it is commonly known and recognized as Taiwan with their own government and sovereignty.
