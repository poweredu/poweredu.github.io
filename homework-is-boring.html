18. Mikey worked on her mathematics homework for ¾ hour and her science homework for ⅞ hour. How long did she spend in all doing homework?​


Mikey spent 1 ⅝ hour on doing all her homework.

Step-by-step explanation:

Fraction Word Problem

The problem above is asking for the total number of hours that Mikey spent on doing all her homework. To find the answer, just add the amount of hour she spent on her Mathematics homework and her Science homework.

Given:

amount of time on Mathematics homework - ¾ hour

amount of time on Science homework - ⅞ hour

Equation:

¾ + ⅞ = n

Solution:

To add dissimilar fractions, make them similar by finding their GCF. Then, divide the GCF by the denominator, times the numerator.

¾ + ⅞ (LCD: 8)

= ‌6⁄8‌ + ⅞

= 13⁄8‌ or 1 5⁄8‌

Final Answer:

Mikey spent 1 ⅝ hour on doing all her homework.
