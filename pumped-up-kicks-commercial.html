19. why is the human heart called a double pump? A.its pump inside and outside, to circulate blood throughout the body.B.it pumps on upper and lower side to circulate blood throughout the body .C. it pumps alternating up and down to circulate blood throughout the body. D.its pumps on every side,the left and the right, to circulate blood throughout the body​


Answer:

A.Its pump inside and outside,to circulate blood throughout the body

Explanation:

Your heart is a single organ,but it acts as a double pump.

The first pump carries oxygen-poor blood to your lungs,where it unloads carbon dioxide and picks up oxygen.

It then delivers oxygen-rich blood to your heart.

The second pump delivers oxygen-rich blood to every part of your body.
