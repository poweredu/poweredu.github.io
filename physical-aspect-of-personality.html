30. 2. WHICH OF THE FOLLOWING IS NOT AN OBJECTIVE OF RECREATION? A. to revitalize financial aspect of a person C. to revitalize mental aspect of a person B. to revitalize social aspect of a person D. to revitalize physical aspect of a person​


Answer:

A. to revitalize financial aspect of a person is not an objective of recreation.

Explanation:

Recreation is primarily aimed at providing leisure activities and experiences that help individuals revitalize their physical, mental, social, and emotional aspects of life. While recreation may indirectly contribute to a person's financial well-being by providing opportunities for networking and skill development, it is not the primary objective.
