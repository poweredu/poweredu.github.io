Answer:

Step-by-step explanation:

The shortest route from Canine to Thirty – nine is Canine -> Lupine -> Bovine -> Thirty – nine, which is a total of 21 miles. From Canine, it is 3 miles to Lupine, then 9 miles to Bovine, and finally 9 miles to Thirty – nine.
