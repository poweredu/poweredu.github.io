2. give an idea about working students introduction in ppt​


Answer:

Here are some ideas for an introduction about working students in a PowerPoint presentation:

Start with a quote or a startling fact that captures the attention of your audience. For example, you could use a quote such as "I have never let my schooling interfere with my education" by Mark Twain to highlight the challenges faced by working students.

Give an overview of the topic and its significance. Introduce the idea of working students, and why it is an important topic to discuss. Explain that many students have to balance work and school, and this can be a significant challenge that affects their academic performance and well-being.

Provide context and background information. Share statistics on the number of working students in your country or region, and explain how this has changed over time. Talk about the reasons why students work while in school, such as to pay for tuition or support their families.

Discuss the benefits and challenges of being a working student. Explain that while working can provide financial support, it can also be stressful and time-consuming. Talk about the impact that working can have on academic performance, and how it affects the overall well-being of students.

Preview the main points you will cover in your presentation. Give your audience an idea of what to expect from the presentation, and how it will help them understand the topic of working students better.

By starting with a strong introduction, you can capture the attention of your audience and set the stage for an engaging and informative presentation.

14. environmental ethics ppt


Answer:

What is environmental ethics/what does it mean?

It is the discipline in philosophy that people study about the truthful relationship of human beings, and also the value/importance of the environment.

What is an example of environmental ethics?

Things like water and air pollution, the use of natural resources, loss of biodiversity, destruction of the community of living creatures, and global climate change are all part of the environmental ethics.

What is the purpose of environmental ethics?

The purpose of environmental ethics is that they maintain to provide moral grounds for social policies aimed at protecting the earth's environment and setting environmental humiliation/shame right.
