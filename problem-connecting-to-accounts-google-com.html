Prezi is a short term for “presentation” in Hungarian. It is a presentation software company founded in 2009. Presentations features a map-like overview, unlike a traditional slide based presentation. It allows users to pan between topics, and zoom in on details, and pull back to reveal context.

19. what problem do you think is more serious at Google: possible racial discrimination or possible reverse discrimination? why?


Answer:

Racial discrimination is the unfair treatment of individuals or groups on the basis of race or ethnicity. In the context of a workplace like Google, racial discrimination could lead to unequal opportunities or treatment for employees based on their race or ethnicity, such as hiring biases, pay disparities, or limited career advancement.

On the other hand, reverse discrimination is the unfair treatment of individuals or groups on the basis of their race, ethnicity, or gender, where individuals from underrepresented groups may receive preferential treatment or advantages in hiring or promotion decisions over individuals from more represented groups. While reverse discrimination is a controversial topic, some argue that it may be necessary to counteract the effects of past discrimination and create a more diverse and inclusive workplace.

Both racial discrimination and reverse discrimination can have serious impacts on a company's culture, employee morale, and reputation. It is important for companies like Google to strive for diversity, equity, and inclusion in their workforce while also ensuring fair and equal treatment for all employees regardless of their race, ethnicity, or gender.
