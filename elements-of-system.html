Elements of Computer System

-A computer is a system composed of major elements that work together to bring about expected process outcome.The computer system has the following element:hardware , software , peopleware and dataware.

Hardware- collectively refers to the physical electoric equipment which is the computer,including all equipment linked to it,including printers and scanners,as well as more fundamental components such as cabling.Software-is a program which consists of step-by-step intructions that tell the hardware how to perform a task.It coverts data into information.Peopleware-is the most important element in the computer system.It refers to people who operate the hardware,create the software,feed data into the system,and use information that comes to the system.Dataware-refers to the data that are used or evaluated to produce figures,concept,or instructions.
