11. In which province was Sala iti Mais originated​


Answer:

ISABELA PROVINCE

Explanation:

sana makatulong

In which province was Sala iti Mais originated?➡ Isabela province

Sala Ti Mais is occupational dance because it depicts the daily works of people inIsabela province which is farming and the corn is their major crop.

[tex] - Extra[/tex]

➡ Definition and Its Place of OriginSala Ti Mais is an Ilocano wordmeans “Dance of Corn” in English.

It is one of the Philippine Folk dance. This dance is performed at the Festival of Thanksgiving,where farmers pay tribute to corn.
