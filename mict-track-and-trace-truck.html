7. 2. A 2,000 - kg. Pick-up truck moves in a circular track of radius of 50m at 12m/s. Compute the centripetal force.​


[tex]\tt{\huge{\blue{Explanation:}}}[/tex]

The centripetal force exerted on an object is given by

[tex]\boxed{F_{c} = \frac{mv^{2}}{r}}[/tex]

where:

Fc = centripetal force

m = mass

v = velocity

r = radius

[tex]\tt{\huge{\red{Solution:}}}[/tex]

Solving for Fc

[tex]F_{c} = \dfrac{mv^{2}}{r}[/tex]

[tex]F_{c} = \dfrac{(\text{2000 kg})(\text{12 m/s})^{2}}{\text{50 m}}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{F_{c} = \text{5760 N}}[/tex]

Therefore, the centripetal force is 5760 N.

18. an empty truck with a mass of 2500 kg has an engine that will accelerate at rate of 1.5 m/s² to what will the force be if the track is caring and additional load of 1500 kg​


Given : An empty truck with a mass of 2500 kg has an engine that will accelerate at a rate of 1.5 m/s²

To Find : What will be the acceleration when the truck has an additional load of 1500 kg

Solution:

Force = mass * acceleration

Empty truck Mass = 2500 kg

acceleration = 1.5 m/s²

Force = 2500 * 1.5

=> Force = 3750 N

additional load of 1500 kg

=> Total Mass = 2500 + 1500 = 4000 kg

3750 = 4000 x a

=> a = 3750 /4000

=> a = 0.9375 m/s²
