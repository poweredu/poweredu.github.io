11. The circumference of a circular track is 154cm.Find the diameter of the track


Circumstances Formula:

C = 2πr

where:
C = 154cm
π = 22/7
r = ?

so,

C = 2πr
154 = 2(22/7)( r )
154 = 44/7 r
154 / 44/7 = 44/7 / 44/7 r
539/22 = r

the radius is 539/22

Diameter Formula:

D = 2r
D = 2(539/22)
D = 49 cm

Answer:

Three Track Events:

100m Sprint: This is a race where athletes sprint from a starting block to a finish line 100 meters away. The event is one of the most popular and exciting in track and field as it tests the speed, agility, and explosive power of the athletes.
110m Hurdles: In this event, athletes race across the track while jumping over 10 hurdles that are 1.067 meters tall. The event is a combination of speed and agility, and it requires athletes to have excellent technique and coordination to clear the hurdles while maintaining their speed.
1500m Run: This is a middle-distance race where athletes run 1500 meters, or roughly 0.93 miles. The event tests the endurance and pacing abilities of the athletes, as they must maintain a consistent pace throughout the race and have a strong finishing kick to outpace their competitors.

1. Benefits of Track Events:

Improved physical fitness: Track events are a great way to improve cardiovascular fitness, muscular endurance, and overall physical health.Increased mental toughness: Track events require athletes to push through pain and fatigue, which can help develop mental toughness and resilience.Opportunities for competition and teamwork: Track events provide opportunities for athletes to compete against others and work together as part of a team, fostering a sense of camaraderie and sportsmanship.

2. Most Popular Track Event in Olympics:

The 100m sprint is widely considered the most popular track event in the Olympics. This is due to the excitement and drama that comes with the short distance and the explosive speed required to win.

3. Most Popular Track Athlete:

Usain Bolt, from Jamaica, is often considered the most popular track athlete of all time. He won multiple gold medals in the 100m, 200m, and 4x100m relay events in three consecutive Olympics (2008, 2012, 2016), breaking world records and thrilling audiences with his impressive speed and charisma. His dominance in sprinting and his engaging personality made him a fan favorite and a global icon.

26. In order to track typhoon, what is to look for in the tracking data?​


Answer:

Tracking And Forecasting

In the first half of the 20th century the identification of tropical cyclones was based on changes in weather conditions, the state of the sea surface, and reports from areas that had already been affected by the storm. This method left little time for advance warning and contributed to high death tolls. Observation networks and techniques improved with time; with the advent of weather satellites in the 1960s, the early detection and tracking of tropical cyclones was greatly improved.
