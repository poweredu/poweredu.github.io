29. What can folk arts and crafts of Visayas and MIMAROPA tell about Filipinoculture and society?​


Answer:

FOLK ARTS AND DESIGN OF MIMAROPA

(Mindoro, Marinduque, Romblon, Palawan)

· Part of the Southern Tagalog; designated as Region IV-B

· Arts and crafts depict the different aspects of their culture.

MINDORO

• There are 7 ethnolinguistic groups living in Mindoro, collectively they called themselves Mangyans

• Hanunuo Mangyans, which means “truly/real/genuine”, live in the Southern part of Mindoro, while Iraya Mangyans are from Occidental Mindoro

• These groups are distinguished from each other based on their language, customs and their way of living

• The arts and crafts of the Mangyans display their very rich, artistic heritage
