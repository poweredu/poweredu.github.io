7. Andover inc. had a gross margin for the month of february totaling $42,000. they sold 5,000 units during the month at a sales price of $20 per unit. what was the amount of cost of goods sold for the month?


These formulas will be very much helpful:

Gross Margin = Sales - Cost of Goods Sold

Sales = Units sold x Selling Price

First, compute the amount of sales for the month. That is, 5,000 units times $20 per unit. This will give us $100,000.

Finally, use the gross margin formula to get the cost of goods sold.

Cost of Goods Sold = Sales - Gross Margin

That is, $100,000 minus $42,000. Therefore, cost of goods sold is $58,000.
