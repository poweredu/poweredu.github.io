9. Jaden and Abu want to solve the problem below. 1/3 divided by 3 They both get variations of the same answer. Abu gets 1/9 and Jaden gets 9. Who made the mistake? What did they do wrong?


Who made the mistake?

Jaden

What did they do wrong?

They used/got the reciprocal of the first fraction instead of the second, then multiplied the two numbers after.They did this:[tex] \frac{1}{3} \div \frac{3}{1} = \frac{3}{1} \times \frac{3}{1} = 9[/tex]Instead of this:[tex] \frac{1}{3} \div \frac{3}{1} = \frac{1}{3} \times \frac{1}{3} = \frac{1}{9} [/tex]

Abu did the latter and got it right.

The expression [tex]2(d+3)-2[/tex] counts as one factor. It's also the same for [tex]3(d+3)-15[/tex]. Since these two factors are different, they can't be cancelled out.

Cancelling out [tex]d+3[/tex] on both the numerator and the denominator is like cancelling out [tex]1[/tex] from [tex]\frac{1+2}{1}[/tex]; You will get the wrong solution.


23. The ratio of jayden's height to rachel's height is 5:8. If rachel is 57 centimeters taller than jaden, find jaydens height


Answer:

95cm

Step-by-step explanation:

5:8=n:n+57

Cross multiply

8*n=8n

5*(n+57)=5n+285

8n=5n+285

Transpose the number with common variable

Note: when transposing the sign changes

8n-5n=285

3n=285

Divide both sides by 3 to have n

3n/3=285/3

n=95(Jayden)

95+57=152(rachel)
