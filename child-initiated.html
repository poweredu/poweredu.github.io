26. SELECTED HEALTH INITIATIVE IN THE PHLIPINE CONTEX1.)URBANIZATION AND GLOBALIZATION2.)CHILD NUTRITION PROGRAMSPLEASE STATE THEIR ISSUES /PROBLEMS IN THE IMPLEMENTATION​


Answer:

Urbanization and Globalization:

Issue/Problem in Implementation:

Uneven distribution of health resources between urban and rural areas, resulting in inadequate access to healthcare in rural areas.

Insufficient infrastructure and resources to address the health needs of the growing urban population, particularly in informal settlements and slum areas.

Lack of coordination between national and local government units, as well as among various sectors, leading to fragmentation and duplication of efforts.

The growing burden of non-communicable diseases such as diabetes and cardiovascular diseases due to urban lifestyles, which requires a shift from curative to preventive measures.

The effects of globalization, such as the influx of unhealthy food products and lifestyles, and the challenges of regulating and monitoring these trends.

Child Nutrition Programs:

Issue/Problem in Implementation:

Insufficient funding and resources for child nutrition programs, particularly in marginalized and remote areas.

Limited access to nutritious foods, particularly in areas with low agricultural productivity and limited market access.

Cultural and behavioral factors, such as traditional beliefs and practices that may hinder the adoption of new and healthy dietary practices.

Lack of community participation and ownership in the design and implementation of child nutrition programs, which can lead to a lack of sustainability and effectiveness.

Inadequate monitoring and evaluation of program outcomes, which may hinder the ability to identify and address issues in a timely manner.
