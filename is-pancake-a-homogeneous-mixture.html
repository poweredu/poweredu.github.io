20. easter has a recipe for pancakes he can make pancakes with 5 inches in diameter what is the area of the pancake​


Answer:

Given: Diameter = 5 inches, then Radius = 2.5 inches

Formula for area: A=πr²

Solution:

A = πr²

A = (3.14) (2.5)²

A = (3.14) (6.25)

A = 9.625 in²

The area of the pancake that Easter can make is 9.625 in².
