20. Heaven is twice as old as Jessica. The sum of their ages is 72. How old is Jessica?


Answer:

Heaven = 2x

Jessica = x

2x + x = 72

3x = 72

3x/3 = 72/3

x = 24

Hence Jessica is 24 years old.
