It is very popular especially in the US where it is considered the national game. Countries like Japan and South Korea also play this game.

In this sport, the player should hit the ball that throws the pitcher and then to run the field in the clockwise to run through the four bases that are marked on the field. The goal of baseball is to prevent a player from the rival team from reaching the base hitting the ball.
