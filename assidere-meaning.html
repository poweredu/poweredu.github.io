If repeated random samples of a given size n are taken from a population of values for a quantitative variable, where the population mean is μ (mu) and the population standard deviation is σ (sigma) then the mean of all sample means (x-bars) is population mean μ (mu).

18. If the textual aid is removed from the article will the imformation be aesier to decipher why or why not ​


Textual aids are accompanying texts, images, graphs, or charts that are used to provide additional information or to provide information that can easily be picked up at a glance.

If textual aids are removed, an article may still be understood by the reader. However, the level of understanding will also depend on the level of comprehension and existing knowledge of the reader on the subject matter.

For some, it will not affect how they understand an article. For others who are not familiar with the topic or have low reading comprehension, it will make it harder for them to understand the article.
