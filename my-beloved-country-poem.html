9. Tone of the poem Beloved by Usman Awang​


Answer:

Romantic and nostalgic

Explanation:

The tone of the poem "Beloved" by Usman Awang is romantic and nostalgic. Throughout the poem, the speaker expresses deep love and longing for their beloved, using metaphors and repetition to emphasize their feelings. The tone is passionate and emotional, as the speaker describes their beloved's beauty and mystery. However, there is also a sense of sadness and melancholy in the tone, as the speaker is unable to be with their beloved and must content themselves with memories and dreams. Overall, the tone of the poem is one of intense and bittersweet longing, as the speaker expresses their love for their beloved.
