6. joshua is a year older than his sister mckay. The product of their ages is 33 more tha 7 times joshua's age. How old is joshua and mckay


Joshua is 11 years old and Mckay is 10 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Age Word Problem

The problem is asking for Joshua and Mckay's ages. To find the answer, you have to identify the known and unknown in the problem and express them in an algebraic equation. The algebraic equation refers to the statement of equality of two algebraic expressions. The algebraic expression is a combination of operations, variables and constants.

Given:

Joshua's age - a year older than his sister Mckay

product of their ages - 33 more than 7 times of Joshua's age

Equation:

Let "x" be Mckay's age and "x + 1" be Joushua's age.

x (x + 1) = 33 + 7 (x + 1)

Solution:

x (x + 1) = 33 + 7 (x + 1)

x² + x = 33 + 7x + 7

x² + x = 40 + 7x

(Equate to 0)

x² + x - 7x - 40 = 0

x² - 6x - 40 = 0

(Factor out)

(x - 10) (x + 4)

x = 10 ; x = -4

There are two possible values of x, 10 and -4. Since the problem is asking for their ages, use the positive value of x which is 10. Therefore, Mckay is 10 years old. You can now find Joshua's age.

Joshua = x + 1

= 10 + 1

= 11

Final Answer:

Joshua is 11 years old and Mckay is 10 years old.

Checking:

To check if the answers are correct, plug in the value.

x (x + 1) = 33 + 7 (x + 1)

10 (10 + 1) = 33 + 7 (10 + 1)

10 (11) = 33 + 7 (11)

Answer:

Step-by-step explanation:

37+8 = 35

Angela is 35 yrs old while Joshua is 27 yrs old

Problem Solving

Statement of the Problem

Angelo is eight years older than Joshua. If sum of their ages is 62, find their ages.

Answer

Le t x be Joshua's age ; x = Joshua's age

It is stated that Angelo is 8 years older than Joshua.

Thus, x + 8 = Angelo's age

The sum of their ages id 62, hence,

Joshua's age + Angelo's age = 62

Substituting Joshua and Angelo's ages,

Joshua's age + Angelo's age = 62

x + (x + 8) = 62 ; add the variables

2x + 8 = 62 ; subtract both sides by 8

2x + 8 - 8 = 62 - 8 ; solve

2x = 54 ; divide both sides by 2

2x/2 = 54/2 ; solve

x = 27

Therefore, Joshua's age is 28.

Finding Angelo's age,

Angelo's age = x + 8

Angelo's age = 28 + 8

Angelo's age = 36

Therefore, Angelo's age is 36.
