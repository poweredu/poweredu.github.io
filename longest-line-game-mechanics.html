23. Which of the following describes a tangent line?

a. a tangent line is a line on the same plane of the circle that intersects the circle at exactly one point

Explanation: Yes, it is true. Tangent is a line that intersects a circle at exactly one point or touches the circle at one point. The point that it intersected is called point of tangency.

b. a tangent line is a longest chord of a circle

Explanation: It cannot be the longest chord because it do not intersect at two points. Meaning, the tangent line will forever be outside of the circle.

c. a tangent line is a drawn from a circle of a circle to any point on the circle

Explanation: No, because it only touches at one point of the circle.

d. a tangent line is a line that passes through the center of a circle​

Explanation: No, it do not pass at two points of the circle so it is impossible to pass through the center of the circle.
