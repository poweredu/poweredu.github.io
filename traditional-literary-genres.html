What is English literature?

English literature refers to the collection of written literary work in the Great Britain and its colonies since the 7th century to the present day. The most known features of English literature include its wit, depiction of manners, disparity between classes, themes being stressed on plots and characterization.

What is American Literature?

Comparatively, American literature is a notion that emerged in the recent past. It is the production of literary work written in the context of America portraying American culture and themes.
