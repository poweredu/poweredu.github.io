What is a Riddle?

A riddle is a word game in which one player asks a confusing question, and the other play has to guess its meaning. The key to solving the riddle is realizing that one of the words is used for surprising in an unexpected way.

What are Two Types of Riddles?

We have to remember that a riddle is a question that has a double meaning. There are two types of riddles: Enigmas and conundra.

1. Enigma → is a riddle that is expressed metaphorically. We have to think carefully about the riddle and come up with the answer.

2. Conundrum → is a riddle that opens the question or the answer.

What is the Importance of Riddles?

Riddle is made because it is for fun. In ancient times, society used riddles as a test of intelligence and smartness. But nowadays, Riddles are used only for playing with friends and family. The more riddles we solve, the faster our brain processes something. It means it is getting better at processing words and ideas.
