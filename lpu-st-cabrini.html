8. The geographical distribution of hometown of some 80 students at LPU is given as: 50 from Cavite, 10 from Laguna, and 20 from Manila. Suppose three students are selected. Find the probability that no student comes from Cavite.


Answer:

203/4108 (or 0.24)


Step-by-step explanation:

Total number of students = 80


Total number of students from Cavite = 50

Total number of students not from Cavite = 80 - 50 = 30


P(3 students not from Cavite) = (30/80)(29/79)(28/78) = 203/4108  or 0.24


Answer: The probability is 203/4108 (or 0.24)
