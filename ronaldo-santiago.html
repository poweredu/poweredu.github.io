23. V. Solve the1. Ronaldo took a handful of change out of his pocket and noticed that he was only holding five-peso coins and ten-peso coins in his hand. He counted and found that he had 21 coins thatamounted to Php 180. 0. How many five-peso and ten-peso coins does Ronaldo have?​.


Answer:

Let

x = # of pcs of P5 coins

y = # of pcs of P10 coins

Conditions:

x + y = 21 ===> combination # of pcs of total coins

so we can also say:

x = 21 - y

or

y = 21 - x

We know that the total amount of combination of coins amount to P180.00

so we can say that,

5x + 10y = 180

but

y = 21 - x (or use x = 21 - y)

so,

5x + 10(21 - x) = 180

5x + 210 - 10x = 180

-5x = 180 - 210

-5x = -30

x = -30/-5

x = 6

solve for y,

y = 21 - x

y = 21 - 6

y = 15

Therefore there are 6 pcs. P5 coins and 15 pcs. P10 coins.

Check Answer:

(6 x P5) + (15 x P10) = P180

P30 + P150 = P180

P180 = P180 ✓✓✓ True

Hope Helps!
