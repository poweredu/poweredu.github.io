24. I'm your point of view, discuss the effects of global stratification on the Philippine economy ​


Answer:

Global stratification has had both positive and negative effects on the Philippine economy.

Positive effects:

Boost in foreign investments and remittances from overseas workers, contributing to economic growth.

Expansion of business process outsourcing (BPO) and call center industries, creating jobs.

Access to global markets and opportunities for trade and commerce.

Negative effects:

Widening income inequality, with a small portion of the population benefiting from globalization while the majority remains poor.

Dependence on foreign investments and remittances, making the economy vulnerable to global economic shocks.

Outflow of skilled professionals and brain drain, affecting human capital development.

Overall, the impact of global stratification on the Philippine economy has been mixed, with both positive and negative effects. To address the negative effects, there is a need for government policies to promote inclusive economic growth and address issues of inequality.
