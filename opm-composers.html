28. Describe what OPM is


OPM or Original Pilipino Music/ Original Philippine Music is the leading contemporary Philippine music. This type of music was originally used to refer Philippine pop songs such as ballads. The 1980's to 1990's has been regarded as the Golden Era of Philippine ballads. Ryan Cayabyab, George Casenco, Wilie Cruz, Jose Mari Chan and Gary Valenciano had led the OPM to its peak. The compositions followed western type of melody while portraying a unique Filipino message in every song. Today, OPM is not only confined to Tagalog and English songs but has included other ehtno-linguistic group songs such as Kapampangan, Ilocano, Visaya and Bikolano.
