10. A zoo has 151515 Emperor penguins. The Emperor penguins make up 30\%30%30, percent of all the penguins at the zoo. How many penguins live at the zoo?


Answer:

50 penguins

Step-by-step explanation:

Percent means per 100. Percentages can be converted into its fraction or decimal forms. For example, 1% can be express as 1/100 or 0.01. When converting percentages into fractions, take note that the denominator is always 100.

So, onto the problem. The problem asks for the total number of penguins present in the zoo if the Emperor penguins make up 15% of the total.

So, we are looking for a number whose 30% is 15. Mathematically, we have,

30% (X)=15 wherein X is the total number of penguins in the zoo

0.30 (X)=15

X=15/0.30

X=50

Or we can also express the percentage as fraction,

30/100 (X)=15

X=(15*100)/30

X=50

Therefore, the zoo has a total of 50 penguins wherein 15 of those are Emperor penguins.

30. in a zoo there are 2 lions to every 5 monkeys. How many lions are there if the zoo has 45 monkeys?


Answer:


There are 18 lions if the zoo has 45 monkeys.


Solution:


2 : 5
n : 45


or


[tex] \frac{2}{5} = \frac{n}{45} [/tex]

Cross multiply.


2 × 45 = 90


5 × n = 5n


5n = 90


[tex] \frac{5n}{5} = \frac{90}{5} [/tex]

[tex]n = 18[/tex]

SOLUTION:

Proportion:

2:5 = x:45

(2)(45) = (5)(x)

90 = 5x

[tex]\frac{5x}{5}[/tex] = [tex]\frac{90}{5}[/tex]

x = 18


ANSWER: There are 18 lions in 45 monkeys.
