characters: these are the people, animals, or anything personified where the story revolves. They keep the story flowing. This answers the who question

setting:This presents the location, time, social, and cultural conditions in which the characters exist. This answers the where and when questions



Plot: This cosists of the sequence of events that happen in the story

(PARTS OF PLOT)

intruduction— this is where the story takes off
rising action— this is where the problem or conflict starts to build up
climax— this is the highest point of the story or the “moment of truth”
faling action— this is where the problem is slowly being resolved
resolution—this is the closure or where th problem comes to an end

This answers the what happened and what is the problem questions.


Theme: This is the central argument or the universal truth presented it the story
This answers the what is the author trying to convey question or the big idea
