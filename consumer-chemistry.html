17. What is chemistry? Are there types of chemistry? ​


Chemistry is a branch of Science that deals with matter and its composition, reactions with other elements or substances, structures, changes in form and state, and unique behaviors amongst others. It involves the study of elements and compounds composed of atoms, molecules, and ions.

Chemistry has a lot of types but the 5 main types are organic chemistry, inorganic chemistry, analytical chemistry, physical chemistry, and biochemistry.

Organic Chemistry - the study of carbon-containing compounds Inorganic Chemistry - deals with the behavior of inorganic and organometallic compounds Analytical Chemistry - science of determining what is really matter and its limits. Physical Chemistry - the type of Chemistry relating to Physics. Biochemistry - study of chemical processes and how it affects living organisims.
