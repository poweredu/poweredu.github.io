5. What is the significance of the Magtanim ay di biro Song?​


Answer:

The concept of the song portrays the hardship and nobility of the farmers. Farmers are the persons that planting crops for foods. They are diligent in waking up in the early morning and starting on transplanting. Transplanting is the most commonly use method in planting. They are transplanting under the sunshine while bowing their head for a long time. It is very exhausting, having a numbness sensation, and easily to get fatigued.

Magtanim Ay 'Di Biro (sometimes spelt Magtanim Hindi Biro; " transl. "Planting is not a joke") is a popular Tagalog folk song composed by Felipe de León. The song tells of the struggles of farmers, how one must twist and bend to plant rice in the muddy paddies all day, with no chance to sit nor stand.

20. what is the concept song of the magtanim ay di biro


Answer:

The concept of the song portrays the hardship and nobility of the farmers. Farmers are the persons that planting crops for foods. They are diligent in waking up in the early morning and starting on transplanting. Transplanting is the most commonly use method in planting. They are transplanting under the sunshine while bowing their head for a long time. It is very exhausting, having a numbness sensation, and easily to get fatigued.

The dedication and sacrifices of the farmers fed thousands of people all over the country. The author of the song depicts that being a farmer is a noble and honorable work. Author said that being a farmer isn't a joke and we shouldn't take this situation or their life status into a grant. We should give importance their hardship to the work.
