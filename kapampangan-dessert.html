23. differentiate baked dessert from fried dessert. ​


Answer:

Baked desserts are hot desserts made by putting ingredients in a hot oven. These are cakes and muffins, sweet breads such as banana bread and raisin bread, cookies, chocolate chip cookies, puddings and custards. However, fried desserts are made through deep frying.
