19. What is the Procedure of Monggo Plants


If what you mean is "what is the procedure in plating monggo plants", here is it.

What you will need first
- Pots, or simply bottles, cut it then it will serve as your pot.
- Monggo Seeds
- Water and Watering Can
- Seed compost 
- Soil

Procedure
Place the soil in the pot
Put the monggo seed/s in the soil, not to deep and not to shallow
Water it every morning/late afternoon/evening, avoid watering it on the noon or else the 30% of water will evaporate from the heat of the sun.
Put fertilizers, or seed compost for better plant.
