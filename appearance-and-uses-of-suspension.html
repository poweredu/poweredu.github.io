1. What is The Appearance and uses of suspensions.​


Answer:

One way to tell is by looking at the physical characteristics. Homogeneous solutions (like water, milk) are uniform in color, meaning you will not see particles floating in the liquid. Suspensions (heterogeneous solutions) are non-uniform in color and will sometimes look cloudy or murky, like muddy water.
