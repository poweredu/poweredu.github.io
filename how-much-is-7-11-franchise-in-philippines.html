20. The gross sales of a well-known fast food chain from four of its franchises are as follows: P326,578; ₱402,493; ₱377,802; and ₱450,128. How much is the estimated gross sales of four stores for the day?​


Answer:

To find the estimated gross sales of four stores for the day, you need to add up the individual gross sales of each store.

P326,578 + ₱402,493 + ₱377,802 + ₱450,128 = ₱1,556,001

So the estimated gross sales of four stores for the day is ₱1,556,001.
