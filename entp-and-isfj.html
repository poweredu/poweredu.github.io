16. What are the things that you can do to minimize the effects to your weaknesses as a consul or ESFJ​


Answer:

As a Consul or ESFJ, some things you can do to minimize the effects of your weaknesses include:

Working on developing your assertiveness and ability to speak up for yourself and your needs.

Practicing active listening and being more aware of the needs and perspectives of others.

Setting boundaries and learning to say "no" when necessary.

Recognizing and acknowledging your own feelings and emotions, rather than repressing them or ignoring them.

Taking time for self-care and self-reflection to better understand yourself and your motivations.

Building a support system of friends and family who can help you balance your tendency to put the needs of others before your own.

Seeking out new experiences and challenges to help you grow and develop as an individual.

Seeking feedback from others and being open to constructive criticism.

Practice flexibility, as it may help you be more adaptable to change, uncertainty, and diversity of opinion

Be open to learning new skills, perspectives and ways of thinking, as it may help you broaden your perspective.
