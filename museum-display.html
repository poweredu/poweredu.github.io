19. Why most of the Egyptians did not allow to see the display artwork in the museum?​


Answer:

As far as we know, the ancient Egyptian artists had no word that corresponded exactly to our abstract use of the word 'art'. They had words for individual types of monuments that we today regard as examples of Egyptian art - 'statue', 'stela', 'tomb' -but there is no reason to believe that these words necessarily included an aesthetic dimension in their meaning.
