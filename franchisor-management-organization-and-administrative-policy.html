5. what would be the effect of this COVID fever on administrative organization, personnel management, financial and behavioral management of the said hospital?​


Answer:

The effect of COVID fever on the administrative organization, personnel management, financial and behavioral management of a hospital would be significant. The hospital would need to develop and implement new policies and procedures to ensure the safety of its staff and patients, such as the use of personal protective equipment, social distancing, and regular disinfection of high-touch surfaces.

Personnel management would also be affected as the hospital would need to ensure that its staff members are trained on the new policies and procedures and are equipped with the necessary resources to carry out their duties safely. Additionally, the hospital may need to hire additional staff to manage the increased workload and demands placed on the healthcare system due to the pandemic.

Financial management would be affected as the hospital would need to allocate resources to respond to the pandemic, such as purchasing additional medical equipment and supplies, hiring additional staff, and providing hazard pay to frontline workers. The hospital may also experience financial challenges due to a decrease in elective procedures and a decrease in patient visits.

Behavioral management would also be affected as the hospital would need to work to maintain the mental health and well-being of its staff and patients during this challenging time. This may involve providing counseling services to staff and patients, promoting healthy coping mechanisms, and creating a supportive work environment.

12. Public policy and program administration


The scientific study of the politics of institutions, structures, and organizations is known as public policy or public administration. The study of public policy is primarily concerned with the evaluation and justification of governmental and non-governmental responses to social issues. It aims to clarify how institutions, groups, and the general public collaborate with various forms of government to create the policies they do. It is also interested in how these policies are evaluated and how they affect the populace, governmental bodies, and organized interests.

The creation of three different sorts of policies distributive, regulatory, and re-distributive related to the decision-making process can be characterized as public policy. The collection of functions, institutions, processes, and other elements that make up public administration. The creation and, more specifically, execution of public policy, as well as the analysis of the choices and methods involved in that process, are all parts of public administration. Additionally, public administration can be described as a profession, academic topic of study, and public initiatives and projects. In the creation of governmental policy and public administration, there is no distinct line separating administration from politics. In addition to making judgments about how to implement the laws, administrators also recommend legislation.

Contingency Theory​


Answer:

A.Classic Theory

Explanation:

Some of the elements of classical theory are still with us, in one form or another:

(a) Inter-related functions- Management consists of several inter-related and inter-dependent functions such as planning, organising, staffing, directing and controlling.

(b) Universal principles of management- It is possible to understand, study and practice management quite effortlessly and to facilitate this classical writers (Taylor, Fayol, Weber etc.) developed certain principles—purely based on experience.

c) Bureaucratic structure- Traditional theory prescribed that organisations be built around work to be done. Work is cut into small parcels and handed over to people having requisite qualifications. The work is supervised and controlled by a common superior following certain rules and regulations.

(d) Reward-punishment nexus- “Follow the rules, obey the orders, show the results and get the rewards” Juicy carrots for those who run the race ahead of others and those who fall behind are severely penalized. Great emphasis was also put on efficient use of resources while producing results.

15. TRADITIONAL REPRESENTATION OF THE DIFFERENCES BETWEEN POLITICS AND ADMINISTRATIONthe representation of the differences between policy and administration begs the question of the level of precision, specificity, and detail in policy. briefly explain ​


Answer:

The traditional representation of the differences between politics and administration suggests that politics is concerned with the formulation of policies, while administration is responsible for their implementation. This distinction implies that policy is a broad, general framework that provides direction and guidance for administrative action, while administration involves the concrete, detailed tasks of carrying out the policy.

However, this representation raises questions about the level of precision, specificity, and detail in policy. While policy is often framed in general terms, it must also be sufficiently specific and detailed to provide clear guidance to administrators. Without specific guidance, administrators may have difficulty implementing policy effectively or may interpret it in ways that are inconsistent with the intent of policymakers.

Thus, while there is a difference between policy and administration, the two are intimately linked, and policies must be formulated with sufficient detail and specificity to guide effective implementation by administrators.
