The school will be the heart of the formal education system. This is where children learn. Schools will have one goal of providing the best possible basic education for all students.

Management of basic education will begin at the national level. In regions, divisions, schools, and learning centers—referred to here as field offices—where basic education management policies and principles are transl4ted into programs, projects, and services developed, developed, and offered to match local needs.

The State will encourage local initiatives for improving the quality of basic education.
