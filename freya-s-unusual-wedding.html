20. Miko and Freya saved the same amount of money.After Miko lent $73 to Freya.Freya had 5 times as much money as Miko.How much did freya have in the end


Answer: 182.5

Step-by-step explanation:

M = Miko’s money F = Freya’s money

M = F

5 ( M - 73) = F + 73

You can use substitution

5 (F - 73) = F + 73

5F - 365 = F + 73

Subtract F from both sides

4F - 365 = 73

Add 365 to both sides

4F = 438

Divide both sides by 4 (to get the F by itself)

F = 109.5 (that is how much Freya had in the beginning)

M = F

M = 109.5 (that is how much Miko had in the beginning)

Miko lent $73 to Freya

Freya: 109.5 + 73 = 182.5 (how much she had after Miko lent to her)

Miko: 109.5 - 73 = 36.5 (how much he has after he lent to Freya)

To make sure it is correct:

36.5 times 5 = 182.5
