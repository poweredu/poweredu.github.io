1. is there a prefix in regulatory?​


Answer:

When you break the word unregulated down, it's easy to figure out what it means. The prefix un- reverses an action, so here it means “not.” Then you have regulated, which describes something that has rules or people who check that things are working properly

Answer:

Regulatori

ang wika ay may kakayahang makaimpluwensiya at magkontrol sa pag-uugali ng iba.
