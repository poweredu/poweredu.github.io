1. What is Philippine Electronics Code ?


Answer:

PURPOSE OF Philippine Electrical Code (PEC).

The primary objective of the code is to establish basic materials quality and electrical works standards for the safe use of electricity for light, heat, power, communications, signaling and for other purposes.

2. In philippine electronics code, the term "should or should not" are _________.


Permissive Rules

In the Philippine Electronics code, the term "should" or "should not" are called permissive rules. These are used to identify actions or activities that are not required but allowed.

What is permissive?

The word permissive also means optional or not compulsory. We use the term should and should not to identify which methods of ideas and actions are allowed and which are not. They are often associated with the line 'should be permitted' and 'should not be permitted.'

Should be permitted

When the ideas, concepts, or methods are allowed or permitted. Also, these actions are not required and compulsory but they still need to ask permission if the superior approves or not.

Should not be permitted

This refers to the ideas, concepts, and methods that are not allowed to do to try, When an employee or student suggests an alternative method and the superior will not allow, they include it in the list of should not be permitted concepts.

6. what is the importance of the philippine electronics code in the product assembly of workplace​


Answer:

The importance of the PEC(Philippine electronic code) is that it Protects against static electricity with its attendant shock and fire hazards. No cut-out switch or fuse shall be placed in the ground lead and it Keeps the voltage to ground of metallic enclosures and other structures.
