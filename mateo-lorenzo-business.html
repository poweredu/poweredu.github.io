5. What is the summary story of saint lorenzo?​


Answer:

Lorenzo Ruiz was born in Manila in 1594 to a Chinese father and a Filipino mother who were both Catholic. He was falsely accused of killing a Spaniard in 1636 and sought asylum in Japan. He and his companions were tortured by being hung upside-down over a pit. Despite his suffering, Lorenzo refused to renounce Christianity and died from eventual blood loss and suffocation. His body was cremated, with the ashes thrown into the sea. He married a native and had two sons and a daughter. Lorenzo was a member of the Cofradia del Santísimo Rosario (Confraternity of the Most Holy Rosary) and a scrivener. He died in Japan in 1637, aged 42, after being tortured by the Tokugawa Shogunate. The method of torture was alleged to have been extremely painful, with one hand left free so that the individual may signal their desire to recant, leading to their release.

7. Write a reflection of Saint Lorenzo​


Saint Lorenzo was a Christian deacon and martyr who lived in the 3rd century AD. He is venerated as a saint by the Catholic and Orthodox Churches.

According to tradition, Saint Lorenzo was serving as a deacon in Rome during the persecution of Christians under the Roman Emperor Valerian. He was arrested and sentenced to death by being roasted on a gridiron, which became one of his most commonly depicted symbols.

Saint Lorenzo is remembered for his bravery and faith in the face of persecution and suffering. His feast day is celebrated on August 10th, and he is the patron saint of cooks and librarians.

His legacy has inspired many works of art, including paintings, sculptures, and stained glass windows. He serves as a reminder of the importance of standing up for one's beliefs and the power of faith in difficult times.

15. talambuhay ni lorenzo ruiz​


Answer:

Lorenzo Ruiz (Filipino: Lorenzo Ruiz ng Maynila; Chinese: 李樂倫; Spanish: Lorenzo Ruiz de Manila; Latin: Laurentius Ruiz Manilensis; November 28, 1594 – September 29, 1637), also called Saint Lorenzo of Manila, is a Filipino saint venerated in the Catholic Church. Matinding hirap ang dinanas ni Lorenzo Ruiz sa kamay ng mga tagausig na Hapon dahil ipinakita niya ang matatag niyang pananampalataya sa Panginoon. Sinabi niyang walang kailangan kung ihandog man niya ang nalalabi niyang buhay sa Diyos.

27. who is Lorenzo monaco ?


Answer:

Lorenzo Monaco (born Piero di Giovanni; c. 1370 – c. 1425) was an Italian painter of the late Gothic-early Renaissance age. He was born Piero di Giovanni in Siena.

In 1390 he joined the Camaldolese monastery of Santa Maria degli Angeli. He was thenceforth generally known as Lorenzo Monaco (English: "Lawrence the Monk").

In 1414 he painted the Coronation of the Virgin (now at the Uffizi), characterized by a great number of saints and brilliant colours.
