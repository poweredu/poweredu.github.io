10. Direction:Using venn Diagram compare and contrast saturated and super saturated solutions by giving the similarities and differences."SATURATED"."SUPER SATURATED"


SATURATED

• A saturated solution is a solution is a chemical solution containing the maximum concentration of a solute dissolved in the solvent

• Has the maximum amount of solutes that can be dissolved in the solvent at room temperature

• Can be prepared by evaporating a solvent from a solution until the solute begins to crystallize or precipitate

• Does not show precipitation at the bottom of the container

SUPERSATURATED

• A supersaturated solution is a solution which contains more dissolved solute than could ordinarily dissolve into the solvent

• Has more than the maximum amount of solutes that can be dissolved in the solvent at room temperature

• Can be prepared by adding the solute in excess at a high temperature and suddenly cooling down at a rate that is faster than solute's precipitation

• Shows precipitation at the bottom of the container if the physical conditions are changed
