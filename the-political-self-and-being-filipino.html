12. How does being a Filipino shape your political self-concept?​


Being a Filipino shapes my political self-concept in several ways. First, it gives me a sense of national identity and belonging. Second, it instills in me a pride in my country and its history. Third, it makes me aware of the political process and the importance of participating in it. Fourth, it teaches me the value of democracy and the importance of upholding it. Fifth, it helps me understand the dynamics of power and how it can be used for good or for ill. Sixth, it hones my critical thinking skills and helps me develop a well-rounded perspective on current affairs. Seventh, it gives me a sense of hope and optimism for the future of my country.
