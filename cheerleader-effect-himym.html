19. in a cheerleading pyramid created by one team, there are 4 cheerleaders on the level, 3 on the third, and 2 in the fourth. If there are five levels, how many cheerleaders are there at the bottom? find the general term of the sequence.


Answer:

5 cheerleaders at the bottom

the number of cheerleaders at the bottom is just equal to the total number of levels.

General term:

x-1=y-z

Step-by-step explanation:

x is the level

y is the number of people at the bottom

z is the number of people on level x

Example: 3 cheerleaders on the 3rd.

x=3 and z=3 and y is unknown.

x-1=y-z

3-1=y-3

2=y-3

5=y

Or

x-1=y-z

4-1=y-2

3+2=y

5=y
