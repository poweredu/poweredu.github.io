1.  Anung english ng Kinikilig ?


depends on the way you're going to use it
if it means the kinikilig with someone, it means having butterflies in your stomach, twitterpatted, love-struck or smitten
if it means the kinikiling whenever you taste sinigang (omgangsarapnito) or feel cold, it means shivering or shudderinggiggle or giggling ang sagot

2. kinikilig in Englishkinilig in Englishkilig in Englishplease answer it correctly thank you​


There is no clear definition of the concept or a definite translation into English. Some of the not so accurate translations include "giddiness," "shudder," "tremble," "tingle" and "thrill." The word is much closer to the idiomatic expression "tickle pink."

Halimbawa nito ay ang pakikipag-usap sa ating kinahuhumalingang tao o sa ingles ay tinatawag ding “crush”.

Madaming bagay at gawain ang nakakapagpadama sa atin ng kilig.
