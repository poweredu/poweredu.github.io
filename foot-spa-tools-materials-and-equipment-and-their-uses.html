Is a small open-top, rounded cup like container used for mixing the aromatic oils and other fluids for the hand or foot spa.

Pumice Stone

A type of volcanic rock, which is actually frothy lava soldified to form a porous rock to remove dry and annoying skin as well as callouses.

Alcohol

An organic compound used as sanitizer.

Antiseptic Solution

An agent that reduces or prevents the multiplication of microorganisms.

Body Scrub

Is a mixture of natural ingredients to keep feet soft, smooth and moisturized and to removed dry skin cells.

Cling Wrap

A thin plastic film used for sealing the paraffin during a hand spa.

First Aid Kit

Is a collection of supplies for use in giving first aid.

Lotion

Is used to soften and moisturize the skin.

Mittens

Are slips over plastic liners to increase heat retention when used with paraffin.

22. How important it is to sanitize, disinfect tools and equipment used in foot spa​


Answer:

Sanitizing nailcare tools should be a habit because these tools are shared among a number of people. By proper sanitation, it prevents the spread of fungal and bacterial diseases from one person to the other person. It kills the leftover pathogen on the tools and ensures the safety of using the tools.

Nail Technicians need to be concerned with fungi and bacterial diseases and the proper sanitation and disinfection procedures that help prevent the spread of diseases from one client to another. All foot spa systems must be drained, cleaned and disinfected between customers and at the end of each day.

28. task. tools used material used equipment 1. foot spa 2.Frimming nailas 3.cleaning and decorating the nails.4Hand spa 5.srerilizing tools ​


Answer:

Foot Spa:

Task: Pampering and relaxing the feet by soaking them in warm water and using different tools to clean and massage them.

Tools Used: Foot basin, nail clippers, foot scrub, pumice stone, lotion, essential oils, towel.

Materials Used: Warm water, epsom salt, essential oils, foot cream.

Equipment: Foot Spa machine.

Filing Nails:

Task: Shaping and smoothing the nails to give them a neat appearance.

Tools Used: Nail filer, nail buffer, cuticle pusher, nail polish remover.

Materials Used: Nail polish, nail polish remover, cuticle oil, lotion.

Equipment: Nail filer machine.

Cleaning and Decorating Nails:

Task: Cleaning the nails by removing dirt and applying nail polish for a more attractive appearance.

Tools Used: Nail brush, cuticle pusher, nail polish remover, nail clippers, nail file, nail polish.

Materials Used: Nail polish, nail polish remover, cuticle oil, lotion.

Equipment: Nail polish machine.

Hand Spa:

Task: Pampering and relaxing the hands by soaking them in warm water and using different tools to clean and massage them.

Tools Used: Hand basin, nail clippers, hand scrub, lotion, essential oils, towel.

Materials Used: Warm water, epsom salt, essential oils, hand cream.

Equipment: Hand Spa machine.

Sterilizing Tools:

Task: Ensuring that all tools are clean and sterilized to prevent infection or contamination.

Tools Used: Autoclave, UV sterilizer, cleaning solution, gloves.

Materials Used: Cleaning solution, gloves.

Equipment: Autoclave, UV sterilizer.
