Jose received 38 votes for class president. If 50 votes were cast, what percent of the votes did he received?

A. 76%

R = [tex]\frac{P}{B}[/tex]

n% × 50 = 38

[tex]\frac{n}{100}[/tex] = [tex]\frac{38}{50}[/tex]

n × 50 = 50n

100 × 38 = 3,800

3,800 ÷ 50n = 76
