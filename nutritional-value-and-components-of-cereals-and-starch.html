19. different kinds of starch and give its nutritional value


Answer:

Cornstarch, tapioca, arrowroot, and wheat, rice, and potato starches

Explanation:

The following are the different commercially available starches:

cornstarch - 389 caloriestapioca - 381 caloriesarrowroot - 78 calories per cupwheat - 371 calories per cuprice - 403 calories in a cuppotato starches - 363 calories per cup

One cup is 110 grams.

Note that arrowroot has the lowest caloric count for one cup and rice remains that number one. Hence, when trying to go on a low calorie diet, go with starches with low caloric count. Or better yet avoid starchy food altogether as these are rich in carbohydrates, which when consumed by the body becomes glucose. Unused glucose is stored as fat and increases the risk of diabetes.

The best way to get the body to a health state is by eating green and leafy vegetables as well as fruits that are not so sweet.
