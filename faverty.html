Definition of Adjectives

An adjective (https://brainly.ph/question/249020) is a word that highlights a noun or a pronoun. It gives additional information about the noun or the pronoun being referred to.  It is common that adjectives come right before the noun or the pronoun that it modifies. However, we can use more than one adjective to describe a noun or a pronoun; and when a noun comes before another noun, it becomes its adjective.  

Type of Adjectives

Here are the different types of adjectives:  

Descriptive adjectives describe a noun.  Proper adjectives formed from proper nouns  Adjective of quantity draws attention to the number of nouns and pronouns in a sentence.Numeral adjectives specify how many things or persons are in the sentence. Probing adjectives are used to describe nouns to ask questions.  Demonstrative adjectives emphasize nouns.  Adjectives of possession show ownership.
