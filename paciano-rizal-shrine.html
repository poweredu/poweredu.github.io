10. ano ang buong kwento ni heneral paciano Rizal? ​


Answer:

Siya ay isang Pilipinong Heneral at ang kapatid ng ating pambansang bayani na si Jose Rizal

Siya ay ipinaganak noong Marso 9, 1851  sa Calamba, Laguna, Captaincy General of the Philippines

Sumali at aktibong sinuportahan ni Paciano ang Kilusang Propaganda para sa mga repormang panlipunan, at suportado ang pahayagan ng Kilusang, Diariong Tagalog. The house was made of lime and stone for its ground floor and the upper story was made of best quality hardwood.

21. Differentiate Paciano from Joe or "Pepe"Rizal in two way.


Paciano was older than Jose Rizal. Paciano was born on March 9, 1851, while Jose Rizal was born on June 19, 1861.

Paciano was a more active participant in the Philippine Revolution. While Jose Rizal is known for his literary works and advocacy for reform, Paciano was actively involved in the armed struggle for independence from Spain. He served as a general in the Philippine Revolution and was a close adviser to Andres Bonifacio, the founder of the Katipunan, a secret society that played a key role in the Philippine Revolution. Jose Rizal, on the other hand, did not participate in the armed struggle and advocated for peaceful reform through his writings.
