Problem:

a wire length of 12 1/2 meters is cut into 10 peces of equal length what is the length or each piece

Solution:

[tex]12 \: \frac{1}{2} \div 10[/tex]

[tex] = \frac{25}{2} \div 10[/tex]

[tex] = \frac{25}{2} \times \frac{1}{10} [/tex]

[tex] = \frac{5}{2} \times \frac{1}{2} [/tex]

[tex] = \frac{5}{4} [/tex]

[tex] = 1 \: \frac{1}{4} [/tex]

Checking:

[tex]1 \: \frac{1}{4} \times 10[/tex]

[tex] = \frac{5}{4} \times 10[/tex]

[tex] = \frac{5}{2} \times 5[/tex]

[tex] = \frac{25}{2} [/tex]

[tex] = 12 \: \frac{1}{2} [/tex]

Final Answer:

The length or each piece is 1 ¼ meters
