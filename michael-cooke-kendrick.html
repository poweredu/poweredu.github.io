21. the sum of Michael and Angelo's age is 40, Michael is 4 years older than Angelo. How old is Michael? Representation:Solution:Conclusion:Checking:​


Answer:

Michael is 22 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Representation:

A(age) = x

M(age) = x + 4

Solution:

x + x + 4 = 40

2x + 4 = 40

2x + 4 - 4 = 40 - 4

2x = 36

2x/2 = 36/2

x = 18

A(age) = 18

M(age) = x + 4

= 18 + 4

= 22

Conclusion:

Michael is 22 years old.

Checking:

36 + 4 = 40

28. Michael is 27 years older than his son. Six years ago, Michael's age wasfive more than thrice his son's present age. How old is Michael now?​


Answer:

Michael's age is 35 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

In mathematics, solving word problem required knowledge and skills in basic mathematics that will improve your analytical and critical thinking skills.

In the given problem, you need to apply your knowledge and skills in alegbra and basic math.

ProblemHow old is Michael now?

Statement GivenLet x age of his sonMichael is 27 years older (x+27)Six years ago, Michael's age was five more than thrice his son's present age (5+3x)

Solution

Since present is equal to x+27 as age of Michael, and 6 years ago equals to (5+3x).

The equation would be (x+27) - 6 = (5+3x).

Simplify and solve for x (age of his son).

(x+27) - 6 = (5+3x).

x + 27 - 6 = 5 + 3x

3x - x = 27 - 6 - 5

2x = 16

x= 8

His son is 8 years old.

x+27, age of Michael

x+27

= x + 27

= 8 + 27

= 35

Michael's age is 35 years old in the present.
