25. your insights about the Philippine Educational System in the historical and legal context


HISTORICAL PHILIPPINE EDUCATIONAL SYSTEM

Education in the Philippines is offered through formal and non-formal systems. Formal education typically spans 14 years and is structured in a 6+4+4 system: 6 years of primary school education, 4 years of secondary school education, and 4 years of higher education, leading to a bachelor's degree.

LEGAL CONTEXT

Section 1, Article XIV of the 1987 Constitution provides that the state shall protect and promote of all citizens to quality education at all levels and shall take appropriate steps to make such education accessible to all.
