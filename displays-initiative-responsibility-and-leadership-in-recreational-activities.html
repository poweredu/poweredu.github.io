8. how manage organization, initiative, responsibility and leadership in respective Barangay like sports and fitness activities.​


Answer:

1. Organizing: Develop a team of volunteers and leaders to develop a plan for sports and fitness activities in the Barangay. Establish ground rules, timelines, and objectives for the project.

2. Initiating: Once the plan has been developed, initiate the process by creating a network of people who are willing and able to help. Use social media, local media outlets, and other forms of communication to spread the word and attract participants.

3. Responsibility: Establish a system of accountability, with each team member taking responsibility for certain tasks. Make sure each team member is aware of their tasks, the timeline, and all associated expectations.

4. Leadership: Appoint a leader or a group of leaders to oversee the project. They should have the authority to make decisions, provide direction, and ensure that tasks are completed on time.

5. Sustainability: Develop a system to ensure that the project continues to be successful over the long-term. This could include creating a budget, establishing fundraising activities, and recruiting volunteers for future projects.

19. Initiative responsibility and leadership in sports activities


You can elevate your participation by joining events that promote health and fitness as well as address health issues like diabetes, obesity, nutrition, smoking, and many more. By joining these events, you can learn more about these health concerns, what is being done to handle them—and at the same time, you also get to increase your physical activity. The following are the physical activities that you can initiate:


Fun run are usually 1-day events that focus on running various distances (i.e., 3k, 5k, 10k, or longer). They cater to a wide variety of participants—competitive or recreational runners, and even families.

 

Dance events, competitions, or marathons  are events focus on dance as the main activity, whether as competition (e.g. streetdance, dancesport) or as recreation (e.g. aerobic dance marathons, ZumbaTM events). These may last for hours depending on the event and variety of dances, intensity levels, and music usually used, thus, elevating participation.

 

Sports Tournament are the most common type of health- and fitness-related organized events. They mainly focus on sports in a tournament type of play where individual players and/or teams compete. They are usually organized for school teams [(e.g. University Athletic Association of the Philippines (UAAP)].

 

Summer Sports Clinics are short-course sports programs catering to school children. Sports clinics are offered by schools and product brands when school is over and students have their summer break.

 

Outdoor recreation events are specialized events that target sports enthusiasts and athletes. These are held in specialized venues and locations, and usually have different categories for different levels of participants.

 

Talks, seminars, or conferences are usually lecture-based events that cover topics discussed with an audience.

 

School, club, community, or company events are events that are organized by schools, clubs, communities, and companies to encourage an active lifestyle.


20. being a leader what is your opinion how to manage your organization, initiative, responsibility and leadership in your respective barangay ​


Answer:

Clear Vision and Goals: It is important for a leader to have a clear understanding of the organization's vision, mission, and goals. This will guide decision-making and prioritize actions.

Effective Communication: Good communication is key to effective leadership. Leaders should listen to the needs and concerns of their constituents and provide clear and concise updates on the organization's activities.

Empowerment: A leader should empower their team members and delegate responsibilities to build a strong and supportive team. This helps build trust and fosters collaboration.

Responsibility and Accountability: A leader should take responsibility for the actions of their organization and ensure accountability among team members. This helps to build a culture of integrity and honesty.

Continuous Improvement: A leader should encourage and facilitate continuous improvement within their organization. This includes regular review and evaluation of processes, systems, and practices to identify areas for improvement.

These principles can help a leader in their barangay to effectively manage their organization, initiatives, responsibilities, and leadership effectively.
