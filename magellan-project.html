history of magellan:In search of fame and fortune, Portuguese explorer Ferdinand Magellan (c. 1480-1521) set out from Spain in 1519 with a fleet of five ships to discover a western sea route to the Spice Islands. places of magellan visited:On September 20, 1519, Magellan set sail from Spain in an effort to find a western sea route to the rich Spice Islands of Indonesia. Ferdinand Magellan

Sa paghahanap ng magandang kapalaran, ang manlalakbay na Portuges na si Ferdinand Magellan nabuhay noong c. 1480 hanggang 1521 ay umalis ng Espanya noong 1519 kasama ang limang barko upang matuklasan ang isang rutang pangkanluranin patungo sa Spice Islands.

Sa ruta niya ay natuklasan niya kung ano ang binansagan ngayon bilang Strait of Magellan. Siya rin ang naging unang European na tumawid sa Karagatang Pasipiko. Ang paglalakbay ay napakatagal at mapanganib, at isang barko lamang ang nakauwi pagkalipas ng tatlong taon. Labing walo lamang sa orihinal na tripulante niyang 270 ang nakabalik lulan ng barko. Si Magellan din ay napatay sa labanan sa paglalakbay, siya ay nakitil ng hukbo ni Lapu lapu sa Cebu. Siya ang nakapagcircumnavigate ng ating mundo.

Kaya naman siya naglalakbay sa ilalim ng pamumuno at suporta ng Espanya dahil noon ay mayroon siyang ideya na maaaring may ibang ruta sa pamamagitan ng paglalakbay sa kanluran at sa paligid ng Amerika ngunit hindi sumang-ayon ang Hari ng Portugal at nakipagtalo kay Magellan. Kaya nagpunta si Magellan kay Haring Charles V ng Espanya na pumayag na suportahan o pondohan ang paglalakbay niya.

24. 1.What remarkable activity did the coming of Magellan?2.what was the most important contribution of Magellan expedition?3.What were two important achievements of Magellan expedition?4.What are the 5 expeditions to the Philippines after Magellan?5.What is the purpose of Magellan expedition in the Philippines?6.What are the accomplishment of Ferdinand Magellan?​


Answer:

1. The most remarkable activity of the coming of Magellan was the first circumnavigation of the globe, which was completed by his expedition.

2. The most important contribution of Magellan's expedition was the first circumnavigation of the globe, which proved that the earth is round and opened up new trade routes and possibilities for exploration.

3. Two important achievements of Magellan's expedition were the first circumnavigation of the globe and the discovery of a passage through South America, now known as the Strait of Magellan, which greatly reduced the time and difficulty of sailing from the Atlantic to the Pacific Ocean.

4. Five expeditions to the Philippines after Magellan are:

-Ruy López de Villalobos (1542–1546)

-Martín de Goiti and Juan de Salcedo (1570–1574)

-Lope de Vega (1583–1593)

-Sebastián Vizcaíno (1602–1603)

-Juan de la Isla (1668)

5. The purpose of Magellan's expedition in the Philippines was to find a route to the Spice Islands of the East Indies, which were considered a valuable source of spices and other valuable goods.

6. The accomplishments of Ferdinand Magellan were the first circumnavigation of the globe, the discovery of a passage through South America, now known as the Strait of Magellan, the discovery of the Philippines, and the opening of the Philippines to Spanish colonization.
