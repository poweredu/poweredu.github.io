Creative Portfolio Journal

A creative journal portfolio is required of the students. A summary of all the learning experiences from the CFE 101 course will be included, along with an introduction, a synthesis, a creative presentation, and a conclusion.

Rationale:

This assignment is a test of your knowledge as demonstrated by your critical and reflective thinking abilities as well as your creativity. Your relationship with God as the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit is summed up in the portfolio. You are required to live out your Christian faith as a Louisian by reflecting on all the principles learned in this course and learning from the wise. Additionally, by exchanging your knowledge, insights, and ideas, you help the entire Christian community to grow and change.

Parts and Sequence of the Portfolio:

A. Cover/ Title Page: In Partial Fulfillment of Creative Journal Portfolio on Course CFE 101

B. Introduction

I'm Sam, a senior at Ateneo de Manila University and a high school student. I've been studying too arduously. Since first semester, I've been on the dean's list. The stories of God's ongoing journey with His people are covered in CFE 101. The biblical tales will act as a source of inspiration and a road map for realizing God's boundless and unwavering love for people and the entire world. In regards to my trust in our Creator and Savior, I have grown significantly. I can trust him because of his example and teachings.

C. Content

It enables the pupils to understand and appreciate God's initiative in interfering in human history to guide people back to the correct path and prevent them from making the same mistakes as their forefathers.Jesus is significant to us because He empowers us to transform our lives, face challenges, and advance in faith as we make our way back to Him and His Father through His Atonement, teachings, hope, peace, and example.

D. Conclusion

In times of weakness, darkness can aid us by giving us power. We have no value without faith. Faith is the same as trust. You must have complete faith in God's protection, knowing that he will look out for you and provide for your needs. He knows what is best, but in order to fully accept what he has in store for you, you must have complete faith in him. Our faith is not irrational since our God is dependable and just.
