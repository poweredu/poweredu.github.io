28. Triangle, square, hexagon, __ what is the pattern?? ​


Answer:

Triangle, Square, Hexagon, Nonagon

According to the number of sides of the given shapes:

Triangle = 3

Square = 4

Hexagon= 6

Nonagon = 9

3,4,6,9

if you subract the first number to the second, the third to the second, so on...

4-3=1

6-4=2

9-6=3

1,2,3

if you add another shape with 14 sides, the differences between the sequence 3,4,6,9 would become another sequence called Fibonacci Sequence

4-3=1

6-4=2

9-6=3

14-9= 5

1,2,3,5
