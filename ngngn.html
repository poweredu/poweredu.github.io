24. liwanag at Ningning essay​


"Liwanag at Ningning" is a Filipino phrase that translates to "Light and Brightness." It is also the title of a popular Filipino television drama series that aired in the 1980s. The show tells the story of two young girls, Liwanag and Ningning, who come from different backgrounds but share a passion for singing.

Liwanag is a poor girl who lives with her mother and siblings in a small house. Despite their financial struggles, Liwanag remains optimistic and dreams of becoming a famous singer someday. Ningning, on the other hand, comes from a wealthy family and has everything she could ever want. However, she feels unfulfilled and longs for something more.

The two girls meet when they audition for a singing competition, and they quickly become friends. Liwanag's talent and determination inspire Ningning, who begins to see the world in a new light. Together, they face various challenges and obstacles, but their friendship and love for music help them overcome these difficulties.

The show's title, "Liwanag at Ningning," represents the positive outlook and hope that the two girls bring to each other's lives. Liwanag's optimism and determination serve as a light that shines on Ningning's life, helping her find her own path and purpose. Ningning, in turn, brings brightness to Liwanag's life, showing her that she is not alone in her struggles.

The show's popularity can be attributed to its heartwarming story, relatable characters, and catchy music. It also highlights the importance of friendship, perseverance, and following one's dreams. "Liwanag at Ningning" has become a cultural icon in the Philippines, and its message of hope and positivity continues to inspire generations of Filipinos.

Ang Ningning at Ang Liwanag

(Mula sa Liwanag at Dilim)

ni Emilio Jacinto

Ang ningning ay nakasisilaw at nakasisira sa paningin. Ang liwanag ay kinakailangan ng mata, upang mapagwari ang buong katunayan ng mga bagay-bagay.

Ang bubog kung tinatamaan ng nag-aapoy ng sikat na araw ay nagniningning; ngunit sumusugat sa kamay ng nagaganyak na dumampot.

Ang ningning ay maraya.

Ating hanapin ang liwanag; tayo’y huwag mabighani sa ningning.

Sa katunayan ng masamang kaugalian: Nagdaraan ang isang karuaheng maningning na hinihila ng kabayong matulin. Tayo’y magpupugay at ang isasaloob ay mahal na tao ang nakalulan. Datapua’y marahil naman ay isang magnanakaw, marahil sa isang malalim ng kaniyang ipinatatanghal na kamahalan at mga hiyas na tinataglay ay natatago ang isang sukaban.

Nagdaraan ang isang maralita na nagkakanghihirap sa pinapasan. Tayo’y napapangiti, at isasaloob.

Saan kaya ninakaw? Datapua’y maliwanag nating nakikita sa pawis ng kaniyang noo at sa hapo ng kaniyang katawan na siya’y nabubuhay sa sipag kapagalang tunay.
