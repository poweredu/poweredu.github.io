10. national arnis association of the Philippines (NARAPHIL) was formed​


Answer:

Arnis is the official national sport and the martial art of the Philippines. It is also known as "Kali" or "Eskrima" and is a form of martial arts that focuses on weapon-based fighting with knives, sticks, bladed weapons and some improvised weapons. The sport also includes hand to hand combat, grappling, joint locks and weapon disarming techniques. Practitioners of the sport are called arnisador (male athletes) and arnisadora (female athletes). Arnis is a combat sport to defend oneself from attacks using hand to hand combat, grappling, weapon disarming in weapon-based fights.
