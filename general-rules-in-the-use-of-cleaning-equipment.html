16. give atleast 3 general rules in the use of cleaning equipment​


Answer:

3 GENERAL RULES IN THE USE OF CLEANING EQUIPMENT:

- Never use any appliance that is defective. Handle equipment with care and make sure it does not bump on hard surfaces.

- Clean and store equipment in their custodial room immediately after use.

- Empty dust bags of dry vacuum cleaners before they overload and after each use.

RULES OF CLEANLINESS:

- Clean it up sooner than later.

- Clean from the top down.

- Think dry, then wet.

- Start with the least harmful approach.

- Let time do the work for you.

- Carry your supplies with you.

- When in doubt, make a stealth test.

- Don't deluge easily damaged items.

IMPORTANT OF CLEANNING TOOLS AND EQUIPMENT:

- The methods used in cleaning will determine how clean and healthy a place will be. The tools you use will dictate how well an area will be free from germs, contaminants, and viruses which are hazardous to health
