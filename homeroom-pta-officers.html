3. out of 400 PTA members and officers,320 attended the PTA meeting.what percent of the OTA members and officers were present ​


ANSWER:80% of the OTA members and officers were present.

SOLUTION:

1. Identify the given values and the unknown

Percentage (P) - 320

Base (B) - 400

Rate (R) - ? (Unknown)

2. Identify the formula for the unknown

Percentage - B × R

Base - P/R or P ÷ R

Rate - P/B or P ÷ B

Since the rate is the unknown, we use the formula Rate = P/B

3. Solve

Rate = P/B

Rate = 320/400

Rate = 0.8 (move two decimal places to the right to convert to percent)

Rate = 80%

13. 2. Homeroom class officers are president, vice-president, secretary, treasurer, a PIO and a peace officer. From the class of forty students, how many different groups of officers could students select?​


Answer : 27, 907, 200 ways

Step-by-step explanation:

Given : n = 40

r = 6

Formula : nPr = n! / (n-r) !

Solution : nPr = 20! / (20-6) !

= 20! / 14!

= 20•19•18•17•16•15•14! / 14!

= 20•19•18•17•16•15

= 27,907,200 ways
