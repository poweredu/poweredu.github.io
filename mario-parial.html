19. Mario enters in a kart race. He completes the 1250m race around a circular track in 31.7532 s1. What is Mario’s average speed? 2. What is Mario’s average velocity?ps: with solution​


AVERAGE SPEED = 39.37 m/s

VELOCITY = 1.24 m

Step-by-step explanation:

1. What is Mario’s average speed?

[tex]average \: speed \: = \frac{distance}{time} [/tex]

[tex]average \: speed \: = \frac{1250 m}{31.7532 s} [/tex]

AVERAGE SPEED = 39.37 m/s

2. What is Mario’s average velocity?

V = SPEED ÷ TIME

V = 39.37 m/s ÷ 31.7532 s

VELOCITY = 1.24 m
