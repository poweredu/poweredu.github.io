5. How is informal language different from formal language? ​


In informal language, you can be more flexible with your grammar and you can use slang. Also, feel free to use contractions like I'll, you'll, we're, aren't, etc. In short, write as if you're texting a friend. Meanwhile, in formal language, you must have proper grammar and punctuation. You also shouldn't use slang and contractions. Write as if you're addressing a high-ranking person or you are engaged in scientific research.
