14. Which one of the following is NOT a part of a summary?A. summery is shorter than the textB. including main ideas from the passageC. you tell what you have read in your own wordsD. including all of the extra supporting details in writing a summary​


D. Include all additional supporting details in writing the summary.

Further explanation:

The statement is not part of the summary because including all the additional supporting details in writing the summary makes the summary long and contains many things that are not very important for a summary.

Summary

Summary is a presentation of a long essay or writing into a shorter, denser, clearer, and effective form while maintaining a certain order (both content and point of view) of the summarized essay/writing.

The purpose of summarizing is to simplify the content of a text so that it is easier for readers to remember and understand.

Explanation of other answers:

A. summery is shorter than the text

A summary is a presentation of a long essay or writing in a shorter, denser, clearer, and more effective form. The summary will be shorter than the original text.

B. including main ideas from the passage

The summary contains the main ideas of the text. In making a summary after reading the original manuscript, the next step is to record the main ideas or important ideas in the manuscript.

C. you tell what you have read in your own words

Writing a summary is a restatement of a text in a shorter and denser form, so that writing a summary can be done by reading the original text and then presenting it again with its own choice of words but still has the same purpose or intent as the original text.

School is an educational institution where students go to learn and acquire knowledge and skills. Summary of Philippines:

The Philippines is a country located in Southeast Asia. It is an archipelago consisting of more than 7,000 islands. The country has a population of over 100 million people and is known for its beautiful beaches, rich biodiversity, and delicious cuisine. Summary of Elephants:

Elephants are large mammals that are native to Africa and Asia. They are known for their distinctive long trunks, large ears, and tusks. Elephants are social animals that live in herds led by a matriarch. They are herbivores and can consume up to 300 pounds of vegetation in a single day. Elephants are also intelligent animals and have been observed exhibiting behaviors such as empathy and self-awareness. Unfortunately, elephants are also threatened by habitat loss and poaching for their ivory tusks. Conservation efforts are underway to protect elephant populations and their habitats.
