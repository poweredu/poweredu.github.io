1. who is Blanche Dubois


Blanche DuBois (married name Grey) is a fictional character in Tennessee Williams' 1947 Pulitzer Prize-winning play A Streetcar Named Desire. The character was written for Tallulah Bankhead.

2. who is eugene dubois​


Answer:

Marie Eugène François Thomas Dubois was a Dutch paleoanthropologist and geologist. He earned worldwide fame for his discovery of Pithecanthropus erectus, or "Java Man". Although hominid fossils had been found and studied before, Dubois was the first anthropologist to embark upon a purposeful search for them.

Answer:

eugene dubois was a Dutch anatomist and geologist who discovered the remains of Java man, the first known fossil of Homo erectus. He earned worldwide fame for his discovery of Pithecanthropus erectus, or "Java Man". Although hominid fossils had been found and studied before, Dubois was the first anthropologist to embark upon a purposeful search for them.

(born January 28, 1858, Eisden, Netherlands—died December 16, 1940, de Bedlaer)
