13. Properties of a well written text slideshare


The properties of a well-written text are:

1) Clarity of Ideas - this means that the text should have a clear focal point where the statement is grounded (basis, example, argument, etc.)

2) Organization - the flow of thoughts should be written in a logical and coherent way. It means that you have to arrange your ideas and connect them

3) Language Use - this will show how well-versed you are in the language you are using. Make sure that your word choices are the best fit for the point you’re trying to make.

4) Voice - personal touch on your writing. This is the manner, style and approach/direction you add onto your piece.

30. Martines by anna felicia sanchez slideshare


The summary of Anna Feliza Sanchez’ story about Martines is the socio-cultural context pretaining to Filipino folklore and superstitions. Before, Filipinos believed creatures like engkantos that guard a single tree or a whole forest, and some lost spirits that wander in some area. The main character Richard, represents trait of average Filipinos, wherein he has this mentality to adore non-Filipino practices. Some Filipinos think that they have better opportunities outside the country, so they worked overseas and the result is they have improve the economy of that country. The theme of Sanchez’ story is the love for the country’s culture and respect the others.
