6. Mr. Ambayen had 3 6/7 hectares of land. He planted 1/5 of it for coconut trees and 1/2 of it for avocado trees.What fraction of Mr. Ambayen's land was planted with trees? Show your solution.​


Answer: The fraction of Mr. Ambayen's land planted with trees was 7/10.

Step-by-step explanation:

1/5 of 3 6/7 hectares is 27/7 hectares * 1/5 = 27/35 hectares

1/2 of 3 6/7 hectares is 27/7 hectares * 1/2 = 27/14 hectares

The fraction of Mr. Ambayen's land that was planted with trees would then be:

27/35 hectares + 27/14 hectares divided by 27/7 total hectares, or

( 27/35 + 27/14 ) / ( 27/7 )

= ( 54/70 + 135/70 ) * ( 7/27 )

= ( 189/70 ) * ( 7/27 )

= ( 189/10 ) * ( 1/27 )

= 7/10
