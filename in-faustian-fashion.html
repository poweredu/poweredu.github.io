7. para sa Report: fashion designing trade designing at fashion illustration at writing​


Answer:

Fashion designing is a creative and competitive field that involves designing and creating apparel, accessories, and shoes. To become a successful fashion designer, you will need to have a strong knowledge of the industry, including the latest trends and designs, fabrics, and construction techniques.

Fashion illustration is an important aspect of the fashion design process, as it helps designers to communicate their ideas and concepts to clients. It involves the use of hand-drawn sketches and technical drawings to illustrate the shape, silhouette, and details of a garment.

Writing is also an important part of fashion design, as it helps designers to create portfolios, marketing materials, and press releases. Writing can also be used to create trend reports and fashion trend articles.
