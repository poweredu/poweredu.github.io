12. how to use cylinder leakage tester?​


Answer:

Step 1: Take out the spark plugs and turn the engine over to position the test cylinder at TDC. Tip: Turn the engine by hand using a socket on the crankshaft and a long screwdriver or extension inserted into the spark plug hole. You've reached TDC when the screwdriver stops rising or sinking. If TDC is not reached, do not reverse the engine. Repeat the circuit. Cylinders one and four, as well as two and three, are all at TDC simultaneously in a four-cylinder engine.

Step 2: Select the spark plug adapter that works best and insert the gauge. To stop the engine from turning when air is pushed into the cylinder, engage the gears and apply the parking brake. To zero the incoming pressure, start by turning the regulator counterclockwise. attach a pressurised air line. To pressurize the cylinder with air, turn the regulator in a clockwise direction.

Step 3: Calculate the cylinder's leakage percentage. Remove the radiator cap, oil filler cap, and oil dipstick. Remove the air cleaner or open the throttle body. Watch and listen. Any area where air is escaping will show where the issue is. Move on to the next cylinder after disconnecting the gauge.

15. Alin ang mas mainam para sa ekonomiya, mas mataas ang leakages sa injections, o ininjections sa leakages? Bakit? ​


Answer:

—Ang mas mainam para sa ekonomiya ay mas mataas na injections kaysa leakages. Ang injections ay tumutukoy sa pagpapasok ng pera sa ekonomiya, habang ang leakages ay tumutukoy sa paglabas ng pera sa ekonomiya. Kapag mayroong mas mataas na injections, ito ay nangangahulugan na mayroong mas maraming pera na nakakapagbigay ng demand sa mga produkto at serbisyo, na nagpapalakas sa ekonomiya. Sa kabilang banda, kapag mayroong mas mataas na leakages, ito ay nangangahulugan na mayroong mas kakaunting pera na nakakapagbigay ng demand sa mga produkto at serbisyo, na nagpapahina sa ekonomiya.
