25. What is Good PowerPoint design?


Answer:

A good PowerPoint design means that the arrangement of the images and the content including the template used is clear, clean, simple (or not too complex to view), accurate, and appropriate to the topic of the presentation.

When creating a presentation, always remember to make sure that your design is appropriate to the topic being discussed. As much as possible, limit the words you are going to use. Avoid long paragraphs as it will strain the viewers' eyes. The lesser the words, the easier the topic be understood and remembered.Make sure to make the design simple by not putting too many images at once. Or by enlarging the images too much (unless it is used as a background image) or by shrinking it too much that the viewers cannot see the image well.Choose a good combination of fonts. As much as possible use at most 2-3 types of fonts.Choose a good color scheme or color combinations for your fonts and background color. You can add gifs and animations to make your presentation modern and lively. These will keep the audience entertained and focused on your presentation throughout the discussion.

In conclusion, a good PowerPoint Presentation relies on a good PPT design or template. So, make sure to format a good one!


26. Enumerate the steps on how to apply background on PowerPoint presentation.​


Answer:

Explanation:

Format the slide background with color

You can fill the background with a solid color. PowerPoint for the web doesn't support gradient fills for slide backgrounds.

1. On the Design tab, select Format Background.

2. Select Solid Fill, and pick a color from the gallery. (To reset the background to its previous state, select Home > Undo Undo your last action .)

3. If you want to have all slides have this same background color, on the Design tab, select Format Background > Apply to All.

Format the slide background with color

You can fill the background with a solid color. PowerPoint for the web doesn't support gradient fills for slide backgrounds.

1. On the Design tab, select Format Background.

2. Select Solid Fill, and pick a color from the gallery. (To reset the background to its previous state, select Home > Undo Undo your last action .)

3. If you want to have all slides have this same background color, on the Design tab, select Format Background > Apply to All.

Format the slide background with a photo

When you insert a picture as a background, PowerPoint for the web resizes the image as best as it can to fill the entire area of the slide. For best results, choose a picture that has the same orientation as your slides.

1. On the Design tab, select Format Background.

 2. Select Picture From File.  (To reset the background to its previous state, select Home > Undo Undo your last action .)

3. To make all slides have this same background picture, on the Design tab, select Format Background, and then select Apply to All.

PowerPoint for the web supports the following picture formats: .jpg, .jpeg, .png, .gif, .bmp, .wmf, .emf, .tif, and .tiff.

PowerPoint for the web doesn't have the ability to make a picture transparent. Use the desktop PowerPoint app if you want to make a background picture transparent.

PowerPoint for the web doesn't have the ability to remove a picture's background. Use the desktop PowerPoint app to remove a picture's background.

1.Select the slide you want to add a background picture to.
