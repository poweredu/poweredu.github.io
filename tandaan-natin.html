Answer:

The spiritual aspect is valuing one's relationship with God as the Creator, building faith and being faithful to the arrangements and principles written in the Bible. For example: Man's devotion to God motivates him to keep the commandments and its principles even when his life is in danger.

The emotional aspect is about how a person feels about something or an event. For example: Families are emotional when they hear that their great ancestor has died.

The physical and mental aspect is about the strength, skill and ability of a person according to his physical strength, intellect and talent.

The social aspect is the interaction of one person with another. This includes camaraderie and friendship. Whenever interacting, socializing and empathizing, it refers to the social aspect of living.
