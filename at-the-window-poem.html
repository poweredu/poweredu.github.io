13. and on a Learn Some More Read the poem below. Identify the main idea and the supporting details. Write your answers on a separate sheet of paper. Where is the Caterpillar? Where is caterpillar hiding today? We looked under the table. We looked on the chair. We looked out the window. But he was not there. Where is caterpillar hiding today? We looked in the fishbowl. We looked in the sink. We looked in the flowers. What did we see? A beautiful butterfly looking at me. Main Idea Detail​


Main Idea: The caterpillar has transformed into a butterfly.

Supporting Details:

The narrator and others looked for the caterpillar in various places such as under the table, on the chair, out the window, in the fishbowl, and in the sink but did not find it.

Finally, they saw a beautiful butterfly looking at them when they looked in the flowers. This indicates that the caterpillar has transformed into a butterfly.

Main idea: The poem describes the search for a caterpillar and the surprise of finding a butterfly instead.

Detail: The speaker and the person they are addressing are searching for a caterpillar, looking in various places such as under the table, on the chair, and out the window, but they cannot find it. They continue their search in other places like the fishbowl and the sink, and eventually, they see a beautiful butterfly looking at them among the flowers. The poem highlights the transformation of the caterpillar into a butterfly and the surprise and wonder that can come from unexpected discoveries.
