30. Pa helpCreate a Timeline History of Mass Media.​


Answer:

1880s - The first mass-produced newspapers become available, allowing for the spread of information to a wider audience.

1920s - Radio becomes a popular mass media platform, allowing for the broadcast of music, news, and entertainment to a wide audience.

1950s - Television becomes a widespread mass media platform, bringing visual media into people's homes and allowing for the broadcast of programming to a large audience.

1980s - The internet becomes widely available, opening up new possibilities for communication and information sharing on a global scale.

1990s - The World Wide Web becomes widely available, allowing for the creation and sharing of multimedia content online.

2000s - Social media platforms become popular, allowing for the creation and sharing of user-generated content and facilitating communication and connection among users.

2010s - Streaming services become popular, allowing for on-demand access to a wide variety of audio and video content.

2020s - Online content creation and sharing continues to evolve, with the rise of virtual and augmented reality technologies offering new ways for people to connect and engage with media.
