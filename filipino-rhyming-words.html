Ano ang salitang magkatugma?

Ang salitang magkatugma ay mga salita na may parehas na tunog sa hulihan. Ang magkatugma ay tinatawag din na magkasingtunog. Sa Ingles, ito ay rhyming words.

Ang magkakatugmang salita o magkakasingtunog na salita ay kadalasang ginagamit sa panitikan dahil sa madali silang masaulo at masarap pakinggan. Ito ay maaaring gamitin sa kanta, tula, slogan, balagtasan o dulaan.

End rhyme is defined as “when a poem has lines ending with words that sound the same.” End rhyme is also called tail rhyme or terminal rhyme. It is one of many types of rhyme. Two or more lines of the poem have to rhyme for it to be considered end rhyme, but they don't have to be consecutive lines.

Internal rhyme, or middle rhyme, is rhyme that occurs within a single line of verse, or between internal phrases across multiple lines. By contrast, rhyme between line endings is known as end rhyme.
