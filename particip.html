What is a value proposition?

A value proposition is a statement that highlights why a client should buy a company's product or use its service by identifying clear, measurable, and demonstrable benefits. Simply expressed, it is a promise to offer value to the client.

What are the methods of segmentation?

There is 4 segmentation strategy and these are as follows?

Demographic segmentation

is one of the most prominent and widely utilized market segmentation techniques. It is statistical information about a group of people.

Psychographic segmentation

Audiences and customers are divided into groups based on personality traits and attributes.

Behavioral segmentation

Behavioral segmentation is different from demographic and psychographic segmentation in that it focuses on how a customer behaves.

Geographic segmentation

The most basic type of market segmentation is geographic segmentation. It divides clients into groups based on their location.

18. In a Math contest, the total number of participants was 132. Twice the number of female participants exceeded the number of male participants by 48. How many male participants were there in the contest?


Male Participants = 72

Step-by-step explanation:

Let ;

x = Female Participants

y = Male Participants

We have 2 Equations :

x + y = 1322x = y + 48

Lets solve for "y" on our 2nd equation.

2x = y + 48

2x - 48 = y

or

y = 2x - 48

Now that we have the value of "y", lets use it on our 1st equation (x + y = 132)

x + (2x - 48) = 132

Combine like terms

3x - 48 = 132

3x = 132 + 48

3x = 180

Divide both side by 3

x = 60

And now we have the value of "x".

Again, x = female participants. We're looking for male participants which is "y".

On our equation "x + y = 132", we can now solve for the value of "y" because we have our "x = 6"

x + y = 132

60 + y = 132

y = 132 - 60

y = 72

Our Answer is 72.


19. participant vs non-participant observation​


Answer:

•Participant observation is a qualitative research method in which the researcher studies a group not only by observing the group, but also by participating in the activities of the group.

•Non-participant Observation involves observing participants without actively participating. This option is used to understand a phenomenon by entering the community or social system involved, while staying separate from the activities being observed.

Difference of Participant and Non-participant Observation.

The participant observation means watching the events or situation or activities from inside by taking part in the group to be observed. ... Young, “the participant observer using non-controlled observation, generally lives or otherwise shares in the life of the group which he is studying”.
