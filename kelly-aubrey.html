25. KELLY,MARVIN AND INDHU SAVED $100 ALTOGETHER. KELLY SAVED $16 MORE THAN INDHU. MARVIN SAVE TWICE THE AMOUNT INDHU SAVED. HOW MUCH DID KELLY SAVED?


Answer:

Kelly saved $37.

Step-by-step explanation:

Indhu's savings: x

Kelly's savings: x + 16  (16 more than Indhu's)

Marvin's savings: 2x  (twice the amount Indhu saved)

Equation:

x + (x+16) + (2x) = 100

4x + 16 = 100

4x = 100-16

Solve for Indhu's savings, x:

4x/4 = 84/4

x = 21

Hindhu saved $21.

Find how much Marvin and Kelly saved:

Kelly: x+16  ⇒ 21+16=  $37

Marvin: 2x  ⇒  2(21) = $42

Kelly saved $37.

Check:

$21 + $37 + $42 = $100

$100 = $100 (True)
