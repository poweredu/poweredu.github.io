The players are divided into two teams of equal numberWhen a stick is not available you can use your hand.Each teams form a line according to their assigned number and takes it places immediately behind one of the two parallel lines, facing the opponents.At the given signal the referee will announce a numbers then the player from each team, run towards the handkerchief. 20. paglalaro ng agawang panyo. Mga pamamaraan sa paglalaro ng agawang panyo. 1 2 3 4 5pa help ​


Answer:

How to play the game?

1. Agawan Panyo is played

outdoors. Two parallel lines

are drawn about 15 meters

apart. A handkerchief is

placed on stick planted on a

line drawn midway between

the two parallel lines.

When a stick is not available,

a referee is chosen where the

stick is supported to be,

extends his hand forward,

and holds the handkerchief

2. The players divide into two teams of equal number. If a referee is chosen

it is done at this point. Each team forms a line and takes its places

immediately behind one of the two parallel lines, facing the opponents.

3. At a given signal, player from each team run towards the handkerchief.

They each stand, one hand poised to snatch the handkerchief and ther

other hand ready to tag the player who touches the handkerchief first.

4. One player grabs the handkerchief and tries to run away with it. If

the snatcher successfully returns to his line with the handkerchief, he

scores a point for the team. If the snatcher is tagged, no point is

scored for either team.

5. The team scoring the greater number of points wins the game.
