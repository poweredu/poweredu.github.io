29. 1. if m<TJU=x+50 and m<JUS=x+30,find m<JTS2. if JT= (2x-5) cm and US=(x+10)cm, find the length of JT​


In the given problem, apply your knowledge in algebraic expression and basic mathematics especially in identifying angles and lenght of parallelogram.

Problem: 1. If m<TJU = x + 50 and m<JUS = x + 30, find m<JTS

The consecutive angle of parallelogram are supplementary. Thus, m<TJU + m<JUS = 180°

(x + 50) + (x + 30) = 180

2x + 80 = 180

2x = 180 - 80

2x = 100

x = 100/2

x = 50

The opposite angles of parallelogram are congruent, thus m<JUS = m<JTS

x = 50

m<JTS = x + 30

m<JTS = 50 + 30

m<JTS = 80°, ans.

2. If JT= (2x-5) cm and US = (x+10) cm, find the length of JT.

Opposite side of parallelogram are congruent, thus JT = US

(2x-5) = (x+10)

2x - x = 10 + 5

x = 15

JT = 2x - 5

JT = 2(15) - 5

JT = 30 - 5

JT = 25 cm, ans.
