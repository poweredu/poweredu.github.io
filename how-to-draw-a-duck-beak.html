20. How important is beak trimming in poultry production


Answer:

Beak trimming is performed in poultry production to reduce cannibalism, aggressive behaviors, and feather pecking (for review see: Cunningham, 1997). This is an economic advantage for the producer, but a welfare concern for the animal.

Answer:

The practice of beak trimming, which is intended to control feather pecking and cannibalism in poultry, is viewed by the poultry industry as an indispensable practice to promote the well being of chickens by preventing cannibalism.

27. 5. There are 36 legs of animals in a zoo. These are the legs of ducks and dogs. If there are 4 more legs of dogs than that of the ducks, how many are ducks? How many are dogs?


Answer:

there are 18 ducks and 24 dogs in a zoo

Let x be the legs of ducks

y be the legs of dogs

x + y = 36

y = x + 4

substitute method

y = x + 4

x + y = 36

x + x + 4 = 36

2x + 4 = 36

2x = 36 - 4

2x = 32

x = 32/2

x = 16

x + y = 36

16 + y = 36

y = 36 - 16

y = 20

Therefore,

There are 16 legs of ducks and 20 legs of dogs.

(Note: Ducks have 2 legs and dogs have 4 legs.)

16 legs of ducks ÷ 2 = 8 ducks

20 legs of dogs ÷ 4 = 5 dogs

There are 8 ducks and 5 dogs in the zoo.
