20. Write a short essay in how social contexts surroundings a person can contribute to his or her upbringing, beliefs and overall quality of life. Take sample from your personal experience.​


Answer:

In my personal experience, the social contexts surrounding a person can greatly contribute to their upbringing, beliefs, and overall quality of life. Growing up, I was fortunate enough to be raised in a loving and supportive family, with parents who instilled in me strong values and a good work ethic. This positive home environment provided me with a solid foundation and allowed me to develop into a confident and independent individual.

However, the social contexts surrounding me didn't stop at just my family. I was also heavily influenced by the community I grew up in, which was full of diverse people from different backgrounds and experiences. This exposure to different perspectives and ways of life helped me to develop empathy and understanding for others, and also challenged me to think critically about my own beliefs and assumptions.

Moreover, the education system I was a part of played a significant role in shaping my beliefs and values. Through my schooling, I was exposed to a wide range of subjects and ideas, which helped me to broaden my horizons and develop a more well-rounded perspective on the world. I also had the opportunity to participate in extracurricular activities and volunteer in my community, which taught me valuable life skills and the importance of giving back.

Overall, the social contexts surrounding a person can have a profound impact on their upbringing, beliefs, and quality of life. Through my own experience, I have seen how the love and support of my family, the diversity of my community, and the education I received all contributed to the person I am today.
