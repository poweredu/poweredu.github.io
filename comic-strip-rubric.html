30. Written Recitation (Activity) 2b Create a humorous comics strip (4 frames) with manifestation of variou aggression based on forms and intention. The comic strip will be rated based on the rubric below: Drawing prowess-The quality of the drawing of the comics strip (2 pts) Vividness-The color quality of the comics strip (2 pts) Humor-The content of the comics is truly laughter-invoking (3 pts) Content-3 types of aggression is shown in the comics strip (3 pts)​


Answer:

Here is a humorous comics strip that showcases different forms of aggression:

Frame 1: A group of people are standing in line at a coffee shop. One person is tapping their foot impatiently, clearly showing signs of verbal aggression.

Frame 2: The person at the front of the line suddenly spills their coffee on the person behind them, causing the person to jump back in surprise. This is an example of physical aggression.

Frame 3: Another person in the line pulls out their phone and begins to take pictures of the spill, laughing and making fun of the person who spilled their coffee. This is an example of social aggression.

Frame 4: The person who spilled their coffee looks around frantically, trying to figure out what to do. They see a sign that says "Employees Only" and quickly make a run for it, hoping to avoid any consequences. This is an example of avoidance aggression.

Overall, this comics strip showcases three different forms of aggression: verbal, physical, and social. It is also humorous, with the person trying to avoid the consequences of their actions being a comedic moment. The drawings are of good quality and the colors are vibrant, adding to the humor of the strip.
