3. Kahulugan ng Asupre? ​


Answer:
Sulfur (Asupre) - ito ay isang kemikal na elemento na may simbolong S at atomic number 16. Ito ay sagana, multivalent at nonmetallic. Sa ilalim ng normal na kondisyon, ang mga sulfur atom ay bumubuo ng mga cyclic octatomic molecule na may chemical formula na S₈. Ang elemental na asupre ay isang maliwanag na dilaw, mala-kristal na solid sa temperatura ng silid.

Symbol: S

Atomic mass: 32.065 u
Electron configuration: [Ne] 3s²3p⁴
Atomic number: 16
Electronegativity: 2.58
Electrons per shell: 2, 8, 6
Oxidation states: -2, -1, 0, +1, +2, +3, +4, +5, +6 (a strongly acidic oxide)

1. What is molar mass of Sulphur?

Sulfur is a chemical element with the symbol S and atomic number 16. Under normal conditions, sulfur atoms form cyclic octatomic molecules with a chemical formula S8. Molar mass of S atom=32g/mol. Hence, molar mass of Sulphur molecule (S8) is: 8×32 mol/g=256 mol/g.

2. From the chemical formula, we have one Pb atom, so the atomic mass would just be 207.2 g/mol.

3. How do you find the molar mass of copper?

Image result for molar mass of copper

If you're looking at the element of copper you would look in the periodic table and find that it has an average atomic mass of 63.55 AMU, so that's the molar mass. Now we'll write it with a different unit because it's also our molar mass, and that will be grams over moles: 63.55 gmol. That's the molar mass of copper.
