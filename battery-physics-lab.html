10. Was the guidelines for physics lab reports written in a subjective or objective manner? Why?​


Guidelines:

It is generally considered best practice for guidance for physics lab reports to be written in an objective manner.

Why?:

This is because the goal of a physics lab report is to objectively report the findings of an experiment, without bias or interpretation. The report should present the data and results of the experiment in a clear and concise manner, without making any subjective judgments or conclusions.

19. 4. Rechargeable batteries are called nickel-cadmium batteries a. b. d. primary batteries secondary batteries storage batteries​


Answer:

Secondary batteries

Explanation:

Rechargeable batteries, sometimes known as secondary batteries, are intended to be recharged and reused several times. They differ from primary batteries, which are intended to be used once and then destroyed, and storage batteries, which are often huge and used to generate electricity in off-grid applications. Nickel-cadmium (NiCd) batteries are a type of secondary battery.

24. A common way of analyzing the motion of objects in physics lab​


Answer:

You can calculate the acceleration of an object by dividing the change in its velocity by time. We can change the state of motion of an object by changing its speed, its direction of motion, or both. Acceleration is the rate at which the velocity is changing.
