3. the sum of the ages of karen and kristel is 41. If karen is 7 years old than kristel, how old is karen?


Answer:

Karen is 24 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x be the age of Kristel and x+7 be the age of Karen.

[tex]x+(x+7)=41\\x+x+7=41\\2x=41-7\\2x=34\\x=17[/tex]

[tex]x+7=17+7=24[/tex]
