29. what is gimbal instrument​


Answer:

The gimbal (tubular drum)

Explanation:

The gimbal (tubular drum) begins the music with a basic rhythm, then enter the sanang (pair of small gongs with boss and narrow rims) and one to three agungs (gongs with high bossed and wide turned – in rims). Basal ensemble playing is an accurate and wonderful metaphor for the basic custom of sharing among the Palawan.
