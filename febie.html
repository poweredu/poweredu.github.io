7. What are the factors that febie can consider in choosing a bank?.


Answer:

1. Financial Stability: The bank should have a good standing and financial strength.

2. Low Fees and Minimum Balance Requirement: Check to see what fees the bank charges, and if they require you to have a minimum balance in order to maintain an account with them.

3. Interest Rates: Consider what interest rates are offered on savings accounts and loans.

4. Accessibility: Compare the number and location of branches, ATMs, and online banking services offered.

5. Customer Service: Find out what type of customer service the bank offers, including online assistance and phone support.

6. Security: Check what security measures the bank has in place to protect your funds, such as secure online banking and secure mobile banking.

7. Technology: Consider what type of digital services the bank offers and how up-to-date the technology is.

8. Bonuses and Rewards: See if the bank offers any bonuses, rewards, or other incentives for using their services.
