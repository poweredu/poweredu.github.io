Answer:

D.MY PASSION, TEACHING, IS FUN

Explanation:

A gerund is the –ing form of a verb that functions the same as a noun. For example, “Running is fun.” In this sentence, “running” is the gerund. It acts just like a noun. ... You can only use a gerund after the verb “suggest.”

Answer:

Here are some words that might be associated with social dances and their benefits:

Movement: Social dances involve physical movement and can help improve coordination, balance, and physical fitness.

Community: Social dances often bring people together and can foster a sense of community and connection with others.

Culture: Different social dances are associated with different cultures and can help people learn about and appreciate different traditions and ways of life.

Self-expression: Social dances can provide a way for people to express themselves creatively and nonverbally.

Confidence: Learning and performing social dances can boost self-confidence and self-esteem.

Fun: Many people enjoy social dancing simply because it is enjoyable and can be a great source of entertainment.

Stress relief: Dancing can be a form of stress relief and can help reduce anxiety and tension.

Health benefits: Social dancing can provide various health benefits, such as improving cardiovascular health, increasing flexibility and strength, and reducing the risk of chronic conditions such as obesity and diabetes.
