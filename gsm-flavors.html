20. a box of lollipop contains 5 Stawberry flavor, 6 chocolate flavor, and 4 milk flavor. how many combinations of flavors are form if we choose 4 repetition is allowed?​


Answer:

Step-by-step explanation:

To find the number of combinations of flavors that can be formed if we choose 4 lollipops with repetition allowed, we can use the concept of combinations with repetition.

We can use the formula (n+r-1) choose (r-1) to find the number of combinations of r items that can be chosen from a set of n distinct items with repetition allowed.

In this case, we have 3 types of lollipop flavors (strawberry, chocolate, and milk), and we want to choose 4 lollipops. Therefore, we can calculate the total number of combinations as:

(3 + 4 - 1) choose (4 - 1)

= 6 choose 3

= 20

So there are 20 different combinations of flavors that can be formed if we choose 4 lollipops with repetition allowed from the box of lollipops with 5 strawberry flavor, 6 chocolate flavor, and 4 milk flavor.
