17. which function/s of contemporary arts do you commonly use or apply in your artworks ?


Answer:

Art has no single “role” - in fact any theory that “art has a job” is far too mundane and pedestrian to be honored.

Further, contemporary art should not be thought of as any different from any other art through history.

All that said, art does many things - and different things for different people.

Art can make our surroundings more beautiful and interesting.

Art can help us make more sense of our lives and culture.

Art can help us contact and understand our emotions.

Art can reflect tensions that surround us, and like brilliant poetry, help us be comfortable living with the contradictions.

Art can be a money maker for some.

Art can be an expressive medium for making sense of life for some artists.

For some, choosing the art which they enjoy is a way to make those choices which make us human - kind of a solution to an existential crisis.

Art can be…

The list is endless.

But what about contemporary art? Unfortunately, conceptual theorization has taken over too much contemporary art. When this happens, that art becomes like an intellectual exercise - devoid of serious emotional content. It becomes dehumanized by not relating to the human being in the way in which art which is beautiful does.

Truth is that I don’t like much contemporary art at all - the hyper-intellectualizing has destroyed it’s robustness and power. But that’s my reaction.

Ryan Cayabyab

also known as Mr. C, is a Filipino musician, composer and conductor. He was the Executive and Artistic Director for several years for the defunct San Miguel Foundation for the Performing Arts. He was named as National Artist of the Philippines for Music in 2018.[4]

His works range from commissioned full-length ballets, theater musicals, choral pieces, a Mass set to unaccompanied chorus, and orchestral pieces, to commercial recordings of popular music, film scores and television specials.

Cayabyab's current project includes the Ryan Cayabyab Singers (RCS), a group of seven young adult singers comparable to his group Smokey Mountain in the early 1990s. 29. Example of Contemporary today s Society.​


AnswerExplanation:

Contemporary society refers to the modern society, in existence at the time being, and the social features it relates to. It is a dynamic reference, with the exact features being referred to changing from generation to generation.

The contemporary society at the moment features great technological dependence and innovations, longer average life spans in people, higher levels of literacy, and such things as greater levels of gender equality. Technological advances have also led to the notion of globalization and a higher awareness of international concerns. Towards the future, these features of the society are going to evolve, and either develop further, or recede into oblivion, as other contemporary issues take over.
