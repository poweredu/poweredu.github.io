The word comes from the ancient Greek word plastikos, which means to mold or give  form. Plastic surgery is a surgical specialty involved with both the improvement in  a person's appearance and the reconstruction of facial and body tissue defects caused  by illness, trauma, or birth disorders.

Apart from repairing physical damage caused by illness and injury, plastic surgery can also play an important role in boosting patients’ self-esteem (take out:, confidence,) and quality of life.
