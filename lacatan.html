3. HOW MANY CALORIES WILL YOU GET FROM EATING ONE BREAKFAST MEAL OF LACATAN,SCAMBLED EGG .PLAIN RICE, AND HOT MILK CONTAINS 12 GRAMS PROTEIN,5 GRAMS FAT,AND 58 GRAMS CARBOHYDRATES? WITH SOLUTION PLEASE!


Answer:

Answer:

Total amount of calories = 325

Explanation:

Given:

Amount of carbohydrate = 58 gram

Amount of protein = 12 gram

Amount of fat = 5 gram

Find:

Total amount of calories

Computation:

1 gram protein = 4 calories

1 gram carbohydrate = 4 calories

1 gram fat = 9 calories

Total amount of calories = (58)(4) + (12)(4) + (5)(9)

Total amount of calories = 232 +48 + 45

Total amount of calories = 325
