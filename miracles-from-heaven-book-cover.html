1. miracles from heaven summary?​


Answer:

The film is based on the true story of a young girl named Annabel Beam who suffers from a rare and incurable digestive disorder that threatens to take her life.

The film begins with Annabel living in Burleson, Texas with her parents, Christy and Kevin Beam, and her two sisters. Annabel's condition continues to worsen despite numerous visits to doctors and hospitals. One day, Annabel falls into a hollowed-out tree while playing and is trapped for several hours. When she is finally rescued, she miraculously seems to be cured of her illness.

9. 1. What is miracle? 2. How many miracles did Jesus perform? 3. Choose 1 miracle of Jesus and share what lesson you learned from it.​


Answer:

1.What is miracle?

A miracle is an event that seems inexplicable by natural or scientific laws and accordingly gets attributed to some supernatural of praeternatural cause.

2.How many miracles did Jesus perform?

In the Gospel of John, Jesus is said to have performed seven miraculous signs that characterize his ministry, from changing water into wine at the start of his ministry to raising Lazarus from the dead at the end. For many Christians and Muslims, the miracles are actual historical events.
