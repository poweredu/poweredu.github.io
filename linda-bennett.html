3. What is the theme of the poem "Tamara's Opus" by Joshua Bennett?


Answer:

his struggle to communicate with his deaf older sister.

Bennett's signature piece, “Tamara's Opus,” is about his struggle to communicate with his deaf older sister. Its central image depicts her 20th birthday party, where Bennett, then 11, saw his sister's deaf friends dancing to the vibrations of hip-hop blaring from speakers.

23. Carla is 21 years older than her daughter, Linda. The sum of their ages is 37.How old is Linda?


Let Carla's age be C, and Linda's age L. "Carla is 21 years older than Linda" can be represented as

C = 21 + L

"The sum of their ages is 37" can be represented as

C + L = 37

We can put these two equations into a system of linear equations in two variables

C = 21 + L
C + L = 37

(21 + L) + L = 37
21 + 2L = 37
2L = 16
L = 8

Therefore Linda is 8 years old.

If also solving for Carla's age,

C + 8 = 37
C = 29

Therefore Carla is 29 years old.Linda: x
