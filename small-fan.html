3. A small desk fan operating on 220V expends 1.5kW of power. (a) How much current is flowing in the circuit? (1pt) (b) What is the resistance of the electric fan? (1pt)​


a. Current is 6.82 A

b. Resistance is 32.26 Ω

Explanation:

Step 1: Enumerate the given values.

Given:

P = 1.5 KW = 1500 W

V = 220 V

Step 2: Identify what is being asked.

Required:

a. Current

b. Resistance

Step 3: Solve for Current and Resistance

Solution:

a. The formula for Current is given by:

I = P/V

where: I - Current

P - Power

V - Voltage

Substituting the value of P and V in the formula

I = (1500 W)/(220 V)

I = 6.82 A

Final Answer:

Final Answer:Current is 6.82 A

b. The formula for Resistance is given by:

R = V/I

where: R - Resistance

I - Current

V - Voltage

Solution:

Substituting the value of V and I in the formula,

R = (220 V)/(6.82 A)

R = 32.26 Ω

Final Answer:

Resistance is 32.26 Ω

21. how was CPU fan similar to system fan?​


Answer:

A computer fan is any fan inside, or attached to, a computer case used for active cooling. Fans are used to draw cooler air into the case from the outside, expel warm air from inside and move air across a heat sink to cool a particular component. Both axial and sometimes centrifugal (blower/squirrel-cage) fans are used in computers. Computer fans commonly come in standard sizes, and are powered and controlled using 3-pin or 4-pin fan connectors.
