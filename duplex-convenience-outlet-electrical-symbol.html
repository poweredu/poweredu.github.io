What is the symbol for a duplex receptacle?

The waterproof outlet symbol refers to an outlet that is located outdoors and is required to have a cover over the outlet that will make it waterproof. The quad outlet symbol refers to two duplex receptacles that are mounted side by side in a two gang electrical box.

What is a double outlet called?

Duplex receptacles can be installed side-by-side in a double-gang electrical box and connected to the same or different circuits. This is called a double gang outlet, or a quad outlet. ... Outlets are designed to make connecting them in this way easy.
