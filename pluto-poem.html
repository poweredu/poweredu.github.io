8. Pluto is also a planet?


PLUTO

Pluto was named after the Roman god of underworld or Hades in Greek Mythology.

From its discovery until the year 2006, Pluto was a legitimate planet and the ninth planet of the Solar System next to Neptune. However, due to the discovery of Makemake and Eris, both are small celestial bodies located way beyond the solar system called Kuiper Belt Objects or KBO, the planetary status of Pluto was reduced and redefined to just dwarf planet.

Planet to Dwarf Planet

In 2006, the Astronomers recognized that Pluto was in fact a large Kuiper Belt Object or KBO located way beyond the orbit of Neptune in the Kuiper Belt and not the ninth planet of the solar system, that's why the IAU or International Astronomical Union decided to reevaluate and redefine the status of Pluto, becoming the first member of the new group of celestial bodies called Dwarf Planets.
