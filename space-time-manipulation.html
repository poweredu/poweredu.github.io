Answer

Earth Art is the manipulation of space that uses different elements and uses Earthworks to Show Land Arts​.

What is Earth Art?

Earth Art is an art form that is created in nature using natural resources like soil, any type of rock, organic material (e.g. logs, branches, leaves, etc.), and water. Then, man-made materials like concrete, metal, mineral pigments, asphalt, and other synthetic materials are also added.

What is the purpose of Earth Art?

As a Contemporary Art movement, the objective is to heighten public awareness of Man's relationship with the natural world by modifying the landscape to be thought-provoking constructions.
