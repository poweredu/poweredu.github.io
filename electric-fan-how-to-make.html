15. how to disassemble electric fan ​


The process of disassembling an electric fan may vary depending on the specific model and type of fan. However, here are some general steps that you can follow:

Turn off and unplug the fan from the electrical outlet.Locate the screws that hold the front and back grills of the fan in place. Usually, these screws are found at the back of the fan or underneath the fan.Use a screwdriver to remove the screws from the front and back grills.Remove the front and back grills by gently pulling them off.Locate the blade nut or clip that holds the fan blade in place. This is usually found at the center of the fan blade.Use a wrench to unscrew the blade nut or clip and carefully remove the fan blade.Depending on the type of fan, you may need to remove the motor housing or the oscillator knob to fully disassemble the fan. Refer to the user manual or online resources for specific instructions on how to do this.Once you have disassembled the fan, you can clean the various parts, lubricate the motor, or repair any damaged components.

Remember to follow all safety precautions when disassembling an electric fan, and if you are unsure about any step, seek the help of a professional.
