5. characteristics of ideal community


These are some ideal community characteristics, according to Kathleen Kovner Kline.

(1) A best community is a social institution that includes children and youth.

(2) It treats children as ends in themselves.

(3) It's warm and nurturing.

(4) It establishes clear limits and expectations.

(5) The core of the work is performed largely by nonexperts.

(6) It reflects and transmits a shared understanding of what it means to be a good person.

(7) It encourages spiritual and religious development.
