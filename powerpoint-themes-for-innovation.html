3. how do you choose a theme in powerpoint?


Answer:

1. In PowerPoint, click File, and then click New.

2. Do one of the following: Type a key word or phrase into the Search for online templates and themes field, and press Enter. Choose a template.

3. When you find the template that you want, click it to see the details, and then click Create.

Sample Slogan:  (Dis)-connect from technology using books

Inclusive, Innovative and Interconnected is this year's National Book Week theme. The Philippine Library Association. This year is the 85th such celebration and it comes at a rather interesting circumstances because of the boom in internet usage versus that of traditional books. So when making a slogan for the campaign, think of the following:

inclusive -  this means that book reading brings together everyone, regardless of race, gender and preferencesinnovative - book reading is now integrated into different platforms--PDFs, ebooks, etc. This can also cover audio books among other innovations.interconnected - in an online world, everything is now interconnected. What is book reading's role in the world where one gadget can "talk" to one another?

When making the slogan, it is also best to take into consideration the historical context of the National Book Week, which was first celebrated in 1934 to instill interest in reading among the youth.

How are the youth then when it comes to book reading? Is this still something relevant to them? Is it still something they want? What would happen if reading is not done?  These are some questions that could help come up with a slogan on the national book week.

For a poster, you can think of how you would illustrate the above concepts. Again, think of how book reading is amidst technological advancements.
