11. Why airship was not considered the first powered flight?


The airship is not considered to be a powered flight because it did not suffice the requirements or qualities of being a genuine powered flight. The Wright brothers were the first ones to have a true powered flight by inventing the airplane, which was manned, controlled, heavier than air, and sustained – qualities that the airship does not have.

18. Who invented airships? Pls answer and i give 15 points


Answer:

Alberto Santos-Dumont

Alberto Santos-Dumont was a Brazilian inventor and aviation pioneer, one of the very few people to have contributed significantly to the early development of both lighter-than-air and heavier-than-air aircraft.

Henri Giffard

Baptiste Jules Henri Jacques Giffard was a French engineer. In 1852 he invented the steam injector and the powered Giffard dirigible airship.

Konstantin Tsiolkovsky

Konstantin Eduardovich Tsiolkovsky was a Russian and Soviet rocket scientist who pioneered astronautic theory. Along with the Frenchman Robert Esnault-Pelterie, the Transylvanian German Hermann Oberth, and the American Robert H. Goddard, he is one of the founding fathers of modern rocketry and astronautics.

Bartolomeu de Gusmão

Bartolomeu Lourenço de Gusmão was a Brazilian-Portuguese priest and naturalist, who was a pioneer of lighter-than-air airship design.

Hermann Ganswindt

Hermann Ganswindt was a German inventor and spaceflight scientist, whose inventions are thought to have been ahead of his time. He was born in Voigtshof near Seeburg, East Prussia. During his youth, he showed an interest in technology.
