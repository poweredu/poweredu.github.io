25. Write what you currently know about vocal and instrumental music of mindoro instumental music 1. Kudyapi 2. Gitgit (luke)3. Kalutang ​.


Answer:

KUDYAPI-kutiyapi, or kudyapi, is a Philippine two-stringed, fretted boat-lute. It is four to six feet long with nine frets made of hardened beeswax. The instrument is carved out of solid soft wood such as that from the jackfruit tree

KALUTANG-A pair of percussion bars which are struck against each other at specific angles to produce a pitch. These sticks are a part of an entire ensemble of kalutang which when playing together produce melodies. The instrument is cut from the kwatingan tree which grows in Marinduque
