16. Erin is 3 years younger than twice Alex's age. Their ages combined are 33 years. How old are Alex and Erin. If x=Erin's age and y=Alex's age, choose the system that matches the situation.


Problem:

Erin is 3 years younger than twice Alex's age. Their ages combined are 33 years. How old are Alex and Erin. If x = Erin's age and y = Alex's age, choose the system that matches the situation.

Solution:

Let x be Erin's age

Then let y be the Alex's age

In representation of their ages.

Equation 1:

Erin is 3 years younger than twice Alex's age.

x = 3 + 2y

Equation 2:

Their ages combined are 33 years.

x + y = 33

Then, let's solve the linear equation using by substitution method.

Substitute the given value of x into the second equation which is the Alex's age. Then, solve the equation.

x + y = 33(3 + 2y) + y = 333 + 3y = 333y = 33 - 33y = 303y/3 = 30/3y = 10

Substitute the given value of y into the first equation and then, solve the value of x which is the Erin's age.

x = 3 + 2yx = 3 + 2(10)x = 3 + 20x = 23

Checking:

Check the values of the variables obtained by substituting to the linear equations in the problem.

x = 23 and y = 10

Equation 1:

x = 3 + 2y(23) = 3 + 2(10)23 = 3 + 2023 = 23 ✔

Equation 2:

x + y = 33(23) + (10) = 3333 = 33 ✔

Answer:

∴ Therefore, the present age of Erin is 23 years old and Alex is 10 years old.
