16. climax of the story anak by vilma santos​


The climax of the story happens towards the end of the film, when the main character, Josie, finally confronts her daughter, Carla, who flew all the way from the Philippines to Hong Kong to find her mother.

Josie had been working abroad for years and had not been in contact with her family for a long time. She had sent money and gifts for her children's education, but had not been able to come home to see them. Carla had grown resentful of her mother's absence and felt neglected. When Carla finds her mother, she tells her how angry she is and how much she has suffered. Josie, on the other hand, tries to explain her situation and how hard it was for her to work abroad to support her family.

The climax of the story happens when Carla finally understands her mother's sacrifices and they both break down in tears, hugging each other tightly. The emotional scene highlights the importance of family, sacrifice, and forgiveness. It shows that no matter how hard the circumstances, a mother's love for her child will always be strong.

19. Consider the reaction: SiO2 + C → SiC + 2 CO When 100.0 kg of sand (SiO2) is processed, 51.400 kg of SiC is recovered. What is the percent yield of SiC from this process?


Answer:

% yield = 77.02 %

Explanation:

To get the percent yield. We have to calculate the theoretical yield.

First, we convert 100 kg of SiO₂ to moles.

MM  SiO₂=60.08 g/mol

Mole  SiO₂ =100 kg /60.08 g/mol x 1000g/1 kg

Mole  SiO₂ =1664.4474 mole

From the reaction, 1 mole of SiO₂ gives 1 mole of SiC. Using this we know that 1664.4474 mole of  SiO₂ will give 1664.4474 mole SiC.

Converting mole SiC to kg SiC

MM SiC=40.0962 g/mol

kg SiC=1664.4474 mole x 40.0962 g/mol

kg SiC=66,738.01584 g x 1kg / 1000 kg

kg SiC=66.7380 kg

Now we calculate the percent yield:

% yield = (actual yield / theoretical yield ) x 100%

% yield =51.400 kg /66.7380 kg x 100%

% yield = 77.02 %

Kabanata 13: Ang Klase Sa Pisika (Buod)

Ang silid sa pisika ay maluwag at parihaba ang sukat. Malalaki rin ang mga bintana na mayroong rehas na bakal. Sa magkabilang panig ng kuwarto ay mayroong upuang kahoy na aabot sa tatlong baitang ang taas. Doon umuupo ang mga mag-aaral na nakaayos batay sa titik ng kanilang apelyido.

Kahit malaki ang silid, makikita namang wala itong anumang palamuti. May mga kagamitan at instrumento para sa pag-aaral sa pisika ngunit nakasalansan naman sa isang aparador na nakakandado.

Si Padre Millon ang maestro sa Pisika na tanyag sa paaralan ng San Juan de Letran. Isa-isa niyang tinatawag ang mga mag-aaral upang tanungin ng aralin. Ang magkaibigang Pelaez at Placido ay nagsesenyasan na magturuan. Inapakan ni Pelaez ang paa ng kaibigan bilang hudyat ngunit napasigaw si Placido.

Siya tuloy ang napagbalingan ng inis ng guro at tinanong. Utal-utal siyang sumagot at nagalit ang pari at binigyan siya ng mababang marka habang nakatanggap pa ng lait at mura mula sa pari. Nainis si Placido at biglang umalis sa klase na ikinagulat ng lahat.
