1.It contains basic biblical texts, including the Lord's Prayer, Hail Mary, and the Salve Regina, as well as the Ten Commandments, and the Sacraments of the Holy Church. The Doctrina Christiana is not only one of the earliest books printed in the Philippines.

2.The Doctrina Christiana was printed in the Philippines in 1593 as part of the effort to introduce Christianity.

3.The book was printed on paper made from mulberry.

4.The size of the volume, which is unbound, is 9⅛ by 7 inches

5.Fray Juan de Plasencia

6.King Philip II

7.It has seminal significance to the Christianity of the Philippines, and to the faith that marks a huge majority of Filipinos.
