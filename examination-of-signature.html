Summary:

Registering as a voter is a crucial step in nation-building, but it is important to ensure that one meets the eligibility requirements and follows proper procedures to ensure that the voter registration record is active and up to date. Eligibility requirements include Filipino citizenship, age requirement, residency requirement, and no disqualifications. To register, one must bring a valid ID to the local COMELEC office or satellite registration areas, fill out 3 copies of the application form, and have biometrics captured. Other voter-related services include transfer, reactivation, change of name, correction of entries, reinstatement of name, and inclusion of registration record. It is important to contact the local COMELEC office if unsure of the voter registration status.
