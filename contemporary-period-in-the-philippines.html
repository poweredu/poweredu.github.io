Answer:
Mass media has played a significant role in building nationalism in the Philippines during the contemporary period. The media, through various platforms such as television, radio, newspapers, and the internet, has helped to shape national identity and to promote a shared sense of national pride. It has also been used to educate the public about Philippine history and culture, and to promote the values and ideals that are important to the nation. Mass media has also been instrumental in covering and reporting on issues and events that are relevant to Philippine society, including issues related to nationalism and patriotism. Through its reporting, the media has helped to foster a sense of unity and belonging among Filipinos, and has helped to build a sense of national pride and identity. Additionally, mass media has also been used to promote political and social causes that are aligned with national goals and objectives, and to mobilize support for these causes among the public.

18. Learning Tasks 4: What are the Philippine Contemporary Art Forms? Define each in your own words based on the timeline presented. PHILIPPINE ART PERIOD TIMELINE Pre-Colonial Art Period Spanish Colonial Art Period Post War Colonial Art Period American Colonial Art Period Contemporary Art Period ​


Answer:

There are five Philippine contemporary art forms: Pre-Colonial, Spanish Colonial, Post War Colonial, American Colonial, and Contemporary. Each art form has its own unique features and characteristics.

Pre-Colonial art is the earliest form of Philippine art. It is characterized by simple designs and geometric shapes. The most common pre-colonial art forms are pottery and weaving.

Spanish Colonial art is more ornate and elaborate than pre-colonial art. It is often characterized by religious imagery and themes. Common Spanish Colonial art forms include painting, sculpture, and architecture.

Post War Colonial art emerged after the Philippines gained independence from Spain in 1898. This art form is a mix of pre-colonial, Spanish colonial, and American influences. The most common post war colonial art forms are painting and sculpture.

American Colonial art emerged after the United States acquired the Philippines from Spain in 1898. This art form is characterized by realistic depictions of everyday life. Common American Colonial art forms include painting, photography, and architecture.

Contemporary art is the most recent form of Philippine art. It is characterized by abstract or experimental designs. The most common contemporary art forms are installations, performance arts, and digital arts.PA BRAINLY THANKS
