19. HOUSEHOLD WORKERS ETHICAL RESPONSIBILITY AS PROFESSIONAL 1. Competence. Household workers should accept responsibility or employment only on the basis of existing competence or the intention to acquire the necessary competence.Paki explain po English ​


Competence is an essential ethical responsibility for any professional, including household workers. They should have the necessary skills, knowledge, and training to perform their duties competently. If they lack competence in a particular area, they should not accept responsibility or employment until they acquire the necessary competence.

This means that household workers should strive to maintain and update their skills and knowledge regularly. They can do this by attending training sessions, workshops, or conferences, or by reading relevant materials. By doing so, they can ensure that they are competent in their work, which can lead to better job performance and job satisfaction.

Moreover, household workers have a responsibility to be honest about their competence levels. If they lack a particular skill, they should communicate this to their employer and seek opportunities to acquire the necessary competence. Dishonesty about one's competence can lead to poor job performance, which can be harmful to both the employer and the worker.

In summary, competence is a crucial ethical responsibility for household workers as professionals. They should only accept responsibility or employment if they have the necessary competence or are willing to acquire it. By doing so, they can ensure that they provide high-quality services to their employers and maintain their professional integrity.
