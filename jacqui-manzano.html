16. On their family, Manzano family allotted 45% for the education of their children. If the family has a monthly income of 13,540,00 how much is allotted for the education their children?


Answer:

To compute for the amount allotted for the education of the Manzano family's children, we can follow these steps:

Convert the percentage to decimal by dividing it by 100. So, 45% is equal to 0.45.

Multiply the decimal by the monthly income to get the amount allotted for education. Thus, 0.45 x 13,540,00 = 6,093,000.

Therefore, the Manzano family allotted 6,093,000 pesos for the education of their children.
