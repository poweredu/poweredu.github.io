10. You are a young entrepreneur who ventures into selling MILK TEA. On the soft opening day, you sold all 50 glasses of classic and flavored milk tea, and brought in ₱4700. You need to determine how many glasses of classic milk tea and how many glasses of flavored milk tea were sold on that day if a glass of classic milk tea costs ₱100 and a glass of flavored milk tea costs ₱80.


Answer:

27 classic milk teas and 25 flavored milk teas.

Step-by-step explanation:

Suppose that there are equal numbers of classic milk teas and flavored milk teas sold that day. (25 flavored and 25 classic)

Classic Milk Tea: 25 x 100(P100, refers to the cost of the classic milktea)

You get: 2,500.

DO the same to flavored milk tea:

25 x 80(flavored milk tea price)= 2000

Add the sum of 25 flavored milk tea and 25 classic milk tea's price:

2500 + 2000= 4500.

Now, to get the value of ₱4700, you need to add ₱200 more, right?

to get that value, you need 2 more classic milk teas that costs ₱100 each. It would be wrong if you add 2 flavored milk tea because it would cost ₱160 only. adding 1 more will make it ₱240.

If you add the 2 classic milk teas to the 25 supposed classic milk tea value, you get 27 classic milk teas ordered.

Do the math again.

27 x 100= 2700

25 x 80= 2000

2700+2000=4700

Final Answer: ₱4700.
