12. effect on increasing academic performance using remedial class ​


Answer:

effect of using remedial classes on increasing academic performance is a topic that has been studied in education research. Remedial classes are classes that are designed to help students who are struggling academically by providing additional support and instruction in specific subject areas.

Research has shown that remedial classes can be effective in improving academic performance for students who are struggling. One study found that students who participated in remedial classes were more likely to pass college-level courses and were more likely to graduate from college.

Additionally, other studies found that students who participated in remedial classes had higher grades, higher retention rates and were more likely to persist in their academic programs.

Remedial classes can be an effective way to provide additional support and instruction to students who need it and can help to improve their academic performance
