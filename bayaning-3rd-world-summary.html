2. gumawa ng sariling reports sa bayaning 3rd world (2000)​


Bayaning 3rd World (lit. '3rd World Hero') is a 1999 Philippine mockumentary film directed by Mike de Leon and written by De Leon and Clodualdo del Mundo Jr. It examines the heroism of Philippine national hero Jose Rizal, particularly on his supposed retraction of his writings against the Catholic Church in the Philippines during the Spanish colonization period in the country.

Pano mo masasabi na Ang Isang bansa ay 3rd World country "

Ang konsepto ng "third world country" ay unang ginamit sa panahon ng Cold War upang magbigay ng kategorya sa mga bansa batay sa kanilang kaugnayan sa kanyang kalapit na bansa o bloke ng bansa. Sa kasalukuyan, ang terminolohiyang ito ay hindi na gaanong ginagamit dahil sa mga pagbabago sa pandaigdigang pulitika at ekonomiya.

Gayunpaman, kung isasaalang-alang pa rin ang konsepto ng "third world country," maaaring masabing isang bansa ay 3rd World country kung ito ay nagpapakita ng mga sumusunod na katangian:

Kakulangan sa kaunlaran at mahinang ekonomiya, kung saan ang karamihan ng populasyon ay nabubuhay sa kahirapan.

Mataas na antas ng kahirapan, kawalan ng trabaho, kawalan ng disenteng kalagayan sa buhay at pagiging hindi pantay-pantay sa lipunan.

Kakulangan sa mga pangunahing serbisyong pangkalusugan at edukasyon, kung saan ang mga mamamayan ay hindi nakakatugon sa kanilang pangangailangan sa kalusugan at edukasyon.

Kawalan ng kagyat na pagtugon sa mga suliranin tulad ng kalamidad at kawalan ng seguridad sa mamamayan.

Gayunpaman, mahalagang tandaan na ang pagtukoy sa isang bansa bilang "third world country" ay hindi nakapapakita ng kanyang tunay na kalagayan at potensyal. Kaya't mahalagang maglaan ng mga hakbang upang mapabuti ang kalagayan ng bansa at mga mamamayan nito.

24. How do 3rd World Countries balance their need for development with the necessity to protect the environment?


Answer:

Third World countries face a difficult challenge in balancing their need for development with the necessity to protect the environment. Here are some ways they can address this challenge:Implementing Sustainable Development: Third World countries can focus on sustainable development practices that meet the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs. This can be done by implementing policies and strategies that promote economic growth, social progress, and environmental protection.Investing in Renewable Energy: Third World countries can reduce their dependence on fossil fuels by investing in renewable energy sources like solar, wind, and hydropower. This can not only help protect the environment but also reduce energy costs and increase energy security.Implementing Environmental Regulations: Third World countries can implement environmental regulations to protect the environment from harmful practices. This can be done by creating laws and regulations that limit the use of harmful chemicals, protect biodiversity, and reduce pollution.Promoting Sustainable Agriculture: Third World countries can promote sustainable agriculture practices that are less damaging to the environment. This can be done by promoting organic farming, crop rotation, and the use of natural fertilizers.Increasing Public Awareness: Third World countries can increase public awareness about environmental issues and the importance of protecting the environment. This can be done by educating the public about the impact of environmental degradation and the benefits of sustainable practices.
