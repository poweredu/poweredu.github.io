16. Describe the following terminologies from the traditional Folk music of Japan 1. Gagaku - ____________________________________________________ 2. Kangen - ____________________________________________________ 3. Bugaku - ____________________________________________________ 4. Honkyoku - __________________________________________________ 5. Sakura - ____________________________________________________


Answer:

1. GAGAKU

Gagaku (雅楽, lit. "elegant music")[1] is a type of Japanese classical music that was historically used for imperial court music and dances.

2. KANGEN

Kangen (寛元) was a Japanese era name (年号,, nengō,, lit. "year name") after Ninji and before Hoji.

3. BUGAKU

Bugaku (舞楽, court dance and music[1]) is a Japanese traditional dance that has been performed to select elites, mostly in the Japanese imperial court, for over twelve hundred years.

4.HONKYOKU

Honkyoku (本曲, "original pieces") are the pieces of shakuhachi music played by mendicant Japanese Zen monks called komusō.

5.SAKURA

A cherry blossom is a flower of many trees of genus Prunus or Prunus subg. Cerasus. They are also known as Japanese cherry and Sakura
