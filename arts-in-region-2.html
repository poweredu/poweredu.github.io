27. 1. Why do we need to know the Various Contemporary Art forms from different regions?2. After learning the Various Contemporary Art form from the different regions, how this lesson helps you in appreciating Contemporary Art forms?​


Knowing contemporary art from different regions is important due to its historical value being a product and witness of a specific time and era. Understanding the process of creating the artwork can help one appreciate it. It allows us to express, understand, and challenge specific sociocultural or ideological/political developments, so promoting discussion, and raising consciousness.

It's crucial to be familiar with modern art from many places. It allows us to express, understand, and challenge specific sociocultural or ideological/political developments, so promoting discussion, raising consciousness, and stopping the world. Contemporary art has historical significance. This value is as crucial as it has ever been in human history. A particular historical moment is captured in art. It is a crucial component of our collective memory and history. It is also a nuanced representation of a historical way of thinking, the outcome of a certain worldview from a particular time.

Art has educational significance as well. Contemporary art can aid in personal growth. It enables the individual to mature as a person, broadening their perspective on the world or even discovering or inventing their own viewing point or viewpoint onto a given issue, just like other kinds of cultural expression like film, literature, or performing arts.

Understanding the process of creating the artwork can help one appreciate it. Get rid of the idea that art must resemble something identifiable. In modern art, realism of any kind almost never appears unless it serves as a deliberate counter-statement. Think about the work both near and far. After that, embrace it and sense its life and spirit. Look for its cunningness and analyze its approach. If it's a painting, pay particular attention to the use of color, paint, and draftsmanship. Take into account line, composition, texture, scale, and light. Analyze your own subjective reaction and contrast it with other works. Consider the work's novelty at the time it was created as well as the artist's artistic goals. Pay close attention and make an effort to understand the decisions the artist made and how they affect the work. Consider how it links to what comes before, what comes after, and what comes after it if you have any knowledge of art history.
