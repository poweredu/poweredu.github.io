Highly Achievable Goals:

Eradicate extreme poverty and hunger: The Philippine government has implemented several programs to address poverty and hunger, such as the conditional cash transfer program and the national feeding program. Not Highly Achievable Goals:

Develop a global partnership for development: The Philippines faces significant challenges in developing a global partnership for development, such as weak economic and political ties with other countries and limited access to international aid.
