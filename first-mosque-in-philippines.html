the answer is letter B.

The best statement is:

(B.) A mosque provided a location for Muslims to pray and gather.

The Muslims are devoted to their Islamic faith. They would prioritize the Mosque after conquering land for the Muslims to worship and give thanks to their "Allah" after a successful conquest. They would convert temples, churches, synagogues and, any major religious establishments into mosques. However, choice (A.) could also be one of the best statements because the Muslims wanted to convert non-believers to the Islamic faith after the death of their prophet Muhammad. Thus, every Muslim conquest would use an existing religious building temporarily before they build a new Mosque to display their Islamic Faith.
