1. advantage and disadvantages of churros?​


Advantages:

Churros are most delicious when eaten fresh, but they also reheat very well. It has simple ingredients; found in most pantries. Churros satisfy our cravings for sweet things.

Disadvantages:

One churro contains about 116 – 260 calories. churros have a high saturated fat content (143% DV), raising concerns.

Churros are popular snacks in Spain, Portugal, Mexico, and South America, where they’re enjoyed as breakfast, dessert, or even just a snack.
