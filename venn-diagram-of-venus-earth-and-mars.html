3. GUIDE QUESTIONS: 1. What are the similarities and differences of the 3 planets? Write your answers on the Venn ? Diagram. VENUS EARTH MARS​


Answer:

Similarities:

Venus, Earth, and Mars are sibling planets. They all have atmospheres, weathered surfaces, massive volcanoes, and chemically and thermally evolved interiors. Their atmospheres all possess clouds and circulate in response to the thermal forcing by the Sun, modulated by the effects of surface friction and planetary spin.

Difference:

Its diameter is 12,104 km, which is 95% the diameter of Earth. Mars is much smaller, with a diameter of only 6,792 km. And again, in terms of mass, Venus is almost Earth's twin. It has 81% the mass of Earth, while Mars only has 10% the mass of Earth.Dec 1, 2009
