We ignore the thickness of the walls of the cylinders and the box.

:

Let's split the box into 2 squares, each with a volume of 50 cu in.

Write an expression for volume:

V = x^3 Substitute:

50 = x^3

x = cuberoot50 = 3.684 This is the length of each of the sides of the square box, which in this case happens to be a cube. It is also the diameter and the height of our cylinders.

Let's get our data together:

The box is rectangular, it measures:

 

width: 3.684

length: 2(3.684) = 7.368

height: 3.684

Volume: 100 cu inches

The cylinders:

Diameter: 3.684 each

Height: 3.684

12. Dairy Farming explanation​


Answer:

For the long-term production of milk that is processed (either on the farm or at a dairy plant), dairy farming is a category of agriculture.

Dairying, sometimes known as dairy farming, is a subset of agriculture that deals with the breeding, care, and use of cows and other dairy animals to produce milk and other dairy products.

Explanation:

A long-term method of producing milk that is later processed for sale as a dairy product is known as dairy farming. In many parts of Europe and Africa, dairy production dates back to the early Neolithic period, around the seventh millennium BC.
