5. What are the achievements of ninoy aquino


1. War-time journalist.He is the youngest war correspondent to cover the Korean War for the Manila Times at age 17. For that effort, he received the Philippine Legion of Honor award from President Elpidio Quirino at the age of 18.His eloquence on print and speech served him well.‎

2. Defense adviser and negotiator.An adviser to then Defense Secretary Ramon Magsaysay at age 21. In early 1954, was assigned as his personal emissary to Luis Taruc, leader of the Hukbong Bayan Laban sa Hapon (HUKBALAHAP) rebel group. After four months of negotiations, he was credited for Taruc’s unconditional surrender and was given a second Philippine Legion of Honor award with the degree of Commander on October 14, 1954.

3. Practical lover.Ninoy chose to ditch one beautiful Imelda Romualdez for a Corazon Cojuangco. Aquino made a logical choice as he was part of a ruling elite political family that had high ambitions. The Aquino-Cojuangco union has in effect produced politicians who have become Presidents (wife Corazon and son Noynoy), senator (Noynoy) and congressman (Noynoy).

4. Astute politician.He became mayor of Concepcion, Tarlac in 1955 at the age of 22. In 1959, he was elected the nation’s youngest vice-governor at 27 (erased by Jolo Revilla, who got elected Cavite vice governor at 25 years old, in 2013). In 1961, Ninoy became Tarlac provincial governor at age 29. He became secretary-general of the Liberal Party in 1966 and a year later was elected as the youngest senator in the country’s history at age 34. Note that he was a member of the Nacionalista Party before jumping to the LP.

5. Palace critic in the Senate.Ninoy upped the ante as a fiscalizer and critic of Malacañang in the Senate. In his first year as senator, he alleged that Pres. Ferdinand Marcos was “establishing a garrison state by increasing the armed forces budget, saddling the defense department with overstaying generals and militarizing civilian government offices.”

6. Anti-dictatorship fighter. President Marcos declared martial law on September 21, 1972 and had all opposition figures, including Aquino arrested and imprisoned on trumped-up charges. Ninoy went on a high-profile 40-day hunger strike on April 4, 1975 to protest the injustices of his military trial. After years of exile in the United States, he went home and was assassinated on August 21, 1983. His assassination galvanized various reluctant sections of the opposition to join the struggle marked by widening protests to overthrow the Marcos dictatorship that culminated in People Power I on February 22–25, 1986.

25. tungkol kay benigno ninoy aquino


Answer:

Explanation:

Benigno Simeon "Ninoy" Aquino Jr. (Nobyembre 27, 1932 - Agosto 21, 1983) ay isang pulitiko na Pilipino na nagsilbing Senador ng Pilipinas (1967–1972) at gobernador ng lalawigan ng Tarlac. Siya ang asawa ng dating Pangulo ng Pilipinas na si Corazon Aquino at ama ng dating Pangulo ng Pilipinas na si Benigno Aquino III. Si Aquino, kasama sina Gerardo Roxas at Jovito Salonga, ay bumuo ng pamumuno ng oposisyon patungo kay Pangulong Ferdinand Marcos. Ilang sandali matapos ang pagpapataw ng batas militar, siya ay naaresto noong 1972 kasama ang iba na nauugnay sa armadong pag-aalsa ng mga Komunista at isinulong sa loob ng pitong taon. Itinatag niya ang kanyang sariling partido, si Lakas ng Bayan, at tumakbo sa halalan ng parliyamentong Pilipino noong 1978, ngunit ang lahat ng mga kandidato ng partido, kasama si Aquino, ay nawala sa halalan. Noong 1980, pinahintulutan ni Aquino na maglakbay sa Estados Unidos para sa paggamot sa medisina kasunod ng isang atake sa puso. Pinatay siya sa Manila International Airport noong 1983 nang bumalik mula sa kanyang ipinataw sa sarili. Ang kanyang kamatayan ay napulot ang kanyang balo, si Corazon, sa limelight sa politika, at hinikayat siya na matagumpay na tumakbo sa isang anim na taong termino bilang Pangulo bilang isang miyembro ng partido ng UNIDO sa halalan ng 1986.

Kabilang sa iba pang mga pampublikong istruktura, ang Manila International Airport mula nang pinalitan ng pangalan ang Ninoy Aquino International Airport bilang karangalan, at ang anibersaryo ng kanyang pagkamatay ay isang pambansang holiday.

29. ninoy Aquino administration health​


Answer:

I explain why

Explanation:

PRESIDENT Benigno Aquino 3rd on Monday declared that the “padrino system” in health services for the poor has ended as he vowed to help local government officials needing health support programs from the national government.

Speaking before officials of the Health department, Philippine Health Insurance Corp and various local government units in Metro Manila, the President said his administration was successful in stamping out “patronage politics” especially in availing health services.

The President launched the Alaga Ka program which aims to disseminate health projects for the poor.

Unlike in the past when people had to queue to avail themselves of free government services, Aquino said the new program aims to educate the 14.7 million poor families about the services offered at no or very little cost by the Department of Health and Philhealth.

He said the public must maximize the benefits they can derive from these health programs, including micronutrient supplements, maternal and neonatal care package and family planning. He added that the sick can get free medical treatment in government hospitals.

With the president during the launch were Health Secretary Enrique Ona, Education Secretary, Brother Armin Luistro, PhilHealth President and CEO, Alex Padilla and other Philihealth and local government officials.

Saying “information is power,” Aquino said many Filipinos are still unaware of the various health programs being offered by the government. Because of this, the ALAGA KA program aims to achieve the widest dissemination of existing projects in the health sector.
