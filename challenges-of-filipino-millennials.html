25. as a filipino millennial what can you do to a further uplift our filipino martial arts in today's generation​


Martial Arts Promotion  

Martial arts is attracting an increasing number of people, and it isn't just girls. Adult martial arts classes are becoming more popular, and there are no signs of this trend slowing down.

However, as the number of studios and companies grows, so does rivalry.  

1. Encourage Couples to Engage in Martial Arts - There are many advantages to couples who participate in martial arts together, and it's past time you took advantage of them (if you haven't already). Among the advantages are:

Increased time spent together leads to serious bonding. Communication has improved. Physical and mental interaction is improved.  

2. Give Martial Arts Lessons to Friends - Selling classes to friends is a perfect way to advertise to adults, in our opinion. It not only brings in new customers, but it also allows the regulars to create memories with their families.  

3. Host Age-Friendly Tournaments - Adult tournaments may be held yearly, quarterly, or weekly. Consider doing an experience at least once every three months. When you host an event every quarter, beginners will learn more about what you do (even if they show up for classes halfway through the season).  

4. Create a Vibrant and Diverse Martial Arts School Website - Today's websites must be dynamic and varied. Images, animations, and text work together to convey what makes your school unique and persuade people to contact you. It should use testimonials and ratings to create confidence. It must, of course, be helpful to current students, offering information about classes, plans, and event announcements.  

5. Establish a Solid Reputation - Today's consumers value ratings to the point that they want to see them with a business. A favorable review profile goes a long way toward persuading people that you follow through with your promises. A bad one will put your company out of business.
