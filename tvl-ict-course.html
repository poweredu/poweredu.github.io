1. What is Work Immersion for TVL ICT?​


Answer:

Work Immersion is one of the course requirements for graduation. A Senior High School student has to undergo Work Immersion in an industry that directly relates to the

student’s postsecondary goal. Through Work Immersion, the students are exposed to and become familiar with work-related environment related to their field of specialization

to enhance their competence. Specifically, the students are able to:

(i) gain relevant and practical industrial skills under the guidance of industry experts and workers;

(ii)  appreciate the importance and application of the principles and theories taught in school;

(iii) enhance their technical knowledge and skills;

(iv) enrich their skills in

communications and human relations; and

(v) develop good work habits, attitudes, appreciation, and respect for work. These prepare them to meet the needs and challenges  of employment or higher education after graduation

2. scope and delimitation to tvl ict css​


Answer:

The scope of the TVL ICT CSS (Technical Vocational Livelihood Information and Communication Technology Computer Systems Servicing) program includes the study of hardware and software installation, configuration, and maintenance. The program also covers computer networking, web development, and database management. The students will learn the necessary skills to become computer technicians, network administrators, and IT support staff.

The delimitations of the TVL ICT CSS program include the restriction of the program to the study of computer systems servicing and related fields. It does not cover other areas of information technology such as programming or software development. The program also focuses on the technical skills needed for entry-level positions in the field, rather than on the management or strategic aspects of IT. The program may also have limitations in terms of available resources, equipment, and facilities, which may impact the depth and breadth of the curriculum.

Explanation:

Bioinformatics is a combination of biological sciences and information engineering science (IT). In general, bioinformatics is defined as the application of computational and analytical tools to capture and interpret biological data.

This science is a new science that covers various disciplines including computer science, mathematics and physics, biology, and medical science, all of which are mutually supportive and mutually beneficial to each other. Interaction of disciplines related to Bioinformatics

Bioinformatics was born on the initiative of computer science experts based on artificial intelligence. They think that all the phenomena that exist in nature can be artificially created by simulating these phenomena.

Bioinformatics is important for the management of data from the world of biology and modern medicine. The main tools of Bioinformatics are software programs and supported by internet availability. Currently, the development of biological sciences is strongly influenced by the development of bioinformatics. It is undeniable that bioinformatics has accelerated the progress of biological sciences.

Furthermore, when viewed from a more specific field, the progress of a field is strongly influenced by advances in bioinformatics. The more advanced bioinformatics in a field (marked by the number of available software), the more advanced the field will be.

Why is Bioinformatics a trend?

Modern biotechnology is characterized by the human ability to manipulate the genetic code DNA, the “blueprint of life”. Its various applications have penetrated the medical, food, environmental, etc. sectors.

The progress of the science of Bioinformatics starts from a genome project that is carried out throughout the world and produces a pile of gene information from various living things, from low-level living things to high-level living things.

The application of IT life sciences which gave birth to the field of Bioinformatics in the field of biology/will become increasingly important in the future, not only accelerating biotechnology advances but also bridging the two new economic waves (IT & biotechnology).

The existence of a database is the main requirement in bioinformatics analysis. With Bioinformatics, these data can be stored regularly in a short time and with a high degree of accuracy and at the same time analyzed with programs made for a specific purpose. 28. process of Significance of Technology in ICT courses ​


Answer:

The significance of technology in ICT (Information and Communication Technology) courses lies in the fact that technology is an integral part of our daily lives, and the use of technology has transformed the way we communicate, learn, work and do business. ICT courses focus on the study of various technologies that are used in the field of information and communication, including hardware, software, networking, databases, web design, and multimedia.

The process of understanding the significance of technology in ICT courses involves the following steps:

Identifying the role of technology: The first step in understanding the significance of technology in ICT courses is to identify the role of technology in the field of information and communication. This involves analyzing the various technologies that are used in ICT, their functions, and how they have evolved over time.

Evaluating the impact of technology: The next step is to evaluate the impact of technology on the field of ICT. This involves examining how technology has changed the way we communicate, learn, work, and do business, and how it has impacted the economy and society as a whole.

Understanding emerging technologies: Another important aspect of understanding the significance of technology in ICT courses is to keep up-to-date with emerging technologies. This involves staying informed about the latest advancements in the field of technology, and how they can be applied to various industries and fields.

Developing skills: Finally, understanding the significance of technology in ICT courses involves developing the skills needed to work with various technologies. This includes developing skills in programming, web design, networking, and database management, among others.

By following these steps, students can gain a better understanding of the significance of technology in ICT courses, and how it can be applied in various industries and fields.

30. 1. how is technology used in ICT courses?​


Answer:

Technology is used extensively in ICT (Information and Communications Technology) courses. ICT courses are designed to teach students how to use technology to solve problems and perform various tasks. Students learn how to use computer hardware, software, networks, and the internet to create, store, retrieve, and transmit information. They also learn about the social and ethical implications of technology, such as privacy, security, and copyright issues. Technology is used in ICT courses in various ways, such as online learning platforms, virtual labs, multimedia presentations, and interactive simulations. These tools help students to learn and practice ICT skills in a dynamic and engaging way.
