13. Nolaine ate 1/4 rice cake while Alejandra eat 1/4 the same cake. How much cake did both of them eat together?A. ½B. ¼C. ¾D. 4/4​with solution


Solution:
1/4 + 1/4 = 2/4 or 1/2 (reduce the answer "2/4" to lowest term by multiplying both the numerator and denominator with "2")

So the answer is letter A. 1/2
