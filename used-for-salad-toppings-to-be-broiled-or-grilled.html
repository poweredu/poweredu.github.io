23. discuss the difference between broiling ( grilling) and roasting.​


Explanation:

Heating elements on the top, bottom and sometimes the back of the oven are used for an all-over cooking method. Roasting uses the same all-over heat as baking but at higher temperatures. Broiling uses top-down heat at high and extra-high temperatures to brown or crisp the top of food.
