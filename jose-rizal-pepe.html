5. In the town of balaoan three children namely pepe, juan and jose deliver newspapers. pepe delivers 3 times as many papers as jose and juan delivers 13 more than pepe. if the 3 children delivered 496 newspaper how many papers does each deliver?


Answer:

Let x, 3x and 3x + 13 be the newspapers

x + 3x + (3x + 13) = 496

Combine like terms.

7x + 13 = 496

Transpose 13 and change its sign.

7x = 496 - 13

Subtract.

7x = 483

Divide both sides by 7.

7x/7 = 483/7

x = 69

Jose delivered 69 newspapers.

3x

3(69) = 207

Pepe delivered 207 newspapers.

3x + 13

3(69) + 13

207 + 13 = 220

Juan delivered 220 newspapers.

Checking:

x + 3x + (3x + 13) = 496

69 + 3(69) + ((3)(69) + 13) = 496

69 + 207 + 220 = 496

496 = 496

Therefore,

Jose delivered 69 newspapers.

Pepe delivered 207 newspapers.

Juan delivered 220 newspapers.

29. Differentiate Paciano from Joe or "Pepe"Rizal in two way.


Paciano was older than Jose Rizal. Paciano was born on March 9, 1851, while Jose Rizal was born on June 19, 1861.

Paciano was a more active participant in the Philippine Revolution. While Jose Rizal is known for his literary works and advocacy for reform, Paciano was actively involved in the armed struggle for independence from Spain. He served as a general in the Philippine Revolution and was a close adviser to Andres Bonifacio, the founder of the Katipunan, a secret society that played a key role in the Philippine Revolution. Jose Rizal, on the other hand, did not participate in the armed struggle and advocated for peaceful reform through his writings.
