9. what tree main objectives study of cookery?1.2.3.​


Main Three Objectives of Cookery:

1.Palatability and food quality is improved by cooking - Appearance, flavour, texture and taste of food are enhanced while cooking.

2.Introduces variety - Different dishes can be prepared with the same ingredients. (Eg.) Rice can be made into biriyani and kheer.

3.Increases food consumption - Cooking brings about improvement in texture and flavour thereby increasing consumption of food.

Increases availability of nutrients - Example in raw egg, avidin binds biotin making it unavailable to the body. By cooking, avidin gets denatured and biotin is made available.
