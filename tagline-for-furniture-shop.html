4. You just moved into a new house. Your family members plan to buy a new furniture. What you should consider when you visit a furniture shop?​


Answer:When you visit a furniture shop, there are a few things you should consider. First, think about the style and theme of your home. Are you going for a modern look or a more traditional feel? This will help you choose furniture pieces that will complement your existing decor. Next, consider the size and layout of the room. Measure the space before you go and bring those measurements with you to ensure the furniture you choose will fit properly. Finally, think about the functionality of the furniture. Will it serve a specific purpose, such as a sofa bed for guests or a desk for a home office? By keeping these factors in mind, you'll be able to make informed decisions and choose furniture that will be both stylish and functional for your new home.
