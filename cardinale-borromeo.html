Luis F. Borromeo, also known as Borromeo Lou, was a Filipino jazz musician and entertainer. Known as the "King of Jazz" in the Philippines, Borromeo popularized the music genre of jazz in the Philippines. He is also noted for popularizing bodabil, a Filipinized variation of the Western performance of vaudeville.

27. What is the meaning of the poem "Restless" by Cecilia Borromeo?​


Answer:

Structure:  The poem’s structure consists with three stanzas with four lines each. The poem does not follow any rhyme scheme and the theme seems to be sad most of the time.

Scheme: The poem is written in free verse, meaning there is no restriction of form on how the poem was constructed. On the other hand, the author is able to express what was in her thoughts; that she somehow wanted attention from all of the hard work she had encountered.

Figurative Language: The poem is mostly consisted with metaphors, as to describe an idea that the author would like to say to the reader. The writing style and the words also included personification, as it brings the objects to life to resemble her history. They are also traces of simile within poem.
