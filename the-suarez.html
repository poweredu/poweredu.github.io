22. narrate the life and work of santiago suarez​


Answer:

SANTIAGO SUAREZ

One of the most prolific Filipino musical composers, Santiago S. Suarez was born on May 23, 1901, in Sampaloc, Manila.

Suarez studied at the Ateneo de Manila in Intramuros. He took up music at the University of the Philippines and trained under the noted musicians like Cayetano Jacobe, Pedro Eloriaga and Nicanor Abelardo.

He composed more than 400 pieces of harana, balitaw, danzas and ballads.

Among his compositions:

Bakya Mo Neneng

Neneng’s Wooden Clogs

Sa Libis ng Nayon

In the Meadow

Caprichosa

Capricious Woman

Tapis Mo, Inday

Your Overskirt, Inday

Bulaklak at Paruparo

Flower and Butterfly

As the story unfolds, Gabriela becomes embroiled in a power struggle between the wealthy and powerful Santibañez family and the workers of the steel mill owned by Alejandro Beltrán, played by Jorge Luis Pila. Gabriela takes on the role of the "patrona," or boss, of the steel mill, and fights for the rights of the workers against the Santibañez family, who are determined to maintain their power and control over the town.

Throughout the series, Gabriela faces many challenges and obstacles, including threats to her life, as she fights for justice and tries to uncover the truth about her father's case. Along the way, she falls in love with Alejandro, who helps her in her quest for justice.

Gabriela Suarez is portrayed by actress Aracely Arambula, while Veronica Dantes is a separate character played by Christian Bach. Veronica is a wealthy and powerful woman who is also involved in the power struggle between the Santibañez family and the steel mill workers.
