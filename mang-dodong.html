3. Mang Dodong's farm has cows and ducks. The owner said that his cows and ducks had a total of 48 legs and 15 heads. 14. How many ducks are there in the farm of Mang Dodong? A. 15 B. 6 C. 9 D. 48 15. How many cows are there in the farm of Mang Dodong? A. 15 B. 6 C. 9 D. 48​


SYSTEM OF LINEAR EQUATIONS

Mang Dodong's farm has cows and ducks. The owner said that his cows and ducks had a total of 48 legs and 15 heads.

QUESTION #1: How many ducks are there in Mang Dodong's farm?

A. 15B. 6C. 9D. 48

QUESTION #2: How many cows are there in Mang Dodong's farm?

A. 15B. 6C. 9D. 48

SOLUTION:

» Form a system of linear equations.

We let [tex] x [/tex] = the number of cow heads/legs, and [tex] y [/tex] = the number of ducks heads/legs.

We know that both cows and ducks have one head. In Mang Dodong's farm, the total number of duck heads and cow heads is 15. So the first equation is:

[tex] \qquad \large x + y = 15 [/tex]

We also know that a cow has four legs and a duck has two. In Mang Dodong's farm, the total number of duck legs and cow legs is 48. So the second equation is:

[tex] \qquad \large 4x + 2y = 48 [/tex]

So the system of linear equations is:

[tex] \qquad \large \begin{cases} x + y = 15 \\ 4x + 2y = 48 \end{cases} [/tex]

» Find the number of ducks and cows.

We will use substitution method to solve the given system.

In the first equation, solve for y in terms of x.

[tex] x + y = 15 [/tex][tex] y = 15 - x [/tex]

In the second equation, substitute the value of y for 1a to solve for x.

[tex] 4x + 2y = 48 [/tex][tex] 4x + 2(15 - x) = 48 [/tex][tex] 4x + 30 - 2x = 48 [/tex][tex] (4x - 2x) + 30 = 48 [/tex][tex] 2x = 48 - 30 [/tex][tex] 2x = 18 [/tex][tex] x = \frac{18}{2} [/tex]

[tex] \large \green {x = 9} \qquad \tiny \textsf{(There are 9 cows in Mang Dodong's Farm.)} [/tex]

Substitute the value of x in any of the two equations to solve for y.

[tex] x + y = 15 [/tex][tex] 9 + y = 15 [/tex][tex] y = 15 - 9 [/tex]

[tex] \large \green {y = 6} \qquad \tiny \textsf{(There are 6 ducks in Mang Dodong's Farm.)} [/tex]

Therefore, there are 9 cows and 6 ducks in Mang Dodong's farm. The correct answers for questions 1 and 2 is choice B and choice C, respectively.
