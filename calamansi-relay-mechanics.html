13. calamansi juice is composed of calamansi extract, water, and sugar. Is calamansi juice a homogeneous or a hetergeneous mixture explain


Answer:

Homogenous mixture

Explanation:

Homogenous means that the composition of a mixture are all the same. In other words, a homogenous substance is uniform in terms of its composition. Another cue that the mixture is homogenous is to determine if the components can still be differentiated and separated from each other.  In the case of calamansi juice, despite that it's a combination of three substances (calamansi extract, water, and sugar), once the mixture is stirred, these substances mixes uniformly, making it extremely difficult if not impossible to separate them into the three components again.

Heterogenous means that the components of the mixture can still be separated and differentiated from each other. Other food preparations allows the separation of the individual recipe, such as halo-halo, making them heterogenous mixtures.
