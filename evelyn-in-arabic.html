7. The sides of square ABCD are chords of circle O while the sides of square PQRS are tangents to circle O as shown. Evelyn claims that the area of the circumscribed square PQRS is twice the area of the inscribed square ABCD. is evelyn's claim correct?


Yes.

Note: 

1) The measure of diagonal of the inscribed square ABCD is equal to the diameter of the circle.

2)  The diameter of the circle is equal to the measure of the side of the circumscribed square PQRS.

Let's say:

diagonal of inscribed square = 4       
Side:
4² = 2s²
s² = (√16)/2
s = 2√2
Area of inscribed square ABCD = (2√2)² = 8²

Diagonal = diameter = side of the circumscribed square PQRS.
Area of PQRS = (diagonal of square ABCD)²
= (4)²
= 16 units²

Area of PQRS = 2 (Area of ABCD)
16 units² = 2 (8 units²)

Answer:

Arab nationalism is a nationalist ideology that asserts the Arabs are a nation and promotes the unity of Arab people, celebrating the glories of Arab civilization, the language and literature of the Arabs, calling for rejuvenation and political union in the Arab world.

The Arab world, formally the Arab homeland, also known as the Arab nation, the Arabsphere, or the Arab states, consists of the 22 Arab countries which are members of the Arab League.

Pan-Arabism (Arabic: الوحدة العربية‎ or العروبة) is an ideology that espouses the unification of the countries of North Africa and Western Asia from the Atlantic Ocean to the Arabian Sea, which is referred to as the Arab world.
