18. The force exerted on the smaller pistons of a hydraulic lift is 160 N. The areas of the smaller piston and the larger piston are 0.5 m2 and 1.0 m2, respectively. What is the output force of the larger piston?


Given:

[tex]F_{1} = \text{160 N}[/tex]

[tex]A_{1} = 0.5 \: \text{m}^{2}[/tex]

[tex]A_{2} = 1.0 \: \text{m}^{2}[/tex]

Required:

[tex]F_{2}[/tex]

Solution:

[tex]\frac{F_{1}}{A_{1}} = \frac{F_{2}}{A_{2}}[/tex]

[tex]F_{2} = F_{1} × \frac{A_{2}}{A_{1}}[/tex]

[tex]F_{2} = \text{160 N} × \frac{1.0 \: \text{m}^{2}}{0.5 \: \text{m}^{2}}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{F_{2} = \text{320 N}}[/tex]
