8. Reaction of the Story "A Blind Man's Love"​


True love can never be fathomed because it is way beyond sacrifice and sincerity.  It is easy to love someone based on attraction.  We are moved by the beauty they possess because that is visible.  However, to love someone based only on the personality is not as easy as the first one.  Beauty shall surely fade, but the character does not.  It is therefore remarkable to have someone who loves us for all the beautiful and ugly reasons.  Finding this kind of love is a blessing – one that has to face all the trials and victories together no matter how terrible it may seem.
