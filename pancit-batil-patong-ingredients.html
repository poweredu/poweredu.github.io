1. Ano Ang Isinasahog sa Pancit Batil Patung help please


Answer:

Pancit Batil Patung is a popular noodle dish in Tuguegarao City, Cagayan. It is composed of two parts: the noodles with toppings, and the sauce which looks like an egg drop soup. Poached egg along with sautéed meats and vegetables are topped over the fresh miki noodles, while a piece of egg is cracked and stirred-in simmering beef stock and poured in a bowl. Both components should be present when eating this dish to experience optimal flavor. The sauce is gradually poured over the noodles and meat and it is further enhanced by adding a tablespoonful or two of the chopped onion and vinegar dip.

12. Ano ang kahulugan ng katawagawang Batil Patung?


Answer:

Ang pancit batil patung ng Tuguegarao, Cagayan ay kakaiba dahil carabeef ang isa sa pangunahing distinct ingredients nito. Distinct ito dahil sa Cagayan, mas posibleng makakuha ng karne ng kalabaw kumpara sa baka.

Hinahain din ito ng may sabaw na galling sa pinakuluang carabeef.

Nilalagyan din ito kasabay ng itlog na binati kaya tinawag na batil ang pansit nila na ang ibig sabihin ay “beat the egg”. Ang salitang “patung” naman ay may ibig sabihin na “to put on top”, kung kaya ang sahog ng pansit batil patung ay nasa ibabaw nito.

29. Convert the following measurements of ingredients of Chicken Pancit for the following servings: a. 36 servings b. 2 servings​


Convert recipes for different serving quantities.

To convert a recipe into different serving sizes, we must multiply or divide each ingredient in the original recipe by the number of servings we want. In the question, there is no information about the number of servings from the recipe, so we will assume the amount is for 1 serving.

1. Convert to 36 servings.

1 1/2 lbs. chicken × 36 = 54 lbs.1/2 lb. bihon noodles × 36 = 18 lbs.1/2 lb. sotanghon noodles × 36 = 18 lbs.1/2 piece cabbage × 36 = 18 piece2 pieces carrots × 36 = 72 pieces1 tablespoon garlic × 36 = 36 tablespoons1/2 cup flat leaf parsley × 36 = 18 cups1 piece onion × 36 = 36 piece3 stalks celery × 36 = 108 stalks6 cups water × 36 = 216 cups1/2 cup soy sauce × 36 = 18 cups1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper × 36 = 18 teaspoons or 6 tablespoons2 tablespoons cooking oil × 36 = 72 tablespoons

2. Convert to 2 servings.

1 1/2 lbs. chicken × 2 = 3 lbs.1/2 lb. bihon noodles × 2 = 1 lb.1/2 lb. sotanghon noodles × 2 = 1 lb.1/2 piece cabbage × 2 = 1 piece2 pieces carrots × 2 = 4 pieces1 tablespoon garlic × 2 = 2 tablespoons1/2 cup flat leaf parsley × 2 = 1 cup1 piece onion × 2 = 2 piece3 stalks celery × 2 = 6 stalks6 cups water × 2 = 12 cups1/2 cup soy sauce × 2 = 1 cup1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper × 2 = 1 teaspoon2 tablespoons cooking oil × 2 = 4 tablespoons

Note: If the number of servings of the recipe is more than the number of servings that we want to cook, then we must divide it. For example, a recipe is for 4 servings. If we want to make 2 servings, then we divide all the ingredients into 2.
