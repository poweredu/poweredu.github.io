12. What location is lanzones and soursops? Hill, slope, valley, or plain?


Answer:

Lanzones and soursops are typically grown in tropical climates and are often found on plains or flatlands, rather than on hills, slopes, or valleys. These fruits thrive in warm and humid environments with good soil drainage and access to water. They are commonly cultivated in orchards or gardens and require a sufficient amount of sunlight to grow and ripen.

14. what is the tempo of lanzones festival​


Answer:

The Province of Camiguin is known to many as the home of the sweetest Lanzones in the Philippines. As a way to honor the bounty harvest of the Lanzones fruit, Lanzones Festival is celebrated annually every third week of October with a pack of activities. Locals and tourist from different places gather together to witness the most awaited vibrant event of the province.

As October is fast approaching, the Province of Camiguin is already on the road for preparation for the 39th Lanzones Festival Celebration this October 20-28, 2018. Numerous activities have been prepared including trade fair, sport events, inter-agency dance showdown, live bands, parade and a lot more. The main events of the week-long celebration include the Lanzones Festival Street Dancing and Tableau Competition and the Miss Camiguin Tourism 2018.
