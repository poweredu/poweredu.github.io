18. What can u say about the movie cruella give an movie review


Answer:

I recently watched the live-action film Cruella, which serves as a prequel to the classic Disney movie 101 Dalmatians. I was immediately drawn to the stunning visual and costume design of the movie, which perfectly captured the punk rock vibe of 1970s London.

Emma Stone gave an outstanding performance as the titular character, and Emma Thompson's portrayal of the Baroness von Hellman provided an excellent counterpoint to Cruella's rebellious nature. The music choices were also a great addition to the overall ambiance of the film.

However, I did feel that the plot was convoluted at times and the pacing was uneven, which detracted from the overall experience. The movie also had a darker tone compared to the original animated film, which may not be suitable for all viewers.

I found Cruella to be an entertaining movie with strong performances and impressive visual style. While it may not appeal to everyone, especially those looking for a faithful adaptation of 101 Dalmatians, it is definitely worth a watch for fans of Emma Stone and those who appreciate unique and stylish visual elements in film.
