18. what ive learned about the cordillera, mindoro, palawan and visayas region?​


Answer:

Cordillera instrumental music is distinctively made up of two sound characteristics based on their respective materials . The first one is made of bamboo (flutes and percussion instruments), while the second is made of metal (gongs). These traditions, however, are on their way to extinction due to several factors.Music of the Cordillera,Mindoro, Palawan and Visayas have similarities in terms of function of music in their daily lives. Music is often used in rituals and ceremonies. Most of their musical instruments are made from materials found in their locality.
