17. the out conduct coastal clean up drive​


INTRODUCTION

         We used to have fresh air to breath, greenish surroundings and taste clear and clean water. Nowadays, tables have turned and we can see a big difference from a clean into a polluted environment. As we all know, we are facing a lot of environmental issues such as floods, climate change, melting of the polar ice caps and pollution. May it be from air, water or land. The main cause of these problems are the wastes that are improperly thrown.

          Restore what was lost, we aim for the surrounding that’s greenish and full of grasses and tall trees. Those grasses that have turned into cemented roads, those trees that are placed on such a beautiful area have turned into enormous buildings. Deforestation is the main cause of this, people who illegally cut down trees just for their own convenience mostly results landslide. These trees can prevent floods, it can fight against global warming, so if people continue todo the same thing all over again, it would disturb the balance of its system, causing so much loss not just with our environment but also with our way of living.

         As students of Don Bosco Tarlac, we are planning to think of a program that reduces the chance of those problems from growing. Therefore, we thought of a program that not only helps our own community but also the world we live in. It will also serve as an inspiration in other communities to come up with the same activities in order to maintain a clean and green environment.

II. PURPOSE

      Our purpose in our project is to help reduce waste in our surroundings and to recycle most of the waste like plastics and metals for a better purpose as we sell it to the junk shops. As we sell this waste, we use the money earned to buy plant/tree seeds for them to plant in their surroundings. We want to help each and every barangay to stay clean and have a surrounding that is beneficial to each and every person that resides in that barangay, since we want an environment free from liters and an environment full of trees.

       We aim to provide awareness of how much we are wasting every day. We want to make a statement that we could reuse this waste as we recycle it. This is for us to teach them and make it as “persuasive” as possible for them to participate.

III. PROJECT DESCRIPTION

          We are going to have a competition every month. Each barangay are task to maintain the cleanliness in their area, and they are task to reduce the waste by collecting and selling them itin a junk shop. The residents of each barangay are required to participate in the said event. At the end of every month, one barangay will be announced with the cleanest environment and will earn a point. Then at the end of the year, the barangay with the most points will be awarded with a prize for the efforts they made throughout the year.

          The money from selling the waste materials will be collected each month; the money will be used to buy seedlings. These seedlings will be equally distributed to each barangays and will be planted on open areas around the barangay. This will help us to have a good environment as we progress to have a better future.

       It deals with different programs that raise awareness in different aspects, from a clean-up drive activity, into the fund raising scenario and lastly the tree planting, all of which are connected with each other. Through the clean-up drive activity, another activity came out of our mind, which is to raise funds from selling the waste that has been collected, from these collected fund, we came up with the tree planting program.
