1. (CALVO CEVOI) The human voice is produced by the _____________


Answer: The larynx, or voice box, is located in the neck and performs several important functions in the body. The larynx is involved in swallowing, breathing, and voice production. Sound is produced when the air which passes through the vocal cords causes them to vibrate and create sound waves in the pharynx, nose and mouth.

=================================================================

The human voice is produced by the Vocal Voice

To produce sound, your vocal chords vibrate. Small bands of muscle and other tissues located inside the larynx make up the vocal cords. When the margins of the vocal cords come together, the air from your lungs causes them to vibrate.
