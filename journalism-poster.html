Directions in the photo gallery of your cellphens, choose a picture that showe participation in any school district activities or you may eut pictures from magazines/journals of any related activities Paste in another sheet of Activity # Direction: Draw a poster that shows your participation in any school activities​


Answer:

1. Choose a background image that represents your school or the activity you participated in. For example, if you participated in a sports event, you can use an image of the sports field.

2. Use bold and colorful fonts to write the name of the activity you participated in. You can also use different font sizes to create emphasis.

3. Add a tagline or a catchy phrase that describes your experience at the activity. For instance, if you participated in a music concert, you can write "Making beautiful music with my schoolmates."

4. Include images of yourself participating in the activity. If you don't have any, you can use free stock images that relate to the activity.

5. Use icons or illustrations to represent the different aspects of the activity. For example, for a science fair, you can use images of test tubes, microscopes, and lab coats.

6. Finally, don't forget to add your name and the date of the activity to personalize the poster.

These are just some ideas to create a poster that shows your participation in any school activities. Remember to be creative and have fun!

Pakinabang ng mga Likas na Yaman

Kung ako ay gagawa ng isang poster na nagpapakita ng pakinabang na pang-ekonomiko mula sa mga likas na yaman ng bansa, narito ang aking iguguhit:

Una, hahatiin ko sa apat na bahagi ang aking poster, na kumakatawan sa yamang lupa, yamang tubig, yamang mineral, at yamang kagubatan.Sa bahagi ng yamang lupa, iguguhit ko dito ang ating mga magsasaka at ang kanilang mga inaning pananim.Sa bahagi ng yamang tubig, iguguhit ko ang ilalim ng karagatan at ang lahat ng mga lamang dagat na mahuhuli dito, kabilang na ang iba’t-ibang uri ng isda, kabibe, tahong, alimango, pusit, pugita, hipon, pating, at marami pang iba.Sa bahagi ng yamang mineral, iguguhit ko ang mga minahan sa ating bansa kung saan nakukuha ang mga metal kagaya ng ginto, pilak, at bakal.Sa bahagi ng yamang kagubatan, iguguhit ko ang mayayabong na puno na matatagpuan sa ating mga kabundukan.
