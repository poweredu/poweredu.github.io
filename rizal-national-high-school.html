Answer:

Spanning over six-hectares, it is a big and remarkable institution situated in the heart of the city. It has modern facilities, spacious classrooms, excellent laboratories and a wide-array of world-class extracurricular activities to choose from.

The school also boasts of a diverse student population. Its student body is composed of children from all walks of life, spread out in different economic levels. This gives students the opportunity to interact and learn from different cultures and social backgrounds.

Moreover, San Jose National High School has a number of club activities that students can join. These include student organizations, service clubs, literacy, social and cultural activities, and sports competitions. It also serves as a platform for students who seek to go beyond the classroom and participate in activities outside the campus.

San Jose National High School is proud of its consistent academic performance that shows the excellence of its students. It has also earned recognition as one of the best schools in the Division of Rizal for three consecutive years. With a dedicated and committed faculty and staff, the school guarantees that each student graduates with the best education to propel them to the next level.
