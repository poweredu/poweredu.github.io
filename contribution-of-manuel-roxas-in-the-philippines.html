3. Contribution of Manuel Roxas that we still rely on


CONTRIBUTION OF MANUEL ROXAS THAT WE STILL RELY ON

Answer:

Manuel Roxas was a key figure in the generation that helped the Philippines gain independence from the United States. His administration demonstrated unequivocally that political autonomy without economic independence breeds reaction, perpetuates social inequalities, and encourages exploitation.

President Roxas also arranged the acquisition of 8,000 hectars of land in Bata ngas with the Ayala - Zobel family. These were sold to farmers who were landless.

In his term as well, the Republic Act No. 34 established a 70-30 split between the tenant and the landlord. 70% of the produce will be distributed to the person who covered the costs of planting, harvesting, and work animals. It also limited the amount of interest that landowners could charge tenants to no more than 6%.

All of these things matter and may be counted on nowadays.

7. What are the accomplishments of Manuel A. Roxas?


He is credited for revitalizing the Philippine economy after the World War II. He implemented the Bell Trade Act which granted the United States parity rights, preferential tariff, and 2:1 fixed exchange rate for $800 million post-war rebuilding funds. He also implemented the US-Philippine Military Bases Agreement.

18. talambuhay manuel roxas​


Kasagutan:

Maikling talambuhay ni Pangulong Manuel Roxas:

Si Manuel Roxas ay ipinanganak sa probinsya ng Capiz noong January 1, 1892.

Siya ay nagtapos ng kolehiyo sa College Of Law Of The University Of The Philippines noong 1914. Naging gobernador naman siya noong 1916.

Noong 1941 naging lieutenant colonel din siya sa US Army Forces In The Far East o USAFEE.

Naging presidente naman siya noong 1946 hanggang sa maaga niyang kamatayan noong 1948.
