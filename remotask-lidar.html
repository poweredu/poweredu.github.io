8. what is LIDAR?how it is used in atmosphere monitoring?​


Answer:

Lidar uses a laser to measure distance by illuminating an atmospheric target like particles or water vapor and analyzing the light that reflects back to its sensor, called backscatter.

11. why is LIDAR way better than RADAR?​


Answer:

LiDAR uses lasers with a much lower wavelength than the radio waves used by RADAR. Thanks to this, LiDAR has better accuracy and precision, which allows it to detect smaller objects, in more detail, and create 3D images based on the high-resolution image it creates.

Answer:

LiDAR uses lasers with a much lower wavelength than the radio waves used by RADAR. Thanks to this, LiDAR has better accuracy and precision, which allows it to detect smaller objects, in more detail, and create 3D images based on the high-resolution image it creates.
