13. General Mathematics Title: Budget Plan Instruction: Given an amount of P2,000.00, make Budget Plan For New Year's Celebration​


Answer:

Here is a sample budget plan for New Year's Celebration using an amount of P2,000.00:

Food and Drinks - P800.00

This can include simple yet delicious dishes and snacks such as spaghetti, fried chicken, lumpia, fruit salad, and chips. Drinks can include soda, juice, and water.

Decorations - P300.00

Decorations can include balloons, streamers, party hats, and noise makers to make the celebration more festive.

Party Favors - P200.00

Party favors can be given to guests such as small gift bags filled with sweets, candies, and small trinkets.

Games and Entertainment - P500.00

Plan for games that can be played indoors or outdoors, depending on the venue. This can include board games, card games, or outdoor activities like badminton or frisbee. You can also opt for a movie marathon or karaoke session as entertainment.

Miscellaneous - P200.00

This can be set aside for any unexpected expenses or last-minute purchases that may arise.

Total Budget: P2,000.00

Note: The budget plan can be adjusted depending on the number of guests, venue, and specific preferences of the host.


14. work sample of budget plan ​


Answer:

Here is a simple work sample of a budget plan:

Step-by-step explanation:

Budget Plan for the Month of May 2023

Income:

- Salary: $4,000

- Freelance Work: $500

Total Income: $4,500

Expenses:

- Rent: $1,200

- Utilities: $150

- Groceries: $400

- Transportation: $200

- Internet & Phone: $100

- Entertainment: $150

- Savings: $1,000

- Miscellaneous: $200

Total Expenses: $3,500

Net Income: $1,000

In this example, the budget plan is for the month of May 2023. The income sources for the month include a salary of $4,000 and $500 from freelance work, bringing the total income to $4,500. The expenses for the month include rent, utilities, groceries, transportation, internet and phone bills, entertainment, savings, and miscellaneous expenses, totaling $3,500. The net income for the month is $1,000, which can be used for additional savings or unexpected expenses.

This is a simple example of a budget plan, and the actual plan may vary depending on individual circumstances and financial goals. It is important to create a budget plan that is realistic and achievable, and to revisit and adjust the plan as needed.
