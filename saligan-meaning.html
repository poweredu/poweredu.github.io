16. Sentro ng alternatibong lingap panligal (saligan) leni robredo


Answer:

Sentro ng Alternatibong Lingap Panligal, also known as Sentro ng Alternatibong Lingap Panligan (abbreviated as SALIGAN),[1] is a non-governmental legal organization based in Ateneo de Manila University in Diliman, Quezon City. Founded in 1987, it works with women, farmers, workers, the urban poor, the indigenous peoples and local communities, and marginalized sector. Vice President Leni Robredo[2] is best known as the coordinator of the non-government organization in Naga City, especially in her winning case for the Sumilao Farmers of Bukidnon.[3]
