2. list down the advantage and disadvantages of desktop computer and laptop​


Advantage: Upgradable

Tower PCs come with the advantage of easy access into the internal hardware. This also directly translates into how uncomplicated upgrading can be.

Unlike a laptop that constricts your spec freedom to the built-in features, you can modify a desktop PC’s RAM, storage, graphics chip, monitor, keyboard, mouse, and more. With all of this made possible, replacing damaged desktop parts proves far easier than a promising laptop screen crack or keyboard spill.

Disadvantage: Movability

It’s a simple matter of fact that desktop computers are heavier than laptops. With the many joined parts that make it whole, desktop PCs are next to impossible to move around easily. Once set up, desktops stay where they are stationed.

Advantage: High-performance

The processor chip and other component manufacturers are always coming up with faster tech to power the devices of the future. It’s these same companies that make their latest inventions first available for desktop PCs.

Computer towers, all-in-ones, and workstations are capable of packing in more hardware due to their increased size. With more hardware comes stronger and more reliably efficient performance that comes through every time you power on.

Disadvantage: Space

Due to the general immovability of desktop computers, they are usually stored on a desk or countertop in productivity focused spaces. While laptops can be closed and stowed away like a book, desktop setups function as a piece of furniture wherever you set it up.

29. James bought a laptop and a mobile phone. The laptop cost 3 times as much as the mobile phone. The cost of the laptop was $3642.


Let x be the cost of the mobile phone.

According to the problem, the cost of the laptop is 3 times the cost of the mobile phone. So:

Cost of laptop = 3 * Cost of mobile phone

$3642 = 3x

Dividing both sides by 3, we get:

x = $1214

Therefore, the cost of the mobile phone is $1214.

To check:

Cost of laptop = 3 * Cost of mobile phone

Cost of laptop = 3 * $1214 = $3642

So the cost of the laptop is indeed $3642.

Answer:

In this problem, we are given that James bought a laptop and a mobile phone, and we know that the laptop cost 3 times as much as the mobile phone. We are also told that the cost of the laptop was $3642.

To find the cost of the mobile phone, we can use algebra. Let x be the cost of the mobile phone. Then, we can write an equation based on the information given in the problem:

cost of laptop = 3 times cost of mobile phone

$3642 = 3x

To solve for x, we can divide both sides of the equation by 3:

$3642 ÷ 3 = x

Simplifying the right-hand side, we get:

$1214 = x

So, the cost of the mobile phone is $1214.

We can check our answer by verifying that the cost of the laptop is indeed 3 times the cost of the mobile phone:

3 times $1214 = $3642

Therefore, our answer is correct.
