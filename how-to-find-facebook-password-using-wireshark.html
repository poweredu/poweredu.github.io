11. A group of 25 high school student were asked whether they use eigther facebook or twitter or both. Fifteen of these student use facebook and twelve use twitter.


Answer:

2 users use both. 13 users use facebook only and 10 users use twitter only.


Step-by-step explanation:

Total number of students = 25

Number of facebook users = 15

Number of twitter users = 12


Find the number of users that use both facebook and twitter:

Number of users = 15 + 12 - 25 = 2


Find the number of users that use facebook only:

Number of users = 15 - 2 = 13


Find the number of users that use twitter only:

Number of users = 12 - 2 = 10


Answer: 2 users use both. 13 users use facebook only and 10 users use  twitter only.

17. how do you use Facebook as a tool or as a medium for communication?​


We all know that Facebook is made by Mark Zuckerberg to help us connect to people, that are in certain distances, in a fast way. Thusly, the message we send through this platform are now what we call Instant Messages.

To use this platform as a tool for communication, it has many features to make our communication better and faster. These are the following:

"Create Post"

Clicking that button allows you to write a message for all of your friends in your profile. In this feature, you can do a verbal and non-verbal communication as it allows text, audio, video, and images to be its input.

"Share"

As a user you have the ability to share of what you have seen in the platform if the author itself allows you. Sharing contents helps your friends to be aware or learn more about you.

"Add Friend"

This allows you to not only communicate with a certain person but also learn about his/her profile and findings that may help or interest you. In other words, when you added someone, you will able to see her contents, that may be valuable or not, in an instant.

"Like"

If you place over your cursor to that button, there will be emoticons, other than "like", that will pop out, namely, the "heart", "care" (the new one), "sad", "laugh", and "angry". These are used to relay what we feel about a post. This is  a form of non-verbal communication.

"Comment"

In this section, we can give our opinions through text, sticker, photo, video, and GIF. We can also use emoticons here if the given emoticons are not enough to express ourselves,

Page and Group

Being an attendant to certain groups or pages, makes you aware of the information that they have. In their shoes or rather if you're a page or group creator, you will have the ability to spread your word to a specified audience.

Chatbox

This allows you too to have verbal and non-verbal communication, just like in number one ("Create Post").

Newsfeed

This is where all of the shared contents come together. The posts that you are seeing may be from your friends or external entities. External entities may include itself when it choses to be promoted or advertised.

Audience

This is one of the ways that platform takes care of your privacy. If you're going to post or share something you can choose who can reach your content - the public, your friends, specified ones, or just you.
