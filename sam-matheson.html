7. The age of Sam,Tonton and Ramon are consecutive integers. Sam is younger than Ramon but older than Tonton. Sams mother is thrice as old as Sam and 14 years years older than twice as old as Ramon.Find the age of each


Sam is 16 years old.
Tonton is 15 years old.
Ramon is 17 years old.
Sam's mother is 48 years old.Representation:
Tonton: x
Sam: x + 1
Ramon: x + 2

Equation (Mother's age):
   3 (x + 1) = 14 + 2 (x + 2)
   3x + 3 = 14 + 4 + 2x
   3x + 3 = 2x + 18
   3x - 2x = 18 - 3
   x = 15

Substitute 15 to x to find their ages:
Tonton: x = 15 
Sam: 15 + 1 = 16 
Ramon: 15 + 2 = 17
Mother: 3 (16)  or  2(17) + 14    =  48

Their ages are:

Tonton = 15 years old
Sam = 16 years old
Ramon = 17 years old
Mother = 48 years old

18. kit is twice as old as his friend sam. sam is 5 years older than cara. in 5 years, kit will be three times as old as cara, how old is sam?


Present ages:
x = Cara's age
x + 5 = Sam's age
2(x+5) or 2x + 10 = Kit's age

Ages in 5 years:
x + 5 = Cara's age
x + 5 + 5  or x + 10 = Sam's age
2x + 10 + 5 or 2x + 15 = Kit's age

Equation:
2x + 15 = 3 (x + 5)
2x + 15 = 3x + 15
2x - 3x = 15 - 15
x = 0

Present Ages:
Cara:  x ⇒ 0  (below 1 year old)
Sam:  x + 5  ⇒ 0 + 5 = 5 years old
Kit: 2x + 10  ⇒ 2(0) + 10 = 10 years old

ANSWER:  Sam is 5 years old.
