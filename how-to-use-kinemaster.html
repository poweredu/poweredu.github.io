7. Write 2 paragraphs explaining the steps on how to edit a video using android phone and applications in video editing such as filmora Go, Kinemaster pro and capcut​


The first thing that you need to do is to download the app that you’re going to use (for this I am using cap-cut) after that open the app and tap on the “new project” button. Give cap-cut an access to all the media or files that is on your device. Following this a new screen will pop up showing the media or videos that you intend
To edit, choose your desired video and start editing. You can crop, snip, add animations and even do voice overs. Finally after being satisfied with your editing, click the arrow like in the left upper corner and choose your desired quality and fps (frames per second), and export your edited video. The edited video will be automatically saved on your gallery.
