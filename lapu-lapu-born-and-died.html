10. who kill Magellan and lapu lapu A. lapu lapu kill Magellan B. Magellan kill lapu lapu C. there died together D. A C​


Answer:

A. Lapu-lapu killed Magellan

Explanation:

It has been widely accepted that Lapu-lapu killed Ferdinand Magellan because they were seen as invaders who did not offer to make peace upon docking at Mactan.

However, there is no historical account to confirm that Lapu-lapu was the one to personally kill Magellan. This opens us to two possibilities:

Maybe Lapu-lapu killed Magellan during the Battle at Mactan; orOne of Lapu-lapu's warriors killed Magellan

There is also no historical account of how Lapu-lapu died. So there are three possibilities which no one has been able to confirm.

Maybe Lapu-lapu died in a different battleMaybe Lapu-lapu died of sickness; orMaybe Lapu-lapu died of old age

29. Who killed lapu lapu? and how old lapu lapu died?


Answer:

There are no official records showing how Lapu-Lapu died or if he was killed.

Explanation:

Many kids today are misled by a rap song claiming that Magellan killed Lapu-Lapu. This is not true at all!

Lapu-Lapu and his men defended the shores of Mactan from invasion from the Spanish led by Magellan. The truth is, if you were listening carefully in History classes, Lapu-Lapu and his men killed Magellan.

After the battle, Lapu-Lapu continued to live. However, there were no historians or scribes back then who kept record of the lives of pre-colonial tribes. Some say that Lapu-Lapu died of old age or natural causes.

Some believe that Lapu-Lapu was no ordinary man. That it is possible that Datus or chieftains were rewarded and that Lapu-Lapu became a stone or a statue in his death so that people would remember him.
