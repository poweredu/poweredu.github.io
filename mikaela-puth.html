Answer:

The equation will be :       [tex]y = 5(2^x)[/tex]

let's say on her first drop of coin x = 1 then her piggy bank would have :

                                           [tex]y = 5(2^1)\\y=5(2)\\y=10[/tex]

On her second drop (x =2) the amount inside her piggy bank would be :

                                           [tex]y = 5(2^2)\\y = 5(4)\\y=20[/tex]

3rd drop (x = 3)

                                           [tex]y = 5(2^3)\\y = 5(8)\\y = 40[/tex]

4th drop (x = 4)

                                           [tex]y = 5(2^4)\\y = 5(16)\\y = 80[/tex]

5th drop (x =5)

                                            [tex]y =5(2^5)\\y = 5(32)\\y = 160[/tex]

Based on the values

10 , 20 , 40 , 80 , 160  the amount doubles everytime she drop coins (x).

Mikaela would save php 160 on her 5th drop.
