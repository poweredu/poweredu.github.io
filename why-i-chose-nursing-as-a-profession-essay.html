Whether it's helping a student understand a difficult concept, inspiring them to pursue a new interest or passion, or simply making them feel valued and supported, teaching is a truly fulfilling profession.

In conclusion, I chose to become a teacher because I believe that education is one of the most powerful tools for positive change, and because I want to be a positive influence in the lives of others. I believe that teaching is a calling, and I am eager to answer that call and make a difference in the world.
