14. Almira types 50 pages in 2 hours. How long will it take her to type 80 pages?


Answer:

3 1/5 or 3.2 hours

Step-by-step explanation:

Let n be the missing number

50:2=80:n

50n=(80)(2)

50n=160

divide both side by 50 to find the missing number n

50n/50=160/50

n= 3 1/5 hours or 3.2 hours
