26. Find the force in an accident where the momentum of a motorcycle rider wearing a helmet changes from 80kg m/s to 0 in 0.03s ​


Answer:

2666.67 N

Explanation:

Formula:

Δp = F * Δt

Rearrange this formula to solve for F:

F = Δp / Δt

Δp = 80 kg m/s - 0 kg m/s = 80 kg m/s and Δt = 0.03 s

F = 80 kg m/s / 0.03 s

F = 2666.67 N
