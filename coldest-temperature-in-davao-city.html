30. The hottest temperature recorded in the Philippines was in Tuguegarao at 42.2. On the other hand, the coldest temperature recorded in the Philippines was in Baguio City at 6.3. Determine the difference between the hottest and coldest temperatures recorded in the Philippines in degrees Fahrenheit.​


Answer:

Celsius: 35.9°C

Fahrenheit: 96.62°F

Step-by-step explanation:

42.2°C - 6.3°C = 35.9°C

Formula of ℃ to ℉:

[tex]^oF = (\frac{9}{5} \times ^oC) + 32.[/tex]

Formula of ℉ to ℃:

[tex](^oF - 32) \times \frac{5}{9}=^oC[/tex]

°F = ([tex]\frac{9}{5}[/tex] x 35.9℃) + 32

°F = (64.62) + 32

°F = 64.62 + 32

°F = 96.62

So, the answer is 96.62°F
