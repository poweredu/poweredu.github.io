9. PRACTICAL RESEARCHSCOPE AND DELIMITATIONWhat are the effective of the ABM strqnd In developing entrepreneurship skills?SCOPE: AND DELIMITATION:WHAT? -YOU WILL JUST WHY? EXPLAIN IT.HOW?​


SCOPE:

The scope of this practical research will focus on the effectiveness of the Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) strand in developing entrepreneurship skills among senior high school students in a specific school or educational institution. The research will cover the assessment of the ABM curriculum's content, teaching methods, and the overall learning experience of the students in developing their entrepreneurship skills.

DELIMITATION:

This research will be limited to the following:

1. The study will only focus on the ABM strand as the primary source of data.

2. The study will only cover senior high school students in a specific school or educational institution.

3. The research will only assess the effectiveness of the ABM strand in developing entrepreneurship skills and will not include other factors that may affect the students' entrepreneurial development.

4. The research will only use qualitative research methods, such as interviews and surveys, to gather data.

5. The study will be conducted within a specific time frame and will not cover a long-term assessment of the students' entrepreneurial development.

WHAT:

The research aims to determine the effectiveness of the ABM strand in developing entrepreneurship skills among senior high school students. It will assess the ABM curriculum's content and teaching methods and the students' overall learning experience in developing their entrepreneurial skills.

WHY:

The study is important because it will provide insights into the effectiveness of the ABM strand in developing entrepreneurship skills among senior high school students. The findings of this research can be used as a basis for improving the ABM curriculum and teaching methods to better equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to become successful entrepreneurs in the future.

HOW:

To conduct this research, the following steps will be taken:

1. Identify the specific school or educational institution where the study will be conducted.

2. Gather data on the ABM curriculum's content and teaching methods.

3. Gather data on the students' overall learning experience in developing their entrepreneurial skills.

4. Conduct qualitative research methods, such as interviews and surveys, to gather data from the students and teachers.

5. Analyze the data and draw conclusions on the effectiveness of the ABM strand in developing entrepreneurship skills among senior high school students.

12. scope and delimitation​


Answer:

Scope and delimitations are two elements of a research paper or thesis. The scope of a study explains the extent to which the research area will be explored in the work and specifies the parameters within which the study will be operating. For example, let's say a researcher wants to study the impact of mobile phones on behavior patterns of elementary school children. However, it is not possible for the researcher to cover every aspect of the topic.

Thus, delimitations are the characteristics that limit the scope and describe the boundaries of the study, such as the sample size, geographical location or setting in which the study takes place, population traits, etc. Additionally, the researcher might also choose to use some research tools and methodologies to collect data but not others. These delimitations might be imposed for practical reasons, such as lack of time or financial resources to carry out a more thorough investigation. The delimitation section of the study should explain why specific choices were made while others were excluded and how this might affect the outcome of the research.
