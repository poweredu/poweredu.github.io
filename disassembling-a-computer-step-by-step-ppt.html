14. Why does important to follow a step-by-step procedures in disassembling and assembling computer hardware?​


Answer:

The importance of following proper procedures in assembling / disassembling computer hardware and software systems is mainly for safety purposes.

Basically, you to observe proper procedures in handling computer parts and installation of software to ensure that you are safe from any hard that the computer can cause you. Also, you must ensure that the system is safe from any destruction or harm as well. For example, your hands must be completely dry before you must touch any metal or chips in the motherboard as you assemble the insides of CPU. You must also be using the right tool to cut wires to ensure that you don't destroy any wires as it may result to damage parts and may cause the computer parts not to function properly.

For software part, you have to follow what you should install and what not to prevent files deletion and destruction. Damaging programs and software may affect applications and can do something to your computer performance and other files.

Stated below are examples of proper procedures that are normally observe in handling software and hardware systems:

1. Remove any metallic objects such as rings and jewelries to avoid yourself from getting grounded.

2. Make sure that the computer is unplugged and off before you touch anything.

3. Do not wear loose clothes.

4. Do not forced parts into computer ports.

5. Keep your area dry and clean.

30. what are step by step procedure in disassembling of electric fan​


Answer:

How to Disassemble a Tower Fan

- Unplug the fan from the outlet.

- Use the flat-head screwdriver to pop up the top controlpanel.

- Pull the control panel up slowly.

- Use the flat-head screwdriver to unlatch the remaining snapsholding the - two main plastic covers together (down both edges of the tower fan).

- Pull the central blade assembly up and out.
