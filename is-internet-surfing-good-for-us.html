23. Topic: Is Internet Surfing Good For us?what is your main positive reason?​


Internet Surfing in the first place is a method of visiting different pages and helpful websites used for browsing different information and topics to gain new knowledge and learnings from different features available like online articles, news updates, social media, and many more.

Surfing the Internet has become a part of our daily lives in this generation. We can use the Internet to do a variety of things, including shopping for clothes, making friends, and gathering information. In our daily lives, we rely on the Internet to solve problems.

First and foremost, shopping for clothes online is my favorite thing to do on the Internet. By browsing the websites, I can find a wide variety of clothing. I save a lot of time by not having to go outside to buy them. It is especially useful when the weather is bad.

Second, the Internet allows us to make all kinds of friends. Through the Internet, we can make foreign friends who can tell us about their experiences, cultures, jobs, and so on. On the other hand, we don't have to travel far to meet them; we can simply sit in front of the computer and communicate with them in English. Making friends on the Internet can thus not only broaden our horizons but also improve our language skills.

Third, the Internet provides a wealth of information. We would have to spend a lot of time searching for information if we didn't have the Internet. We only need to type key-words into a search engine these days to find a wealth of relevant information. It is extremely convenient and will save us a significant amount of time. Furthermore, we can instantly obtain current event news via the Internet. On the Internet, we can also select what we want to learn. We can not only save time searching for news but also money on newspaper purchases.

Finally, the Internet has made our lives more convenient and varied. Consider my own case. I used to go to the movies to watch movies. With the Internet, I can almost always stay at home and watch movies while eating my favorite foods. I believe that the Internet is one of the most significant inventions of our time.

To summarize, the Internet not only changes people's interests, but it also accelerates the world. Because the Internet saves us so much time, we have more time to do other things. The Internet is a wonderful invention of our time. However, online abuse and the spread of misinformation through online websites are the prior variables affecting the negative side of internet surfing. Checking for credible and reliable online websites and resources is wonderful for gaining knowledge through online or internet surfing.
