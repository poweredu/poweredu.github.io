Step-by-step explanation:

1. Find V if h = 9,000 m.

Formula V = 3.5√h, V is in km so h must be in km also

Convert 9,000 m into km

9,000 m × 1 km/1000 m = 9 km

Substitute

V = 3.5√9

V = 10.5 km

2. Find h if V = 35 km

Substitute

35 = 3.5√h

To cancelled out √, square both sides (raise both sides by 2)

(35)² = (3.5√h)²

35² = (3.5)²h

h = 35²/(3.5)²

h = 100 km or 100,000 m
