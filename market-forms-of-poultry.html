10. Identify the market form of poultry based from the given word clues


Answer:

Market Forms

It is the form poultry is in when it is purchased and received at an establishment. Poultry is available in multiple market forms, classes, and styles. There are three main market forms: fresh, frozen, and fully cooked. Fresh poultry is a good choice when it will be used within one to two days.

Parts Identification

Further processing of whole carcasses has allowed poultry to be sold in many forms. Identifying poultry parts commonly found in a retail store is important to the consumer. Below are photographs and definitions for 24 of the more common chicken parts found in a meat display case. Parts from any of the three weight groups (broilers, heavy broilers, and turkeys) may be used in the contest.

1. Whole Breast

The WHOLE BREAST is the intact breast separated from the remainder of the chicken at the junction of the vertebral and sternal ribs. The sternal ribs remain attached to the breast bone and the vertebral ribs are attached to the back. May be displayed with skin-side up or skin-side down.

2. Breast with Ribs

The BREAST WITH RIBS is the intact breast separated from the backbone at the juncture with the back. The entire rib cage is attached to the breast. It may be displayed with the skin side up or skin side down.

3. Breast Quarter

The BREAST QUARTER is half of the breast with the wing and back portion attached.

4. Breast Quarter without Wing

As the name indicates, the BREAST QUARTER WITHOUT WING is the breast quarter with the back portion attached, but without the wing.

5. Split Breast

The SPLIT BREAST is the whole breast cut in half parallel to breast bone to create approximately two equal halves. One or both halves may be displayed with or without ribs.

19. Explain the different market forms of poultry​


Answer:

Poultry is available in multiple market forms, classes, and styles.

There are three main market forms: fresh, frozen, and fully cooked.

Fresh poultry is a good choice when it will be used within one to two days. However, fresh poultry has a very short shelf life, so frozen poultry is typically purchased. Frozen poultry may be kept for up to six months.

Fully cooked poultry is often purchased because of its convenience, and it is a good alternative for establishments that lack professional experience.

Fully cooked poultry may be either frozen or canned.


20. what market form of poultry is the most used now days? explain ​


The most commonly used market form of poultry nowadays is processed poultry. This includes a wide range of products such as fresh or frozen chicken and turkey cuts, ground poultry, and value-added products like breaded and marinated poultry.

Processed poultry products are popular because they are convenient and versatile. They can be prepared quickly and easily, making them a popular choice for busy consumers. Additionally, processed poultry products are often sold at a lower price point than whole birds or premium cuts, making them an affordable option for consumers on a budget.

Moreover, processed poultry products have a longer shelf life than whole birds, making them easier to store and transport. This has made them a popular choice for food service providers and retailers, who can stock a wider range of products without needing to worry about spoilage or waste.

Overall, the popularity of processed poultry products is due to their convenience, affordability, and versatility, making them a staple in many households and food service establishments.
