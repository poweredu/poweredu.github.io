14. 1. There were 60 pupils joined the Boy Scout of the Philippines. If 18% of the Boy Scout did not registered. How many scouters are registered?​


Answer:

If 60 pupils joined the Boy Scout of the Philippines and 18% of them did not register, then the number of scouters who did not register is 60 x 18% = <<6018.01=10.8>>10.8 scouters.

Since a percentage represents a fraction of 100, to convert it to a decimal number, you need to divide it by 100. For example, 18% is equal to 18/100 = 0.18.

The number of scouters who registered is 60 - 10.8 = <<60-10.8=49.2>>49.2 scouters.

Therefore, 49.2 scouters are registered.

If there are 80 girl scouts, we can find the total number of scouts by dividing 80 by 8 and then multiplying by 13:
