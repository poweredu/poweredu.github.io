12. Look for 3 songs that caught your attention. Find at least 3 lines or sentences from the lyrics which shows parallel structure. Identify and explain the type of parallelism used in the songs.helppp:((​


Explanation:

1. "Fix You" by Coldplay -

• "The tears streaming down your face" / "When you lose someone you cannot replace" / "I wish I could fix you"

-The parallelism used in the lyrics is synonymous parallelism, where similar or synonymous words or phrases are repeated for emphasis or a special effect. The repeated phrases in the lyrics describe different aspects of loss, while the repeating structure creates a sense of symmetry and unity in the lyrics.

2. "Take Me to Church" by Hozier -

• "Take me to church" / "I'll worship like a dog at the shrine of your lies" / "I'll tell you my sins, so you can sharpen your knife"

-The parallelism used in the lyrics is anaphora, where a phrase or word is repeated to create a sense of emphasis or repetition. The repeated phrase "take me to church" acts as a leitmotif throughout the song, while the repetition of the phrase "I'll tell you my sins" serves to create a sense of desperation and despair.

3. "I Will Always Love You" by Whitney Houston -

•"I will always love you" / "I will always be your friend" / "I will always be your lover"

-The parallelism used in the lyrics is epizeuxis, where a word or phrase is repeated for emphasis. The repeated phrase "I will always love you" acts as a central theme in the song, reflecting the strength and intensity of the singer's feelings for their lover. The repetition of the phrase emphasizes both the dedication and permanence of the singer's love.
