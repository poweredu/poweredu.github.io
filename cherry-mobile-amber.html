Answer:

If the scientist prepares 1.77 grams of amber resin and adds it to the 0.41 grams of amber resin she already has, the total amount of amber resin she will have in all is:

1.77 + 0.41 = 2.18 grams

Therefore, the scientist will have 2.18 grams of amber resin in total.

27. amber has 2 quarters, 1 dime, and 3 pennies. how much money does amber have


amber has 25+25+10+3= 63 so she has 63cents

If Amber has 2 quarters, 1 dime, and 3 pennies, the amount of his total money is 63 cents.

A quarter is equivalent to 25 cents, a dime is equivalent to 10 cents, and a penny is equivalent to 1 cent. Therefore, his money is (2 * 25 cents) + (1 * 10 cents) + (3 * 1 cent) = 63 cents. Amber has a total of 63 cents.
