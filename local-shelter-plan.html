19. Mie is a volunteer at the animal shelter. The animal shelter has only dogs and cats. 2/5 of the animals are dogs. There are 7 fewer dogs than cats. How many animals are there in the animal shelter?


Answer: 35 animals

Step-by-step explanation:

If dogs = 2/5, the ratio will be 2:3 (for every 3 cats, there are 2 dogs)

Percentages are as follows:

Dogs = 2/5 =  40%

Cats = 3/5 =   60%

Total             100%

Difference in the percentage (60% - 40%) = 20%

*According to the statement, the number of cats is greater than the number of dogs by 7. Hence, 7 is the 20% difference between the 2 animals.

To get the 100%: 7/20% = 35.
