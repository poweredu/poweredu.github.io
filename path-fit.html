2. what are the paths of physical fitness?


Physical fitness refers to the ability of your body systems to work together efficiently to allow you to be healthy and perform activities of daily living. It is a state of health and well-being and, more specifically, the ability to perform aspects of sports, occupations and daily activities. It is generally achieved through the following:  

1. Proper Nutrition

2. moderate-vigorous physical exercise

3.  sufficient rest

Physical Fitness is made up of the following parts:  

1. Health related - can reduce your risk of chronic disease and promote good health and wellness. These parts of fitness are body composition, cardiorespiratory endurance, flexibility, muscular endurance, power, and strength. They also help you function effectively in daily activities.

2. Skill related - perform well in sports and other activities that require motor skills.

The seven paths of exercise

1. Yama: Self vs Not-Self

2. Niyama: Inner selves

3. Asana: Postures

4. Pranayama: Breath of Life

5. Pratyahara: Withdrawal of the senses

6. Dharana: Concentration

7. Dhyana: Unity
