2. dance steps of alitaptap folk dance​


ALITAPTAP FOLK DANCE: DANCE STEPSAlitaptap folk dance is a native dance to the tagalog.It mimics the movements of the fireflyThe music of Alitaptap folk dance is 3/4 time signature composed of A and BThe costume in Alitaptap folk dance for male is Barong Tagalog and for female is Balintawak.

DANCE STEPS

FIGURE 1

1-2 Waltz R&L in place

3-4 Stand in place and lean R&L on each measure, arms Kumintang R/L

5-8 Repeat measure 1&2 with reverse footwork and direction

9-16 Repeat all of measures 1-8

FIGURE 2

1-2 Waltz forward R/L (arms in lateral position with flutters)

3-4 Point R foot to R and then cross L, no weight (arms; waist/skirt)

5-6 Backward to place in waltz (arms lateral with flutters)

29. what are the basic dance steps in folk dance​


Answer:

The basic dance steps in folk dance may vary depending on the culture or country, but generally, they include:

1. Step - taking a step forward, backward, or sideways.

2. Hop - lifting one foot and landing on the same foot.

3. Jump - lifting both feet and landing on both feet at the same time.

4. Slide - stepping to the side with one foot while the other foot slides to meet it.

5. Grapevine - stepping to the side and crossing one foot behind the other, then stepping to the side again and crossing the other foot in front.

6. Pivot - turning on one foot while the other foot stays in place.

7. Spin - turning around on both feet.

8. Stamp - stomping one foot on the ground.

9. Clap - clapping hands together.

10. Partner dance steps - such as holding hands and circling around each other or performing mirror steps with a partner.
