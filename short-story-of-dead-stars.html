18. summarize the story "Dead Star"​


SUMMARY OF THE STORY "DEAD STARS" BY PAZ MARQUEZ BENITEZ

Dead Stars is a short love story that revolves around a dashing lawyer named Alfredo Salazar, who has a fiance named Esperanza. Alfredo and Esperanza have been together and engaged for about four years already. Their families and everyone else thinks that they're a couple made in heaven. Their love for each other was so passionate until Alfredo met a charming woman named Julia. The lawyer met the girl when he visited the house of a judge with his father. Whenever he gets the chance to do so, he would have conversations with her, and they usually have their rendezvous by a river. Alfredo hates to admit it but, he thinks that he loves Julia more than Esperanza now. He started keeping secrets from his fiance, Esperanza, and he didn't tell Julia that he was already engaged. One day, Julia told Alfredo that she's going back to her hometown and Alfredo didn't want to lose her so he confessed his love for her. But soon, Julia found out that Alfredo lied to her when someone talked about Alfredo getting married soon to Esperanza. Julia wished them a happy marriage and left. For years, Alfredo always thought about Julia and wished of seeing her again. His love and affection for Esperanza weren't the same as before as well. Then, for about eight years, Alfredo met Julia again, who was still unmarried. To his surprise, he isn't attracted to Julia anymore. Alfredo compared his love for her that was already long gone to a dead star; whose light would still be visible from the Earth for years even though it doesn't exist anymore.

29. Create a plot diagram (freytag’s pyramid) with the short story ""dead stars"" by paz marquez-benitez.


DEAD STARS by PAZ MARQUEZ-BENITEZSUMMARY

Exposition:

Alfred Salazar has faith in real love and is hopeful of finding bliss in its midst. The first lady he falls in love with is Esperanza. They begin a romantic friendship since the families of the couple are acquainted. After three years of dating, both become engaged.

Initial Incident

Esperanza is a passionate lady with a strong will and morals, in contrast to Alfredo who is a lawyer with strong desires and craves love and compassion. When Alfredo meets Julia, his affection for her quickly grows cold. He now has Julia as a fresh object of desire.

Rising Action

Alfredo meets Julia who is optimistic and enthusiasticAlfredo is immediately drawn to her when he comes upon her.He also hides things from Esperanza so he won't have to reveal his engagement to her. When he finds out that Julia is going back to his hometown, his eyes are doomed. Alfredo makes the decision to confess his actual feelings for Julia out of fear of losing her.

Climax

Julia already is aware about Alfredo's engagement to Esperanza so Julia leaves him.Esperanza is found discussing commitment and faithfulness with her buddy when he arrives. Alfredo has a need for communication. He affirms the justification for desiring and preference for honesty.

Falling Action

Esperanza quickly admits that she was aware of his relationship with Julia. She encourages him to call off the wedding in order to satisfy his passion and heart's desire. But the wedding goes on as planned, and Alfred gives in to logic.

Denouement

Because Julia is still unmarried, Alfred is forced to fantasize about getting married to her. However, he quickly discovers that things are not the same as they were before. Additionally, Julia has changed and lost something.
