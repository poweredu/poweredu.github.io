14. diffentiate intimate speech style and casual speech style


Intimate speech style is used when talking to the people closest to you. For example: your family, very close friend and your partner (couple). Example of intimate is: love, honey, sweet etc.

On the other hand Casual speech style is used in an informal way in conversations. You use casual speech style when talking to your friends, peers, colleagues or family. Casual speech style uses slang (nonstandard words or even expression). Colloquial words; like “you’re nuts”. Or even informal contractions; Example: “ain’t” instead of using “I wont”, “dunno” instead of using “I don’t know”. It’s not used when talking to someone who has high authority or rank.
