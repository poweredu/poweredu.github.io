1. There are many different online platforms that can be used for ICT content development, including:

•Content management systems (CMS) such as WordPress, Joomla, and Drupal

Project management tools such as Asana, Trello, and Basecamp

•Learning management systems (LMS) such as Moodle, Canvas, and Blackboard

•Collaboration platforms such as Google Workspace (formerly known as G Suite), Microsoft Teams, and Slack

•Development platforms such as GitHub, GitLab, and Bitbucket

2. Here are some ways in which these platforms differ:

•CMSs are designed for creating and managing websites and blogs, whereas project management tools are focused on organizing and tracking tasks and projects.
