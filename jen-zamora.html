Step-by-step explanation:

Let J be Jen

Let S be Sandra

Jen gives Sandra 10 of her beads, they have

the same number of beads.

J - 10 = S + 10

J = S + 10 + 10

J = S + 20 ------------- Equation 1

Sandra gives 20 of her beads to Jen, Jen would have thrice as many as Sandra

J + 20 = 3S

Substitute eq 1

S + 20 + 20 = 3S

40 = 3S - S

2S = 40

Divide both sides by two

S = 20

Solving for J

J = S + 20

J = 20 + 20

J = 40

Therefore

Jen has 40 beads and Sarah has 20 beads

Let:

x = age of Jen

3 + x = age of Mark

Solution:

x + (3 + x) = 29

2x + 3 = 29

2x = 29 - 3

2x = 26

2x/2 = 26/2

x = 13

Jen's age = x = 13 years old

Mark's age = 3 + x = 3 + 13 = 16 years old
