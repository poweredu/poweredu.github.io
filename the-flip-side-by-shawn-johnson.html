Hello!

It took shawn 6 hours to walk to susans house at 2 kilometers per hour. how far is it bewteen shawns house and susans house

We have the following data:

ΔV (Average Speed) = 2 km/hr  

ΔS (distance) = ? (in km)  

ΔT (time) = 6 hours

Applying the data to the formula:

[tex]\Delta{V} = \dfrac{\Delta{S}}{\Delta{T}}[/tex]

[tex]\Delta{S} = \Delta{V}*\Delta{T}[/tex]

[tex]\Delta{S} = 2\:\frac{km}{\diagup\!\!\!\!\!hr}*6\:\diagup\!\!\!\!\!hr[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{\boxed{\Delta{S} = 12\:km}}\Longleftarrow(distance)\end{array}}\qquad\checkmark[/tex]
