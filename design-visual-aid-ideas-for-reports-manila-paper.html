Possible essay scenario:

As a graphic designer, you may face different challenges and opportunities in your work, depending on your clients, projects, skills, and goals. In this essay, I will describe a scenario where you are asked to design a promotional campaign for a new beverage brand that targets young adult consumers.

The Brief

Your client is a start-up company that wants to launch a line of energy drinks that promise to enhance mental performance, focus, and alertness. The company aims to compete with established brands such as Red Bull, Monster, and Rockstar, but also wants to differentiate itself by using natural ingredients, eco-friendly packaging, and social responsibility messaging. The target audience for this campaign is men and women aged 18 to 34, who are active, social, and health-conscious. The client wants you to create a logo, packaging design, advertising campaign, and social media strategy that reflect the brand's values and vision.

The Process

To start this project, you would need to research the market trends, consumer behaviors, and competitor strategies in the energy drink industry. You would also need to interview the client about their business goals, target audience insights, and brand identity guidelines. Based on this information, you could create a mood board that captures the look and feel of the brand, such as colors, typography, imagery, and tone. You could also sketch some logo concepts and packaging prototypes that incorporate the natural, eco-friendly, and socially responsible aspects of the product. Once you get feedback from the client, you could refine your designs and present them in a compelling way.

For the advertising campaign, you could think of various media channels that reach young adults, such as social media platforms, websites, events, and influencers. You could create a series of visual ads that use catchy slogans, bold colors, and engaging visuals to showcase the benefits of the product, such as increased energy, focus, and mood. You could also add some educational and inspirational messages
