10. Who is lucia mercado rizal


Answer:

Lucia Mercado Rizal – Herbosa (1857-1919) was the 5th child of Don Francisco Mercado and Doña Teodora Alonso and elder sister of the Philippine national hero, Dr. Jose Rizal.

Lucia was married to Mariano Herbosa, nephew of Fr. Casañas, a witness of Jose Rizal's baptism. Together, they had eight children: (1) Paz; (2) Delfina; (3) Concepcion; (4) Virginia; (5) José; (6) Teodosio; (7) Patrocinio; and (8) Estanislao. She was widowed young but did not remarry. She died on 25 December 1919 at the age of 62.

José Rizal came from a 13-member family consisting of his parents, Francisco Mercado II and Teodora Alonso Realonda, and nine sisters and one brother. ...

The Rizals is considered one of the biggest families during their time.

Domingo Lam-co, the family’s paternal ascendant was a full-blooded Chinese who came to the Philippines from Amoy, China in the closing years of the 17th century and married a Chinese half-breed by the name of Ines de la Rosa.

Researchers revealed that the Mercado-Rizal family had also traces of Japanese, Spanish, Malay and Even Negrito blood aside from Chinese.

26. Kahulugan ng apelyidong mercado ni jose rizal


Nabatid na ang buong pangalan ni Jose Rizal ay José Protacio Rizal Mercado y Alonso Realonda. Ang pangalang “Rizal” ay pinagtibay bilang apelyido ni Jose noong 1849 ay inutusan ni Gobernador-Heneral Narciso Claveria ang lahat ng pamilyang Pilipino na pumili ng mga bagong apelyido mula sa listahan ng mga pangalan ng pamilyang Espanyol. Alinsunod dito, iminungkahi ng isang Spanish provincial governor na “kaibigan ng pamilya” sa ama ni Jose, Francisco Mercado (orihinal na Lam-co), na gamitin ang apelyidong “Rizal” na orihinal na Ricial, na nangangahulugang “the green of young growth or mga luntiang bukid."

Mercado ang apelyido ni Paciano noon, na kilala ng mga awtoridad bilang paboritong estudyante at pinagkatiwalaan ni Padre Burgos sa kolehiyo ng San Jose. Si Paciano ay kilala ng mga awtoridad bilang isang paboritong mag-aaral, at si Jose Rizal ang una sa kanilang magkakapatid na gumamit ng apelyido Rizal dahil kapag ginamit niya ang Mercado, mainit ang loob ng mga Kastila sa kanya.

Answer:

Among the earliest known ancestors of Jose Rizal were Siang-co and Zun-nio of Fujian, China. ... To steer clear of the anti-Chinese hostility of the Spanish authorities, Lam-co changed the family surname to the Spanish Mercado (“market”), which also signified their merchant roots.
