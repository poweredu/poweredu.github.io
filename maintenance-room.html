3. What is the difference between room’s maintenance and maintenance of public areas


Answer:

Maintenance of public areas

-maintaining the cleanliness and orderliness in all public areas which include the lobby, corridors, function rooms, grounds etc.

-maintaining the upkeep of the surroundings at the building by keeping it clean and free of liters.

-keeping the public areas free of safety hazards.

Room maintenance

-room for the maintenance of mechanical, electrical, heating and other building systems, e.g. boiler rooms, gas and electric meter rooms, elevator control rooms, and workrooms for maintenance employees, but excluding such spaces as janitors' broom closets.
