The term bi-racial refers to two races, while the term multi-racial refers to three or more races. 20. what is the difference between a normal air conditioner and an inverter air conditioner​


Answer:

The key difference between an inverter and non-inverter air conditioner is that an inverter air conditioner can regulate the speed of its compressor motor. Once the room is cool, inverter air conditioners lower the speed of the motor to save the energy and refrigerant used to cool the air.
