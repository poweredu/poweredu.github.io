16. At a market, a pear cost b¢ and an apple cist 5¢ less than a pear Mrs Ravi bought 5 pears and an apple. a.) Find the total amount in cents Mrs Ravi paid in terms of b. b.) If each pear cost 60¢, how much did Mrs Ravi pay?Leave your answer in cents.​


Answer:

a.)5bc + 5c - bc

b.) 355c (cents)

Step-by-step explanation:

for a:

pear-bc

apple- 5c-bc (5c less than a pear)

what Mrs Ravi bought: 5 pears and an apple

soo: pear=  5(bc)= 5bc

        apple=5c-bc  

  add it all up: 5bc+5c-bc

for b:   if the pear=60c, and an apple is 5c less than pear;

apple:         60c-5c  = 55c

pear: 60c

since she bought, 5 pears and apples, 5 x 60= pears is 300c total,

    apple is 55c= 300 +55= 355c total for b.
