9. What did Estrada Administration contribution in agriculture


During Estrada's Administration, the Comprehensive Agrarian Reform or CARP was expanded to the landless peasants of the country side. The administration distributed 266,000 hectares of land to 175,000 landless farmers, including lands owned by rural elites. On September 1999, Estrada issued an Executive Order 151, also known as Farmer's Trust Fund, which allowed the voluntary consolidation of small farm operation into medium and large scale enterprise which would access long-term capital.

The Magkabalikat Para sa Kaunlarang Agraryo or MAGSASAKA was also launched during his administration. Huge funds were invested for agricultural programs, such as, ''Agrikulturang Maka Masa'' which then resulted to a record high at that time with an output growth of 6% and lowered the inflation rate from 11% to a little over by 3%.

16. what are the contribution of the estrada administration on education?


During the administration of former president Joseph Estrada, he focuses on education reforms. That is why on December 7, 1998, Estrada issued the Executive Order no. 46 which established the President Commission on Educational Reform (PCER). PCER is a multi-sectoral body which is compromised of representatives of the government line agencies, the University of the Philippines, the Open University- University of the Philippines; public and private schools at all levels; teachers, the agriculture and industry sectors; the information technology sector; state colleges and universities and other concerned sectors.



However, these reforms were shot down even before they were implemented because the Estrada administration was cut short by EDSA 2 last January 20, 2001.

20. What is the contributions of Estrada administration in transportation


The two-year term of Estrada Administration had contributions in transportation. Estrada had planned from the start to get the PNR Southern Line and Northrail be constructed through private sector participation. He also planned the feasibility study of Mindanao, Sorsogon and Panay Railways. Because of this, the modernization of Commuter Line South Project was completed.


In 2000, the he had implemented the PNR's rehabilitation. He started his project on Line 2 from Recto Avenue to Santolan, Pasig. He has plans of putting Line 4 and extension of Line 1 as well. Traffic systems in Metro Manila were planned but unfinished. There were concluded projects of Phase 1 MRT Line 3 along EDSA, the Skyway from Bicutan to Buendia, Makati, and some interchanges in C5. Construction of NAIA Passenger Terminal 2 was finished. Initial expansion of the Batangas port was also finished.


Unfortunately he was booted out of the office in early January the following year.

23. What is the significant contributions of Estrada Administration in Finance


Answer:

The Estrada administration has introduced three Republic Acts that have significant relationship to the financial state of our country. These are:

1. Incentives for Regional Headquarters of Foreign Multinationals (Republic Act No. 8756)

2. Retail Trade Liberalization Act (Republic Act No. 8762)

3. New General Banking Act (Republic Act No. 8791)

Explanation:

RA 8756 allowed multinational companies to establish their business in the country with perks such as flat rate tax of 15% and multiple visa free entries. RA 8762 is the opening of the retail trade industry to foreign players. RA 8791 is allowing foreign banks to invest and operate in our country.

All these three have played an important role in establishing the financial state of our country. It opened doors to jobs, investments, and profit that strengthen the country during the Estrada administration.
