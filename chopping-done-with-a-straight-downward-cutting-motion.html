The correct answer is Back Spin or Chop Stroke

Back Spin (Chop Stroke)

A defensive stroke like back spin or chop stroke is identified when the ball is struck with a downward and forward  motion with a short chopping motion. The player should adjust the angle of  the racket to find the most effective spin.

Push Shot  

The common service done by beginners.

Top Spin Serve

A main offensive stroke. The swing of the racket is done forward and  upward forming an arc in the swinging action of hitting the ball.
