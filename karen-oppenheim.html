4. Karen is twice as old as Lori.three years from now the sum of their ages will be 42.how old is Karen?


Let 'x' be Karen's age
      'y' be Lori's age
------------------------
x = 2y     ----equation 1
------------------------
-three years from now
(x+3) + (y+3) = 42    ---equation 2
----------------------
Substitute equation 1 to equation 2
(2y+3) + (y+3) = 42
2y + 3 + y + 3 = 42
2y + y = 42 - 3 - 3
3y = 36
y = 12
---------------------
Substitute y=12 to equation 1
x = 2y
x = 2(12)
x = 24
----------------------
Therefore Karen is 24yrs old

5. the sum of the ages of karen and kristel is 41. If karen is 7 years old than kristel, how old is karen?


Answer:

Karen is 24 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x be the age of Kristel and x+7 be the age of Karen.

[tex]x+(x+7)=41\\x+x+7=41\\2x=41-7\\2x=34\\x=17[/tex]

[tex]x+7=17+7=24[/tex]

18. Karen will be f years old and x years from now. How old Karen now?

Present age: y
Future age: f
Number of years to future age from present age: x

Equation:

Present age + Number of years to future age or years added to yield the future age = Future age

y + x = f

y = f - x

Therefore, the expression for present age is:  (f - x)
