23. Reflection of "SHHH" by Moon In-soo ​


Answer:

"SHHH" is a really short poem that a famous Korean poet named Moon In-soo wrote. It talks about how sometimes it's better to be quiet and not talk too much.

The first word in the poem is "SHHH," and it means to be quiet. The poem says that sometimes it's better to say just one word that really means something, instead of saying lots of words that don't mean much.

The poem also says that being quiet can be more powerful than being loud. Even though loud noises can be scary and attention-grabbing, sometimes being quiet can get people to listen even more closely.

Finally, the poem says that sometimes you don't even need words at all. Just a look can say everything you need to say.

So basically, "SHHH" is a cool poem that says it's okay to be quiet sometimes, and that you don't always need to talk a lot to make a point.
