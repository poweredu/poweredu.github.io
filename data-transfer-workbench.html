6. Place in workbench to prevent build-up of static electricity."​


Answer:

Static-safe workbenches must be grounded, and a grounding cord is one way to do so. This connects the surface of the workstation to the ground below it, and is best utilized when the grounding cord uses firm fitting connecting devices like metallic crimps, snaps, and banana plugs to link to specific ground points.
