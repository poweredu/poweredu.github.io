10. explaination of general cessation of hostilities in CDO.​


"CDO" likely refers to "Central African Republic" (CAR) or "Cagayan de Oro" (a city in the Philippines). Assuming you are referring to the Central African Republic, a general cessation of hostilities refers to a formal agreement or declaration by the parties involved in an armed conflict to stop fighting and to pursue a peaceful resolution to their disputes.

In the context of the Central African Republic, the term has been used in reference to various peace agreements and initiatives aimed at ending the ongoing conflict in the country, which has been characterized by violence, displacement, and humanitarian suffering. Such agreements typically involve a commitment by the parties to halt military operations, to respect human rights, and to engage in negotiations aimed at addressing the underlying causes of the conflict, such as political grievances, social and economic inequalities, or ethnic and religious tensions.

It is worth noting that while a cessation of hostilities can be an important first step towards resolving a conflict, it is not a guarantee of lasting peace, as many factors can undermine its implementation and lead to a resumption of violence. Therefore, sustained efforts and resources are usually required to build trust, promote reconciliation, and address the root causes of the conflict.

30. 5. Jazvienne's mom is buying hotdogs and hotdog buns for the family barbecue. Hotdogs come in packs of 12 and hotdog buns come in packs of 9. The store does not sell parts of a pack and Jazvienne's mom wants the same number of hotdogs as hotdog buns. What is the smallest total number of hotdogs that Jazvienne's mom can purchase? A. 36 hotdogs B. 21 hotdogs C. 18 hotdogs D. 3 hotdogs​


Answer:

A. 36 hotdogs

Step-by-step explanation:

Because there are 12 hotdogs in a pack and 9 buns in a pack. So in order to have the same number of hotdogs and buns, you must buy 3 packs of hotdogs (12x3=36) and 4 packs of buns (9x4=36). 36 is the smallest divisible number of both 9 and 12.
