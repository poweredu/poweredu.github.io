How does discovering personal challenge create a deeper understanding of your innermost self?

Discovering personal challenge can create a deeper understanding of your innermost self as you go through the process of tackling them.

Your tolerances strengths determination willingness moral boundaries

Basically, unless you are a hypocrite, personal challenges will tell you what kind of person you are and this will be crystal clear to you if you are honest to yourself.

Am I willing to step over people to get what I want? Can I live with myself knowing I did that?

Going through personal challenges will tell you what you have inside, but it is in answering the last 2 questions and your acceptance or non-acceptance of it that will reveal to everyone around you what you really are.
