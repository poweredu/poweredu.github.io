Structuring her story as eight vignettes, Roy recounts how Irena Sendler helped transport thousands of Jewish children out of the Warsaw Ghetto. The bulk of the story takes place between 1940 and 1944 as Sendler joins Zegota, a Polish Resistance organization, and smuggles children into orphanages, convents, and foster homes, eventually being imprisoned by Nazis herself. 13. 3. A jar is filled with 350 lollipops. There are 148 lollipops with mangoflavour and the rest have orange flavour. How many lollipops haveorange flavour?​


A jar is filled with 350 lollipops. There are 148 lollipops with mango flavor and the rest have orange flavour. How many lollipops have orange flavour?​

Subtraction method:

350 lollipops total - 148 lollipops in mango = 202 lollipops in orange

Solving for x method:

  350 - lollipops  in total

  148 - lollipops in mango flavor

  x - lollipops in orange flavor

  148 + x = 350

  x = 350 - 148

  x = 202

There are 202 lollipops in orange flavor.
