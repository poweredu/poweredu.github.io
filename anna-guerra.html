17. Six years ago, Mary was five times as old as Anna. In five years, Mary will be five years as old as Anna, what is the present age of Anna?​


AGE MATH PROBLEM

Given the problem involving age:

Six years ago, Mary was five times as old as Anna. In five years, Mary will be three times as old as Anna, what is the present age of Anna?​

Here is the following step-by-step solution to obtain the required data, which is the present age of Anna.

Let the variable x be Anna's age six years ago. Since Mary was five times as old as Anna, we will let Mary's age as 5x. At the present, Anna's and Mary's age can be represented by x+6 and 5x+6 respectively. In the future, Anna's age can be represented by x+6+5 since it was indicated in the problem that they are referring the their age five years later. On the other hand, Mary's age can also be represented by 5x+6+5. Simplify the following expressions. For Anna's future age, it can be further simplified to x+11 since 6+5=11. For Mary's age, it can be further simplified to 5x+11.Formulate an equation. Since in five years, Mary's age will be three times as old as Anna, using the variable x, Mary's age can be represented by 3(x+11) where x+11 is Anna's age from what is presented above. We can take the two expressions in terms of Mary's age to calculate the value of x. So, the equation will be:

             Mary's age in the future = Mary's age in the future

                                           5x + 11 = 3(x+11)

We will distribute 3 based on the distributive property, and the resulting equation will become:  

                                           5x + 11 = 3x + 33

Transpose 3x to the left side of the equation, changing its sign from positive to negative, and 11 to the right side, changing its sign from positive to negative also. The resulting equation is:

                                           5x - 3x = 33 - 11

                                                  2x = 22

Divide both sides by 2 to isolate x.

                                                   x = 22/2

                                                     x = 11

Hence, the value of x is 11.

This is not yet the correct answer because as you can see from the representations we listed above, x represents the age of Anna six years ago. What we are looking for is her present age which is represented by x+6.

So, by direct substitution:

Anna's present age = x + 6 = 11 + 6 = 17

Therefore, Anna's present age is 17 years old.

28. explique porque as guerras de reconquista cristã foram assim nomeadas


Answer:

As Guerras de Reconquista Cristã foram assim nomeadas porque foram um esforço feito pelos cristãos da Península Ibérica para reconquistar territórios que foram conquistados pelos mouros durante a Idade Média. A palavra "reconquista" vem do termo em espanhol "reconquista", que significa "reconquista". Estas guerras tiveram lugar ao longo de cerca de 700 anos, começando no século VIII e terminando no século XV. Durante este período, os cristãos lutaram para reconquistar os territórios da Andaluzia, Aragão, Navarra e Castela. A Reconquista terminou com a captura de Granada, a última cidade moura na Península Ibérica, pelos Reis Católicos, Isabel e Fernando, em 1492.

As Guerras de Reconquista Cristã são assim chamadas porque foram um esforço dos reinos cristãos da Península Ibérica para recuperar territórios que haviam sido conquistados pelos mouros, um grupo de pessoas que seguiam o islã. Estas guerras começaram no século VIII e duraram até o século XV, e foram motivadas tanto pela vontade de expandir territórios quanto pelas crenças religiosas.

Os cristãos enxergaram a reconquista como uma "reconquista" do território da Península Ibérica para o cristianismo, e por isso deram a estas guerras o nome de Guerras de Reconquista Cristã. Além disso, a reconquista foi vista como uma forma de purificar o território da influência muçulmana e restaurar a "verdadeira" fé cristã na região.

Em resumo, as Guerras de Reconquista Cristã foram assim chamadas porque foram uma campanha militar cristã para reconquistar territórios controlados por mouros na Península Ibérica, e para restaurar a hegemonia cristã na região.
