23. what are the different 21st century literary genres​


21st Century Literary Genres

1. Chick Lit

– written by women for women; uses strong female character who is trying to live in the modern world; plot is lighthearted, smart and funny

2. Hyper-Poetry

– also known as cyber poetry; could not be presented without the aid of computers and the internet; includes verse with links to sub poems or footnotes, poetry generators or poetry with movements or images

3. Blogs

– shortened of the term web blog; a regularly updated journal on the internet; blogger is the term used for a person who has blog

4. Speculative Fiction

– setting is futuristic and involves supernatural elements; it encompasses horror and science fiction words

5. Texttula

– has its origin in Tanaga (a Filipino poem that has four lines and seven syllables each

6. Flash Fiction

– notable for its brevity; also called short short story; it is micro-fiction, micro-narrative and sudden fiction

7. Graphic Novel

– utilizes pictures in narrating a long story; it has growing population i.e. manga
