11. Why is recognizing National Artists and GAMABA artists


Answer:

Recognizing National Artists and GAMABA artists (Gawad sa Manlilikha ng Bayan or the National Living Treasures Award) is important because it acknowledges the contributions and achievements of individuals in the arts and culture sector. These individuals have made significant contributions to the development and promotion of Philippine arts and culture, and their recognition serves as an inspiration for other artists and cultural practitioners. It also helps to promote and preserve the country's cultural heritage.

National Artists and GAMABA artists are chosen based on their body of work and their contributions to the development of Philippine arts and culture. The recognition serves as a way to honor their achievements and to celebrate their talents and contributions to society. It also helps to bring attention to the importance of arts and culture in the Philippines and to promote the appreciation and support of these fields.
