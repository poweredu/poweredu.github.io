jade vine

Strongylodon macrobotrys, commonly known as jade vine, emerald vine or turquoise jade vine, is a species of leguminous perennial liana endemic to the tropical forests of the Philippines. Its local name is tayabak. A member of the Fabaceae, it is closely related to beans such as kidney bean and runner bean.

23. what is the importance of jade rocks​


Answer:

Jade symbolizes prosperity, success, and good luck. It is also a symbol of renewal, longevity, and even immortality. The Chinese call jade 玉 (or yù). This is closely related to the word for Emperor, which is 王 (wáng).
