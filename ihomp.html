The statement states that if a triangle is considered as an obtuse triangle if it has an obtuse angle.

Step-by-step explanation:

As you can see on the picture, there are 4 questions that are needed to be answered.

And those are the if-then,converse,inverse and the contrapositive form of the given statement.

But first,We will explain the 4 types of conditional statements.

If-then form states that if p,then q.

The converse statement states that if q,then p.In simple words,the converse of the statement is the exact opposite of the if-then form.

The inverse of the statement is the negative version of the if-then form.It states that if -p,then-q.

The contrapositive of the statement is the negative version of the converse of the statement.It states that if -q,then-p.

After we discussed the 4 types of conditional statements,we will proceed to the answers.

If-then form-If a triangle is an obtuse triangle,then it has an obtuse angle.

Converse-If a triangle has an obtuse angle,then it is an obtuse triangle.

Inverse:If a triangle is not an obtuse triangle,then it has no obtuse angle.

Contrapositive:If there isn't an obtuse angle in a triangle,then it is not an obtuse triangle.
