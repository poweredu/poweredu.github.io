15. is water heterogeneous or homogeneous , is softdrinks heterogeneous or homogeneous , is coffee heterogeneous or homogeneous, is cookies heterogeneous or homogeneous, is smog heterogeneous or homogeneous


water- homo
softdrinks- homo
coffee-homo
cookies- hetero

Verified Answer ✅:

WATER

Water - another example of homogeneous mixture; all but the purest water contains dissolved minerals and gases; These are dissolved throughout the water, so the mixture presents in the same phase and is homogeneous.

SOFT DRINKS

In a homogeneous mixture, all the components are uniformly distributed and in the soft drink, we find components like sweetener, carbon dioxide and water forming a single phase. Therefore, a soft drink is a homogeneous mixture

COFFEE

White coffee is certainly heterogeneous as it contains milk, which is a colloidal dispersion of fat droplets in water. This comprises two immiscible liquid phases, so is also heterogeneous making white coffee heterogeneous too.

COOKIE

A chocolate chip cookie is homogeneous because you can pull out the separate parts of the cookie.

SMOG

Smog is another example of a heterogeneous mixture. This murky collection of pollutants can be a mixture of water and contaminants from burning gasoline or plastics mixed with nitric oxide derivatives and ozone.

Explanation:

Homogeneity and heterogeneity are concepts often used in the sciences and statistics relating to the uniformity of a substance or organism. A material or image that is homogeneous is uniform in composition or character (i.e. color, shape, size, weight, height, distribution, texture, language, income, disease, temperature, radioactivity, architectural design, etc.); one that is heterogeneous is distinctly nonuniform in one of these qualities. In short, Homogeneous means that something is uniform throughout the system while heterogeneous means there is no  uniformity throughout the system.
