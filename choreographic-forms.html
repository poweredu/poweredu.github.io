2. identify choreographic forms​


Answer: The AB choreographic form is one of the simplest forms. It consists of a beginning phrase/section, A, followed by a second phrase/section, known as B. Although A and B share a common feeling/mood, they contain elements that contrast in tone or quality. Phrase A and Phrase B are connected by a transition.

Cha Cha is a dance of Cuban origin, and derives its name from the rhythm developed by a syncopation of the fourth beat.
