14. A collection of coins has 16 more 1 peso than 5 pesos coins. If the total value of the collection is 88 pesos. How many 1 peso and 5 peso coins are there?​


Answer:

Let x= 1 peso coin

y= 5 peso coin

[tex]x= y+16. eqn1\\ x +y=88. eqn2[/tex]

Substitute eqn. 1 to eqn. 2

[tex]x+y=88\\ y+16+y=88\\ 2y+16=88\\ 2y=88-16\\ 2y=72\\ \frac{2y}{2} =\frac{72}{2} \\ y=36[/tex]

Using eqn.1 substitute the y value to find the value of x

[tex]x=y +16\\ x=36+16\\ x=52[/tex]

Hence, there are 52 pieces of one peso coin and 36 five peso coin

26. a wallet containing only 5 peso and 10 peso coins, has 6 more 10 peso coins than 5 peso coins. the total value of the money is Php360. how many 5 peso coins and 10 peso coins are in the purse?​


Let x be the number of 5 peso coins.

Then, the number of 10 peso coins is x+6.

The total value of the coins is given by:

5x + 10(x+6) = 360

Simplifying this equation, we get:

15x + 60 = 360

15x = 300

x = 20

Therefore, the number of 5 peso coins is 20 and the number of 10 peso coins is x+6 = 26.

5 = 20

10 = 26

20 x 5 = 100

10 x 26 = 260

260 + 100 = 360
