29. 1 po 5. This is a Latin phrase which means “into the middle of things”. * a. in tres marias b. in medias res c. in mesias ter​


Answer:

In Medias res

Explanation:

WHAT IS IN MEDIAS RES?

In medias res means the practice of beginning an epic or other narrative by plunging into a crucial situation that is part of a related chain of events.

It means the middle story is in the start.

Examples:

Homer's narrative story The lilad.

Mitchells vs. The machines

Divine comedy
