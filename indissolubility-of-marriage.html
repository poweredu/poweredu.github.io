13. (Subject: Religion - Topic: Sacrament of Matrimony)1. Explain why marriage is indissoluble.2. List down 3 ways on how you can help the marriage of your parentsPls help I need it now


Answer:

1. Marriage is indissoluble because it is a sacrament that is instituted by God Himself. In the Gospel of Mark 10:9, Jesus said "What therefore God has joined together, let not man put asunder." Therefore, a validly contracted marriage can neither be dissolved by the Church nor by the spouses themselves, even if they undergo difficulties, problems or even if one of the spouses commits grave offenses that could lead to separation or divorce. In the Catholic Church, the sacrament of Matrimony is a sanctifying grace that establishes an unbreakable bond between the spouses, which mirrors the indissoluble union between Christ and His holy Church.

2. Ways to help the marriage of your parents are:

- Pray for them: Offer prayers for your parents' marriage, especially during times of difficulty. Praying for their struggles, patience, and love can help your parents realize the value of the sacrament of Matrimony, which they vowed to cherish and uphold for their lifetime.

- Be a good example: As their child, try to live a good example by showing kindness, respect, and patience towards your parents. Respect their differences, settle conflicts peacefully, and be a source of encouragement and support.

- Seek professional help: If necessary, encourage your parents to seek professional counseling, especially if they are struggling with communication, trust, or infidelity issues. A counselor can help them gain insights and skills that can strengthen their marriage and improve their relationship.
