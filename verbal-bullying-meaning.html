9. What are the differences and similarities of verbal bullying and physical bullying?Please,respect question.Need help​


Answer:

Physical bullying is using one’s body and physical bodily acts to exert power over peers. Punching, kicking and other physical attacks are all types of physical bullying.

The definition of verbal bullying is when an individual uses verbal language (e.g., insults, teasing, etc) to gain power over his or her peers.

Explanation:

The similarities of physical and verbal bullying are the intentions, behaviors, and harassment. Both have the same intentions, to hurt a human being.

The differences between physical and verbal bullying are the instances or ways they hurt a human being.

Physical bullying is to hurt someone by physical or direct contact.

Verbal bullying is to hurt someone by language.

18. Verbal bullying defination ​


qt ko tlga

Explanation:

The definition of verbal bullying is when an individual uses verbal language (e.g., insults, teasing, etc) to gain power over his or her peers.

For example, a less athletically inclined peer may be called nerd or wimp. Unlike physical bullying, by definition verbal bullying is harder to see and stop. It tends to occur when adults aren’t around to stop it and the effects of it are not obvious.

Verbal bullying can be very damaging and may have long term psychological effects on the victim.

28. Isang mag aaral at verbal bullying​


Answer:

Ang verbal bullying ay isang uri ng bullying na nangyayari sa pamamagitan ng salita o pananalita. Mga halimbawa nito ay pagmumura, pagpapahiya, at pambabastos sa isang mag-aaral sa pamamagitan ng mga salita o pananalita. Ang ganitong uri ng bullying ay maaaring magdulot ng malalim na emotional at psychological na trauma sa mga biktima nito. Sa kasalukuyan, maraming mga programa at aktibidad ang inilalaunan upang labanan ang verbal bullying sa mga paaralan at komunidad.

Explanation:

Ang verbal bullying ay isang uri ng pambu-bully na nangyayari sa pamamagitan ng paggamit ng salita o wika. Sa kaso ng isang mag-aaral, maaaring masalanta ng verbal bullying sa pamamagitan ng pambabatikos, pambu-bastos, o pambu-bully sa pamamagitan ng mga salita sa kanilang mga kaklase, guro, o iba pang mga tao sa paaralan. Ang verbal bullying ay maaaring magdulot ng masamang epekto sa kaligtasan at kabutihan ng isang mag-aaral, tulad ng pagkabigo, pagkalungkot, o pagkakasira ng kanyang pagkakakilanlan. Sa kabuuan, ang verbal bullying ay dapat labanan upang maprotektahan ang mga mag-aaral mula sa mga masamang epekto nito.
