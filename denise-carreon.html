5. As partners in a business venture, Ms. Carreon puts in 46 3/5% of their capital, while Ms. Abad shared ₱1,700,000. How much did Ms. Carreon contribute? Please show solution, thanks!


Partnership Business

Ms. Carreon's contribution is worth a total of ₱1,483,780. Mr. Carreon and Mr. Abad are in a partnership-style business. Since Ms. Carreon shared 46 3/5% shares, the other percentage will be shouldered by Ms. Abad which is Php 1,700,000.

Here is the step-by-step solution:

Since the share should be cut to two, Ms. Abad's share is equal to:

[tex]100% - 46\frac{3}{5} %[/tex]% - [tex]46\frac{3}{5}[/tex]% = [tex]53 \frac{2}{5} %[/tex]% which is also equal to Php 1,700,000.

In order for us to solve the problem easier, we should convert the percentage form into decimal form. That is:

[tex]\frac{53\frac{2}{5} }{100}[/tex] or dividing the fraction by 100%. We shall get 53.4% so we need to move the decimal twice to the left twice so we will get 0.534.

Set the equation to find the value of x.

0.534x = ₱1,700,000
x = ₱1,700,000 / 0.534
x = ₱3,183,520.60

So the total capital is ₱3,183,520.60 but what we are looking for is Ms. Carreon's contribution.

To know Ms. Carreon's share. we need to multiply her share by the total capital. We will obtain:

0.466 x ₱3,183,520.60 = ₱1,483,780.

Ms. Carreon's share is ₱1,483,780.
