24. I. The peregrine falcon is the fastest of flying birds. A falcon can fly 1.73 km downward in 25 s. What is the average velocity of a peregrine falcon?​


Answer:

0.0692 km/s

or

69.2 m/s

Explanation:

Velocity is distance divided by time.

The distance here is 1.73 km and the time is 25 seconds.

We just need to divide them.

1.73 km ÷ 25 s = 0.0692 km/s

There's a catch:

The Standard Unit (SI) for velocity is meters/second (m/s).

Just in case, I converted 1.73 km to meters.

1.73 km x 1000 = 1730 m

1730 m ÷ 25 s= 69.2 m/s

At its core, "true love waits" is about the importance of waiting for the right person and building a strong foundation of trust and respect before entering into a romantic relationship.
