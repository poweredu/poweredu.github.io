17. 2. Substance that fulfill our daily nutrition. _o__


NUTRIENTS

Nutrients are substance that fulfill our daily nutrition. Nutrients are used by our body to function properly and maintain our health. Nutrients are substances found in foods that are important for our life and health, such as energy, building blocks for repair and growth.

Examples of nutrients that our body needs these are :

1. proteins- that are usually found in meat & beans which is important for  the growth and development especially in children.

2. fats - uses by our body as a fuel source of energy.

3. carbohydrates- also provides energy to our body that we need everyday.

4. vitamins and minerals - are nutrients which help boosting our immune system and helps repair the damage in our cells.
