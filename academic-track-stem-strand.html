29. "Influence of Family Background and Support on Students participation and success in Stem Academic Strand"Patulong po pagawa ng Rationale of the Study​


Answer:

Family background and support has a significant influence on students' participation and success in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) academic strands. Parents or guardians play a vital role in instilling confidence and motivation in their children, which can be the difference between success and failure in STEM fields. Students from a disadvantaged background may lack the resources to access resources related to STEM courses, such as tutors and educational materials, thereby limiting their progress. Furthermore, some students may have family or cultural values that discourage their STEM participation or limit their access to STEM education.

On the other hand, supportive family environments can give students the confidence and support needed to achieve success in the STEM fields. Supportive family members can provide access to resources such as tutors, books, and other educational materials. Additionally, they may provide emotional and psychological support, which is vital to students who can become overwhelmed by the difficulty of the work in STEM studies. Furthermore, family members may provide advice and guidance through difficult academic periods and provide support during failure, both of which are essential for students to experience successful STEM learning outcomes.

Ultimately, family background and support can significantly influence students' participation and success in STEM academic strands. Disadvantaged students may lack the resources necessary to succeed while students with greater support may experience better outcomes. Therefore, it is essential that individuals, schools, and organizations work together to ensure that students from all backgrounds have equal access to resources which may facilitate better STEM learning outcomes.
