4. is a species of fish that can be raised in farm cultured ponds.this type of fish is usually grown in freshwater and its culture started in the 1970's in laguna de bay. A.carp B.milkfish C.tilapia D.Catfish​


Answer:

B . Milk Fish

Explanation:

. In the early 1970s milkfish farming expanded to include culture in bamboo and net pens set in Laguna de Bay - the country's largest freshwater lake. In the early 1990s milkfish culture in fish pens spread to shallow marine bays and estuaries, particularly in the Lingayen Gulf area. Milkfish culture soon spread to net cages which were fixed or floating in both freshwater and marine water. The culture of milkfish in cages depended upon and was hastened by the development and marketing of commercial feed by the feed millers (Yap, 1999).

17. Viva Laguna ba ang dating katawagan sa laguna?​


Answer:

pulilan

Explanation:

Ano ang tawag sa Laguna noong 1500-1600?

Saysaybagin (Nobyembre 14, 2014)

Ayon sa pinakaunang diksiyonaryo (1613 Vocabulario de Lengua Tagala), ang tawag sa Laguna ay “Pulilan” :

“Laguna : Pulilan pp : M. de Bay en respeto de Manila, namumulilan .3.ac. ir a contratar de Manila y sus contornos a la laguna de Bay, ypinamumulilan .3.P. lo q llenan, aco, y, namumulilan, voy a la laguna a tratar, tagapulilan ca, eres natural de la laguna? preguntan en Manila “ (pahina 383)

(“Namumulilan” ang tawag kung pupunta sa Laguna mula Maynila. “Taga-Pulilan” ang tawag sa mga taga-Laguna ng mga taga-Maynila).

“Doongan” naman ang tawag sa Laguna ng mga Tinguianes:

“Laguna : Doongan pp : respeto de los del Bondo y Tinguianes, tutugpa aco sa doongan, boy a la laguna doon sasang doongan man, aunque sea alla en toda la laguna.” (pahina 383).
