4. Question Number 10 An attic exhaust fan is designed to start when its sensors detect a temperature of 35°C. All of your thermometers provide readings using the Fahrenheit scale (°F). What is the equivalent Fahrenheit reading for the point at which the exhaust fan turns on?


Answer:

95°F

Step-by-step explanation:

The formula for this is:

35 multiply by 9, then divide by 5, then add 32.

35*9=315

315 / 5 =63

63 +32= 95

95°F
