15. persuade,entertain,inform example


Persuade is to convince or to influence someone to do something you want them to do.

Example :
It wasn't really easy, but I persuaded him to do the right thing.

Entertain is to provide someone with amusement or enjoyment. It is to give attention or consideration to someone.

Example :
A tremendous game thoroughly entertained the crowd.

Inform is to give someone facts or information about something.

Example :
They were informed about the suspension of classes earlier than the usual announcement.

The author would like to teach the readers something.

cookbooks, texts books, posters, pamphlets, encyclopedias

TO PERSUADE :

The author would like the reader to agree his or her opinion.

EXAMPLE:

advertisements, commercials, newspaper editorials

TO ENTERTAIN :

The author would like to tell stories and make readers laugh.

EXAMPLE:

plays, poems, joke, books, stories, comic strips
