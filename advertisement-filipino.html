18. 5 advertisments that shows filipino values​


FIVE ADVERTISEMENTS THAT SHOWS FILIPINO VALUES

Many famous products in the Philippines have become the talk of the town as a result of their commercials, which have a storyline fit for a movie. A Jollibee commercial with the tagline Kwentong Jollibee.

1. MCDONALD'S "KAREN PO"

The ad features Karen with her forgetful Grandpa, Grandpa Rudy. Her grandfather always mistaken her as the other granddaughter as Gina. In the end, Grandpa Rudy acknowledges Karen by giving a half of his burger. Then followed by saying: "Ang paborito kong apo, si Karen." My favorite granddaughter, Karen.

VALUE SHOWN: Filipinos hold families valuable especially towards the aging generation. In here, Karen is being a good granddaughter.

2. RC COLA'S AMPON

The surrealistic RC Cola's ad features about a boy being different and asked his parents if he is adopted. The whole ad  rolls differently into saying that they might be different, they are still family.

VALUE SHOWN: Adoption is a very sensitive topic in the Philippines since they value blood. The ad shows how families are formed even without the bond of blood.

3. TANG: WE ARE HOME MADE

The ad talks about the judgmental comments children and parents often faced but never understanding what is behind their stories.

VALUES SHOWN: Everyone has battles and they should be kind to each other.

4. JOLLIBEE: FOUND THE PEARL

The ad takes the audience into a conquest of finding a single pair of a missing earring then go to celebrate to the famous Fast food chain.

VALUES SHOWN: Filipinos celebrate small victories and moments. We love to celebrate as Filipinos.

5. FITA: WISH KO, SPORTS CAR

The hilarious and classic ad is about a man dividing a cracker to a homeless lady. Because of this, the elderly turned into a wish-granting fairy and the man wished for a sports car. However, his wish was  as half as the half Fita he gave.

VALUES SHOWN: Altruism is shown in the ad. In equivalent, we have bayanihan which helps the community.

26. 1.why do some filipino think that white is more than brown?2.how do advertisers confuse Filipinos' idea of beauty?3.Explain the tittle of the selection: "Brown is the New White."​


Answer:

1. The Love of Whiter Skin was Taught to Filipinos

Our fixation with fair skin is a result of how colonial forces formed our national psyche by elevating white insulares, peninsulares, and mestizos to nobility and relegating brown-skinned indios to lower class people.

2. "Brown skin, white mind" describes how the Philippine media and the advertising sector have influenced Filipinos' views of beauty.

3. BROWN IS THE NEW WHITE

Brown skin, which we Filipinos refer to as "tan," is different from white skin in that it has a distinct and varied style.


27. Guys pahingi script sa filipino advertisement radio tagalog 30second needd talaga salamat sa maka bigay​


script para sa 30-segundong Filipino advertisement sa radyo:

Magsisimula na ang tag-init at alam nating lahat kung gaano kainit ito sa Pilipinas. Pero huwag mag-alala dahil may solusyon tayo sa init ng panahon - ang [pangalan ng produkto].

Sa [pangalan ng produkto], nag-aalok kami ng mga produktong magbibigay sa iyo ng kaginhawahan sa panahon ng tag-init. Mayroon kaming mga air cooler at aircon na magbibigay ng malamig na hangin sa iyong tahanan o opisina.

Hindi lang basta malamig ang hangin na ibibigay namin, dahil mayroon din kaming [feature ng produkto], na magbibigay ng [benefits ng feature] sa iyong kalusugan at kapaligiran.

Kaya huwag nang magpahirap sa init ng tag-init. Maglagay na ng [pangalan ng produkto] sa iyong tahanan o opisina. Mag-order na sa aming website o bisitahin ang aming [tindahan ng produkto]. Siguradong malamig na ang iyong panahon ng tag-init.

[Closing tagline]

Note: Ang mga nakalagay sa loob ng [ ] ay dapat palitan ng pangalan ng produkto, feature ng produkto, benefits ng feature, tindahan ng produkto, at closing tagline na nais gamitin sa advertisement.
