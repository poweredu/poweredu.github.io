6. Madison is 7 less than four times as old as Paige. The product of their ages is 47 more than the sum of their ages. How old are Madison and Paige? Solution po.​


Answer:

Madison = 13 years old

Paige = 5 years old

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x be the age of Paige

The age of Madison is 4x - 7 (7 less than 4 times as old as Paige)

x (4x - 7) = [x + (4x - 7)] + 47

4x² - 7x = (5x - 7) + 47

4x² - 7x = 5x + 40

4x² - 12x - 40 = 0

x² - 3x - 10 = 0

(x - 5)(x + 2) = 0

x = 5, -2

DIscard -2 and use only 5 then substitute

x = 5 years (Paige's age)

4x - 7 = 4(5) - 7 = 13 years (Madison's age)

12. Madison invested in acertificate of deposite for 4years at a 6% interest rate. At the end of the 4 years the value of certificate of deposit was $3,100. How much did Madison deposit originally​


Answer:

P = $2,500.00

Step-by-step explanation:

Equation:

P = A / (1 + rt)

Calculation:

First, converting R percent to r a decimal

r = R/100 = 6%/100 = 0.06 per year.

Solving our equation:

P = 3100 / ( 1 + (0.06 × 4)) = 2500

P = $2,500.00

The principal investment required to get a total amount, principal plus interest, of $3,100.00 from simple interest at a rate of 6% per year for 4 years is $2,500.00.
