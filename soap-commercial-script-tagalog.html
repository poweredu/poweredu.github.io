24. Hand washing commercial script​


Answer:

Title: Clean Hands, Healthy Life

Opening shot: Close up of a pair of hands, covered in dirt and grime.

Voiceover: Our hands are the most essential tools we use in our daily lives.

Cut to: A busy street, where people are going about their daily routine.

Voiceover: We use them to shake hands, hold things, and touch different surfaces.

Cut to: A close-up of a person holding a door handle.

Voiceover: But these same hands can also carry germs and bacteria that can make us sick.

Cut to: A woman sneezing into her hand.

Voiceover: Germs and bacteria can spread quickly from person to person.

Cut to: A man shaking hands with another man.

Voiceover: That's why it's important to wash your hands properly.

Cut to: A person turning on the tap and washing their hands.

Voiceover: Washing your hands with soap and water can help remove germs and bacteria.

Cut to: Close up of soap being lathered onto the hands.

Voiceover: It's a simple, yet effective way to prevent the spread of illnesses.

Cut to: A person drying their hands with a towel.

Voiceover: So remember, wash your hands frequently throughout the day, especially before and after eating, after using the bathroom, and after being in public places.

Closing shot: A group of people with clean hands, smiling and looking healthy.

Voiceover: Clean hands mean a healthy life.
