Ati-Atihan Festival – Aklan

Similar to Cebu's Sinulog Festival, this event is celebrated to honor Santo Niño. Ati-Atihan Festival in Aklan is considered to be one of the best festivals in the Philippines. This religious festivity is also one of the oldest Philippine celebrations.

Dinagyang literally means “merrymaking” in the local dialect, but around the country, it is (unofficially) hailed as the queen of Philippine festivals. The weekend is divided into three parts: Friday’s Fluvial Procession, Saturday’s Kasadyahan Cultural Competition and Sunday’s Ati Tribe Competition.

Panagbenga

This month-long flower festival takes place in Baguio City, the “summer capital of the Philippines” four hours north of Manila. Named after the Kankanaey word for “season of blooming,” Panagbenga began as a way to honor the strength and resilience of the people of Baguio after a devastating earthquake in July 1990.

Kadayawan

Just like Pahiyas in Quezon Province, this thanksgiving festival observes the bounty of Davao, the largest city by land area in the Philippines. But rather than a religious holiday, Kadayawan is a cultural fiesta honoring the island’s natural resources and varied cultures — the urban area is home to 11 different tribes, after all.

6. how can church popular devotions nourish my relationship with God?​


How Popular Church Devotions Nourish Our Relationship with God

Popular church devotions nourish our relationship with God as we spend time with Him through these devotions. Besides, giving time to the Lord by participating in church services is the best way of honoring and worshipping God. On top of that, popular church devotions also help us understand more about our faith, the works of God, the saints whose lives point towards God, and about Jesus Himself.

Further Notes on How Popular Church Devotions Nourish Our Relationship with God

Below are additional notes on how popular church devotions nourish our relationship with God.

Through these popular church devotions, we learn more about the story of our salvation. We come to understand the meaning of every practice and how it points us to God.They're also a great way to offer ourselves, our time, and effort to Jesus who sacrificed His life for us. Church devotions, in general, are ways to show gratitude for all the blessings and graces we receive.
