PROBLEM:

➢Nea left San Miguel, Jordan, Guimaras at 9:30 A.M., and arrived in Boracay Island at 5:15 P.M. How long was her trip?

Answer: [tex]\begin{array}{c} {\tt{\underline{\purple{7 \: \: hours \: \: and \: \: 15 \: \: minutes}}}}\end{array}[/tex]

[tex]======================[/tex]

GIVEN:[tex]\boxed{\begin{array}{c} \tt{\red{9:30 \: \: A.M}} \\ \tt{\red{5:15 \: \: P.M}}\end{array}}[/tex]

[tex]======================[/tex]

SOLUTION:

↬ To solve this word problem, we can use vertical time line.

Equation:

[tex]\tt \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: 1 \: hour \tt\left \{ {{9:30 \: am} \atop {10:30 \: am}} \right. \\ \\ \tt \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: 2 \: hour \tt\left \{ {{10:30 \: am} \atop {11:30 \: am}} \right. \\ \\ \tt \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: 3 \: hour \tt\left \{ {{11:30 \: am} \atop {12:30 \: pm}} \right. \\ \\ \tt \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: 4 \: hour \tt\left \{ {{12:30 \: pm} \atop {1:30 \: pm}} \right. \\ \\ \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \tt 5 \: hour \tt\left \{ {{1:30 \: pm} \atop {2:30 \: am}} \right. \\ \\ \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \tt 6 \: hour \tt\left \{ {{3:30 \: pm} \atop {4:30 \: am}} \right. \\ \\ \tt {\blue{7\: hour \: and \: 45 \: minutes}} \tt\left \{ {{4:30 \: pm} \atop {5:15 \: am}} \right.[/tex]

↬ To check if our answer is correct, let's add the answer we've got to 9:30 am.

[tex] \tt \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: 9 : 30 \: am \\ + \: { \underline{\tt{7 : 45}}} \\ \: \: {\boxed{\tt{\green{17 : 15 }}}}[/tex]
