7. i per mil is persent​


Per-mille (‰)

Per-mille or per-mil means parts per thousand.

One per-mille is equal to 1/1000 fraction:

1‰ = 1/1000 = 0.001

Ten per-mille is equal to 10/1000 fraction:

10‰ = 10/1000 = 0.01

One hundred per-mille is equal to 100/1000 fraction:

100‰ = 100/1000 = 0.1

One thousand per-mille is equal to 1000/1000 fraction:

1000‰ = 1000/1000 = 1

Example

What is 30 per-mille of 80$?

30‰ × 80$ = 0.030 × 80$ = 2.4$

Per-mille sign

The per-mille sign is the symbol: ‰

It is written to the right side of the number. e.g: 600‰

Per-mille - percent conversion

One per-mille is equal to 0.1 percent:

1‰ = 0.1%

One percent is equal to 10 per-mille:

1% = 10‰

9. what is MILthankss ​


Answer:

MIL stands for Media and Information Literacy. It is a set of competencies that enable individuals to access, evaluate, understand, and use media and information effectively and responsibly. MIL emphasizes critical thinking, analysis, and reflection to make informed decisions about media content and information sources. MIL includes skills such as information seeking, evaluating credibility, identifying bias and propaganda, understanding privacy and intellectual property rights, and using technology effectively and safely. It is important in the digital age as the explosion of information and media sources can make it challenging to determine what information is accurate and reliable. By developing MIL competencies, individuals can become more informed, engaged, and responsible members of society. MIL is increasingly being integrated into education and training programs at all levels to support digital citizenship and promote lifelong learning.
