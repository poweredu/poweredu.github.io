1. why do people calibrate the pulilan carabao festival (pulilan bulacan)​


Answer:

Celebration of the Pulilan Carabao Festival

People from Pulilan, Bulacan celebrate the Kneeling Carabao Festival every May 14 and 15 because they wanted to give thanks to the patron saint of the town, San Isidro Labrador. He is also the patron saint of the farmers who are using the carabaos for their work in the field.

Explanation:

Every May 14 and 15, the townspeople of Pulilan, Bulacan line up the streets to witness the annual Kneeling Carabao Festival. Many tourists from all over the Philippines also come to the town to see what the celebration is all about. The parade starts at 2 PM, in Barangay Dampol 1st, and the carabaos, along with the dancers and the musicians, would have to walk more than 1.5 kilometers just to reach the San Isidro Labrador Parish Church in Barangay Poblacion.

8. The smoothness of the vocal delivery. The flow of words in the delivery of speech​


Answer:

Vocal delivery includes components of speech delivery that relate to your voice. These include rate, volume, pitch, articulation, pronunciation, and fluency. Our voice is important to consider when delivering our speech for two main reasons. First, vocal delivery can help us engage and interest the audience. Second, vocal delivery helps ensure that our ideas are communicated clearly. Fluency refers to the flow of your speaking. To speak with fluency means that your speech flows well and that there are not many interruptions to that flow.

12. Differentiate cash before delivery and cash on delivery​


Answer:

Cash on delivery (COD) stipulates that goods must be paid for at the time of delivery, or else the goods are returned to the seller. Delivery-versus-payment (DVP) is an arrangement whereby securities are only delivered to the buyer once payment has been made.

Kwentong Bayan sa Pulilan, Bulacan

Ang aking ikukwento ay ang alamat ng bayan ng Pulilan. Noong unang panahon, wala pa ang bayan ng Pulilan. Ang itsura ng bayan noon ay isang malawak na lupain.
