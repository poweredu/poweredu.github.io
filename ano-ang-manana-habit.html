25. what is mañana habit​


WHAT IS MAÑANA HABIT?

Mañana habit is one of the bad habits of most Filipinos. The word mañana is a Spanish word that means 'mamaya na' in Tagalog or 'later' in English. Mañana habit is basically a habit of procrastinating. If you have the Mañana habit, you tend to do a certain thing at a later time.

One clear example of this habit is doing school works that have been given a long time ago on the day before the due date or even a few before the due date. Another example is reviewing for an exam on the day of the examination. Because of this habit, people get stressed and they don't get to be productive. If you have this habit, you should break this stubborn habit.

27. B. Directions: You must have experienced bahala na, ningas-cogon, and mañana habit as a student. Recall what happened or in what situation did you experience each of these. Write your answers on a separate sheet of paper. Bahala Na Ningas-cogon Mañana Habit​


Answer:

Bahala na is a Filipino term that means "leave it to God." People who believe in this phrase usually believe that things will work out in the end, no matter what.

Ningas-cogon is a Filipino term that means "to burn out." This phrase is usually used to describe someone who is so determined to finish a task that they work so hard that they eventually wear themselves out.

Mañana habit is a Filipino term that means "to do tomorrow." This phrase is usually used to describe someone who is not willing to do something today, but will do it tomorrow.
