15. opening remarks for christmas party tagalog


Welcome words/Opening remarks

Kumusta, lahat, at maligayang pagdating sa aming Christmas party! Lubos kaming natutuwa na makakasama kayong lahat dito ngayon upang ipagdiwang ang pinakamagagandang oras ng taon. Sulitin natin ang espesyal na okasyong ito.

Welcome words are speeches spoken at celebrations, parties, and others. The meaning of the opening speech is a sentence that is delivered before the main event begins. Generally it contains a welcome from the presenter and is continued by important parties or figures who give advice or hope to the audience.

Purpose of ForewordMake the audience follow the directions from giving the speech (Usually done by the MC or host)Give an overview of the main events.Encouraging core activities to be carried out as well as possible.Motivate the audience to take an action.Making events less boring and more lively.

Types of GreetingsThe opening remarks are usually spoken by the master of ceremonies before inviting stakeholders to give official remarks.Briefing remarks, namely remarks that direct the audience to follow the event as well as possible. Usually read by the host.The inauguration speech, can be in the form of a spontaneous speech without text or using a speech text that has been prepared on a piece of paper.Accountability remarks, namely the opening speech that opens an accountability report on events or activities that have been or have been held.

19. examples of opening remarks in christmas party


Opening Remarks

There are lots of ways to render an opening remarks to a Christmas party. One way is to give everyone a warm welcome and allow them to feel that they are part of the event and that the party is for everyone. The speaker might also cite an experience relating to Christmas and what he/she had gained from that experience. He/She might also give a message that includes the birth of Jesus Christ and how important it is to recognize him as the Savior.
