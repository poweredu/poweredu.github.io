1. Mariela thanked her teacher for helping her what is the gerund function?


Answer:

The gerund is the word "helping".

In this case, it is used as the object of the preposition.

The preposition used is "for".

(Gerunds will be considered as the object of the preposition if: (1) it is in -ing form, (2) it is not the main verb, and (3) it is found after the preposition)
