24. What festivity in the Philippines can you closely relate the festival in Talisay City? Explain the difference and similarity of the said festivals.


Festivity in the Philippines Related to the Festival in Talisay City

An example of a festivity in the Philippines related to the festival in Talisay City is the Salakayan Festival of Miagao, Iloilo. Minuluan Festival commemorates the courage and heroism of the inhabitants of Talisay as they fought against the moro or Muslim raiders during the Spanish era. Likewise, Salakayan Festival is an observance of the battle against the same enemies.

Differences of the Festivity in the Philippines Related to the Festival in Talisay City

Some of them include:

Minuluan is celebrated every first week of September while Salakayan every first week of February.Aside from commemorating a battle against the Moros, Minuluan is also a celebration of the Feast of St. Nicholas, their patron saint. This is not the case with Salakayan.

Similarities of the Festivity in the Philippines Related to the Festival in Talisay City

Some of them include:

Their main events include street dance depicting the battle against the Moros. The battles being honored took place in the 1700s. Both festivals also celebrate the glorious life of their inhabitants in the past and even in the present.
