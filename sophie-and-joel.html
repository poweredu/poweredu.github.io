15. Sophie and Simon are peeling a pile of potatoes for lunch in the cafeteria. Sophie can peel all the potatoes by herself in 45 minutes, while it would take Simon 30 minutes to do the job working alone. If Sophie and Simon work together to peel the potatoes, how long will it take them?


Answer:

The time it will take both  Sophie and Simon to peel the potatoes is 18 mins.

Step-by-step explanation:

Step 1: Write the given

Sophie takes 45 minutes in peeling all the potatoesSimon takes 30 minutes in peeling all the potatoes

Step 2: Find the time that it will take them both to peel the potatoes

Sophie does 1/45 of work per minuteSimon does 1/30 of work per minuteThe time that it will take them to finish the work is x. Thus they should do their work at 1/x per minute.[tex]\frac{1}{45 min}+\frac{1}{30 min}=\frac{1}{x}[/tex]By simplifying the equation and solving for x, we wold arrive at x is equal to 18 mins.

Therefore, the time it will take both of them to peel the potatoes is 18 mins.
