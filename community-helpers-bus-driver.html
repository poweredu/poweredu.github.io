27. A bus is moving at 25m/s,the driver steps ln the brakes and the bus stops in 3.0 seconds.What is the acceleration of the bus while stopping?​


Answer:

8.333 m/s²

Explanation:

The formula for acceleration is initial velocity divided by time. So the solution is 25 m/s / 3s the answer is 8.333 m/s²

Answer:

Given: Speed (v): 25m/s

Time (t): 3.0 seconds

RTF (Related to Find) : Acceleration

Formula: a = vf-vi / t

Substitution:

a= 25/3

= 8.33333333 or 8.3 or 8 m/s^2
