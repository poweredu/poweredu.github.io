28. how to make background od the study about Impact of Francis Magalona into the Heart of Patriotism of Filipinos​


Explanation:

Filipino Rap's Patriotic Icon

Francis Magalona, also known as "Master Rapper" and "Francis M," is a legendary figure in the Philippine music industry. He is widely regarded as the "godfather of Pinoy rap" and has made a significant impact on Filipino music, culture, and society.

Magalona was born on October 4, 1964, in Manila, Philippines, to a musical family. He started his career as a rapper in the 1980s, and his unique blend of rap, hip-hop, and Pinoy rock quickly caught the attention of the Filipino public. His socially conscious lyrics and patriotic themes resonated with the youth and the working class, making him a voice of the people.

Through his music, Magalona championed Filipino identity, culture, and values. He celebrated the country's diversity, paying tribute to the different regions, languages, and traditions that make up the Philippines. He also spoke out against social injustices, corruption, and inequality, using his platform to raise awareness and inspire change.

Magalona's impact on the hearts and minds of Filipinos is undeniable. He instilled a sense of pride and patriotism in his fans, inspiring them to embrace their heritage and fight for a better future. His music and message continue to resonate with Filipinos today, long after his untimely death in 2009.

Given the profound influence that Francis Magalona has had on Philippine society, it is important to understand the extent of his impact on the hearts of Filipinos. This study aims to explore the ways in which Magalona's music and message have shaped Filipino identity, culture, and patriotism. By examining the views and experiences of Filipinos who have been touched by Magalona's legacy, this study seeks to shed light on the enduring significance of his contributions to the Philippines.
