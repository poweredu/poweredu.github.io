How I get the answer?..

1. x - 10 = 22

   (left) = (right)

Transpose all numbers in the left side to the right side and simply change their sign. If negative on the left side, when transposed, it becomes positive.

1. x - 10 = 22

... becomes ...

x = 22 + 10

... giving us the answer of ...

x = 32 ... Letter C.

2. x = 8 + 10 + 27

x = 45 ... Letter E.

3. -11 + x + (-48) = 25

x + (-48) = 25 + 11

Again, -11 becomes a +11 when transposed to the right side.

Thus, x + (-48) = 25 + 11

Then, we should add first the right side before transposing giving us...

x + (-48) = 36

Now, we all know that when multiplying a negative sign to a positive sign, it is always negative.

Hence, x - 48 = 36

Then, we transpose -48 to the right side which becomes...

x = 36 + 48

x = 84 ... Letter D

4. x - (-16) = 12 + 13

x - (-16) = 25

In contrast with number 3, when multiplying negative sign to negative sign, it becomes positive.

Therefore, x - (-16) = 25 becomes...

x + 16 = 25

Then, transpose...

x = 25 - 16

x = 9... Letter A.

5. x - (-27) = 35 - 12

The same situation with #4...

So, x + 27 = 23

gives us...

x = 23 - 27

x = (-4)... Letter B.
