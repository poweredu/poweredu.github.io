Answer:

Viva Vigan Binatbatan Festival of the Arts

Explanation:

Binatbatan was introduced in 2002 to be part of the festival to commemorate the Abel Iloco hand-loom weaving industry. Viva Vigan Binatbatan Festival of the Arts is celebrated yearly from the last week of April to the first week of May.
