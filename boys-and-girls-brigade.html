1. What is the difference between classic kitchen brigade and modern kitchen brigade​.


Answer:

The term "classic kitchen brigade" refers to the way kitchens in restaurants and hotels are organized, with a hierarchy of ranks and corresponding responsibilities and duties. On the other hand, the modern kitchen brigade divides the kitchen into many divisions that assist and ensure kitchen operations run smoothly.

2. Do all food establishment follow the Classic Kitchen Brigade or Modern Kitchen Brigade?


No, not all food establishments follow either the classic kitchen brigade or the modern kitchen brigade. Many restaurants adapt their kitchen organization to fit the needs of their particular business and cuisine, or operate without any form of set system at all.
