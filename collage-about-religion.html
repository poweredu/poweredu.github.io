A value proposition is a statement that highlights why a client should buy a company's product or use its service by identifying clear, measurable, and demonstrable benefits. Simply expressed, it is a promise to offer value to the client.

There is 4 segmentation strategy and these are as follows?

Demographic segmentation

is one of the most prominent and widely utilized market segmentation techniques. It is statistical information about a group of people.

Psychographic segmentation

Audiences and customers are divided into groups based on personality traits and attributes.

Behavioral segmentation

Behavioral segmentation is different from demographic and psychographic segmentation in that it focuses on how a customer behaves.

Geographic segmentation

The most basic type of market segmentation is geographic segmentation. It divides clients into groups based on their location.
