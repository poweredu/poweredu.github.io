26. GIVE 3 QUOTES FROM ROMEO JULIET AND EXPLAIN."wisely and slow, they stumble that run fast""Don't waste your love on somebody, who doesn't value it""Do not swear by the moon, for she changes constantly. then your love would also change"​


Answer:

1. The quote "wisely and slow, they stumble that run fast" means that it is better to take things slowly and thoughtfully rather than rushing and making mistakes. In the context of the play, the Friar is advising Romeo to slow down and be careful in his actions, particularly in his relationship with Juliet, as their young love has the potential to cause conflict and danger. The quote also reflects the theme of haste and impulsiveness versus careful planning and consideration, which is a recurring theme in the play.

2. The quote "Don't waste your love on somebody, who doesn't value it" encourages individuals to value their own worth and not invest their emotions in people who do not deserve or respect them.

3. The quote "Do not swear by the moon, for she changes constantly. then your love would also change" means that making promises on something as unreliable and fickle as the moon is not a good idea. Juliet is cautioning Romeo to not make promises that he cannot keep or that are subject to change. She is warning him that if he were to swear his love on the moon, which is constantly changing, then his love may also change or be subject to the same inconsistency. The quote underscores the importance of honesty, sincerity, and authenticity in love, and encourages people to avoid making frivolous promises or commitments.
