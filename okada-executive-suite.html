Answer:

it will take 18 days to stitch 180

Answer:

Hey!

18 days will it take them to complete an order of 180 suits.

Step-by-step explanation:

Note that the more to work the more the days.

Given;

9 days for 90 suits 180 days = ? days

Solution:

180 × 9 ÷ 90

[tex] \frac{ \cancel{180} \times 9}{ \cancel{90}} \\ = 2 \times 9 \\ = 18[/tex]

Therefore in 18 days will it take them to complete an order of 180 suits.
