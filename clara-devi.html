12. Devi, Jodhi and Fiona have saved some allowance to buy a gift for their father. The ratio of Devi's to Jodhi's savings is 1 : 3. The ratio of Jodhi's to Fiona's savings is 5 : 3. If Devi has saved $80, how much has Fiona saved?


Given:

The ratio of Devi's to Jodhi's savings is 1 : 3
The ratio of Jodhi's to Fiona's savings is 5 : 3
Devi has saved $80

Question:

How much has Fiona saved?

Solution:

1:3 and 1=$80 so replace 1 with the given value $80
and it becomes $80:3 after you replace the given value, multiply them with each other (we multiply it because 1 is less than 3) $80x3=$240 and $240 is Jodhi's savings so the the ratio of Devi's to Jodhi's savings will become $80:$240

5:3 and 5=$240 so replace 5 with the value that we solved which is $240 and it becomes $240:3 after you replace the value, divide it with the 3 (we divide it because 5 is greater than 3) $240/3=$80 and $80 is Fiona's savings so the ratio of Jodhi's to Fiona's savings will become $240:$80

Answer:

Fiona's savings is $80, the same as Jodhi's savings

26. what is the effect of electronic devis in globalazation


What is the effect of electronic devices on globalization?

Globalization has accelerated the dissemination of knowledge and technology across boundaries. Technological improvements lower the cost of transportation and communication between countries, making global procurement of raw materials and other inputs easier. Patented technology promotes globalization since the company that owns the patent may profit from overseas markets with little competition. Technology has aided us in overcoming key barriers to globalization and international trade, such as trade barriers, a lack of a shared ethical standard, transportation costs, and information transmission delays, resulting in a shift in the marketplace. Technological advancement has aided globalization by providing infrastructure for cross-national connectivity. The revolution in information and communication technology, according to Ajayi (2000), has been the core and driving factor for the globalization process.
