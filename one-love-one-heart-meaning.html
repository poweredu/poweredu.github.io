Isaac was not only his only beloved son, but the son he had waited for so long. But Abraham trusted God more than he trusted his own human reason. Without any doubt and resistance, he obeyed God. Abraham's pure heart saw everything with the eyes of faith. This means that he had no other desire than to do what God commanded him to do. He relied on God and His promise. A clean heart is one that is not half-hearted, but one constantly devoted to God. One does not rely on personal perception and understanding in doing what is right.
