What is Needs?

These are the basic things needed by a person to survive. These includes food, clothing, shelter, water and medicines.

What is wants?

These are the luxury, a feel a need and a desire.

What is expectation?

These refers to the perceived benefits that the customer can get to the product or services they purchased.

For the meaning of customer expectation read more on:

brainly.ph/question/737463

Example:

Penshoppe product

If the customer will buy a penshoppe ( this is one of the brand in apparel found in the department store) let's say T-shirt or a pants. We can identify the difference of wants, needs and expectation based on the examples explained below:We can say that it is a need if the customer buy T-shirt or pants without choosing the color, design, style, quality or price the customer buy the product so that they will have a clothes to wear.We can say that it is wants if the customer will select the color that is right for his skin-tone, design that is fit him, the quality and it will cost high price. The buyer has really a desire to buy the product even it costly. The customer is willing to change his money for the satisfaction of his desire.We can say that it is expectation if the customer can satisfy on his perception on that product. Example if the customer will hear the brand penshoppe, what comes in his mind is that the product is quality. Therefore, if the product don't meet that expectation there will no repeat purchase for the product.
