1. batas Underwood Simmons​


Answer:

Ang Batas Underwood-Simmons o mas kilala rin bilang Revenue Act of 1913 ng Estados Unidos ay ang pagsasabalik ng pederal na buwis at pagbababa ng taripa mula 40% tungo 25%, na naunang naipataw sa pamamagitan ng Payne-Aldrich Tariff Act ng 1909.

Sa ilalim din ng Underwood-Simmons Act, tinanggal na ang mga limitasyon sa mga produktong nilalabas ng Pilipinas papasok ng Estados Unidos gaya ng asukal at tabako.

17. ano ang underwood-simmonous act


The Underwood-Simmons Act was supported by President Woodrow Wilson, who believed the act would lead to an increase in the import of foreign materials and reduce their cost. The Underwood-Simmons act also introduced the first income tax in the United States as an attempt to recover the revenue that had been lost by lowering the tariffs on foreign imports.
