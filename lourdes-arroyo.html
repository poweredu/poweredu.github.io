22. What is the important to know the History of Our Lady of Lourdes as NCeans​


Answer:

The history of Our Lady of Lourdes is an important part of understanding the Catholic faith. It is a story of faith, hope, and miracles. The story began in 1858 when the Blessed Virgin Mary appeared to a young peasant girl, Bernadette Soubirous, in the small town of Lourdes, France. Mary asked Bernadette to dig in the ground at the grotto of Massabielle, and a spring of healing water soon appeared. Since then, millions of pilgrims have visited the grotto in search of healing and spiritual renewal.
