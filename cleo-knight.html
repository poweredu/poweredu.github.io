PROBLEM

Cleo traveled 45 miles to her sister’s house in 1 hour. The returned trip took him 1.5 hour. What was Cleo’s average rate for the entire trip?

Given:

45 miles for 1 hr

45 miles for 1.5 hr (return trip)

Solution:

= 45 miles / (1hr + 1.5hr) ÷ 2

= 45 miles / 2.5 hr ÷ 2

= 45 miles / 1.25 hr

Answer:

The average travel rate of Cleo to and from his sister's house is 45 miles per 1.25 hour.
