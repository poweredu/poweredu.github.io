30. In a Math contest, the total number of participants was 132. Twice the number of female participants exceeded the number of male participants by 48. How many male participants were there in the contest?


Male Participants = 72

Step-by-step explanation:

Let ;

x = Female Participants

y = Male Participants

We have 2 Equations :

x + y = 1322x = y + 48

Lets solve for "y" on our 2nd equation.

2x = y + 48

2x - 48 = y

or

y = 2x - 48

Now that we have the value of "y", lets use it on our 1st equation (x + y = 132)

x + (2x - 48) = 132

Combine like terms

3x - 48 = 132

3x = 132 + 48

3x = 180

Divide both side by 3

x = 60

And now we have the value of "x".

Again, x = female participants. We're looking for male participants which is "y".

On our equation "x + y = 132", we can now solve for the value of "y" because we have our "x = 6"

x + y = 132

60 + y = 132

y = 132 - 60

y = 72

Our Answer is 72.
