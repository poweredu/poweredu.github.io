15. parts of newspaper1.front page2.filio3. news article4. future article5. editor6. editorial7. editorial cartoons8. letters to the editorpahingi po ng explanations sa mga yan thank you po!​


Answer:

Sure, here are the explanations for the parts of a newspaper you listed:

1. Front Page - The front page is the first page of a newspaper, which usually contains the most important news stories of the day. It is designed to grab the reader's attention and entice them to read more.

2. Folio - A folio is a small line of text at the top of each page of a newspaper that includes the newspaper's name, the date, and the page number.

3. News Article - A news article is a written report that provides information about a current event or news story. It is typically written in a straightforward, objective style and includes the who, what, when, where, why, and how of the story.

4. Feature Article - A feature article is a longer, in-depth article that explores a particular topic or issue in more detail. It is typically written in a more creative or narrative style than a news article.

5. Editor - An editor is a person who is responsible for overseeing the content of a newspaper. This includes selecting and editing articles, writing headlines, and ensuring that the newspaper is produced on time.

6. Editorial - An editorial is an opinion piece that expresses the views of the newspaper's editorial board on a particular topic or issue. It is typically written in a persuasive style and aims to influence the reader's opinion.

7. Editorial Cartoons - Editorial cartoons are illustrations or cartoons that accompany an editorial or news article. They are typically satirical or humorous and aim to comment on current events or public issues.

8. Letters to the Editor - Letters to the editor are written by readers and addressed to the editor of a newspaper. They express the reader's opinion on a particular issue or topic and are usually published in a separate section of the newspaper.
