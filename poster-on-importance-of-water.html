2. create a poster that explains the importance of conserving water resources in relation to the current COVID-19 pandemic​


Answer:

Title: "Conserve Water, Protect Our Health"

Image: A graphic depicting a droplet of water turning into a mask

Subtitle: "In the face of COVID-19, conserving water is more important than ever"

Bullet points:

Water scarcity can lead to overcrowding at water sources, increasing the risk of virus transmission

Proper hygiene, such as handwashing, is crucial in preventing the spread of COVID-19 and requires a steady supply of clean water

Drought conditions can strain local water systems, making it harder to treat and distribute safe drinking water

Call to action:

Reduce water usage at home by fixing leaks, taking shorter showers, and using drought-resistant plants

Support community efforts to conserve and protect water resources

Advocate for policies that prioritize water conservation and access to safe drinking water

Tagline: "Together, let's conserve water and protect our health during these challenging times."

The central person is the ordinary human being, with a health professional and a scientist on the side. Make sure to make this as the centerpiece to signify how our relationship with water is very important especially to the current pandemic.

Around the central image, illustrate some symbols that represents our actions and experiences during this pandemic, such as being healthy mentally and physically (books and barbells), the significance of technology (laptops), prevention (syringes, and fruits) and unity.

Importance of Air- Air is important for living things.
