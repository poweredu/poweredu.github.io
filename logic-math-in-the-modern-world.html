27. Math Logic1. If the word MODERN can be encrypted as OQFTGP, how can you code the word WORLD? A. YQSNF C. YQUNF B. YQTNF D. YQPNF 2. What is the next number in the pattern: 5, 7, 10, 15, 22, ____? 3. Find the next two numbers in the sequence: 5, 6, 10, 19, 35, _____, ______ ?​


[The first question may have an error in the encryption. The question will be solved using the following corrections: E should be G and R should be T.]

(1) Let us first list how the word MODERN was encrypted:

M = OO = QD = FE = GR = TN = P

Now, let us stop and think. If you take a closer look, you will notice that the letters skip two in the encryption. Take, for example, the letter M; based on our alphabet, the following two letters would be N and O, and based on what was given to us, M was indeed encrypted as O!

So, for the encryption of the word WORLD, we get:

W = YO = QR = TL = ND = F

(2) Think about the given sequence and make out a pattern to determine the subsequent numbers:

5 -- 7 -- 10 -- 15 -- 22 -- ?

What pattern can you think of? One of the easiest ways to try to determine a pattern is to get the difference between the numbers.

7 - 5 = 2. But 10 - 7 = 3, so it doesn't make sense, for now, so keep getting a difference.

7 - 5 = 2; 10 - 7 = 3; 15 - 10 = 5; 22 - 15 = 7.

Notice anything? 2, 3, 5, and 7 are prime numbers, which are those that are greater than 1 and are only divisible by 1 and themselves.

Following that pattern, our missing prime number would be 11. And getting the answer,

22 + 11 = 33.

(3) This question is similar to the previous one. Let's try and get the difference again.

6 - 5 = 1; 10 - 6 = 4; 19 - 10 = 9; 35 - 19 = 16.

1, 4, 9, and 16... anything special about these numbers? If you haven't noticed, these are square numbers, meaning they are products of a number multiplied by themselves.

1 multiplied by itself is 1. Likewise, 2 will be equal to 4, 3 to 9, and 4 to 16. According to this, our next number should be 5, and the square number is 25. Getting the answer,

35 + 25 = 70.
