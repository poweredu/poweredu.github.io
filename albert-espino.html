Answer:

Who is Albert Einstein?

German-born physicist who developed the special and general theories of relativity and won the Nobel Prize for Physics in 1921 for his explanation of the photoelectric effect.

When Albert Einstein born?

born March 14, 1879, Ulm, Württemberg, Germany — died April 18, 1955, Princeton, New Jersey, U.S.

Where Albert Einstein study?

After studying at the ETH university in Zurich, Einstein worked at the patent office in Bern, during which time he produced several pioneering works in the field of physics. He was later employed at universities in Bern, Zurich, and Prague, and from 1914, in Berlin.

Why Albert Einstein was smart?

There were, in fact, unique features to Einstein's brain that may be the answer to how he was so smart. Some parts of the brain were thicker than average, which could mean he had a stronger connection between the two hemispheres. LINGAYEN, Pangasinan – Non-essential travel of Pangasinenses to the National Capital Region (NCR), provinces of Bulacan, Cavite, Laguna, and Rizal, as well as the entry of persons from these areas to Pangasinan, are prohibited.

In Executive Order (EO) No. 0027-A-2021 issued on Monday, Governor Amado Espino III said this is in line with the directive of the Inter-Agency Task Force for the management of Emerging Infectious Diseases (IATF-EID).

“Persons from above said places shall be denied entry into Pangasinan for as long as the regulations under IATF-EID Resolution No. 104, series of 2021,” he said.

Espino added the persons from Pangasinan shall not be allowed to travel these places during the effectivity of the regulations under IATF-EID Resolution No. 104.

However, essential travels including but not limited to those by frontline services, government personnel, emergency, humanitarian causes, and movement of cargo, shall remain unhampered as stated in the EO.

“The travel must present competent proof of identity, a document stating workplace address, or any proof of essential travel in border checkpoints for the purpose of assessment,” it added.

26. I'm a male. If Albert's son is my son's father, what is the relationship between Albert and me?


Albert is my father.


We know that “Albert’s son is my son’s father.” With only that information, the speaker could be either female or male. (With a female speaker, “my son’s father” refers to her husband. With a male speaker, “my son’s father” refers to himself.) But we know the speaker is male. (“I am a man.”) So “my son’s father” is the speaker. Therefore, Albert’s son, who “is my son’s father,” is the speaker. Which means the speaker is Albert’s son.

28. Albert is 4 times as old as Roxanne. After 5 years, Albert will be three times as old as Roxanne. Find the present age of Albert.


Answer:

Albert's age now = 40 years old

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x = age of Rox now, so:

4x = age of Al now, then:

5 years from now:

x+5 = age of Rox

4x+5 = age of of Al:

Solve for x:

(4x+5) / (x+5) = 3

(4x+5) = 3(x+5)

(4x+5) = (3x+15)

(4x-3x) = (15-5)

x = 10

Age of Al now = 4x = 4(10) = 40

Checking:

After 5 years:

Age of Rox = 10+5 = 15

Age of Al = 40+5 = 45, which will be 3 times as old as Rox (45/15=3)
