Human hair is composed of multiple layers that are bound together. The outer layer is called the cuticle, the middle layer is known as the cortex, and the inner-most layer is the medulla. The cortex layer is what constitutes 95% of hair mass, and is composed of keratin. Keratin is a helical protein formed by several different amino acids. It is primarily found in hair, skin, and nails.
