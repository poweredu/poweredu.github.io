9. The neuron found wholly and completely within the CNS is the. A. Motor neuron B.inter neuron c. Sensory neuron. D. All of these are correct


Ans: B. Interneuron

Neuron (the medical term for nerve cells) are the basic units making up the nervous system. The human nervous system has 2 kinds: Central Nervous System (CNS) that is composed of the brain and spinal cord, and Peripheral Nervous System (PNS) that is made up of the neurons outside CNS. Neuron has different kinds: motor neuron for the movement, sensory neuron for the senses, and interneuron for the connections of the two aforementioned neurons and the central nervous system (CNS). Both motor neuron and sensory neuron made up the other kind of nervous system, which is the peripheral nervous system. For the signals and information to flow from sensory neuron to the CNS then to the motor neuron, interneurons serve as the links for the body to react and produce reflexes.
