≈ 17.1

SOLUTION :

(18.3 kg ÷ 103.5 cm ÷ 103.5 cm) x 10,000

Convert the decimal into a fraction

(18.3/10 ÷ 103.5 ÷ 103.5) × 10000

(18.3/10 ÷ 207/2 ÷ 103.5) × 10000

(18.3/10 ÷ 207/2 ÷ 207/5) × 10000

Note : To divide by a fraction, multiply by the reciprocal of that fraction

(18.3/10 × 2/207 ÷ 2/207) × 10000

(18.3/10 × 2/207 × 2/207) × 10000

Reduce the numbers with the greatest common factor 2

(18.3/5 × 1/207 × 2/207) × 10000

Reduce the numbers with the greatest common factor 3

(61/5 × 1/69 × 2/207) × 10000

Multiply the fractions

122/71415 × 10000
