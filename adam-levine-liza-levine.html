1. locked away by adam levine portrays​


Answer:

Inspired by true events.

Explanation:

In an interview with Radio.com, Theron Thomas told the story behind the song, saying: “Our dad was locked up for five years. And during the time he was gone, our mom held it down. She’d take us to see him.  They’re still together to this day. They’ve been together for 36 years. So we thought that that was a great story to write a song about.”

Theron also talked about his mom setting a high standard example for any prospective girlfriend: “We each hope we can find a woman who will love us like that. Who will be there for us, whether we’re down and out or whether we’re doing great and who will love us for who we are. That’s our standard. Our whole outlook on relationships is based on that.”

18. the sum of ana and liza age is 45 six years ago ana was twive as old as liza then. how old is liza? ​


Ana's Age = A

Liza's Age = L

Ana's Age 6 years ago = A – 6

Liza's age 6 years ago = L – 6

Ana's Age + Liza's Age = 45

A + L = 45

A = 45 – L

6 years ago, Ana's Age is twice Liza's Age

A – 6 = 2(L – 6)

(45 – L)–6 = 2L –12

45 – 6 – L = 2L – 12

39 + 12 = 2L + L

51 = 3L

3L = 51

L = 51/3

L = 17

Liza is 17 years old.
