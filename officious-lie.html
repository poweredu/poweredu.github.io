The deception is intended for the greater good of preventing the drug dealer from peddling further drugs.

5. Anu po ba ang iba pang halimbawa ng Jocose lies, Officious lie, Pernicious lie?


Jocose lies are types of lies which are meant to be a joke. These are statements which are not true but are being told for fun. This does not intend to harm.

Example: Creating stories about your close friend and what they have done in a funny scenario.

Officious lies are also referred as White lies. These are resorted to do good and not to harm. Untruthful statement might be said but the intention is good.

Example: Telling your mom that you are okay and healthy although you are sick for her not to worry about you.

Pernicious lies are those which incurs harm and is fatal to others.

Example: Denying the accusation of stealing when in fact it was you who stole the thing owned by others.

12. If you are officiating official how will you officiate the game


Answer:

Pregame

Arrive at the game site early. Never rush to a game. Allow ample time to get there well in advance of game time.

Park your car in a well-lit area near the building in which you dress. Try to leave sufficient space to avoid being boxed in.

Hold a thorough pregame conference. Do it even if you’ve worked with your partners before.

Attend to administrative duties early. Allow enough time to fix problems.

Dress in proper, clean uniform. Look sharp and you’re off on the right foot.

Inspect the playing area. Correct potential hazards before the game.

Conduct a brief meeting with team captains or coaches. Tell the captains that they are the leaders of the team and may be called on to fulfill that role. Keep it brief.

Observe players during pregame practice. Opponents occasionally try to intimidate each other before a game. Nip that in the bud.

Examine your equipment. Blow your whistle, etc., to make sure it is in working order.

Relax. If you appear tense and nervous, the players will recognize it.
