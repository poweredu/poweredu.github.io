Answer:

C.Count the number of sunny days in March and April

Explanation:

C.Count the number of sunny days in March and April, is the best answer. It is most appropriate experiment to do in order to prove the hypothesis, that it rains more in April than in March.

REASON

Example: If sunny days in April is lesser than the March and sunny days in March tend to become happen more then the hypothesis that it rains more in April than in March is correct. Hence, the choice C.Count the number of sunny days in March and April is the right answer because it matches and proves the given hypothesis.
