17. hello :)) what is common between a theater, auditorium and convention center?​


Answer:

A hall is a large room, that will handle small musical groups (a quartet, for example) dancing for a small group, poetic readings (or readings of a play) and similar activities. Mansions and castles often have halls devoted to chamber music or readings of prose.

A theater is usually a larger space that is built to the needs and specifications of the entertainment. Movie theaters focus on film, a home theater is created to the home owner’s tastes. A theater devoted to live performance will have infrastructure that suits the needs of sets, lighting and special effects that enhance the performance of a play, musical or other show.

An auditorium is a larger space that is more of a multi purpose facility. It likely will sacrifice some of the finer points of comfort and specific technical aspects, focusing on a utilitarian purpose.

27. What monasteries, convents, and castles converted into hotels are called?


Monasteries, convents, and castles converted into hotels are called paradors.

Explanation :

Paradors is a hotel built on the site of a former monastery or castle. Several famous paradors are located in the Mediterranean countries such as Greece, Italy, Croatia, Morocco and the most famous for having various paradors is Spain.

Paradors are generally categorized as luxury hotels, paradors are usually located in converted historical buildings such as monasteries or castles. However, some Paradors in Spain have a more modern concept or building background. Most paradors are built in locations with panoramic views and special locations.

Because it is classified as a luxury feature, the price of Paradors can be considered quite expensive. In 2021 prices are around 600 euros for 1 or 2 people. This equates to 120 euros per night including breakfast.
