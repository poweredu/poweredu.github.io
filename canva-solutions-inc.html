4. 1. The length of a canvas to be used for painting is 3x + 5 feet while its width is 2x – 7 feet. Once the canvas was tacked to its frame, the original dimensions were lessened by a foot. What is now the area that will be covered with paint? GIVEN UNKNOWN FORMULA SOLUTION CONCLUSION


Answer:

3x^{2}-8x-16

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

Before tacking:

l = 3x+5, w=2x-7

After tacking: less by 1 (-1)

l = 3x+5-1, w=2x-7-1

l = 3x+4, w=2x-8

UNKNOWN: Area of a rectangle

FORMULA:  l x w

SOLUTION:

l x w = (3x+4)(2x-8)

= 6x^{2}+8x-24x-32

= 6x^{2}+-16x-32

SIMPLIFY:

=3x^{2}-8x-16

CONCLUSION:

therefore the area covered with paint is 3x^{2}-8x-16.
