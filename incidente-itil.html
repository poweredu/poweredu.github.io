6. how do you differentiate a total itility curve from a marginal utility curve?​


Answer:

Total utility is the total satisfaction received from consuming a given total quantity of a good or service, while marginal utility is the satisfaction gained from consuming an additional quantity of that item. Sometimes, economists like to subdivide utility into individual units that they call utils.

20. warrantless search incidental to a lawful arrest example in Tagalog?​


Answer:

Ang warrantless search incidental sa isang lawful arrest ay nangangahulugan ng paghahanap ng mga bagay na may kaugnayan sa pagkakapatong ng mga kaso o paghuli sa isang suspect ng walang warrant ngunit sa bisa ng lawful arrest.

Halimbawa, kung may isang tao na nahuli dahil sa paglabag sa batas, maaring mag-conduct ng warrantless search ang mga pulis ngunit lamang sa mga lugar na may koneksyon sa pagkakahuli ng suspect, tulad ng paghahanap ng mga armas, droga, o iba pang mga ebidensiya ng paglabag sa batas.

24. what is incidental music? ​


Answer:

Incidental music is music in a play, television program, radio program, video game, film, or some other presentation form that is not primarily musical. The term is less frequently applied to film music, with such music being referred to instead as the "film score" or "soundtrack.

Incidental Music- is used in a film or play as a background to create or enhance a particular atmosphere
