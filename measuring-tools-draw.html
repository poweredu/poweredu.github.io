30. draw appropriate tool for measuring distance​


Answer:

Tape measures are portable tools that can clip to a belt and measure maximum distances ranging from 6 feet to 33 feet, depending on tape length. A long tape measures distances between 100 and 300 feet and unspools from a reel. Measuring tapes are good backups for situations in which other tools won't work.
