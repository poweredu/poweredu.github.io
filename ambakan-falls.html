10. A ball falls vertically after being dropped. The ball falls a distance d meters in a time of t seconds. d is directly proportional to the square of t. The balls falls 20 meters in a time of 2 seconds. Calculate the time the ball takes to fall 605 meters.


d = kt²

Where k is the constant of direct variation/proportion, or the rate.

Equation:

20 meters = k(2 seconds)²

k = 20 m/4 s²

k = 5 m/s²

Find the time in seconds for the ball to fall 605 meters:

605 m = (5 m/s²) (t)²

t² = (605 m)/(5 m/s²)

t² = 121 s²

√(t²) = √(121 s²)

t = 11 s

ANSWER: 11 seconds

The ambahan is a chanted verse, but it is changed plainly or almost recited. The rendering of the ambahan with musical pitch might differ from person to person.
