14. Paano nakatutulong ang cyber ethics sa paggamit ng teknolohiya katulad ng facebook,instagram,twitter,internet at iba pa


Ano ang cyber ethics? Ang cyber ethics ay alituntunin sa paggamit ng internet. Ito ay ang katanggap-tanggap, ligtas at responsableng pag-uugali na kailangang ipakita sa internet. Katulad ng ating pagkilos sa araw-araw na pamumuhay, kailangan din nating maging maayos sa pagkilos sa internet. Narito ang ilang mga ideya kung paano nakatutulong ang cyber ethics sa paggamit ng teknolohiya katulad ng Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, internet at iba pa:

Ang cyber ethics ay nakakatulong upang maging ligtas at kaaya-ayang gamitin ang internet at social media. Dahil sa cyber ethics, naiiwasan ang mga masasamang pananalita at pag-uugali sa internet. Bukod dito, tumutulong ang cyber ethics upang maiwasan ang "cyber bullying" na isa sa mga masasamang epekto ng teknolohiya. Sa pamamagitan ng "cyber bullying", maaaring gumawa ng pekeng account ang ibang tao upang mang-api ng kapwa tao sa internet. Dahil sa cyber ethics, naiiwasan ang mga ilegal na mga transaksyon sa internet kagaya na lamang ang "phishing", "identity theft" at "spamming".

Iyan ang ilan lamang sa mga paraan kung paano nakatutulong ang cyber ethics sa paggamit ng teknolohiya.

26. can sign up to Change.org using your Facebook account? true or false?


True. Yes, you can definitely sign up to Change.org using your Facebook account. The Change.org, a site designed to post petition for global issues and concerns, allows you to log-in in three different ways:

1. Email Sign-up: Sign up with your Full Name, email address, and password.  

2. Google Mail Account: Instantly sign up using your existing Gmail account.  

3. Facebook Account: Instantly sign up using your existing Facebook account.

Steps to sign up using Facebook

1. Go to change.org using your browser

2. Click the Log-in button at the upper right part of your screen

3. Clicking the Log-in button will lead you to a pop-up for logging in. Click the underlined ‘Sign Up’ beside the ‘Don’t have an account?’

4. Click the ‘Sign up with Facebook’ button

5. The button will prompt to a link connected to Facebook. Log in with your Facebook account

6. If your account was already logged in to the browser, the site will prompt a permission to receive your name and profile picture and email address. If you’re fine with it, click ‘Continue as’ and you’ll be redirected to Change.org with a logged account.

Note: Always read terms and privacy before accepting the privacy permission.

Change.org

By signing up to Change.org, you are now free to create your petition for local and global issues or concerns like Climate Change and Racial Justice. The platform for global change has almost received 300,000 petitions and most of them were victoriously called for solutions. By signing up, you can also browse on different petitions, get involve, and take action.
