2. Tula tungkol sa ina tagalog isalin sa english


Title : Thoughts of You

Memories of another time still come

To me and fill my mind, with thoughts

Of you when you were young. I lie awake

'Till the morning sun comes creeping

Through my window shade, as I dwell upon

Mistakes I've made. What I would give to

Go back in time and feel your little

Hand in mine. To cherish each fast and

Fleeting day. To hold you close and kiss

Away, each pain that life will have in

Store and try to give you so much more.

You are part and will always be, imbedded in the soul of me. While I'm

Here, I want to say, that I've loved you

Each and every day and when my time on

Earth is gone.

The privilege was mine to have been your Mom.


3. magbigay ng isang Tagalog na Tula at isalin ito sa English​


Answer:Bayan Ko

Ang bayan kong Pilipinas

Lupain ng ginto’t bulaklak

Pag-ibig na sa kanyang palad

Nag-alay ng ganda’t dilag.

At sa kanyang yumi at ganda

Dayuhan ay nahalina

Bayan ko, binihag ka

Nasadlak sa dusa.

Ibon mang may layang lumipad

kulungin mo at umiiyak

Bayan pa kayang sakdal dilag

Ang di magnasang makaalpas!

Pilipinas kong minumutya

Pugad ng luha ko’t dalita

Aking adhika,

Makita kang sakdal laya.

Jose Corazon De Jesus

explination :Bayan Ko

My country the Philippines

Land of gold and flowers

Love in the palm of his hand

Offering beauty and splendor.

And in her modesty and beauty

Foreigners are fascinated

My people, you are taken captive

Drowned in grief.

Birds are free to fly

shut up and cry

The town can still be perfect beauty

Those who do not wish to escape!

I love the Philippines

The nest of my tears and misery

My aspiration,

See you perfectly free.

Jose Corazon De Jesus

Answer:

Ang aming kultura at tradisyon,

Ang nagbibigay sa amin ng identidad.

Mga kasaysayan at paniniwala,

Nagpapakatatag sa aming mga puso.

Ang aming lahi, wika at musika,

Ay nagbubuklod sa aming mga kamay.

Mga kaugalian at kasanayan,

Ay nagpapahayag ng aming kabuhayan.

Ang aming mga magulang at ninuno,

Ay nagbigay sa amin ng landas.

Mga aral at karunungan,

Na magpapatuloy sa susunod na henerasyon.

Ang aming kultura at tradisyon,

Ay hindi dapat kalimutan.

Dapat itong pahalagahan at itaguyod,

Upang maging buhay sa panahon ng mga pagbabago.
