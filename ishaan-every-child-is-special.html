16. Ben is 4 times as old as Ishaan. 6 years ago, Ben was 6 times as old as Ishaan. How old is Ishaan now?


Answer:

15 years old

Step-by-step explanation:

Let the age of Ishaan be x. This means that the age of Ben is 4x as he is 4 times as old as Ishaan.

6 years ago, Ishaan must have been x-6 years old while Ben must have been 4x-6 years old.

Also, 6 years ago Ben was 6 times as old as Ishaan.

[tex]4x - 6 = 6(x - 6) \\ \\ 4x - 6 = 6x - 36 \\ \\ 4x - 6x = - 36 + 6 \\ \\ - 2x = - 30 \\ \\ \frac{ - 2x}{ - 2} = \frac{ - 30}{ - 2} \\ \\ x = 15[/tex]

Ishaan is 15 years old.
