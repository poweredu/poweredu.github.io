Avoid peer pressure: One of the most common reasons for trying gateway drugs is peer pressure. Find healthy ways to manage stress, such as exercise, meditation, or talking to someone about your feelings.Seek professional help: If you find it difficult to stay away from drugs, seek professional help from a therapist or a support group.
