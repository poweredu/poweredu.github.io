2. in sandwiches is fries a garnish or notâ€‹


Traditionally, fries are not considered a garnish for sandwiches. Fries are typically served as a side dish to sandwiches, rather than being placed directly on the sandwich itself. However, it ultimately depends on personal preference and the specific sandwich being made. Some people may choose to add fries as a topping or ingredient to their sandwich, while others may choose to serve them separately as a side dish.
