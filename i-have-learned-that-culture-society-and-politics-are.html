1. i have learned that culture society and politics are?​


Society, culture and politics are a unified element that provides values, attitudes, guidelines in life.

Society is a group of living things that are closely intertwined because of the same system, traditions, conventions, and certain laws that lead to collective life. Systems in society are interconnected between one human and another human who form a unity. The relationship between society and culture is dual. Culture is the result of a society, culture can only be born, grow, and develop in society. On the other hand, there is no society that is not supported by culture.

Culture is a pattern of community that is complex, comprehensive, and abstract. Culture can also be interpreted as a way of life for people to achieve common goals. The importance of culture in society is as a tradition, noble values, and local wisdom that are shared from generation to generation by a community in a nation. Culture Can be interpreted as a collective identity or the identity of a nation. Society and culture cannot be separated because society is a container of culture itself. So that humans give birth to a culture that is considered as living values ​​for society.

The relationship between politics and society is very close because people make up a political system . The political system is a subsystem of the social system. The system perspective or approach sees the interactions that exist in a system, namely a unit that is relatively separate from its environment and has a relatively fixed relationship between its constituent elements.

Political culture is a pattern of behavior of a society in the life of the state, the implementation of state administration, government politics, law, customary norms that are lived by all members of society every day. Political culture is very interesting to study because it will increase understanding of the political culture of the community, patterns of political behavior, and values ​​which in turn will increase public awareness to participate actively in politics.
