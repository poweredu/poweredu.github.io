1. absolute moral values tagalog


Ano ang Absolute Moral Values?

Ang Absolute Moral Values ay isang paniniwalang etikal na may mga pamantayang moral  kung saan ang lahat ng kilos ng mga tao ay maaaring husgahan. Halimbawa, ang pagnanakaw ng isang tinapay sa nagugutom mong anak dahil wala kang sapat na pera upang bilhin ito ay itinuturing pa ring pagnanakaw, imoral, o maling gawain. May mga katotohanan tungkol sa kung ano ang tama at mali at ang mga katotohanang ito ay hindi maaaring isaalang alang ang pananaw, opinyon, senaryo, at iba pa.

May mga relihiyon na may papanaw na katulad ng Absolute Moral Values. Sila ay nagsasabing - "Nagpapasya kami kung sino ang isang makasalanan, at kung ikaw ay may pagdududa sa aming mga desisyon, ikaw ay isang makasalanan."

Mga Kahinaan ng Absolute Moral Values

Maaaring magkaroon ng mga salungatan sa pagitan ng Absolute Moral Values at mga patakaran.  May mga prinsipyong moral na hindi kinakailangang tama sa lahat ng oras. Nagkakaroon ng kakulangan kung paano haharapin ang mga emosyon at pangunahing pangangailangan.

22. what are the values and morals that make up a good organization?​


Answer:

What Are The 4 Core Values Of An Organization? The four core values of an organization are integrity and ethics, respect, innovation (not imitation), and drive.

Answer:

Values and morals play a crucial role in shaping the culture and behavior of an organization. A good organization is one that upholds the following values and morals:

Integrity: The organization operates with transparency, honesty, and fairness in all its dealings.

Respect: The organization treats its employees, customers, and stakeholders with dignity and respect, valuing diversity and inclusion.

Responsibility: The organization takes responsibility for its actions and decisions, and strives to make a positive impact on society.

Accountability: The organization holds its members accountable for their actions and decisions, and encourages a culture of accountability and responsibility.

Innovation: The organization encourages creativity and innovation, and values continuous learning and improvement.

Collaboration: The organization fosters teamwork and collaboration, promoting a culture of cooperation and open communication.

Fairness: The organization operates with fairness and impartiality, treating all employees, customers, and stakeholders equitably.

Ethics: The organization operates within ethical guidelines, and strives to maintain the highest standards of ethical conduct.

Trust: The organization builds trust with its employees, customers, and stakeholders, by being honest and transparent in all its dealings.

Social Responsibility: The organization takes into account the impact of its actions on society and the environment, and strives to make a positive contribution.

By upholding these values and morals, a good organization creates a positive work environment, attracts and retains the best talent, and builds strong relationships with its stakeholders. These values and morals also help organizations to navigate challenges and to make decisions that are in the best interests of all parties involved.

30. how will you develop good values good morals and good attitudes needed for successful organization and individual effectiveness​


1. Model good values, morals and attitudes: Demonstrate the values, morals, and attitudes that you want to instill in yourself and in others.

2. Set expectations: Set clear expectations for behavior and performance and ensure they align with your desired values, morals, and attitudes.

3. Communicate: Regularly communicate your expectations and ensure everyone is aware of them.

4. Reward good behavior: Reinforce good values, morals, and attitudes with rewards and recognition.

5. Create a culture of respect: Foster an environment where respect for diversity, opinions, and individual differences is valued.

6. Foster collaboration: Foster an environment of collaboration and teamwork in order to encourage everyone to work together to achieve the best possible outcomes.

7. Encourage critical thinking: Challenge people to think critically about their behavior and attitudes and take responsibility for their actions.

8. Lead by example: Lead by example and demonstrate the values, morals, and attitudes that you want to instill in others.

In order to develop good values, good morals, and good attitudes needed for successful organizations and individual effectiveness, it is important to first identify the core values that are important to the organization or individual. Once the core values have been identified, it is important to develop a plan to instill these values in the organization or individual. This plan should include a focus on developing an understanding of the importance of the values, as well as developing skills and behaviors that align with the values.
