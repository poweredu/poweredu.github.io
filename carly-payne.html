The greatest number of guests that Carly can serve is 15.

What is asked?

What is the greatest number of guests that Carly can serve?

What are the given?

amount of ice cream for the party - 9½ pints

amount of ice cream serve for each guest - ⅗ pints

What is the number sentence?

9½ ÷ ⅗ = N

To find the greatest number of guests that Carly can serve, we need to divide the total amount of ice cream for the party by the amount of ice cream serve for each guest.

What is the solution?

9½ ÷ ⅗

= ‌19⁄2‌ ÷ ⅗

= 19⁄2‌ × ‌5⁄3‌

= ‌95⁄6‌

= 15⅚

The greatest number of guests that Carly can serve is 15.

Division of Mixed Numbers

Mixed Number

Mixed number is also know as mixed fraction. It is a combination of a whole number and a fraction.

Example: 9 ½

9 is the whole number and ½ is the fraction.

How to divide mixed numbers?

To divide mixed numbers, we need to convert them to improper fractions first. Once the mixed numbers are converted, we can divide as we would divide any other fractions.

What are improper fractions?

Improper fractions are fractions which numerator is greater than the denominator.

Examples: ‌53⁄6‌, ‌23⁄9‌, ‌83⁄40‌

Converting Mixed Numbers to Improper Fractions

These are the three steps to convert mixed number to improper fraction:

Multiply the whole number by the denominator of its combined fraction.Add the numerator to the product.Place the sum over the original denominator.
