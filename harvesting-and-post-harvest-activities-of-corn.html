27. It takes 8 days for 35 men to harvest corn on the farm how long will 20 men take to harvest corn on the same farm?


Answer:

14 days

Step-by-step explanation:

Lets write this problem into proportion.

8:n = 20:35

Write it in fraction form.

[tex]\frac{8}{n} = \frac{20}{35}[/tex]

Cross multiply

8 × 35 = n × 20

280 = 20n

Divide both sides by 20.

280/20 = 20n/20

14 = n

∴ 20 men will take 14 days to harvest corn on the same farm.
