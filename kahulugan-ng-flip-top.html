9. What is flip top and why it’s important


Answer:

Explanation:

A flip-top, swing-top, or Quillfeldt stopper (after the inventor, Charles de Quillfeldt) is a type of closure frequently used for bottles containing carbonated beverages, such as beer or mineral water. The mouth of the bottle is sealed by a stopper, usually made of porcelain or plastic, fitted with a rubber gasket and held in place by a set of wires. The bottle can be opened and resealed repeatedly and without the use of a bottle opener, with the wires acting in the same way as a latch clamp. The flip-top was the dominant method of sealing beer and mineral water bottles prior to the invention of the crown cork.

The top Filipino rap battle league, FlipTop, usually has two contestants. One-on-one or two-on-two matches can be played. "Dos Por Dos"—"2 on 2"—is the match's name from FlipTop. 5-on-5 is another match. As the craft grows and participants improve, a handicap match and other variations may be developed. Ladies can participate. This "Femcee" match is for women. Hearthy The Bomb, Jammy, and Lil Sisa are renowned female emcees.

Emcee Aric Yuson, alias "Anygma," established FlipTop on February 6, 2010. Emcees are FlipTop match participants. Makati City bar Anygma hosted the first battles. FlipTop Battles (or FlipTop Rap Battle League) were held every two months. They initially provided tattoos and clothing. The league has progressed, and key matches now award financial awards.

Many said that Anygma saved the life of the rap music industry and even restored a Filipino tradition dubbed as the 'Balagtasan', which is a kind of discussion using poetic rhyme.

Another big factor that propelled FlipTop to the top is that Pinoys, high and low, love to debate on anything just to emphasize what they want to be done or accepted in order to simply gain the respect (that anybody desires) or claim the authority as someone (at least) if not the most prominent in the group.
