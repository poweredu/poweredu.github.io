It is important to study the perception of high school teachers towards online shopping because their opinions may influence the attitudes of their students, who are also likely to be online shoppers. Additionally, high school teachers play an important role in shaping the learning experiences of their students, and their attitudes towards online shopping may have implications for how they teach about e-commerce and online safety.

Further research is needed to explore the extent/magnitude of the problem and to understand the underlying factors that shape high school teachers' perceptions of online shopping. This can help to identify strategies for promoting responsible online shopping behavior and for improving online safety education in schools.
