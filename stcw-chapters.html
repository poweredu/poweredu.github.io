12. Explain the importance of observing the standards and regulations of watch keeping in accordance to the STCW Code.​


Answer:

The Standards of Traing, Certification and Watchkeeping (STCW) Code is a set of international regulations that establish minimum training, certification, and watchkeeping requirements for seafarers. These standards and regulations are essential for maintaining the safety of vessels and their crew, as well as protecting the marine environment.

Observing the standards and regulations of watchkeeping in accordance with the STCW Code is important for several reasons. Firstly, it helps to ensure that seafarers are properly trained and certified to carry out their duties, which includes monitoring the vessel's position, course, and speed, as well as detecting and responding to potential hazards.

Secondly, adherence to the STCW Code promotes effective communication and cooperation among the crew, which is crucial for the safe and efficient operation of a vessel. This includes maintaining a proper lookout, carrying out regular safety drills, and ensuring that all equipment and systems are in good working order.

Finally, compliance with the STCW Code helps to prevent accidents, reduce the risk of pollution, and protect the marine environment. By ensuring that seafarers are properly trained and certified, and that vessels are operated safely and efficiently, we can minimize the impact of human activities on the world's oceans and coastal areas

16. The sum of the page numbers of chapter 3 in a certain book is 374, if there are 11 pages in chapter 3 on what page does chapter 3 starts


Answer:

Page 29

Step-by-step explanation:

The chapter begins on page 29

The sum of pages is equivalent to the mean value times the number of pages

Let the number of pages be

n Then n ¯ x = 374

But n = 11 ⇒ 11 ¯ x = 374 So  ¯ x = 374 11 = 34

Note that 11 is an odd number of pages. AS the trick is to split the pages up into pairs it means that you have one over. So you have the middle page and the remaining even number of 10 is split so that you have 5 on either side of the middle page giving 5 pairs of numbers

First page is 34-5 = 29

Last page is 34+5 = 39

check:

(29+39)×5+34=374

29+30+...+34+...38+39

You have 5 pairs of 68 with a single 34 in the middle

If you have an even number of pages there is no middle one to be added.

22. Explain the importance of the STCW 78, as amended in the attainment of the goal-based ships operations through the deployment of competent seafarers


Answer:

The Standards of Training, Certification, and Watchkeeping (STCW) Convention of 1978 was established by the International Maritime Organization (IMO) to ensure the safety and security of seafarers and maritime operations worldwide. This Convention set minimum standards for the training, certification, and watchkeeping of seafarers in various shipboard roles.

The amendments made to the STCW Convention in 1995 and 2010 further strengthened the requirements for seafarer training and certification. These amendments provided a more comprehensive and consistent approach to the development of the skills and competencies required by seafarers in different roles, including deck officers, marine engineers, and electro-technical officers.

The STCW Convention, as amended, is crucial for achieving goal-based ship operations. The Convention ensures that seafarers are properly trained and competent to perform their duties onboard ships, thereby reducing the risk of accidents, injuries, and loss of life at sea. The STCW Convention also promotes the quality and efficiency of maritime operations, as it requires seafarers to possess the necessary knowledge and skills to operate and maintain various types of ships and equipment.

Moreover, the STCW Convention, as amended, provides a framework for the mutual recognition of seafarer certificates and endorsements among different countries, thereby facilitating the international mobility of seafarers and the global operation of ships. This framework helps to ensure that seafarers are treated fairly and equitably, regardless of their nationality or country of origin.

In summary, the STCW Convention, as amended, plays a critical role in the attainment of goal-based ship operations through the deployment of competent seafarers. It provides a comprehensive and consistent approach to seafarer training and certification, promotes safety and security at sea, and facilitates the international mobility of seafarers and the global operation of ships.
