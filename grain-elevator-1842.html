7. EXERCISES A. Fill out the important events that took place during the given significant year of computer revolution. 19401670 1808 1842 1890 1935​


Answer:

1940 - The first computer was completed by John Atanasoff and Clifford Berry at Iowa State University.

1670 - The slide rule, a mechanical calculating device, was invented by Edmund Gunter.

1808 - Joseph-Marie Jacquard invented the Jacquard loom, which used punched cards to control the weaving of patterns in cloth.

1842 - Ada Lovelace, a mathematician and writer, published her notes on Charles Babbage's Analytical Engine, which is considered the first computer program.

1890 - Herman Hollerith invented the tabulating machine, which used punched cards to process and store data for the US Census.

1935 - Alan Turing proposed the concept of a universal machine, which could be programmed to perform any computation that could be carried out by a human computer.

Answer:

Rice classification

IRRI uses the following scale for size: extra long, >7.50 mm; long, 6.61 to 7.50 mm; medium, 5.51 to 6.60 mm; and short, <5.50 mm. Grain shape is characterized based on length-to-width ratio: slender, >3.0; medium, 2.1 to 3.0; bold 1.1 to 2.0; and round, < 1.0.
