c. Mother has cooked dinner for us. (present perfect tense)

b. I was playing in the plaza. (past progressive tense)

c. I had played in the plaza. (past perfect tense)

Here are the guides to follow:

a. Simple Present/Past Tense = subject + verb (singular subject with –s / plural subject without –s)

b. Present/Past Progressive Tense = subject + am/is-was/are-were + verb with –ing

c. Present/Past Perfect Tense = subject + has/have/had + past participle
