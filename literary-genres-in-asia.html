Literature in Asian Countries

Asian Literature is a famous form of literature from the top 3 countries in Asia. The following are the countries that contributed the Asian Literature:  

China

Chinese civilization is the oldest civilization in the world and it is called "The Red Dragon". The greatest philosopher known was from China, his name was Kung Fu-Tze which literally means as Signifying Teacher. Confucius was the westernized form of his name. He was considered as one of the truly great men of his country.  

India

Indian civilization is called "Land of Prayer". Its literature is based on piety, which means a deeply religious spirit. It is also called "Sanskrit", which pertains to the Indian Literature.  

Japan

Also called "Land of the Rising Sun". Their literature considered one of the major Oriental Literature. It uses Katakana as its basic alphabets.
