Pangalay Tribe: Badjao Origin: Zamboanga del Sur A pangalay native to the Badjao, sometimes known as the “Sea Gypsies.” Pangalay is a dance that emphasizes the agility of the upper body. 13. how does the folk dance in visayas born​


Answer:

Rice farmers on the Visayan Islands usually set up bamboo traps to protect their fields, yet tikling birds dodged their traps. Locals imitated the birds' movement, and supposedly, that's how this dramatic traditional folk dance was born.
