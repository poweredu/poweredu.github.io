29. André-marie ampère contribution to electricity


André-Marie Ampère's contribution was in discovering the electric current.

Explanation :

André-Marie Ampère is a French physicist. He is one of the pioneers of knowledge in the field of electrodynamics. In the course of his career as a physicist, Ampere managed to find a strong electric current.

Electric current strength is the flow rate with an electric charge that passes through a certain part or point. We can say that an electric current exists, when there is a net flow of electric charge through a passage. In physics, electric charges are carried by charged particles.

Ampere had done the same research as Orted. This research is related to voltage-conducting wires that can affect magnetic needles.

Later, Ampere discovered a coil that behaved like a bar magnet. The soft iron in the iron coil or wire turns into a strong magnet. When the two irons are close together, they will produce a force that we now know as an electric current

Based on the results of this research, Ampere also discovered a law, which we know as the law of electrodynamics.
