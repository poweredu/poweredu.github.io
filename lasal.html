10. The LaSalle High School senior class raised funds for an end of the year cruise getaway. The city gave them a special package for the port fees and taxes - a flat fee of $1,500 for the entire class's port fees and taxes. It costs $367 per student to board the cruise. If your class raised $16,600, how many students can attend


Answer:

41 students can attend the cruise getaway.

Step-by-step explanation:

Piecewise Function:

f(x) = 1,500 + 367x

Where:

(fx) is the total charges or cost for the class.

Flat rate for port fees and taxes: $1,500

Cost per student: $367

Number of students: x

Find the number of students x when f(x) = 16,600:

f(x) = 1,500 + 367x

16,600 = 1,500 + 367x

367x = 16,600 - 1,500

367x/367 = 15,100/367

x ≈ 41.14  or  41

x = 41 students

41 students can attend the cruise getaway.
