2. give explaination and example ? to persevere, sustain and commit​


Answer:

Persevere, sustain, and commit are verbs that involve determination and dedication to achieving a goal.

To persevere means to continue to persist in a course of action despite facing difficulties or obstacles. For example, "She persevered through the challenges and finally graduated with honors."

To commit means to dedicate oneself to a particular cause or activity. For example, "She committed herself to volunteer work and spent every weekend at the local shelter."

These verbs all involve persistence and dedication, and they are often used in professional, personal, or educational contexts where perseverance and commitment are highly valued traits.
