1. What is the benefits of icT track in your SHS and What are the advantages of taking iCT as your SHS Track??​


Answer:

Enrolling in the ICT strand for SHS gives senior high school graduates valuable knowledge that enables them to either dive immediately into technical professions befitting their interests and specializations, or to pursue further studies aligned with their track.

Advantages taking an ICT:

Advantages

E-learning or Online Learning

The presence of ICT in education allows for new ways of learning for students and teachers.

ICT brings inclusion

Students with special needs are no longer at a disadvantage as they have access to essential material and special ICT tools can be used by students to make use of ICT for their own educational needs.

ICT promotes higher-order thinking skills

One of the key skills for the 21st century which includes evaluating, planning, monitoring, and reflecting to name a few.

ICT enhances subject learning

It is well known these days that the use of ICT in education adds a lot of value to key learning areas like literacy and numeracy.

ICT use develops ICT literacy and ICT Capability

Both are 21st-century skills that are best developed whilst ICT remains transparent in the background of subject learning.

ICT use encourages collaboration

ICT naturally brings children together where they can talk and discuss what they are doing for their work and this in turn, opens up avenues for communication thus leading to language development.

ICT use motivates learning

Society's demands for new technology has not left out children and their needs. Children are fascinated with technology and it encourages and motivates them to learn in the classroom.

ICT in education improves engagement and knowledge retention

When ICT is integrated into lessons, students become more engaged in their work. This is because technology provides different opportunities to make it more fun and enjoyable in terms of teaching the same things in different ways

ICT use allows for effective Differentiation Instruction with technology

We all learn differently at different rates and styles and technology provide opportunities for this to occur.

ICT integration is a key part of the national curriculum

The integration of digital technologies or ICT is a significant part of the Australian Curriculum for example, and this is a trend that many global governments are taking up as they begin to see the significance of ICT in

education.

My chosen Senior High School track is part of the K to 12 Program of Philippine basic education starting from kindergarten, elementary, junior high school, and senior high school. The twelve-year education program provides ample time for mastery of concepts and skills,  convert them into becoming a lifelong learner and prepare students from college enrollment to employment. With this educational reform, every Filipino student receives quality education at par with global standards. Senior High School graduates may go into different paths such as college education, employment, or entrepreneurship. The major shift in the Philippine educational system is under Republic Act no. 10533 commonly called as “Enhanced Basic Education Act of 2013”.

24. persuasive essay on shs track​


Answer:

Senior High School (SHS) tracks provide students with specialized education and training that prepare them for college, technical-vocational courses, entrepreneurship, or employment. It is important for students to choose the SHS track that suits their interests and aspirations, as it can greatly impact their future careers.

Choosing an SHS track is not an easy decision, but it is a crucial one. By selecting a track, students can delve deeper into their desired field and gain more knowledge and skills that are relevant to their career goals. For example, if a student wants to become a doctor, they can choose the STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) strand to develop their analytical and critical thinking skills.

Moreover, SHS tracks also provide opportunities for students to explore different career paths and develop their passions. Students can choose tracks such as the Arts and Design, which can enhance their creativity and skills in various art forms.

Finally, SHS tracks also prepare students for the demands of college and the workforce. They can learn skills such as time management, teamwork, and communication that are vital in any career. This will make them more competitive in the job market and equip them with the necessary skills to succeed in college and beyond.

In conclusion, choosing an SHS track is a critical decision that can impact a student's future career. It provides them with specialized education and training that can enhance their skills, explore their passions, and prepare them for college and the workforce. Therefore, students should carefully consider their interests and aspirations when choosing an SHS track.
