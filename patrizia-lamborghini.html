17. A Lamborghini is travelling at 123m/s,12 seconds later it reached the top of the hill moving at 195m/s. What is the accelaration ot the Lamborghini?


acceleration = velocity/time

[tex]a = \frac{195m/s- 123m/s}{12s} [/tex]

[tex]a = \frac{72}{12} [/tex]

[tex] a = 6[/tex]

The acceleration of the Lamborghini is 6m/s².
