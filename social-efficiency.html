16. What is the difference between technical efficiency and economic efficiency?​


Technical Efficiency in production means that as few inputs as possible are used to produce a given output.

Economic efficiency means using the method that produces a given level of output at the lowest possible cost.

17. . Identification of community needs and enhancement in delivering of social services. Increased community involvement . Academic and research benefits> Partnerships and better linkages guarantee efficient delivery of services​


Answer:

Identifying community needs is a crucial step in enhancing the delivery of social services. By understanding the needs of the community, service providers can tailor their programs to meet those needs effectively. This can be done through community engagement and consultation, including surveys, focus groups, and public meetings.

Increased community involvement can lead to better outcomes for social service programs. When community members are involved in the planning and implementation of services, they have a greater stake in the success of those programs. They can also provide valuable insights into the needs and concerns of their community, which can help service providers improve their programs.

Academic and research benefits can also be gained from partnerships between service providers and academic institutions. By working together, researchers and service providers can develop and evaluate innovative programs that address community needs. This can lead to more effective and evidence-based social services.

Partnerships and better linkages between service providers can also guarantee efficient delivery of services. By working together, providers can avoid duplication of effort and ensure that resources are used effectively. They can also share expertise and resources, which can lead to better outcomes for their clients.

In summary, identifying community needs and enhancing the delivery of social services requires increased community involvement, academic and research benefits, and partnerships and better linkages between service providers. By working together, service providers and community members can develop and implement programs that address the unique needs of their community and improve the overall well-being of its members

20. How does thermal efficiency different from ideal efficiency?


Answer:

Thermal efficiency relates to processes where the input is thermal energy as in a fired power plant, a internal combustion engine or solar energy systems. It is the ratio of the mechanical output to the thermal input. Overall efficiency looks at entire systems from the initial input to the final output.

Moving Into the Long Term

With some colleges and universities planning to continue online learning into the spring, students are now looking at over a year of learning from home. How will their well-being and academics be affected?

By Lilah Burke

October 27, 2020

ISTOCK.COM/KIEFERPIX

Winter break and the start of next term are fast approaching. At this point, most colleges that have announced their plans for the spring are intending to continue their modality and residence choices from the fall. Many have brought students back to campus but are continuing most instruction online. Others have encouraged students to stay home. For students at colleges that have gone remote, that means they will likely experience over a year of online learning.

Research has shown that remote learning can be as good or better than in-person learning for the students who choose it. But thousands of students will soon be entering their third semester of remote instruction despite having self-selected for an in-person college experience.

With remote learning moving into the long term, experts say the mental, emotional and academic impacts of that shift are likely to be challenging.

Amy Bintliff, a developmental psychologist and professor in the University of California, San Diego’s department of education studies, said that mismatch between expectation and reality can be difficult for students. Part of that is because important milestones, like graduation, can’t happen the way they were envisioned. Traditional-age students may struggle uniquely with the loss of certain coming-of-age experiences.
