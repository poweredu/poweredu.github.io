28. What is the reason why the movie "Pursuit of Happyness" is misspelled?​


Explanation:

Yes, the spelling of happiness in the title of this movie is wrong. But it's wrong on purpose, so I guess that makes it right. Supposedly, in the true story of Chris Gardner, he saw happiness spelled that way on a wall of graffiti and it had an impact on his turnaround.

But there is a deeper meaning in its misspelling. First, the "y" makes us look twice at the word, and consider what is wrong. The misspelling causes us not to take happiness for granted.

Second, the misspelling signifies an element of what this movie is really about. Just as "happyness" is not spelled with a "y", so a man's happiness (who happens to be a father) is not pursued by abdicating his responsibilities of fatherhood. When things get rough. For whatever reason. Many fathers in America today don't know how to spell happiness. They reject the idea that happiness is spelled with an "i". That "i" in the context of this movie means that as a father "I" must take personal responsibility for my role if I am to be truly happy. It means "I" don't run away from difficulty. Happiness with an "i" means if it's "my" dream, it's up to "me."

Third, just as good spelling has to be pursued and often corrected, so good fatherhood has to be pursued and often corrected. This story is about a man who is determined to pursue the correct spelling of happiness, (as Chris' character attempts to do literally with the owner of the childcare center building on the side of which the misspelled word appears ... along with another word that begins with "F" and which describe the opposite of happiness). As the protagonist tries to get the word spelled correctly for the sake of his son's education, so he is determined to pursue the correct concept of fatherhood that will bring his character happiness. In short Chris Gardner, as a father, is determined to spell happiness correctly with the example of his life, no matter what difficulties he has to sustain.


29. Recognized stumbling blocks in the pursuit of your happiness and success.​


In life, we always aim to pursue happiness and of course, success. We see life as a continuous pursuit of these two important concepts of our lives and that our success equates to our happiness.      

With the pursuit of happiness and success, there will always be a stumbling block that we will always encounter because the path towards these can be long and requires a lot of effort and changes in your life.           

One of the common stumbling blocks in the pursuit of these two is the way how you perceive things —by thinking that you need to have that particular thing to be happy and successful, by thinking that you need to have someone to make you happy but it is not. We are all responsible for our happiness. We are responsible for the thoughts that we allow ourselves to consume us, but most of the time, we are overwhelmed by these negative thoughts that it became a stumbling block to our happiness as well as our success.       

Second, thinking that we are not enough- the voices can sometimes be loud around us and most of the time we let those voices consume us and believe the words they utter. Once we believe those words, it brings us down and made us think that we can never be great and successful. Aside from robbing the chance to be successful, it is always and 100% sure will take away the happiness that you have in life.       

Third, the self-imposed limitations. Humans tend to limit their minds and actions on what they can and they cannot do and this serves as a stumbling block to fully achieving your goals and happiness. Putting limitations on yourself will only create excuses for yourself to stop doing things and be an underachiever. This life wants you to be your utmost happy self-version and to make the most of your abilities, of yourself for the world, and your own existence.       

Lastly, not taking action. Life is made up of choices and actions that we make, not just the words we utter to do. Success does not happen to people who just dream big, it happens to people who take courage, take risks, and achieve what they want to do. People need to stop dreaming of what could be and start doing what they should do to manifest the big dreams they had. 

Same with happiness, you’ll get stuck on the dark and lonely side of life if you don’t start doing something for yourself to make you happy. You can start doing small things that can make you happy like reading books, watching your favorite movie, or just going outside. 

You need to evaluate your choices and actions which you know will contribute to your well-being and happiness of yourself and try to eliminate those things that aren’t helpful. After all, this life is meant to be lived happy and full of great memories.
