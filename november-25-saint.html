4. A TV set was originally sold for ₱50,000 was given a discount of 20% last November. In December, another 25% discount was applied to its November price. What was the selling price in December?​


₱30,000 since the discount is 25%

Answer:

₱30000

Step-by-step explanation:

Since the original price is 50000 pesos, we multiply the discount to the original price to find out the discount for November,

₱50000 x 20% = ₱10000

The selling price should be ₱40000 since we subtract the original price to the discount for November.

In December, The original price is now ₱40000. We do the same thing in here except the discount is 25%

₱40000 x 25% = ₱10000

₱40000 - ₱10000 = ₱30000

The final answer is ₱30000.

Today’s Gospel: John 18:33B-37 – Solemnity of Our Lord Jesus Christ, King of the Universe

Today, this last solemnity of our liturgical year, we meet Jesus before Pilate. The King of the Universe stands before a prefect of a distant emperor. Truth stands before the culture…and the culture doesn’t like it much.

“Everyone who belongs to the truth listens to my voice,” Jesus tells us. Pilate, of course, listens not to truth but the crowd. Pilate wanted so very badly to set Jesus free, but he wanted even more for the majority culture in which he lived to approve of him. So he washed his hands and sent the King of the Universe, the Truth, to die on a cross.

On this Solemnity of Christ the King, we are surrounded by reminders that this world passes and that God, our true King, is always just, is always true, and will not change with the passing tastes of the passing crowd. The crowd leads to approval, belonging, ease — but if we’ve learned anything this year, it’s that all those things are temporary. The Truth leads to the Cross, of course, but the Cross is the established path to eternal life.

So which will we chose?

14. Find the ordinary and exact interest of an investment amounting to Php 175,000 at the rate of 6.25% from Aprril 25, 2022 to November 16, 2022.


To calculate the ordinary interest, we need to use the simple interest formula:

I = Prt

where I is the interest, P is the principal or investment amount, r is the interest rate per year as a decimal, and t is the time in years.

Using this formula, we have:

I = P * r * t

I = 175,000 * 0.0625 * (236/365) (counting the number of days from April 25, 2022 to November 16, 2022)

I = 6,087.67

Therefore, the ordinary interest on the investment is Php 6,087.67.

To calculate the exact interest, we need to use the compound interest formula:

A = P(1 + r/n)^(nt)

where A is the amount after t years, P is the principal or investment amount, r is the interest rate per year as a decimal, n is the number of times the interest is compounded per year, and t is the time in years.

A = P(1 + r)^t

A = 175,000(1 + 0.0625)^(236/365)

A = 181,049.87

The exact interest is the difference between the amount after t years and the principal P, so we have:

Exact interest = A - P

Exact interest = 181,049.87 - 175,000

Exact interest = 6,049.87

Therefore, the exact interest on the investment is Php 6,049.87.

Discounted price = base - (base × rate)
Discounted price = 70 000 - (70 000 × .20)
Discounted price = 70 000 - 14 000
Discounted price = P 56 000 - Discounted price in November.

Then...
Discounted Price = base - (base × rate)
Discounted Price = 56 000 - (56 000 × .25)
