6. Define the REVIEW PAPER and CRITIQUE PAPER


Answer:

A review paper summarizes the existing literature on a specific subject and tries draw a conclusion from it sometimes along with recommendations for further studies.

Critique papers summarize and judge the book, journal article, and artwork, among other sources. These papers include a critical evaluation of the item under reflection.

A Review Paper summarizes the current state of knowledge of the topic. It creates an understanding of the topic for the reader by discussing the findings presented in recent research papers.

A Critique Paper is sometimes called a response paper. When written by another expert in the field, a critique can also be called a peer review.
