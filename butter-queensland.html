20. 34. Heat causes material to change. How does heat changes the butter placed on the table? A. The butter solidifies C. The butter melted B. The butter turns into ice D. The butter changes to water vapor​


Heat causes material to change. How does heat changes the butter placed on the table?

→ C. The butter melted

Why does butter melt when heated?

→ Butter consist of fat molecules. The molecules in solid butter are close together and do not move past one another. When the butter is heated, the molecules begin to move and glide past one another, becoming a liquid.
