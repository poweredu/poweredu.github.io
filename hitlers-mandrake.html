1. Who is Adolf Hitler in Nazi Germany? And when Adolf Hitler was born? Answer your question...


Answer:

Adolf Hitler was a German politician and leader of the Nazi Party. He served as Chancellor of Germany from 1933 to 1945, and as dictator of Nazi Germany from 1934 to 1945. Under his leadership, the Nazi regime carried out numerous atrocities, including the Holocaust, which resulted in the genocide of approximately six million Jews.

Hitler was born on April 20, 1889, in Braunau am Inn, Austria-Hungary (now Austria). He moved to Germany in 1913 and fought in World War I as a German soldier. After the war, he became involved in politics and eventually rose to power, using propaganda, mass rallies, and his own charisma to appeal to the German people. Hitler's aggressive expansionist policies led to World War II, which resulted in the deaths of millions of people. He committed suicide on April 30, 1945, as Allied forces closed in on Berlin.

15. adolf hitler pagkatulad​


Answer:

Adolf Hitler (German: [ˈadɔlf ˈhɪtlɐ] (About this soundlisten); 20 April 1889 – 30 April 1945) was an Austrian-born German politician who was the dictator of Germany from 1933 to 1945. He rose to power as the leader of the Nazi Party,[a] becoming Chancellor in 1933 and then assuming the title of Führer und Reichskanzler in 1934.[b] During his dictatorship from 1933 to 1945, he initiated World War II in Europe by invading Poland on 1 September 1939. He was closely involved in military operations throughout the war and was central to the perpetration of the Holocaust, the genocide of about 6 million Jews and millions of other victims.

27. Who is Adolf Hitler?

Adolf Hitler, byname Der Führer (German: “The Leader”), (born April 20, 1889, Braunau am Inn, Austria—died April 30, 1945, Berlin, Germany), leader of the Nazi Party (from 1920/21) and chancellor (Kanzler) and Führer of Germany (1933–45).
