22. Science trivia about electromagnetic waves definition and types


Electromagnetic waves are made up of photons that travel through space until they interact with matter, some waves are absorbed and others are expelled. The EM encompasses all wave frequencies such as radios, visible light and x-rays.

The Seven types of EM are as follows.

1. Radio Waves : Instant Communication

They are the lowest frequency in the spectrum. Radio Waves used to carry signals to receivers which translates the signals into understandable form of communication.

2. Microwaves : Data and Heat

Second lowest waves in the EM spectrum. Microwaves carry radar, phonecalls, computer data transmission and cook dinner.

3. Infrared Waves : Invisible Heat

Lower Middle Range Frequency.

4. Visible Light Rays

Comes in the ROYGBIV - red,orange,yellow, green,blue,indigo and violet

5. Ultraviolet Waves: Energetic Light

Can cause sunburns and skin cancers to living organisms. High temperaed processes emit UV rays.

6. X-rays: Penetrating Radiation

7. Gamma Rays: Nuclear Energy
