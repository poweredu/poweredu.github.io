What's the size of short bond paper in Microsoft Word?

The actual short bond paper size in Microsoft Word is 8.5" (inches) x 11" (inches):

The actual short bond paper size  is 8.5" (inches) x 11" (inches): 8.5" (inches) is the width - equivalent to 21.59 cm. 11" (inches) is the height - equivalent to 27.94 cm.

28. What is the half size of short bond paper?


Bond paper is a durable paper that is used in printing a letterheads, stationery or different documents in a Printer. Each bond paper has different GSM, How thick or thin of bond paper you are looking for. GSM stands for Grams per Square Meter. In addition, Bond paper has also different sizes.

Here are the standard sizes of a bond paper commonly used.

A4 8.27" x 11.69"
Letter 8.5" x 11"
Legal 8.5" x 13"

The half size of a short bond paper (Letter) in vertical is 8.5" x 5.5" and 4.25" x 11" in horizontal.
