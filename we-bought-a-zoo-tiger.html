2. There are 22 lions and 24 tigers in a zoo. Express in lowest term the ratio of tigers to the total number of animals in the zoo.


Answer:

12:23

Explanation:

The question is "express in lowest term the ratio of tigers to the total number of animals in the zoo", the ratio of the tiger is 24 and the ratio of the total number of animals in the zoo is 46

(add 22 and 24 to get the answer of 46). Now, get the greatest common factor of 24 and 46, the greatest common factor of 24 and 46 is 2. Divide 24 to 2, the answer is 12, then divide 46 to 2, the answer is 23.
