1. What is the content of jose rizal's letter to his family before his execution?


Rizal penned his final letter to Ferdinand Blumentritt on December 29, 1896, which read: My dear Brother, I will be dead by the time you receive this letter. I will be shot at 7:00 a.m. tomorrow, yet I am not guilty of the crime of rebellion. I intend to die with a clear conscience.
