7. A. VICINITY MAPPING. Create your business vicinity map that is reflective of your own potential market in one’s locality or town. Consider the feasibility of the place and the 7Ps​


Answer:

Here is a sample vicinity map and analysis for a hypothetical coffee shop business:

Vicinity Map for The Coffee Corner (Draft)

[A map showing the location of the coffee shop in the center with key surrounding features like main road, residential areas, office buildings, park, etc. within a 1-2 kilometer radius]

Location Analysis:

1. Place (Location): The proposed location is in the center of a busy mixed-use neighborhood with many offices, residential apartments and a public park nearby. This is a strategic spot with a steady flow of potential customers throughout the day and week.

2. Product (Menu): The coffee shop will serve premium coffee, tea, snacks and light meals catering to office workers, residents and students. The menu offers good value at a slightly premium price point.

3. Price (Pricing Strategy): Prices will be comparable to similar specialty coffee shops. Loyal customers and students will be offered discounts and loyalty programs.

4. Promotion (Advertising and Marketing): Word-of-mouth, social media, flyers, loyalty program, special promotions, sponsoring local events are some ways to promote the new coffee shop.

5. People (Staffing): Friendly and well-trained staff and baristas are key to delivering great customer experience. Competitive salaries and training will be offered to attract good talent.

6. Process (Operations): High quality ingredients, efficient barista station set-up and streamlined processes are required to minimize waiting times and ensure consistent quality and taste.

7. Physical Evidence (Interior Design): An inviting space with comfortable seating, soothing music, aromas of freshly brewed coffee and stylish decor creates an enjoyable atmosphere for customers.

In summary, the proposed location in a mixed-use neighborhood has good potential for a specialty coffee shop. The 7Ps analysis also shows the critical elements required for setting up a successful coffee shop. The next steps would be to develop a detailed business plan and financial projections.
