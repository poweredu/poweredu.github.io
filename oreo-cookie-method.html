Three Types of Plate Boundaries Created by Plate movement:

Transform Fault Boundary -  Two plates are sliding past each other. Divergent Plate Boundary -  Two plates are moving away from each other. Convergent Plate Boundary -   Two plates are moving towards each other.

Expected Geological Event to Occur  in Transform fault boundary:

Earthquake

Expected Geological Events to Occur  in Divergent plate boundary:

Earthquake Rift Valley Mid-ocean Ridge Seafloor spreading Volcanic activity in the form of fissure eruptions

Expected Geological Events to Occur  in Convergent plate boundary:

Earthquake Trench Volcanic arc ( volcanoes ) Subduction process Tsunami Mountain ranges
