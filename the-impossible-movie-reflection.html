4. can you help me to make introduction and body paragraph of mission impossible movieplss for my assignment in English ​


Answer:

Introduction:

Released in 1996, "Mission: Impossible" is an action thriller film directed by Brian De Palma and starring Tom Cruise. The movie is based on the television series of the same name, which aired from 1966 to 1973. "Mission: Impossible" follows the story of Ethan Hunt, a top-secret agent who must uncover a mole within his organization and clear his name after being falsely accused of betraying his team.

Body Paragraph:

The movie begins with a thrilling sequence in which Hunt's entire team is killed during a mission to recover a stolen list of undercover agents. Hunt is the only survivor and is accused of being the mole responsible for the team's demise. With no one to turn to, Hunt is forced to go rogue and clear his name. He is aided by a small team of allies, including hacker Luther Stickell (played by Ving Rhames) and fellow agent Claire Phelps (played by Emmanuelle Béart). The plot thickens as Hunt discovers that the true mastermind behind the mission is a former agent named Jim Phelps (played by Jon Voight), who he had believed to be dead. As Hunt races against time to stop a deadly plot, he must also confront his own loyalty and the consequences of his actions. The movie is full of high-stakes action, suspenseful twists and turns, and unforgettable moments, such as the iconic scene in which Hunt dangles from a ceiling to hack into a computer. Overall, "Mission: Impossible" is a classic action movie that has stood the test of time and continues to thrill audiences with its daring stunts and intricate plot.

13. write your reflection about the short film impossible dream​


A Reflection on the short film ‘The Impossible Dream”

The Impossible Dream is a short animated film from 1983. Its main purpose is to show the problem every woman is facing, which is the tiring job of being a housewife, who does all the house chores and a full-time job.

In the short film, it was clearly shown that there was inequality between men and women. Men will always have more privilege over women in almost all aspects of life. The short film depicted the situation of women in growth in their career was very hard to achieve since the obligation of managing the house only falls on the women. A woman is expected to be a wife and mother which makes it hard for them to establish their own careers. Men, however, are not expected of these which is why they can focus on their careers and the reason why most professions are dominated by men.

The short film showed this issue from a comedic point of view, and yet, we must not laugh at this. Despite being made back in 1983, this is still relevant in modern society. Women of today are still experiencing this. This is something we must solve as a society. Women are just as capable of doing successfully in their respective chosen careers as men.
