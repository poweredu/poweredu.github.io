1. explain experiential intimacy and example​


Experiential Intimacy: When people bond during leisure activities. People may “sync up” their actions in teamwork or find themselves acting in unison. Example: A father and son work together to build a model train, developing a rhythm to their teamwork.


2. what is experiential learning


Experiential Learning is learning through a first-hand experience. Some examples of experiential learning is going on field trips, conducting experiments or doing a project.


3. Why is in experiential learning, there are opportunities for creativity ​


Answer:

Students have the opportunity to be more creative

Experiential learning is one of the best ways to teach creative problem-solving. With real-world content, children learn that there are multiple solutions to challenges, and they are encouraged to seek their unique solution to hands-on task


4. Give me an example of real or experiential knowledge.​


Answer:

One example of experiential learning is going to the zoo and learning through observation and interaction with the zoo environment, as opposed to reading about animals from a book. Thus, one makes discoveries and experiments with knowledge firsthand, instead of hearing or reading about others' experiences.
