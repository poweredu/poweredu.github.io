10. pagkakaiba ni Carlos Garcia at Quirino​


Ipagpalagay ko na ang tinutukoy mo ay sina Carlos P. Garcia at Elpidio Quirino, na parehong dating pangulo ng Pilipinas. Narito ang ilan sa mga pagkakaiba sa pagitan nila:

- Si Carlos P. Garcia ay ipinanganak noong Nobyembre 4, 1896 sa Talibon, Bohol¹, habang si Elpidio Quirino ay ipinanganak noong Nobyembre 16, 1890 sa Vigan, Ilocos Sur.

- Si Carlos P. Garcia ay isang abogado sa pamamagitan ng propesyon at isang makata at orator sa pamamagitan ng passion¹. Naglingkod din siya bilang pinunong gerilya noong Ikalawang Digmaang Pandaigdig¹. Si Elpidio Quirino ay isa ring abogado at politiko na humawak ng iba't ibang posisyon sa gobyerno bago naging pangulo. Nakaligtas din siya sa pananakop ng mga Hapones at nawalan ng asawa at tatlong anak noong digmaan.

- Naging pangulo si Carlos P. Garcia pagkatapos ng pagkamatay ni Ramon Magsaysay sa isang pagbagsak ng eroplano noong Marso 17, 1957¹. Nanalo siya sa halalan sa pagkapangulo noong taong iyon laban kay Jose Yulo kasama si Diosdado Macapagal bilang kanyang running mate¹. Naging pangulo si Elpidio Quirino pagkatapos ng pagkamatay ni Manuel Roxas dahil sa atake sa puso noong Abril 15, 1948. Pagkatapos ay nanalo siya sa halalan sa pagkapangulo noong 1949 laban kay Jose Laurel kasama si Fernando Lopez bilang kanyang running mate.

30. What are the contributions of Levi Celerio and Carlos Quirino to the development of National Literature?​


Answer:

Levi Celerio was a Filipino composer and lyricist who is credited with writing over 4,000 songs. Celerio was recognized as a National Artist of the Philippines for Music and Literature .He is also known for using the leaf as a musical instrument which led to being recognized as the "only man who could play music using a leaf" by the Guinness Book of Records[citation needed]. This led to him making guest appearance in television shows recorded outside the Philippines. Aside from being a musician, Celerio was also a poet and a film actor who appeared in various Philippine films of the 1950s and 1960s.

Carlos Quirino is a nephew of Philippine president Elpidio Quirino. He is a famous filipino. He is best known for his early biography of Jose Rizal. He also wrote several works the Philippine history and biographies of President Manuel Quezon and the painter Damian Domingo. In 1997 he was recognised as a National Artist of the Philippines for Historical Literature.
