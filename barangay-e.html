15. An e-jeep travels at an average speed of 48 km per hour around barangay bulua road write an equation to predict how many hours it will take to travel 449 km on barangay bulua road if it continues at this speed


Answer:

formula: time = distance / speed

48 km/h = 48/60 km/min = 0.8 km/min

Now we can substitute the given values into the formula:

time = distance / speed

time = 449 km / 0.8 km/min

time ≈ 561.25 min

Therefore, it will take approximately 561.25 minutes, or 9 hours and 21 minutes (since 1 hour is equal to 60 minutes), to travel 449 km at an average speed of 48 km/h on Barangay Bulua road.

Answer:
