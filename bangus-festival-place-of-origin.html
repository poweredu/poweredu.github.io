9. what is bangus festival?


Answer:

The Bangus Festival is a ten-day celebration taking place in Dagupan City every April. The festivities include an array of sports competitions, fairs, and street parties. And of course, the festival is full of culinary demonstrations, tastings, and contests.


10. bangus festival of dagupan​


Answer:

The Bangus Festival is an annual celebration in Dagupan to promote the city's milkfish aquaculture industry. The celebrations typically go on for 10 days and involve a mixture of sports events, trade fairs, and street parties. The milkfish is called bangús in the Philippines, and it is the national fish.
