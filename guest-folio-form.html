8. What front office document contains the personal and financial information of guests that would help in the development of the hotel’s marketing strategies? Select one: a. Folio Tray b. Guest History Record c. Account Posting Document d. Feedback Form


Answer:

b. Guest History Record

The Guest History Record is a front office document that contains the personal and financial information of guests, such as their name, address, email, phone number, payment method, length of stay, and room preferences. This document is essential for developing the hotel's marketing strategies because it provides insights into the guest's behavior, needs, and preferences. By analyzing the guest history record, hotels can identify trends, patterns, and opportunities to create targeted marketing campaigns and personalized experiences that enhance the guest's satisfaction and loyalty. The folio tray is a storage unit for guest folios, the account posting document records financial transactions for the guest, and feedback forms collect guest feedback and suggestions.

18. Why are guest folio important?


Answer:

Guest Folio – an account associated with a single hotel guest. All transactions between the hotel and the guest are recorded into the guest folio. All single guest information is also saved into the guest folio. Master Folio – is a collection of sub-folios and can be associated with a single guest or group of guests.

Guest Folio is considered as the master bill in the hotel which is also known as Guest Account Card. A guest folio contain all transactions of both cash and credit occurred by each resident guests. In manual system Folios are maintained manually and kept in the folio rack at the front office cash.
