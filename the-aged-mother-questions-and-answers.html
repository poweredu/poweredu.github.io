What is the difference between Algebraic Expression and Algebraic Equation ?

The difference is that expressions do not include an equal sign and are basically mathematical “phrases.” Equations include an equal sign, and show equivalency between two mathematical expressions. For example, 2x + b is an algebraic expression, but 2x + b = 14 is an algebraic equation.

How will an expression become an equation?

An expression is a number, a variable, or a combination of numbers and variables and operation symbols. An equation is made up of two expressions connected by an equal sign.
