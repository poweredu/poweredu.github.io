22. If 10 housekeepers can handle 15 guests, how many housekeepers are need to handle 330 guests? PA HELP PO PLEASE solution​


RATIOS (Proportion)

Question

If 10 housekeepers can handle 15 guests, how many housekeepers are need to handle 330 guests?

Solve

10 : 15 = N : 330

Identify the means and extremes

Means: 15 and N

Extremes: 10 and 330

Multiply them

15 × N = 15N

10 × 330 = 3,300

Divide 15N and 3,300

3,300 ÷ 15 = 220

Answer:

So therefore, 220 housekeepers are need to handle 330 guests.
