Answer:

There is a need to compare and align one's PEC to that of a successful entrepreneur because doing so help ensure success in one chosen career. PEC refers to Personal Entrepreneurial Competencies, which are the important characteristics that one has to possess in order to be a successful entrepreneur.

PECs is short for Personal Entrepreneurial Competencies.

It is defined as those competencies or personal attributes that an entrepreneur must have in order to be successful. The greater the measure of these attributes displayed in a person, the more likely the person is to succeed as an entrepreneur.

These attributes reveal the probability of success that a person can have as an entrepreneur.

Knowing these factors and evaluating oneself, or a potential candidate or the key leader of a start-up against them can provide valuable insights into the future of the enterprise proposed to be led by the aspiring entrepreneur.

If a person with great ideas for a startup for instance is looking for funding from an investor, a savvy investment analyst while analyzing the competencies of the leadership of the organization will be able to make a well-informed decision.

An aspiring entrepreneur can also leverage information provided by this analysis to determine whether or not they should become an entrepreneur.

The key attributes considered during a PEC evaluation are:

Disposition to Seek OpportunitiesPersistence and GritCommitment to Execution of a Project/ContractAffinity for RiskAffinity for Efficiency and QualityDisposition towards Goal SettingAbility to Learn New things quicklyPlanning and Monitoring executionAbility to Persuade and Network with peopleSelf Confidence in one's abilities

Answer:Ignatius of Antioch-also known as Ignatius Theophorus (Ἰγνάτιος ὁ Θεοφόρος, Ignátios ho Theophóros, lit. "the God-bearing"), was an early Christian writer and Patriarch of Antioch. While en route to Rome, where he met his martyrdom, Ignatius wrote a series of letters. This correspondence now forms a central part of a later collection of works known to be authored by the Apostolic Fathers. He is considered to be one of the three most important of these, together with Clement of Rome and Polycarp. His letters also serve as an example of early Christian theology. Important topics they address include ecclesiology, the sacraments, and the role of bishops.Explanation:
