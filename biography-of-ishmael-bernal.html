Ang buod sa kwento ng Taguan

Ang kwento Taguan ay tungkol sa karanasan ng dalawang magkapatid na sina Openg at Kristal. Sa simula ng kwento, naglalaro ng taguan ang dalawang magkapatid na sina Openg at Kristal. Sa mga panaginip ni Openg inilahad ang mga karanasan nilang magkapatid sa buhay. Sa unang bahagi ng kwento ipinahayag ang karanasan ni Openg sa kanyang ama, ina at kapatid, habang sa bandang katapusan ng kwento naman inilahad ang kwento ng karanasan ni Kristal. Sa katapusan ng kwento muli naglaro ng taguan ang magkapatid.

25. compare ishmael and isaac's attitude from the Bible​


Answer:

Ishmael and Isaac have different attitudes in the Bible, reflecting their different experiences and perspectives.

Ishmael is depicted as a rebellious, independent spirit. In Genesis 16, when his mother Hagar is sent away by Sarah, he is described as "laughing" at the idea of his unborn sibling becoming a great nation. This could indicate a sense of cynicism or disbelief in the promise given to him and his mother. Later in the book of Genesis, he is portrayed as a rival to Isaac, as his descendants engage in conflict with the Israelites.

Isaac, on the other hand, is depicted as a quiet, obedient, and trusting figure. He is the son of promise, the one through whom God's covenant with Abraham will be fulfilled. In Genesis 22, when God asks Abraham to sacrifice Isaac, Isaac submits without question or complaint, showing his faith in God's will. Throughout the rest of the Bible, Isaac is not mentioned often and does not have the same level of character development as his father and brother.

In conclusion, Ishmael is characterized by his independence and skepticism, while Isaac is characterized by his obedience and faith in God.
