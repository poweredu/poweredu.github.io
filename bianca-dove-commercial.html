Customers were furious, and Twitter users blasted the business for failing to show cultural sensitivity. H&M started a campaign to restore the brand's reputation and express regret in the wake of the catastrophe. In order to prevent a repeat of this incident, I believe it is appropriate for all businesses to practice cultural awareness while creating advertisements. 10. 10 years ago, Jane was 4 times as old as Bianca. Now she is twice as old as Bianca. Find their present ages. Show your solution.


Age Problem:

Bianca is 15 years old while Jane is 30 years old.

Solutions:

Given that Jane is 4 times as old as Bianca, represent their ages.

x - Bianca's age

4x - Jane's age

Since this is a representation of their ages 10 years ago then,

4x + 10 = 2(x + 10)

Solve for x.

4x + 10 = 2(x + 10)

4x + 10 = 2x + 20

4x - 2x = 20 - 10

2x = 10

2x/2 = 10/2

x = 5

Therefore, Bianca is only 5 years old 10 years ago.

Find Jane's age using 4x.

4(5) = 20

Thus, Jane's age 10 years ago is 20.

Find their current ages.

x + 10 - Bianca's age

5 + 10 = 15

Therefore, Bianca is now 15 years old.

Find Jane's age.

4x + 10 = 4(5) + 10 = 20 + 10 = 30

Thus, Jane is now 30 years old.
