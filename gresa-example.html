2. How to solve Gresa method Examples of fiding the mass.


#BrrainlyFast

Sample problem:

a dancer lifts his partner above his head with an acceleration of 2.5 m/s². the dancer exerts a force a 200 Newton. what is the mass of the partner?

Note:

The formula for Force is  ( F = m × a ), and to solve for mass we can have the formula ( m = F / a )

Given Data:

a ( acceleration ) = 2.5 m/s²

F ( Force ) = 200 N or 200 kg·m/s²

m ( mass ) = ? unknown

Solution:

We can use the formula of Force ( F = m × a ), and to solve for mass we can have the formula ( m = F / a )

m = F / a

 = 200 kg·m/s² / 2.5 m/s²

 = 80 kg

Answer:

the mass of the partner is equal to 80 kg

width = 3 cm

height = 5 cm

R - volume

S - 9 cm x 3 cm x 5 cm = 135 cm3

26. How to use gresa method


GRESA Method

GRESA method serves as a guide in answering word problems. It helps in analyzing and uderstanding word problems easily. Below is the meaning of each letter in GRESA:

Given - It refers to the important facts in the problem.Required - It is the question being asked.Equation - It is the number sentence.Solution - It shows how you solved for the answer.Answer - It is the complete answer of the word problem.

Word Problem Using GRESA Method

Let us have an example of word problem using GRESA method to understand it more.

Vexana had 12 stamps. Her mother bought more 10 stamps and gave them to her. How many stamps does Vexana have in all?

Given: 12 stamps and 10 stampsRequired: The total number of stamps that Vexana has.Equation: 12 + 10 = NSolution: 12 + 10 = 22Answer: Vexana has 22 stamps in all.
