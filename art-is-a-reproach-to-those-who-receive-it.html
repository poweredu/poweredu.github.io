24. 2. What does the line, "Art is a reproach to those who receive it" mean? Should art be reproach? Should we relateart in our daily life? Explain.​


Explanation:

Art is one of the most creative, expressive form of ideas and insights. Most people find art as a means where they can collaborate and create distinct and unique artworks as their form of expression.

Benefits of Art as an Expression

Since we express through the use of art, here are some benefits we can gain from it:

Art is used for treatment. Visual arts are commonly used for therapeutic purposes. Seeing beautiful artworks or creating one can relax the mind. When our mind is relaxed, our natural body processes can flow naturally, without stress.

Since art is the alternative expression of ideas for some, it has been the best method, so far, of expressing, abstract and complicated emotions and insights. Those who have low self esteem have difficulties in stating their opinions verbally so they use art to express themselves.

What is reproach

Reproach is a statement of disapproval or disagreement on wrong actions. It is used to correct ideas which came from different philosophies, point of views, personal stand on current issues, and more.

In the statement, "Art is a reproach to those who receive it", it means that the meaning of the artwork and the idea that it wants to portray depends on how the views will look and interpret it. Art will always be in neutral position on things, events, issues, and concerns, not unless the creator really intended the artwork to be a direct expression of divergence to a person, group, or distinct personality. Some may see it as an expression of idea but those who have opposing concepts with the creator might have a different interpretation in the same artwork. You may be seeing something good about the drawing but others are taking it negatively, assuming that it has a double meaning.
