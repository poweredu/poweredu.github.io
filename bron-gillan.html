2. what is the meaning of bron chioles​


Answer:

Bronchiole: The tiny branch of air tubes within the lungs that is a continuation of the bronchus.

Answer:

Bronchiole: The tiny branch of air tubes within the lungs that is a continuation of the bronchus. The bronchioles connect to the alveoli (air sacs).

Explanation:

Inside your lungs, tubes split again and again, like the branches of a tree. The smallest of those branches are called bronchioles, and they end in tiny air sacs called alveoli. The alveoli are where the oxygen is picked up by your blood.
