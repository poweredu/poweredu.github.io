Title: The Last Goodbye

Explanation:

Title: The Last Goodbye

Characters:

Ethan, a young man in his 20s

Mia, Ethan's ex-girlfriend in her 20s

Kevin, Ethan's best friend in his 20s

Setting: A park bench overlooking a lake in the late afternoon

Act 1:

(Ethan is sitting alone on a park bench, staring out at the lake. After a few moments, Mia enters and sits down next to him.)

Mia: Hi.

Ethan: (startled) Hi. What are you doing here?

Mia: I wanted to talk to you.

Ethan: (sighs) Okay.

Mia: (pauses) Look, I know things ended badly between us. I just wanted to say I'm sorry.

Ethan: (surprised) Sorry?

Mia: Yes. I know I hurt you and I shouldn't have. I was immature and selfish. I just hope you can forgive me.

(Ethan looks at her for a long moment, then nods slowly.)

Ethan: I forgive you.

Mia: (smiles, relieved) Thank you.

Ethan: (pauses) So, why did you really come here?

Mia: (looks down) I heard about your diagnosis. I'm so sorry.

Ethan: (nods) Yeah. It's not good.

Mia: (hesitates) Is there anything I can do?

Ethan: (smiles weakly) Actually, there is. I want you to help me say goodbye.

Mia: (confused) Say goodbye?

Ethan: Yes. I don't have much time left, and I want to make the most of it. I want to do all the things I never got a chance to do, see all the places I never got a chance to see. And I want you to be there with me.

Mia: (tearfully) Of course I will.

(Ethan takes out a notebook and starts writing.)

Ethan: (voiceover) And so we did. We traveled the world together, saw the Northern Lights, visited the Great Wall of China, and swam with dolphins in Hawaii. We laughed, we cried, we said our goodbyes. And in the end, I knew I had lived a full life, with no regrets.

(Kevin enters and sees Mia crying. He approaches them.)

Kevin: Hey, what's going on?

Mia: (wiping her tears) Ethan just said goodbye.

Kevin: (shocked) What? Why?

Mia: (smiling through her tears) Because he wanted to. And I'm grateful I got to be a part of it.

(Kevin looks at Ethan, who is sitting peacefully on the bench, and nods.)

Kevin: (voiceover) And so we sat there, the three of us, watching the sunset over the lake. And as the last rays of light disappeared, we said our final goodbyes to our dear friend Ethan. But even as we mourned his passing, we knew that he had lived his life to the fullest, and that his memory would live on forever.
