12. define tools equipment and paraphernalia ​


A tool is an device or implement, especially one held in the hand, used to carry out a particular function. Object that can extend an individual's ability to modify features of the surrounding environment.

Equipment most commonly refers to a set of tools or other objects commonly used to achieve a particular objective

Paraphernalia most commonly refers to a group of apparatus, equipment, or furnishing used for a particular activity.
