16. A loan worth $1000 collects simple interest each year for 6 years. At the end of that time, a total of $1270 is paid back. Determine the rate for this loan. * 2 puntos​


RATE

A loan worth [tex]\$ 1 000[/tex] collects simple interest each year for 6 years. At the end of that time, a total of [tex]\$ 1 270[/tex] is paid back. Determine the rate for this loan.

Answer:

[tex]\large\green{\boxed{\purple{\sf{rate=0.045 \ or \ 4.5 \%}}}}[/tex]

Step-by-step explanation:

In order to determine the rate, we have to first solve for the interest because we need it to solve for the rate.

[tex]\sf{I=MV-P}\\\sf{I=1,270-1,000}\\ \sf{I=270}[/tex]

Therefore, the interest is equal to $270.

Now, we can proceed in solving for the rate. First, write the givens that are needed to determine the rate.

Given:

Interest = $270Principal = $1,000term/time = 6 years

Second, derive a formula from the mother formula which is I=Prt and then apply the substitution method.

[tex]\sf{I=Prt}\\ \sf{\dfrac{I}{Pt}=\dfrac{\cancel{P}r\cancel{t}}{\cancel{P}\cancel{t}}}\\\sf{r=\dfrac{I}{Pt}}\\\\ \sf{r=\dfrac{I}{Pt}}\\\sf{r=\dfrac{270}{(1 \ 000)(6)}}[/tex]

Then, solve it.

[tex] \sf{r=\dfrac{270}{(1 \ 000)(6)}}\\ \sf{r= \dfrac{270}{ 6\ 000}}\\ \sf{r=0.045 \ or \ 4.5 \%} [/tex]

Therefore, the rate is equal to [tex]\blue{\boxed{\green{\sf{0.045 \ or \ 4.5 \% }}}}[/tex].
