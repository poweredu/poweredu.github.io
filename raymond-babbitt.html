7. Summary for Raymond's Run. What's the theme of the story.


Answer:

"Raymond's Run" is a short story written by Toni Cade Bambara.

The story is about a young African American girl named Hazel Elizabeth Deborah Parker, who is known as Squeaky, and her younger brother Raymond. Squeaky is a talented runner and takes pride in her running skills, she trains hard and is determined to win the annual May Day race.

The theme of the story is about self-discovery and self-acceptance. Squeaky learns to value her own abilities and skills, and comes to appreciate the talents of others. She learns to see beyond appearances and to understand the value of hard work and dedication. Squeaky also learns to accept her younger brother for who he is, and to value him for his unique qualities and abilities.

30. Raymond is 6 years older than his sister. Four years from now Raymond will be twice as old as his sister. Find their present age


Present ages:

Raymond's sister: x
Raymond: x + 6

Ages in 4 years:
Raymond's sister: (x + 4)
Raymond: x + 6 + 4 or (x + 10)

x + 10 = 2 (x+4)
x + 10 = 2x + 8
2x - x = 10 - 8
x = 2

Present ages, x = 2:

Raymond's sister: x = 2 years old
Raymond: (2) + 6 = 8 years old

ANSWER:  Raymond is 8 years old while his sister is 2 years old.
