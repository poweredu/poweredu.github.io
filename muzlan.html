15. cultural background of the mulan movie​


Answer:

Chinese

Explanation:

Mulan is based on a Chinese folk story originating from the Ballad of Mulan, which was created during the Northern Wei Dynasty (386–534). The real story of Mulan was similar to the Disney version, telling of a brave daughter who replaced her aging father to fight in the army and served valiantly for her country.

19. what is the complication of mulan​


Answer:

Inciting Event: Mulan fails at the matchmaker.

First Plot Point: Mulan takes her father’s place in the army.

First Pinch Point: The Huns send a challenge to the emperor’s army.

Midpoint: Mulan retrieves the arrow from the top of the post, proving herself to the soldiers.

Second Pinch Point: The army is attacked in the pass.

Third Plot Point: Mulan is discovered to be a woman and cast out of the army.

Climax: The Huns kidnap the emperor.

Climactic Moment: Mulan kills the Hun leader.

Resolution: Mulan returns to her family with honor.

29. what is the theme of mulan?


Answer:

Man vs. Environment, or Mulan vs. the cultural standards, is a major, and arguably, the main theme of the movie. Throughout the story, Mulan finds she is unable to do what she feels is right because of the way women are viewed in Chinese society.
