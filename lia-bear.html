Problem: Lia is six years older than Irene. Two years ago, Lia's age was twice as old as Irene's age was. How old is Lia?​

Representation:

1. Lia

2. Irene

Present age:

1. x

2. y

Past age (two years ago):

1. x - 2

2. y - 2

Equation:

x = y + 6

x - 2 = 2(y - 2)

x - 2 = 2y - 4

Solution: SUBSTITUTION

x - 2y = -2

y + 6 - 2y = -2

-y = -2 - 6

-1 [-y = -8] -1

y = 8

x = y + 6

x = 8 + 6

x = 14

Answer:

Lia is 14 years old.

25. Lia is eight years younger than Kath. Find their ages if the sum is 92. ​


Answer:

Lia-42

kath-50

hope it helps

Problem:

Lia is eight years younger than Kath. Find their ages if the sum is 92.

Solution:

Let x be the Kath's age

Then let x + 8 be the Lia's age

In representation:

Find their ages if the sum is 92.

(Kath's age) + (Lia's age) = 81x + (x + 8) = 92

Solve the value of x which is the Kath's age to the problem equations.

x + (x + 8) = 922x + 8 = 92 2x = 92 - 82x = 84/2x = 42

Substitute the Lia's age to the present age which is the value of x to the solving the problem equation.

Lia's age = x + 8Lia's age = (42) + 8Lia's age = 50

Checking:

Checking the age's if the answer is correct.

(Kath's age) + (Lia's age) = 81x + (x + 8) = 92(42) + (42 + 8) = 9242 + 50 = 9292 = 92 ✔

Answer:

∴ Therefore, the present age of Kath is 42 yrs old and Lia is 50 yrs old.
