4. What is Job Mismatch?


Job mismatch means that the employee or job applicant's skills, level of education, or domain does not match with the requirements of the job at all. It is also possible that the person is not fit for the workplace.

This happens when there is a shortage of available jobs that will fit the person's qualifications.

For example, during the last decade, the Philippines had a surplus of nursing graduates. Most were not able to go abroad to find better opportunities, some were not able to pass the board exam, while others have a hard time finding permanent nursing jobs that would pay them. What eventually happened is that these nurses, who had the knowledge and the skill needed for the nursing profession, took off and changed fields by going into the call center industry where.

Another is when a person decides to leave his current job due to sickness or change of residence Let's say this person is a senior architect in Italy but needed to move back to the Philippines to be closer to his family. He comes back to his province but could not find a job that calls for the same skills and responsibilities his old position offers. He might settle for being a junior architect or a CAD operator, instead.

A common scenario which traps OFWs under this situation is the lack of global recognition that their college diploma holds. Take an OFW who is an accountant and graduated from a university in the Philippines. He may move to the United States and may be able to secure a job as a bookkeeper. Over time, he should have been able to get several promotions due to the skills he have picked up from his hands-on work but the company may be held back from promoting him because the university he graduated from is not internationally-accredited.

Another reason for job mismatch could be the person himself. There may be several reasons why he is holding himself back. It may be possible that due to having low self-esteem of confidence, he does not proactively express his desire for a promotion.

It is also possible that health or other personal reasons are holding him back. Maybe he is suited to be a manager out in the field but chose to be a rank-and-file employee confined within the office because his health will be affected.

18. Konklusyon sa Job Mismatch


Answer:

Skills upgrade and solusyon

Explanation:

Ang job mismatch ay nangyayari kung ang mga skills na hinahanap ng isang posisyon ay hindi magampanan ng isang aplikante. Nahihirapan ang mga kumpanya na punan ang isang job opening dahil walang aplikante na may taglay ng mga kakayanan na hinahanap nila. Sa isang banda, pwede din itong tingnan na kakulangan sa mga skills ng isang trabahante.

Iba ito sa usapin ng job availability. Kung walang trabaho wala din mapag-aaplayan ang isang naghahanap ng trabaho. Ngunit ang sitwasyon ay  mayroong trahabo na pwedeng aplayan, wala nga lang nararapat na kakayanan ang mga tao upang gampanan ang nasabing trabaho.

Upang mapunan ito, kailangan iangat ang kakayanan ng mga trabahador. Isang halimbawa nito ay sa mga call center. Kadalasan, kulang sa kaalaman sa pakikipagkomunika sa wikang Ingles ang mga aplikante. Kaya ang ginawa ng mga call center, nagkaroon sila ng tinatawag na near hire training kung saan ang mga halos pumasa na ay sasailalim sa training upang maipasa nila ang mga interview at eksamin.

Maraming hakbang din na pwedeng gawin ang isang tao para magkaroon sya ng mga skills na naayon sa kailangan ng industriya ngayon. Maraming mga online courses na pwedeng kunin upang magkaroon pa ng mas malalim na pag unawa sa mga skills kagaya ng coding, graphic design at web development, mga trabahong popular sa ngayon.
