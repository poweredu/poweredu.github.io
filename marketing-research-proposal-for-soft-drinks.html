25. 7. Abby bought 5 liters of soft drinks. She poured the drink equally into 3 bottles, There were 0.35 liters of soft drinks left. What was the volume of soft drinks in 1 bottle?


Answer:

1.55

Solution:

Let x be the volume of soft drink she poured in the bottle

5 - 3x = 0.35

5 - 0.35 = 3x

4.65 = 3x

[tex]x = \frac{4.65}{3}[/tex] = 1.55
