3. reflection on the movie fight club by chuck palahniuk ​


Answer: The film explores how people with certain psychological conditions or disorders behave and handle their needs. The unnamed narrator cannot sleep due to insomnia and feels detached. Still, his doctor dismisses him and tells him he can see real suffering if he goes to group therapy for men with testicular cancer. The film also explores the subconscious mind, which is often hidden under the surface of the conscious mind and is invaded by Marla Singer, first as the penguin's voice and then as herself. The narrator's subconscious is found with things or people that the narrator does not understand.

The most critical details in Fight Club are the narrator's dissociative identity disorder (DID) and the disenfranchisement and emasculation of men. Fincher's depiction of DID in the film is subtle, as the narrator and Tyler are rarely seen together when other people are around and only interact when alone. The film depicts men's complex and often overlooked mental health, as his physician dismissed the symptoms of his mental health. Discussions on toxic masculinity emphasize this society's refusal to allow men to be emotional and weak, leading to mental health problems not being treated and managed. Recent mass shootings in the US reveal that the actions of these mass shooters are not too different from Project Mayhem.

12. If one card is drawn from an ordinary deck of 52playing cards, what is the probability that it willbe either a club or a face card?​


Answer:

25/52 or there will be 25 out 52 probabilities that either a club or a face club will be drawn.

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

Total number of playing cards: 52

Total number of club in a deck of playing cards: 13

Total number of face card in a deck of playing cards: 12

Solution:

To find the number of probability that either a face card or a club would appear we need to find out first the probability of the occurence of each event:

The formula for probability is given by this: brainly.ph/question/2068003

for Club card:

Since there are 13 club cards in a deck of 52 cards, it means that there are 13 possible occurrences that a club card can be drawn from 52 playing cards.

We will write it as the below

13  → No. of Possible events

52 → TOTAL No. of Possible events

Hence, the probability of drawing out a club card is :

13/52

for Face card:

Since there are 12 face cards in a deck of 52 cards, it means that there are 12 possible occurrences that a face card can be drawn from 52 playing cards.

We will write it as the below

12  → No. of Possible events

52 → TOTAL No. of Possible events

Hence, the probability of drawing out a club card is :

12/52

In order to find the total number probability that a club or a face card will be drawn; we will add the probabilities we got from the above:

13/ 52 + 12/52 = 25/52

Hence, the total number of probability that a club or a face club will be drawn from a deck of 52 playing cards is:

25/52
