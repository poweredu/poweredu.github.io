9. what are the nutritional requirements for children​


Answer:

healthy food

Explanation:

is most important needs for children for their good health.

Answer:

1. Protein helps a child's body build cells, break down food into energy, fight infection, and carry oxygen.

2. Carbohydrates While the latest diet trend is to "cut the carbs," carbohydrates are actually the body's most important source of energy. They help a child's body to use fat and protein for building and repairing tissue.

3. Fats

Fats are a great source of energy for kids and are easily stored in a child's body. They are also important in helping the body to properly use some of the other nutrients it needs. Foods that contain high levels of fats include: Whole-milk dairy products, Cooking oils, Meat, Fish, Nuts

4.Calcium is essential in helping to build a child's healthy bones and teeth. It's also important for blood clotting and for nerve, muscle, and heart function.

5. Iron is necessary for a child to build healthy blood that carries oxygen to cells all over the body.

6. Folate, necessary for soon-to-be moms, is also very important for kids. One of the B vitamins, folate is necessary for healthy growth and development of a child's cells.  

7. Fiber helps produce bowel regularity in a child. It can also play a role in reducing the chances of heart disease and cancer later in life.

8. Vitamin A serves a variety of purposes in kids and adults. It helps growth, assists the eyes in adjusting to dim and bright lights, keeps skin healthy, and works to prevent infection.

9. Vitamin C does more than just fighting off the common cold. It also holds the body's cells together, strengthens the walls of blood vessels, helps the body heal wounds, and is important for building strong bones and teeth.
