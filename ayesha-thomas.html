26. Kris is four years younger than Ayesha. In five years, the sum of their ages is twice the sum of their present ages. How old are they now?​


Answer:

Kris is 3 years old, while Ayesha is 7 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Required Answer:

age of Kris and age of Ayesha

Equation:

(x - 4 + 5) + (x + 5) = 2[(x - 4) + x]

Solution:

(x - 4 + 5) + (x + 5) = 2[(x - 4) + x]

=> x + 1 + x + 5 = 2(2x - 4)

=> 2x + 6 = 4x - 8

=> 4x - 2x = 6 + 8

=> 2x = 14

=> 2x/2 = 14/2

=> x = 7

Hence,

age of Kris = x - 4

                  = 7 - 4

                  = 3

age of Ayesha = x

                         = 7
