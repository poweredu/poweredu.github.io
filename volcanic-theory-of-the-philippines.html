15. how does the plate tectonics theory explain the occurrence of earthquake and volcanic eruptions​


Answer:

As plates move, they get stuck in places, and enormous amounts of energy build up. When the plates finally get unstuck and move past each other, the energy is released in the form of earthquakes. Earthquakes and volcanoes are common features along tectonic plate boundaries, making these zones geologically very active.

22. Ang Volcanic Eruption Theory ay ipinakilala ni


Volcanic Eruption Theory

The Philippine islands were produced as a result of volcanic eruptions, according to prominent geologist Bailey Willis. These volcanoes were discovered beneath the Pacific Ocean in Asia's eastern area. The Pacific Theory, often known as the Volcanic Theory, claims that these volcanoes erupted 200 million years ago. The fracturing of rocks was induced by this natural event, which was followed by the seas surrounding them.

This occurrence occurred in Japan, Taiwan, Indonesia, the Solomon Islands, and New Zealand as well. These countries are part of the Pacific Ring of Fire, as it is now known. Around the region, there are 250 volcanoes. There are 22 active volcanoes in the Philippines. It's no surprise, then, that earthquakes occur often throughout the country. 29. Tinatawag Ding Volcanic Theory ni Dr. Baily Willis​


Sinasabi ng sikat na geologist na si Bailey Willis na ang mga pagsabog ng bulkan ang siyang nagbunga ng mga isla ng Pilipinas. Ang mga bulkang ito ay natuklasan sa ilalim ng Karagatang Pasipiko malapit sa silangang Asya. Ayon sa Volcanic Theory o Pacific Theory, ang mga bulkang ito ay huling pumutok 200 milyong taon na ang nakalilipas. Nahati ang mga bato bilang resulta ng natural na pangyayaring ito, at pagkatapos ay nahati rin ang mga dagat sa kanilang paligid.

Naganap din ang hindi pangkaraniwang bagay na ito sa New Zealand, Japan, Taiwan, Indonesia, at Solomon Islands. Ang mga bansang ito ay mula sa rehiyon na kasalukuyang tinutukoy bilang Pacific Ring of Fire. Sa paligid ng lugar, may 250 bulkan ang maaaring matagpuan. Ang Pilipinas ay tahanan ng 22 aktibong bulkan. Kaya naman, hindi kataka-taka na madalas mangyari ang mga lindol sa buong bansa.

Ang teoryang Asyatiko ni Dr. Leopoldo Faustino ay nagpapahayag na ang diastrophism ay kung paano nabuo ang mga isla. Ipinapaliwanag nito kung paano gumalaw ang lupa, na nagdulot ng pagtaas o paglubog ng ilang lugar.
