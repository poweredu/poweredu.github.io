16. being a filipino, are you proud of your own cultural ?why?​


I am proud of my culture as a Filipino because of its diversity. Filipinos have many different positive parts of their culture. For example, one of the best things about being a Filipino is that thing we so often call bayanihan. This word is described in English is community spirit or simply unity. There are several times when our countrymen show how solid is the bond that we all have.

First, during hard times like typhoon seasons, many people give their helping hand to those who are in need by donating money, food, clothes, and other necessities. Even during the pandemic, many people still try to donate financially although the economy is not in a good condition.

Second, people every where are helpful. If they see an old person carrying heavy items of baggage, they will be there to immediately provide help. This does not only show the spirit ofUnity but also respect for the elderly.

Finally, you can really see the heart of Filipinos whenever a neighbor falls sick. It wouldn’t matter if it is three in the morning. Your neighbor will quickly get up and help you rush your loved one to the hospital if you asked them for help. Filipinos have a sense ofUrgency and responsibility not only for their families but also for their co-vill4gers.

There are many other valuable Filipino cultures but I believe the community spirit is the best among all for it truly shows the love, respect, and kindness in all Filipinos.
