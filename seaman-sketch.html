19. i Want To Be A Seaman? description


Answer:

Seamen

Seamen are responsible for the safe operation of commercial vessels. They work closely with other members of the crew to ensure that all cargo is properly loaded and unloaded, and they may also be tasked with operating cranes or other specialized equipment used for these tasks.

Seamen must have a thorough understanding of all aspects of ship operations, including navigation, safety procedures, communications systems, and more. They must also be able to perform well under pressure—accidents at sea can happen quickly and without warning, so seaman need to be ready to respond effectively in an instant.
