12. what is the negative impact of selling junkfoods inside the school campus?​


Answer:

Selling junk foods inside the school campus can have several negative impacts on students' health, academic performance, and overall well-being. Here are some of them:

Health problems: Junk foods are typically high in calories, sugar, salt, and unhealthy fats. Regular consumption of these foods can lead to various health problems such as obesity, diabetes, high blood pressure, and heart disease. Students who consume junk foods frequently may experience a decline in their physical health, which can affect their ability to participate in sports or other physical activities.Poor academic performance: Junk foods can also have a negative impact on academic performance. Studies have shown that consuming high amounts of sugar and unhealthy fats can lead to a decrease in cognitive function, memory, and attention span. Students who consume junk foods may have difficulty focusing in class, retaining information, and performing well on exams.Negative habits: When junk foods are readily available on campus, it can encourage students to develop unhealthy eating habits that can carry over into adulthood. Students may develop a preference for processed, unhealthy foods, and may have difficulty making healthier food choices in the future.Creating an unhealthy school environment: Selling junk foods inside the school campus can create an unhealthy school environment. Schools have a responsibility to promote healthy habits and lifestyles among their students. Allowing the sale of junk foods on campus can send a message that unhealthy habits are acceptable and can contribute to a culture that values convenience over health.

27. what kind of school campus is conducive to learning?


CHARACTERISTICS OF A SCHOOL CAMPUS THAT IS CONDUCIVE TO LEARNING

A school or a school campus as a whole is a place where learners are molded to become successful in life. This is where the learners are taught and trained with life-skills.

The school campus is an external factor that can affect the learners' grasp of learning. That is, a school campus should be conducive to learning. This means that a school campus should also help the young people effectively and efficiently grasp every bit of learning taught to them.

Here are a few of things that we can say a school campus is conducive to learning.

A school campus is conducive to learning if it is fully fenced.If it is away from the noise outside like noise from cars or from construction sites.If it has enough space for the learners to do their activities.If the school campus is non-threatening, rather should make the learners feel that they are accepted, they are not alone and nothing is to fear about.If the classrooms are well- lighted and well-ventilated and has the proper sanitation.
