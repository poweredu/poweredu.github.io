8. list down at least 5 filipino scientist and 5 foreign and its dicoverier


The 5 Proudly Pinoy Scientists are :

1.) Dr. Camilo C. Roa -> He is a medical doctor at the Philippine General Hospital. He developed a fully functioning resuscitator in 1993. This machine acts as a breathing apparatus for seriously-ill patients who suffer lung failure.

2.) Dr. Leo Gasendo -> He is a U.S inventor who came from the Philippines. He invented several things like the first windmill made of bamboo, canvas, plywood and carbon steel. The windmill has 12 sails with huge wind contact areas. These areas allow it to harvest and convert unlimited amounts of wind into cheap electricity.

3.) Benjamin S. Austria -> He is a teacher of Geology at the University of the Philippines. His work is about the utilization of non-renewable resources such as minerals and fossil fuels.

4.) Gregorio Zara -> The first to invent the alcohol-fueled airplane in 1954.

5.) Rene del Rosario Braquesa -> A biologist and an environmentalist. He worked on National Commission for Wildlife Conservation and Development in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia since 1990. He breeds turtles and takes care of the endangered species which is the sea cow or the “dugong."

5 Famous Foreign Scientists are :

1.) Archimedes -> He is a Greek Scientist. He was born in Syracuse, Sicily. He studied in Alexandria and did many discoveries in mathematics, mechanics and engineering. He discovered how lenses work. He also invented a kind of pulley and weapons such as cranes and the famous catapult.

2.) Benjamin Franklin (1706- 1790) -> He was born in Boston, America. He was a writer, publisher and a famous politician. He studied electricity and the lightning conductor. He also invented the franklin stove and lenses used for spectacles.

3.) Anders Celsius (1701- 1744) -> He was a swedish astronomer. He invented the centigrade scale for measuring temperature in 1742. He also identified 100° as the boiling point and 0° as the freezing point for water.

4.) Albert Einstein -> The most famous Scientist, which is mostly associated to the word “genius." He was the one who discovered the theory of relativity, photo-electric effect and other sorts in physics.

5.) Marie Curie -> Whose real name is Marya Sklodowska, was born in Warsaw, Poland in November 7, 1867. As a student, she was brilliant, mature with rare gift of concentration and had a dream of a Scientific career. At the age of 28, she married another famous scientist Pierre Curie. Both were awarded the Nobel Prize for Physics for their discovery of radiation which was soon used to track or detect cancers. Her work on radiation, was the cause of her death. However her dream of “easing human suffering" through her work came into reality.
