10. Triangle GOA IS inscribed in circle L.If themeasure of angle OGA = 75 and the measureof arc AG=160, find measure of arc OAmeasure of angle GOA, and measure ofangle GAO show your solution.​


Answer:

arc OA measures 150°.

m∠GOA = 80°

m∠GAO = 25°

Step-by-step explanation:

For this problem we need the following definitions and theorems.

If a shape is inscribed in a circle, all the vertices are on the circle. So points G, O and A are on circle L.A secant is a line connecting two points on the circle. In this case, All sides of the ΔGOA are secants of circle L.A circle measures 360°.The sum of the measures of the angles of a triangle is 180°.An inscribed angle on a circle is an angle whose vertex is on the circle itself.An inscribed angle can also be formed between two secant lines that intersect on the circle itself. In this case, ∠GOA is an inscribed angle between secants GO and OA. Their vertex is O.Inscribed Angle Theorem: An inscribed angle is twice the measure of its arc.

We are given that m∠OGA = 75°. The vertex of that angle is G, so via the inscribed angle theorem, arc OA is

[tex]75x2 = 150\\[/tex]

arc OA measures 150°.

We are given that arc AG = 160°. Point O is its vertext; Via the inscribed angle theorem, m∠GOA is half the measure of arc AG.

[tex]160/2 = 80[/tex]

m∠GOA = 80°

We have the measures of 2 angles of the triangle, m∠GOA = 80° and m∠OGA = 75. The total measure of the angles of a triangle is 180.

[tex]GOA + OGA + GAO = 180\\80+75+GAO = 180\\GAO = 180-80-75\\GAO = 25[/tex]

m∠GAO = 25°
