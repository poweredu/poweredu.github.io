23. What is musikong bongbong and pangkat kawayan??


Answer:

D' Original Malabon Musikong Bumbong started as a plain "Banda de Boca" (meaning "bibig "or mouth) in the 1890's. Music or sound was produced by one's voice controlled with a piece of leaf or paper placed between the lips.

founed in 1896

-mostly composed of Katipuneros

Musikong bumbong refers to the music or sound that originated from a bamboo tube, which is the direct translation of bumbong in English, that can refer to any of the wind, percussion or string instruments.

Typically, Musikang bumbong or bamboo tube music is played by a bamboo-based band. One particularly famous bamboo band is the Pangkat Kawayan or the Bamboo Group.

The group has played in many countries and are still creating music through the use of different bamboo musical instruments.

The “Pangkat Kawayan” (literally ‘Bamboo Band’) otherwise known as the “Singing Bamboo of the Philippines ” is a unique orchestra that draws music from unconventional bamboo instruments. This orchestra is composed of musically – talented students from the elementary schools of Quezon City and Manila whose ages range from 8 to 19 years.

The orchestra is managed by Victor O. Toledo, conductor and musical Director. The same group has led the orchestra through the years since September 6, 1966 when it was founded.

The group’s musical instruments, numbering more than a hundred, are made of six genera of the versatile bamboo in various sizes, shapes and designs. Included are the bamboo tube or “bumbong,” the bamboo marimba or” “talungating,” the bamboo piano or “tipangklung,” the bamboo flute or “tulali,” the bamboo knockers or “kalatok,” and the bamboo musiscal rattles, the Philippine “angklung”. Completing the bamboo assortments are the drums, cymbals gong and triangle.

26. bongbong marcos hinahangaan tagalog​


Si Bongbong Marcos ay isang kilalang politiko sa Pilipinas at kilala rin sa kanyang pamilyang Marcos na naghari sa bansa noong dekada '70. Ang kanyang mga tagasuporta ay nagbibigay ng iba't ibang dahilan kung bakit nila hinahangaan siya.

Maaaring dahil sa kanyang karanasan bilang dating senador at gobernador ng Ilocos Norte, kung saan siya ay nagpakita ng kakayahan sa pamamahala at pagpapatakbo ng lugar. Bukod dito, marami rin ang nakakakita sa kanya bilang isang lider na may malasakit sa kanyang mga kababayan, lalo na sa mga mahihirap at nangangailangan.

Mayroon din siyang mga tagasuporta dahil sa kanyang mga paninindigan sa mga isyu tulad ng pagpapalakas ng ekonomiya ng bansa, pagpapaunlad ng imprastraktura, at pagbibigay ng serbisyong pangkalusugan sa mga Pilipino. Bukod pa rito, marami rin ang nakakakita sa kanya bilang isang may kakayahang magdala ng pagbabago sa bansa.

Ngunit mayroon ding mga kritiko at may iba't ibang opinyon tungkol sa kanya, kaya't mahalaga pa rin na maging mapanuri at magkaroon ng malawak na pag-unawa sa bawat isyu at paninindigan ng kanyang mga tagasuporta at kritiko.
