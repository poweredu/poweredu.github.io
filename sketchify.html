19. Why are ability and skills to make drawingg or sketchinf essentual to every drafter


Answer

Its because a Draftsman needs to professionally present his ideas to avoid losing a client. If the client doesn't get the concept or idea or excited about the draft, chances are, there will be no more proposal or project. That means, losing a client is also losing income.

A draft or sketch is not the final plan, but a solid and understandable presentation of the concept or idea of the project. It is essential in planning to achieve what the client wants. Often, drafts would undergo several changes before the clients finally approve of it. Today, drafts are done digitally to easily remove or add changes to the plan.
