The 1998 film adaptation of the epic poem "Beowulf" featured several characters from the original work. Here are some of the main characters:

Beowulf: The hero of the story who comes to the aid of King Hrothgar and the Danes to defeat the monster Grendel and his mother.

Grendel: A monstrous creature who terrorizes the Danes by attacking Heorot, the great hall of King Hrothgar.

Grendel's mother: A powerful creature who seeks revenge for the death of her son at the hands of Beowulf.

King Hrothgar: The king of the Danes who calls upon Beowulf to help him defeat Grendel and his mother.

Wiglaf: One of Beowulf's trusted warriors who accompanies him on his quest to defeat Grendel and his mother.

Unferth: A warrior who initially challenges Beowulf's claims of bravery but later becomes one of his supporters.

Wealtheow: Hrothgar's queen who plays a minor role in the story but is an important character in the poem.

These are some of the main characters in the 1998 film adaptation of "Beowulf."
