If there are 8 girl scouts for every 5 boy scouts, this means that for every 8 + 5 = 13 scouts in total, 8 of them are girl scouts and 5 of them are boy scouts.

If there are 80 girl scouts, we can find the total number of scouts by dividing 80 by 8 and then multiplying by 13:

Total number of scouts = (80 / 8) x 13 = 130

Therefore, there are 5/13 of the total number of scouts who are boy scouts:

Number of boy scouts = (5 / 13) x 130 = 50

So there are 50 boy scouts in the school.
