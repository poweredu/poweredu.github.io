Answer:

D. Dance Suite

Explanation:

A characteristic of the Baroque form was the dance suite. Some dance suites by Bach are called partitas, although this term is also used for other collections of pieces. While the pieces in a dance suite were inspired by actual dance music, dance suites were designed for listening, not for accompanying dancers.

Muslim dance is marked by intricate hand and arm movement along with shimmering costumes.
