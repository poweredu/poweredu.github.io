18. Consider a moving grocery cart. As the person moves, it pushes the grocery cart forward; thegrocery cart pushes the person backward with an equal force. How come the cart moves forward?​


Answer:

Grocery carts have wheels, and most grocery stores have tiled floors which lessens friction between the cart's wheels and the floor. Even if the grocery cart pushes the person back with equal force, it moves since it has wheels and is on a smooth surface while the person has more friction between his/her shoes and the floor with his/her weight strengthening the friction.
