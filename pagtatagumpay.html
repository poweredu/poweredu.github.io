14. Pagtatagumpay n Dr. Jose rizal


Answer:

Ang Tagumpay ni Rizal, Tagumpay ng NasyunalismoKung sa unang paglilitis ay nagawa ng kolonyalismong Espanyol na gapiin si Rizal, matapos ang “ikalawang paglilitis” matagumpay na naibangon ang dangal at alaala ng ating pambansang bayani. Sa orihinal na panukalang inihain ni Recto at Laurel noong 3 Abril 1956, ang SB 438, at ni Gonzales noong 19 Abril, ang HB No. 5561 (“An Act to Make Noli Me Tangereand El Filibusterismo Compulsory Reading Matter In All Public and Private Colleges and Universities and For Other Purposes”), naamyendahan ito sa titulong An Act to Include in the Curricula of All Public and Private Schools, Colleges and Universities Courses on the Life, Works, and Writings of Jose Rizal, Particularly his Novel Noli Me Tangereand El Filibusterismo, Authorizing the Printing and Distribution Thereof, and for Other PurposesSa kabuuan, ang tunay na nagtagumpay sa pagpapasá ng Batas Rizal (RA 1425) ay ang diwa ng nasyunalismo na nakakintal sa imortalidad ng buhay at mga akda ni Rizal. Isang dakilang pagsisikap na nagdulot at magdudulot pa nang malaking epekto sa kaisipang pulitikal, sosyal, at kultural sa henerasyon ng mga kabataan noon at maging sa kasalukuyan. Isang pagpapatunay na hindi nalugmok sa libingan ang hangarin ni Rizal at ng mga martir ng sambayanan na nakibaka para isang malaya at demokratikong Pilipinas. Tagumpay ito ni Jose Rizal! Tagumpay ito ng mga bayani at martir ng sambayanan! Tagumpay ito ng sambayanang Pilipino!

26. Susi sa pagtatagumpay katalinuhan at kalakasan?


Parehong mahalaga ang dalawang ito upang magtagumpay sa anumang pangarap natin. Ang katalinuhan ay nauugnay sa kakayahan ng isa na malaman ang mga bagay-bagay upang higit na makapagdesisyon ng tama. Naiiwasan natin ang mga maling hakbangin o panganib kapag taglay natin ito. Gayunman, hindi laging madali ang mga bagay-bagay, kaya ang kalakasan (panloob na katangian ng pagiging matatag) ang tutulong sa isa na hindi sumuko kung sakaling may mga hadlang. Kailangan ang lakas upang bumangon mula sa mga kasawian upang magsimula ulit.

The Key to Being A Successful Person

Three characteristics that become an investment in success

Optimism

A method for creating a better future is optimism; if you don't think the future can be better, you're not going to take action to make it so.

Self-confidence

You believe in yourself. You're already one step closer to success when you have that unwavering faith in yourself.

Integrity

The wisest course of action in all you do is to be honest; honesty builds character and defines who you are.

Three skills necessary for success

Communication

Speaking, writing, and listening are all aspects of communication. Employers want to see that you can effectively and concisely express your views and ideas. Being a good listener entails making an effort to comprehend others' feelings, develop lasting connections, and settle disputes.

Problem solving

There will be difficulties in whatever job you have. You'll need to be able to weigh options, come to wise judgments, and solve challenges.

Teamwork

Working successfully and with respect for others is a must for many employment, whether it is with clients or coworkers. Employers want candidates that inspire the best in others.

Four values that contribute to success

Accountability

Being held accountable is the act of being held accountable. It's the readiness to assume accountability for one's deeds, accept responsibility for them, and transparently report the outcomes. It also entails being accountable for the assets that have been entrusted to us. Accountability enables us to concentrate, push ourselves, and persevere until we reach our goals.

Innovation

Innovation is the process of developing an idea or invention into a good or service that adds value that customers are prepared to pay for.

Passion

There are two main uses for the term "passion." The intense feeling or emotion you have when doing something you love is known as passion. The person feels so strongly about this that they will work tirelessly or at great personal cost to fulfill it. They find it incomprehensible that anyone would pay them to perform it.
