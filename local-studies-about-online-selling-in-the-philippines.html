2. scope about duration of study in online selling​


Answer:

Explanation:

The duration of study in online selling can vary depending on the individual's level of experience and knowledge. For beginners, it may take several weeks to several months to gain a basic understanding of online selling and the various platforms available.

This may include learning about product research, identifying profitable niches, understanding the basics of search engine optimization (SEO), and how to create effective product listings. It's also important to learn about marketing and advertising techniques, such as social media marketing, email marketing, and paid advertising, in order to drive traffic and sales to your online store.

Intermediate learners may want to dive deeper into topics such as dropshipping, customer service, and inventory management. This can take several months to a year to fully master, depending on the individual's commitment and dedication to learning.

For advanced learners, ongoing education and staying up-to-date with the latest trends and techniques in online selling is key. This may involve attending webinars, reading industry publications, and networking with other successful online sellers.

Ultimately, the duration of study in online selling is a continuous process that requires ongoing learning and adaptation to changes in the industry.

7. SIGNIFICANCE OF THE STUDYWhat are the benefits of your study and who will benefit from it? Topic: Online selling​


Answer:

Create an online shop

Benefits of selling online

Guide

Selling online has a number of advantages over selling by traditional methods, including:

Making savings in set-up and operational costs. You don't need to rent high street premises, pay shop assistants or answer a lot of pre-sales queries.

Reducing order processing costs - customer orders can automatically come straight into your orders database from the website.

Reaching a global audience, thereby increasing sales opportunities.

Competing with larger businesses by being able to open 24 hours a day, seven days a week.

Being able to receive payment more quickly from online transactions.

Improving your offerings using the data gathered by tracking customer purchases.

Using your online shop to showcase your products to existing customers.

Online selling will work best if you have:

well-defined products or services that can be sold without human involvement in the sales process

fixed prices for all types of potential customers

products or services that can be delivered within a predictable lead time

Many businesses can run pilot e-commerce sites without significant investment. However, creating a fully automated online shop tailored to meet your precise requirements could be expensive.

Whatever form of online shop you choose, it's important to take a strategic view. If you launch a website that disappoints your customers or is overwhelmed by traffic, you risk damaging your reputation and losing sales.
