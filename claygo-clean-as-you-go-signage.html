8. What is the importance of CLAYGO​


CLAYGO or Clean as You Go can solve our Garbage or Trash Issues.

During celebrations, travelling, or eating, we ended up gathering a lot of trash. CLAYGO or "Clean as You Go" is a common practice in some food restaurants and public places to minimize garbage, hygiene, health and safety risks. It encourages everyone to practice cleaning and be wary of the surroundings.

Proper implementation of CLAYGO can boost cleanliness and trash issues of our surroundings. Other benefits such as health and safety are just bonus. Practicing CLAYGO can show that we care to the welfare of our environment. We must not rely entirely to the sweepers to safeguard our planet. We must also take action but cleaning our own mess.

Despite CLAYGO being self-explanatory, many people do not practice this act. It has been a recurring issue that is difficult to solve. The changes in our environment really depends upon the people living in it. We must take initiative.

Waste management can be practiced by following CLAYGO "Clean as You Go".
