3. explain and differentiate of wayang kulit and wayang topeng​


Answer:

WAYANG KULIT is a traditional form of puppet-shadow play originally found in the cultures of Java, Bali, and Lombok in Indonesia. In a wayang kulit performance, the puppet figures are rear-projected on a taut linen screen with a coconut-oil light.

WAYANG TOPENG is in East Java, topeng dance is called wayang gedog and is the best known art form from East Java's Malang Regency.
