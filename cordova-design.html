1. the Cebu Cordova link expressway (cclex)​


Answer:

Cebu–Cordova Link Expressway

The Cebu–Cordova Link Expressway, also known as the Cebu–Cordova Bridge is a toll bridge expressway under construction in Metro Cebu, Philippines which will link Cebu City and Cordova.

Construction cost: ₱30 billion

Constructed by: Cebu Link Joint Venture (

Longest span: 390 m (1,280 fit)

Total length: 8,500 m (27,887 ft)

Design: Cable-stayed bridge

The construction of the Cebu-Cordova Link Expressway (CCLEX) has been a hotly debated topic among residents and stakeholders in Cebu and Cordova. While some argue that the construction of the CCLEX will bring economic benefits and improve connectivity between Cebu and Cordova, others argue that it will have negative impacts on the environment and the local community.

However, it is important to consider the facts and evidence surrounding the construction of the CCLEX. First and foremost, the CCLEX will provide an alternative route to the Mactan-Mandaue Bridge, which is currently the only major connection between Cebu and Mactan Island. This means that traffic congestion in the area is likely to be reduced, resulting in faster travel times for commuters and improved access to economic opportunities.

Moreover, the CCLEX is expected to boost tourism and other industries in Cordova and neighboring towns. With the improved accessibility and reduced travel time, tourists and investors are likely to be attracted to the area, which will lead to more job opportunities and economic growth.

In terms of the environment, the construction of the CCLEX has taken into account the potential impacts on the marine ecosystem in the area. The project team has implemented measures such as the use of eco-friendly construction materials, the establishment of a marine protection zone, and the use of dredging methods that minimize the impact on marine life.

Overall, the construction of the CCLEX connected to Cordova is a well-thought-out project that is expected to have positive impacts on the economy and connectivity of Cebu and Cordova. While there may be concerns about its environmental impact, the project team has taken measures to minimize these impacts and ensure the sustainability of the project. 10. Find the mode of the grades of Grade 7 - Cordova learners 76, 84, 87, 80, 84, 77, 90, 79, 80, 86, 77, 92, 80, 75, 84plsss answer ​


Definition

Mode is the number that appears most frequently in a set of numbers.

Application

We can determine the mode by counting the number of times each number appears in the set.

76 - 1

84 - 3

87 - 1

80 - 3

77 - 2

90 - 1

79 - 1

86 - 1

92 - 1

75 - 1

From the above, there are 2 modes which appear most frequent in the set of numbers. These are 84 and 80, both appearing 3 times.
