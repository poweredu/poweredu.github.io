2. It is the vertical mark made when the eyes stop and the film continues to move.A. FixationB. Inter fixationC. RegressionD. Return sweeps​


Question

It is the vertical mark made when the eyes stop and the film continues to move.

A. Fixation

B. Inter fixation

C. Regression

D. Return sweeps

A.Fixation

Fixation is the vertical mark made when the eyes stop, and the film continues to move.

The brief horizontal line formed by the eyes moving from one stopping point to the next is known as interfixation.

The regression is a necessary eye movement that allows the reader to see what is going on right in front of them.

Backward or right-to-left motions in the opposite direction are known as regressions.

18. Nitrogen Fixation meaning


Answer:

Nitrogen fixation is the process by which atmospheric nitrogen is converted by either a natural or an industrial means to a form of nitrogen such as ammonia. In nature, most nitrogen is harvested from the atmosphere by microorganisms to form ammonia, nitrites, and nitrates that can be used by plants.
