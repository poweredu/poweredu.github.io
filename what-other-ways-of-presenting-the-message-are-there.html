A short communication in the form of textsent or received by a portable electronic device, esp. a cell phone. A brief electronic message sent between mobile phones.

There are three purposes for a message: inform, persuade or goodwill. The first purpose is to inform where a message can be used to communicate routine, repetitive, daily tasks, instructions, codes, steps and procedures in the workplace.

3. How is the message conveyed by the text/message?

You can also convey a message or information, which means that you communicate it to someone directly or indirectly through your words or actions. A picture is a great way to convey your message quickly to an audience without them reading through a lot of text.

A target audience is the intended audience or readership of a publication, advertisement, or other message catered specifically to said intended audience.
