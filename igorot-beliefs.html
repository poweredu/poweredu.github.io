5. TYPES OF IGOROT WINE


Answer:

Tapuy, also spelled tapuey or tapey, is a rice wine produced in the Philippines. It is a traditional beverage originated from Banaue and the Mountain Province, where it is used for important occasions such as weddings, rice harvesting ceremonies, fiestas and cultural fairs. It is produced from either pure glutinous rice or a combination of glutinous and non-glutinous rice together with onuad roots, ginger extract, and a powdered starter culture locally known as bubod. Tapuy is an Ilocano name. The wine is more commonly called baya or bayah in Igorot languages.

7. ano ang hanap buhay ng mga igorotpaano naging malaya ang mga igorot sino ang nagsiyasat ng mga gintong ibinebenta ng mga igorot saan naninirahan ang tribong igorot​


Answer:

Ang mga Igorot ay isang pangkat-etniko na naninirahan sa Cordillera Region sa hilagang Luzon. Ang kanilang kabuhayan ay nakabatay sa agrikultura at pagmimina. Ang mga Igorot ay nagtatanim ng mga gulay, mais, at iba pang uri ng pananim. Bukod dito, nagmimina rin sila ng ginto, pilak, at tanso. Ang kanilang kasanayan sa pagmimina at pagtatanim ay ipinamana sa kanila ng kanilang mga ninuno.

Naging malaya ang mga Igorot nang magkaroon ng kasunduan sa pagitan ng mga Amerikano at mga Igorot noong 1907. Sa kasunduang ito, naitatag ang "Mountain Province" na binubuo ng mga lalawigan ng Benguet, Ifugao, Kalinga, at Bontoc. Sa ilalim ng kasunduang ito, naibigay sa mga Igorot ang kalayaan sa kanilang mga kultura at tradisyon. Naitatag rin dito ang mga sistema ng pamahalaan, edukasyon, at kalusugan sa kanilang mga komunidad.

Ang mga gintong ibinebenta ng mga Igorot ay nagsisilbi bilang isa sa kanilang pangunahing produkto sa pagmimina. Ang mga gintong ito ay binibili ng mga tagapaglapit ng mga kompanya ng pagmimina at mga nangangalakal. Ang Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) ay nagpapatupad ng mga patakaran sa pagmimina para maprotektahan ang kalikasan at mga karapatan ng mga katutubo.

Ang tribong Igorot ay naninirahan sa Cordillera Region sa hilagang Luzon. Ang kanilang mga komunidad ay matatagpuan sa mga kabundukan at kanilang mga kapatagan. Kahit na may mga modernong siyudad na matatagpuan sa kanilang rehiyon, ang mga Igorot ay nananatiling nakapagpapalaga sa kanilang mga tradisyon at kultura.


8. difinition of Igorot​


Answer:

IGOROT-Igorot, (Tagalog: “Mountaineer”) any of various ethnic groups in the mountains of northern Luzon, Philippines, all of whom keep, or have kept until recently, their traditional religion and way of life. Some live in the tropical forests of the foothills, but most live in rugged grassland and pine forest zones higher up...

Answer:

Ang mga Igorot ay isang pangkat etniko sa Pilipinas. Matatagpuan sila sa Cordillera, sa isla ng Luzon, sa Hilaga ng bansa. Mayroong anim na lalawigan sa Cordillera Administrative Region (CAR): ang Benguet at Mountain Province lamang ang mga lalawigan na nagmamalaking sila ay Igorot. Nag-iisang lungsod sa CAR ang lungsod ng Baguio. May tatlong na etnolinggwistikong grupo sa parte ng mga pook na tinitirhan ng mga Igorot: ang Bontoc, Ibaloi, at Kankanaey.

24. igorot background history culture norms beliefs and practices​


Answer: Austronesians make up the Igorot people. They used to be famed for their headhunting and battles. During the colonial control of the Philippines, the Spaniards brutally subjugated them to a certain extent, which was finished during the era of US hegemony.

THE ORIGINS OF THE IGOROT

The Spaniards in this ethnic meeting used the term Igorot to refer those tribes they did not settle in Central Luzon. It is derived from the term "Godot," which means "mountain chain," and is supplemented by the prefix "I," which means "live in."

They inhabit in the Cordillera region of Luzon's island of Luzon. The Cordillera Administrative Region (CAR) has six territories. Ifugao, Kalinga, Mountain Province, Abra, Apayao, and Benguet are among them.

One of our forefathers are the Igorots. They had been living in our nation for a long time before the invaders arrived. Their habits and rituals have persisted to this day. Some of them are accomplished, yet they nevertheless adhere to the established culture

IGOROT COSTUMES: THEIR CULTURE AND TRADITION

Their attire is well-known. Ladies wear colorful skirts or long skirts, as well as ornaments and pots, as part of their attire. Handwoven underwear are worn by the guys.

They must have "tattoos" on their bodies because they reflect the grit and reputation of a member of the public. When it comes to modern clothing, they are quite picky. They would love their tradition much more now that they had been raised.

Religious Beliefs are represented through their culture and tradition.

The Igorots, like other ethnic groups, have a plethora of traditions. Early termination, marriage, and multiplication are among the most common petitions they get. There are elements of it that ghosts believe they are preserving.

Fighting, love commitment, career choice, and travel all have a list of petitions or instructions that must be completed through their chief. Despite the fact that the Spanish conquered the Philippines for a long time, the Igorot were able to maintain their independence. The reason for this is that the area is hilly. They are discouraged from clinging to them or, at the very least, persuaded.

30. system writing of Igorot?​


Baybayin is a writing system native to the Philippines, attested from before Spanish colonization through to at least the eighteenth century. The word baybay means “to spell” in Tagalog, which was the language most frequently written with the baybayin script.
