18. A man tastes his wife’s delicious home- cooked meal and exclaims, “I shall never eat this food ever again”.1 pointSimileMetaphorIronyOxymoron​


Answer:

Oxymoron

Explanation:

because both ideas are opposing with each other

FIGURE OF SPEECH

A man tastes his wife’s delicious home- cooked meal and exclaims, “I shall never eat this food ever again”.

ANSWER

I think the given statement above is an example of irony, since it is indicated in the statement that the food is delicious but the man exclaims he will never eat the food again, which is a contradictory claim.

I can't consider it metaphor nor simile since there is no blatant comparison, oxymoron is not also applicable because there isn't direct self-contradictory words in the statement.
