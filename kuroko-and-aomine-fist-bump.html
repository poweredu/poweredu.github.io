25. the particles the bumped into the particles close to them which makes the vibrate to causing them to bump into more particles is called​


Answer:

sound

Explanation:

The source of a sound vibrates, bumping into nearby air molecules which in turn bump into their neighbours, and so forth. This results in a wave of vibrations travelling through the air to the eardrum, which in turn also vibrates. What the sound wave will sound like when it reaches the ear depends on a number of things such as the medium it travels through and the strength of the initial vibration.


26. Write the fist 5 term of geometric whose fist term is 2 and the common ratio is 3​


Answer:

2, 6, 18, 54, 162

Step-by-step explanation:

We need to multiply the common ratio to the previous term in order to get the next term.  

a₁ = 2                  a₁ × r = a₂         →        2 × 3 = 6a₂ × r = a₃        →         6 × 3 = 18a₃ × r = a₄        →        18 × 3 = 54a₄ × r = a₅        →        54 × 3 = 162

Therefore the first five terms of the geometric sequence is 2, 6, 18, 54, and 162.
