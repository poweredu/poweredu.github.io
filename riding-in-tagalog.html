9. riding tricycle versus riding a car​


First of all this is a very generic question as each of them serve a different class of people albeit performing the same purpose.

Tricycling May Be Faster and More Efficient Than Taking a Car. ... But tricylces are often faster than cars in urban areas, especially when city designers have set aside proper tricylce lanes. There's nothing more satisfying as a tricylce commuter than breezing past a long line of gridlocked traffic.

while cars have to analyse where you live and which vehicle suits best for the particular location.

Location:

If you live in a crowded city/country and you commute alone or with one other person often (usually developing countries or European countries) a motorcycle makes sense as you can.

Easily park it.

Easily filter through traffic.

Much faster commuting.

Better Fuel economy in stop and go traffic.

Cheaper to buy and run.

The cons of having a motorcycle in such places are:

Not comfortable since you will have to stop every now and then.

Climate/weather can impact your commute.(Rain/snow)

You cannot go shopping if you suddenly decide to.

Overall you will physically be more uncomfortable on a motorcycle.

If you live in a properly planned huge country/city with lots of open roads like the USA or Australia/Russia . Then a higher capacity Car is the obvious choice.

Having a motorcycle in a huge thinly populated country does not make sense as you will have to drive for a extended period of time and its downright awful on a standard sports bike.

That said there are two vital points which can ultimately be used to decide between the two.

You can very well use a car in a crowded city provided you can afford one, it will be difficult for the reasons explained above but the sheer comfort of a 2 hour drive on a car can offset the unpleasant ride on a motorcycle.

If you are an enthusiast of either then you can go ahead and not bother about any of the pros or cons and buy the vehicle , Your thrill to drive the vehicle you love will shadow the physical/mental pain it causes be it a car or a motorcycle.

in other term, car is better than tricylce

I think car is the winner
