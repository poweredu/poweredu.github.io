6. ano Ang dalawang magkaugnay na serye ng wikang ayon Kay Saussure? ipaliwanag Ang bawat isa.​


Answer:

Ayon naman kay Saussure (1959), ang wika ay binubuo ng dalawang parallel at magkaugnay na serye, and signifier (Language) na Isang kabuuang set ng mga gawaing pangwika na nagbibigay ng daan sa individual na umintindi at maintindihan, at ang signified (parole) na gamit ang wika sa pagsasalita. Kaugnay sa teoryang ito ay nang Ideya ng pagiging heterogenous ng wika dahil sa mga magkakaibang individual at groupo na may magkaibang lugar na tinitirhan, interes Gawain at pinag-aralan.


7. similarities of charles darwin and ferdinand de saussure


Answer:

Charles Darwin and Ferdinand de Saussure were both influential figures in their respective fields of study. Darwin was a naturalist and scientist who is best known for his theory of evolution, while de Saussure was a linguist who is considered one of the founders of modern linguistics.

One of the most notable similarities between the two is that both men were interested in understanding the underlying structures that govern complex systems. In Darwin's case, this meant studying the natural world and the way that living things evolve over time. For de Saussure, it meant studying the structure of language and how it functions as a system of communication.

Both men also made significant contributions to their fields through their work. Darwin's theory of evolution has had a profound impact on the way we understand the natural world and has influenced many different areas of science. De Saussure's work on linguistics laid the foundation for many of the concepts and theories that are still used in the field today.

In addition, both Darwin and de Saussure were able to generate new insights and ideas through their careful observations and analysis of the systems they studied. Their work has helped to shape our understanding of the world and has had a lasting impact on the way we think about and study complex systems.
