6. Why is it important for the person who aspire to follow the command of God to undergo formation?


Answer:

When a person aspires to follow the command of God, the person must first undergo formation in order for him to efficiently and effectively fulfill his role as an aspirant follower.

Explanation:

As human beings, we were considered to have been born with an original sin. Aside from that, as an individual who constantly wanders around to find himself and the meaning of his existence, a person encounters a lot of experiences. These experiences molded him to become a total person according to his learned belief, personality and attitude towards events in life. With this, the person becomes complex an at some point, maybe he committed mistakes which may be a hindrance to his dedication to follow God. In order to follow God effectively without the trace of earthly behaviors and desires, without the hesitation of following the right path due to bad experiences in life, a person must undergo transformation. It is through transformation that the person will get to know his or herself better, change his deeds or behavior which may not be suitable in the chosen path and prepare to live in accordance to God's commandments.
