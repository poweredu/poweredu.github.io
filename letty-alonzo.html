16. After using 2 2/5 kilograms of sugar to make cakes, Letty still have 3 4/6 kilograms of sugar left. How much sugar did Letty have in the beginning?​


Answer:

[tex] \sf{} 6 \frac{1}{15} \\ [/tex]

Step-by-step explanation:

[tex]\\ \sf{} =2 \frac{2}{5} + 3 \frac{4}{6} [/tex]

[tex]\\ \sf{} =2 \frac{2}{5} + 3 \frac{2}{3} [/tex]

Seραrate and add the whole numbers.

[tex]\\ \sf{} =2 + 3 + \frac{2}{5} + \frac{2}{3} [/tex]

[tex]\\ \sf{} =5 + \frac{2}{5} + \frac{2}{3} [/tex]

LCD is 15. Make the denominators the same as the LCD.

[tex]\\ \sf{} =5 + \frac{2 \times 3}{5 \times 3} + \frac{2 \times 5}{3 \times 5} [/tex]

[tex]\\ \sf{} =5 + \frac{6}{15} + \frac{10}{15} [/tex]

[tex]\\ \sf{} =5 + \frac{6 + 10}{15} [/tex]

[tex]\\ \sf{} =5 + \frac{16}{15} [/tex]

[tex]\\ \sf{} =5 + 1 \frac{1}{15} [/tex]

[tex]\\ \huge \boxed{\sf{} = 6 \frac{1}{15} } \\ \\ \\ \\ \\ [/tex]

30. letty and leo danced the chacha.is it passive or active voice?​


QUESTION:

Letty and Leo danced the chacha. Is it passive or active voice?​

ANSWER:Active

Why?Active voice forms "subject + verb + object."Letty and Leo are the subjects, danced is the verb, and chacha is the object.
