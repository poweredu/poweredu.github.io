1. There are 25 rooms in dreamland hotel but only 6 rooms are occupied .calculate the percentage of unoccupied rooms


76% of the rooms are unoccupied.

Step-by-step explanation:

Finding the Percent

The word percent means a part of a hundred. It refers to the ratio of the percentage to the base or the whole. It is the number written with a percent sign (%). To find the percent, you have to divide the percentage by the base, then multiply by 100. Add the percent sign (%).

percent = (percentage ÷ base) × 100

Going back to the problem, you have to calculate the percent of the unoccupied rooms. There are two steps to find the answer.

Find how many rooms are unoccupied by subtracting the number of occupied rooms from the total number of rooms in the hotel.Find the percent using the formula above.

Given:

total number of rooms in the hotel - 25

number of occupied rooms - 6

Solution:

Step 1: Unoccupied Rooms

25 - 6 = 19

19 rooms are unoccupied.

Step 2: Percent

percent = ( percentage ÷ base ) × 100

= ( 19 ÷ 25 ) × 100

= 0.76 × 100

= 76

Final Answer:

76% of the rooms are unoccupied.
