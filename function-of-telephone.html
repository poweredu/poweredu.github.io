1. function of the telephone​


Answer: The function of the telephone is to make communication possible beyond the limitations of distance and time.

Explanation: The breakthrough of telephones has made communication accessible to everyone no matter how far they are from each other. Below are some of the benefits this invention has brought:

1. In comparison to snail mail, telephones make it possible for people to communicate in real time. This is helpful in times of emergencies where prompt replies are needed.

2. Telephones bring families together as well. This is so in the case of family members who work abroad and need time to talk to their loved ones at home.

3. Telephones are also used as a medium for entertainment. When distance doesn’t permit you to talk to friends in person, telephones make it possible for you to converse with them.
