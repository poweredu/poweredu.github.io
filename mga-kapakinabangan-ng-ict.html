1. Magbigay ng mga kapakinabangan ng ICT


Benefits of ICT

The infrastructure and components that allow modern computing are known as ICT (information and communications technology).  

About the fact that there is no single, uniform concept of ICT, it is widely understood to include all computers, networking elements, software, and structures that enable individuals and organizations to communicate in the modern world.  

The value of ICT software goes beyond simple data entry and processing operations. Users may use VoIP tools to make phone calls and hold conferences over IP-based networks, for example. This system is less expensive than mobile telephony and conventional landline networks, which helps enterprises save money.  Email, PCs, and tablets have revolutionized the way businesses and individuals communicate. Without internet connectivity, laptops, file-sharing services, and team management tools, none of this will be feasible.  ICT has a major impact on the world economy and poverty reduction. The internet, email, collaboration apps, and other information and communication technology (ICT) resources help integrate both local and central government offices, raise living conditions, and aid international economic integration, among other things.  This technology will be used by governments to provide people with easy access to information and resources. ICT also aids in the strengthening of the legislative system that strengthens democracy and facilitates the sharing of knowledge between institutions.
