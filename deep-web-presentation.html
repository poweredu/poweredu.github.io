1. what is dark web? and deep web?


Answer:

The dark web is the World Wide Web content that exists on darknets: overlay networks that use the Internet but require specific software, configurations, or authorization to access.

Also, the Deep Web is huge: back in 2001, it was estimated to be 400–550 times larger than the Surface Web, and it's been growing exponentially since then. By comparison, the Dark Web is pretty small: Dark Web sites number only in the thousands.

8.The core–mantle boundary (CMB) of Earth lies between the planet's silicate mantle and its liquid iron-nickel outer core. This boundary is located at approximately 2,891 km (1,796 miles) depth beneath Earth's surface

11. what is your expectation of deep web


Deep Web is defined as content that is not classified by ordinary search engines. It appears to have wide uses such as online banking, web-mail, managed online profiles and pages, that need secured registration for accessing content.

How to Access the Deep Web

Those who desire to enter deep web already understand how to access it. Additional examples are forums or webinars that has registrations. It involves online lessons or teaching materials managed by institutions or universities. There are databases from private companies that can't be listed by the search engines. Scientific researches and educational materials are not included from the indexing systems. Your private business information and banking information that are not classified by search engines are hence recognized as deep web content. Protecting privacy is the main reason of these private registrations.
