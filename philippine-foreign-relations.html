27. Why should Social Constructivism play a significant role in assessing the prospects on the shift of Philippines-China foreign relations from Duterte Administration to Marcos Administration?


Social Constructivism is a theory in international relations that emphasizes the role of ideas and norms in shaping state behavior. It argues that states are not simply rational actors who respond to material incentives, but that they are also influenced by their perceptions of the world and their values.

Social Constructivism can be used to assess the prospects on the shift of Philippines-China foreign relations from Duterte Administration to Marcos Administration in a number of ways. First, it can help us to understand the different worldviews of the two administrations. Duterte's administration has been characterized by a more pragmatic approach to foreign policy, while Marcos' administration has been more nationalistic. These different worldviews could lead to different approaches to China.

Second, Social Constructivism can help us to understand the different norms that are likely to be important to the two administrations. Duterte's administration has been more focused on economic development, while Marcos' administration has been more focused on sovereignty and national identity. These different norms could lead to different priorities in terms of foreign policy.

Third, Social Constructivism can help us to understand the different ways in which the two administrations are likely to interact with China. Duterte's administration has been more willing to engage with China, while Marcos' administration has been more critical of China. These different approaches could lead to different outcomes in terms of Philippines-China relations.

Overall, Social Constructivism can be a useful tool for assessing the prospects on the shift of Philippines-China foreign relations from Duterte Administration to Marcos Administration. It can help us to understand the different worldviews, norms, and approaches that are likely to be important to the two administrations. This understanding can help us to predict how the two administrations are likely to interact with China and what the outcomes of this interaction are likely to be.
