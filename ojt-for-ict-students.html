18. what is the importance of ICT as a student? ​


Answer:

ICT, or Information and Communication Technology, is an essential tool for students in today's digital age. ICT is a broad and all-encompassing term that refers to the various technologies and systems used for transmitting, processing, and storing information.The importance of ICT as a student cannot be overstated. In today's world, where technology is used in almost every aspect of life, having proficiency in ICT is a critical skill for students. With the rise of e-learning and remote work, students who are not familiar with ICT and computer literacy are at a major disadvantage.ICT is essential for students because it allows them to access and process information quickly and efficiently. It also facilitates communication and collaboration, which are essential skills for success in today's workplace. Additionally, the skills and knowledge gained through studying ICT can lead to a wide range of career opportunities, including software development, web design, and cyber security.Overall, ICT is an essential tool for students in today's digital age. Without a proficiency in ICT, students will be unable to keep up with the rapidly evolving world of technology and will be at a major disadvantage in the job market. By studying ICT, students can gain the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in a wide range of careers and in an increasingly digital world.
