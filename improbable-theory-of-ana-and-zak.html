2. tinikling Dance improbs​


How do you describe Tinikling dance?

It imitates the movement of the tikling birds as they walk between grass stems, run over tree branches, or dodge bamboo traps set by rice farmers." Dancers imitate the tikling bird's legendary grace and speed by skillfully maneuvering between large bamboo poles.


Where did Tinikling originate?

island of Leyte
According to historical accounts, the Tinikling dance originated during the Spanish occupation in the Philippines—particularly on the island of Leyte. Rice farmers on the Visayan Islands usually set up bamboo traps to protect their fields, yet tikling birds dodged their traps.


What are the movements used in Tinikling?

Leaping, hopping, skipping are prevalent movements in Tinikling. In other countries, a 4/4 tempo is used but in the Philippines we sustain the use of the ¾ tempo.

ENVIRONMENTAL THEORY

Environmental Theory - it was defined in Nursing by Florence Nightingale as "the utilizing of environment of the patient to assist him in his recovery." It involves the leadership of the nurses on shaping environmental settings appropriate for a progressive restoration of the patient's health and the external factors associated with the patient's area affect the life or his/her development.

Here are some concepts on Florence Nightingale Environmental Theory:

Nursing - according to Florence Nightingale "What nursing has to do… is to put the patient in the best condition for nature to act upon him (Nightingale, 1859/1992)" it was clearly illustrated that as a nurse you should have the patient in the best condition. Accommodation at its finest where Nurse Ana should heed her interest.Environment - Nightingale stresses that physical environment matters because it reflects a community being the role model for those who surrounds human beings concerning their health. Nurse Ana on the other hand, due to its patients that Pulmonary Tuberculosis can spread through air from one person to another. She has to secure an inclusive environment for her patient. Health - Nightingale emphasizes the positive outlook based on observation and experience. Given her the definition that the art of nursing is to " unmake what God had made disease," then the goal of all nursing activities should be clients health that matters. Nurse Ana believed that through her care to her patient and discussing on how to keep on track and what should the nurses should engage in this. Nutrition and and Taking food - the desire of the patient to be healthy again is based on the food that he should be nourished in. It is more beneficial to the patient to eat a large breakfast and have enough rest and medication.  

Based on the Environmental Theory that has been introduced by Florence Nightingale, whom the first theorist in Nursing study.
