1. anong pinag kaiba ng "Im way out of your league" and you're way out of my league"​


Answer:

English version:

"I'm way out of your league" suggests that the speaker believes they are superior to the person they are speaking to in some way, such as in terms of attractiveness, intelligence, wealth, or social status. This phrase can be used to reject someone or to make the speaker feel superior to the person they are speaking to.

On the other hand, "you're way out of my league" suggests that the speaker feels inferior to the person they are speaking to in some way. This phrase is often used to express feelings of inadequacy or to deflect attention away from oneself. It can also be used to show humility or to acknowledge that the person being spoken to is out of one's reach.


Tagalog version:

Ang "I'm way out of your league" ay nangangahulugang nais ng nagsasalita na siya ay mas mataas sa tao na kausap niya sa ilang paraan, gaya ng kagandahan, katalinuhan, kayamanan, o estado sa lipunan. Maaaring gamitin ang salitang ito upang tanggihan ang isang tao o para magpakatino sa tao na kausap.

Sa kabilang banda, ang "you're way out of my league" ay nangangahulugang hindi tiwala ng nagsasalita sa sarili niya kumpara sa tao na kausap niya sa ilang paraan. Karaniwang ginagamit ang salitang ito para ipahayag ang damdamin ng hindi sapat o para iwasan ang pansin sa sarili. Maaari rin itong gamitin upang ipakita ang kahinahon o para tanggapin na hindi maaabot ng tao na kausap ang nagsasalita.

25. What was the Delian league?


Answer:

The Delian League was founded in 478 BCE following the Persian War to be a military alliance against any enemies that might threaten Ionian Greeks. ... For the most part, the League was official affirmation of the rift that had formed between Athens and Sparta during and following the war.

Answer:

After the Persian Wars, Athens took steps to protect itself and its trading practices. Athens had a strong navy, but the Athenians knew they were not strong enough by themselves. They wanted to be able to defend their city-state against other world powers like Persia. Athens believed that if the Greek city-states worked together, they would be stronger. Athens formed an alliance in 478 BCE with many city-states. The alliance was called the Delian League. The city-states agreed to pay money into the league's treasury to help protect each other. They also contributed troops and ships.

28. In an inter-barangay basketball league, the xteam from Barangay Salipongan has won 15 out of 25 games. What is the winning percentage of Barangay Salipongan? ​


Answer:

60%

Step-by-step explanation:

Barangay Salipongan has won 15/25 of its games.

Then, 15/25 = 3/5.

Multiplying both the numerator and denominator by 20, we get 60/100.

Thus, converting it into decimal, we get 0.60 or 60% winning percentage.
