QUESTION

In a basketball game, the player dribbles the ball to the ground as the ball reaches the ground it bunches back. which law of motion is described in the scenario

A. law of inertia

B. law of acceleration

C. law of interaction

ANSWER

Amongst the given choices above, the correct answer is letter C. law of interaction.

Law of Interaction or 3rd Law of motion by Sir Isaac Newton, states that "for every action, there is an equal and opposite reaction".

EXAMPLE

One example is when a ball hits another ball, and then both will have equal but opposite reaction, causing them to bounce back.
