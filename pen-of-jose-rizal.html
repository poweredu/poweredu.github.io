He is one of the writer of Lasodilaridad and the author of the famous historical books Noli Me Tangere and El Filibusterismo that talks about the abuse of spañiards to the Filipinos.

3. What are the three different pen name of jose rizal?


During the colonization of the Spaniards, Filipino writers defend and fight for the rights of their fellow countrymen through their writings. They expressed their deepest concern on the current situation of the Philippines during that time by creating poems, novels, stories, and other forms of literature. Writers used their skills in creative writing to persuade their readers. To secure their own safety especially their families, they decided to use a pen-name on their works.  

Writers with their corresponding pen-names:  Dr. Jose P. Rizal - Dimas-alang, Laong-Laan, Agno, and Calambeño Mariano Ponce - Naning, Tikbalang, and Kalipulako Emilio Jacinto - Dimas-Ilaw and Pingkian Amado Hernandez - Amante Ernani, Herininia de la Riva, and Julio Abril Nick Joaquin - Quijano de Manila Jose Turiano Santiago - Tiktik Antonio Luna - Taga-Ilog Juan Luna - J.B. and Buan
