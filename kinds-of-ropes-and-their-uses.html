1. what kind of pulley uses grooved wheels and ropes/chains to raise, lower, or move a load by pulling on the rope?​


Answer:

SINGLE FIXED PULLY

Explanation:

single fixed pulley, only one rope segment pulls up on the load, so the ideal mechanical advantage is 1. In other words, this type of pulley doesn’t increase the force that is applied to it. However, it does change the direction of the force. This allows you to use your weight to pull on one end of the rope and more easily raise the load attached to the other end.

4. Irina is using a rope that has a length of 150 feet. What is the length, in yards, of Irina’s rope?


Answer:

The length of Irina's rope is 50 yards.

Step-by-step explanation:

Word Problem:Conversion of Feet to Yards

Let us analyze the given problem above.

What is asked?

The length of Irina's rope in yards.

What is the given?

length of Irina's rope - 150 feet

What is the equation?

To find the length of Irina's rope in yards, we need to convert the length in feet to yards.

1 foot = 0.333 yard

150 × 0.333 = N

Solution:

150 × 0.333 = 49.95 yards

Round off to the nearest whole number, 50 yards.

Final Answer:

Irina's rope is 50 yards.
