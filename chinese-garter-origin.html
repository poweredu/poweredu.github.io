4. Safety precautions in playing chinese garter


Answer:

This is how the game played. First, there should be two people holding both ends of the garter which is stretched horizontally while others are trying to transfer across the other side of the garter. The main goal of this game is to across over the garter without being tripped. In each round, the height of the garter increases. The garter generally starts from the ankle – level and then knee – level, and so on until the garter is positioned over head. Higher rounds demand agility since players leap with their feet first in the air and they cross over the garter and end up landing on the side. This means that players should be agile, mentally skilled and must have grace in order to finish the game. Moreover, doing cartwheels (to cross the garter) in higher levels is allowed.

22. what is the rules of "chinese garter"?


Answer:

This game needs you to be physically fit for this will test your stamina, agility and your jumping skills. It needs a flexible body to keep up for the game. Before the game starts you will need a rubber bands, garter or rope whichever you prefer and as to how long the length you will be using.

To play the game, two teams are required with 2 or more players each, although you can still play this with only three players 2 holding the rope and one jumping on it. The holders will stand facing each other at both ends with the garter stretched horizontally around 2 to 3 yards or 7 feet long looped around their ankles.

The player should be able to jump or cross over the garter without tripping. Failure to do so will get you or your team eliminated and make you the one holding the garter as the other player takes turn in jumping.

The players may decide the levels for their game. Generally the garter starts from the ankle level, next is middle of the leg, knee, hips, waist, chest, shoulder, head, tip of the head, few inches above the head and the last level is that the garter is held up with raised arms. Every challenger must be careful not to commit any mistakes during their jump or else her turn is over.

23. five rules in playing chinese garter​?


Answer:

Rules

1. Players are divided into two or more teams. During the game, two members of the “it” team shall serve as “game posts” as they stand opposite each other and hold each end of the garter. The objective of the game then is to be able to successfully jump over the garter as it is gradually held higher by the game posts.

2. Once a jumper fails to go over a particular level, he/she will not be allowed to jump on the next level and only the players who successfully jump the level moves on the next. Then, one member of the team needs to make up the missed jumps of the other players (per level). The process continues up to the highest or last level. If no one from the jumpers successfully jumps over the highest level, they switch role with the “It” group and the game starts a new.

3. If it is your teams turn again, (and the other team didn’t reach the last level) you’ll continue on the level/round on where you stopped. (until you reach the last level or until the time is up)

4. The game usually ends after the highest jump (in the last round). However, some would continue playing the game backwards. Instead of raising the garter higher, the posts shall hold the garter back to its lower position. The game now has a new objective: to be able to go under the garter by bending backwards (limbo rock). The game continues this way until the player reaches back to the lowest level.

5. The game can also end if there is a time limit. The team who is on the farthest round and level wins.

30. what type of game is chinese garter​


Answer:

Chinese Gater, a game requiring flexibility, balance and coordination, is a popular game for Filipino children, most specifically among girls during their elementary years. The game revolves around an ordinary garter, around two to three yards long, which be brought from sewing store at around twenty pesos or less

How to play Chinese Garter:

The primary objective of this game is to crosswise over the garter without being tripped. In each round, the stature of the garter increases. The garter by and large begins from the lower leg – level and after that knee – level, et cetera until the point that the garter is situated overhead.
