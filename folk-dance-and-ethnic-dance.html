14. Difference between folk dance and ethnic dance


Difference Between Folk Dance and Ethnic Dance

A folk dance is a form of social dance that become part of a tradition or custom. Ethnic dance is from an ethnic group that expresses aesthethics of a particular culture. Ethnic dance is a broad terminology. Folk dance is more specific. Folk dance is more popular nowadays.

Folk Dance

The list below is the example of popular folk dance in the Philippines:

Tinikiling – Popular folk dance originated from the Province of Leyte. Itik-Itik – A cultural folk dance originated from the Province of Surigao del Sur. Maglalatik – A popular folk dance from Binan, Laguna. Carinosa – Local folk dance originated from Panay Island.

Ethnic Dance  

The list below is the example of popular ethnic dance in the Philippines:

Pagdiwata – From Tagabnwa, Palawan. Dugso – From Province of Bukidnon. Pangalay and Lunsay – Ethnic dance of Tausug tribe.
