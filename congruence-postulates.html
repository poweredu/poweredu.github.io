15. 2. Describe each of the following.a. SAS Congruence Postulate :________________________________________________b. ASA Congruence Postulate:________________________________________C. SSS Congruence Postulate:________________________________________​


ANSWER:

A.SAS Congruence Postulate

—The Side Angle Side postulate (often abbreviated as SAS) states that if two sides and the included angle of one triangle are congruent to two sides and the included angle of another triangle, then these two triangles are congruent.

B. ASA Congruence Postulate

—The Angle-Side-Angle Postulate (ASA) states that if two angles and the included side of one triangle are congruent to two angles and the included side of another triangle, then the two triangles are congruent.

C.SSS Congruence Postulate

—The SSS Postulate tells us, If three sides of one triangle are congruent to three sides of another triangle, then the two triangles are congruent. Congruence of sides is shown with little hatch marks, like this: ∥. For two triangles, sides may be marked with one, two, and three hatch marks.
