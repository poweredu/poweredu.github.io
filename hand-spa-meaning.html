3. what is the meaning of hand spa?


Hand spaWhat is a hand spa?

It helps remove dead cells and callous from the hands caused by some activities done by an individual such as lifting weights, washing plates, doing the laundry, and many more. Callous appear on hands because of the constant friction of the skin to the things they use or to the materials that creates impact on the skin of the hand. It is also a treatment done by trained personnel. This treatment is done to replenish, rejuvenate, and restore tired hands. It is also done to keep hands beautiful and make the individual confident. It is considered a part or a kind of a beauty care.
