1. Difference between business model canvas and circular business model canvas


Answer:

The Circular Canvas is the first tool created by Circulab to help organizations rethink their business model or projets and create long lasting impacts.

The Business Model Canvas, developed by Alexander Osterwalder, is a visual representation of current or new business models that provides a holistic view of the business as a whole. It is lightweight, simple and easy to understand for visually documenting key sections of a business strategy. The Business Model Canvas gives people a common language through which is easy to discuss your business model with others.

9. pa help po gumawa ng business model canvas about cleaning services​


Answer:

A Business Model Canvas for Cleaning Services:

Key Partners:

Suppliers of cleaning equipment and materials

Marketing and advertising agencies

Staffing agencies for hiring cleaners

Other companies offering complementary services such as pest control, waste management, etc.

Key Activities:

Providing cleaning services to commercial and residential clients

Hiring and training cleaners

Procuring cleaning equipment and materials

Developing marketing strategies and advertising campaigns

Maintaining client relationships and providing customer service

Key Resources:

Skilled and trained cleaners

Cleaning equipment and materials

Marketing and advertising resources

Client database and CRM system

Financial resources for growth and expansion

Value Proposition:

High-quality cleaning services for both commercial and residential clients

Flexible scheduling options to meet client needs

Affordable pricing and customized cleaning plans

Use of eco-friendly cleaning products and methods

Excellent customer service and attention to detail

Customer Segments:

Commercial businesses such as offices, retail stores, restaurants, and hotels

Residential customers such as homeowners, renters, and property managers

Channels:

Online advertising and social media marketing

Referrals and word-of-mouth advertising

Direct mail campaigns and flyers

Partnership with other complementary service providers

Website and online booking platform

Customer Relationships:

Regular communication with clients to ensure their needs are met

Responsive customer service to address any concerns or issues

Building long-term relationships with clients to encourage repeat business and referrals

Revenue Streams:

Cleaning service fees based on type of service and frequency of cleaning

Additional revenue streams from complementary services such as pest control, waste management, etc.

Cost Structure:

Labor costs for hiring and training cleaners

Cleaning equipment and materials

Marketing and advertising expenses

Operational expenses such as rent, utilities, and insurance

Cost of goods sold such as eco-friendly cleaning products

26. How could you possibly apply creativity and innovation in crafting your business model canvas?


Answer:

Creativity plays an essential role in the innovation process because it generates the ideas that will initiate innovation. Ideas emerge at every level of the process and they correspond to various challenges, such as responding to an issue, meeting a target objective, solving a problem, making use of knowledge, or understanding a phenomenon. But it is knowledge that makes it possible to put ideas to work and hence to innovate. In addition, knowledge feeds creativity, and ideas stimulate research. Thus, the success of innovation relies largely on these two activities, which are very dependent on people who perform them.
