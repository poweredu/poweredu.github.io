6. If a+b+c=0, then a3+a2c-abc+b2c+b3 = ?


Answer:

0

Step-by-step explanation:

Required Answer:

Value of a³ + a²c - abc + b²c + b³

Solution:

a³ + a²c - abc + b²c + b³

= (a³ + b³) + (a²c - abc + b²c)

= (a + b)(a² - ab + b²) + (a²c - abc + b²c)

= (a + b)(a² - ab + b²) + c(a² - ab + b²)

= (a + b + c)(a² - ab + b²)

= (0)(a² - 2ab + b²)

= 0

Formula used:

a³ + b³ = (a + b)(a² - ab + b²)

8. B2B vs. B2C: What’s the Difference? Give Example​


Answer:

Sellers visit business customers directly to a much greater extent than  consumers.

Explanation:

There are 2 ways to make $10,000.

Sell a $50 product to 200 people.Sell a $5,000 product to 2 people.

(B2B) business-to-business is shorthand for “business to business.” It refers to sales you make to other businesses rather than to individual consumers and not the end consumers. Sales to consumers are referred to as “business-to-consumer” sales or B2C.

(B2C) business-to-consumer refers to the process of selling products and services directly between consumers who are the end-users of its products or services. Most companies that sell directly to consumers can be referred to as B2C companies

So…

Sell a $50 product to 200 people.  → B2CSell a $5,000 product to 2 people. → B2B

With B2B, you can sell larger ticket products and get ROI, a clear measure of customer success.

B2C has many motivations, including social status, health, fitness, recreation, and wellness.
