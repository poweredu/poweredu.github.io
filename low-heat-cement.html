8. sixty grams (60g) of rosin 30g of beeswax and 35g of shellac are heated in a metal pan to produce benzene soluble cement. calculate the percentage by mass of all the components of a cement​


Answer:

% rosin = 48%

% beeswax = 24%

% shellac = 28%

Explanation:

Total mass (g) = 60g + 35g + 30g = 125g

% rosin = (60g/125g)*100% = 48%

% beeswax = (30g/125g)*100% = 24%

% shellac = (35g/125g)*100% = 28%
