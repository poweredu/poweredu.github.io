8. anak by fredie aguilar, Quote lines which use literary devices. Cite what literary device is used in each line that you quoted,and briefly discuss why.


Answer:

Here are some lines from the song "Anak" by Freddie Aguilar that use literary devices:

"Nang isilang ka sa mundong ito, Laking tuwa ng magulang mo" - This line uses personification, giving the idea that the world is capable of happiness by attributing the feeling of joy to the parents.

"At ang kamay ng magulang mo'y, Itinulak ka sa daraanan" - This line uses metaphor, comparing the hands of the parents to a force pushing the child to his/her chosen path.

"At kung magulang mo'y di mapagbigyan, Ang nais mo'y huwag mong sisihin" - This line uses a rhetorical question, asking the listener whether they should blame their parents for not granting their wishes.

"Ang magulang mo'y nagtitiis, Upang malaman mo ang kanilang ginagawa" - This line uses hyperbole, exaggerating the idea that parents endure a lot of hardships just to ensure that their child's needs are met.

"Ngunit kahit na anong mangyari, Ang magulang mo'y iyong igagalang" - This line uses repetition, emphasizing the importance of respecting one's parents no matter what happens.

These literary devices serve to make the song more engaging and expressive, conveying important messages about the parent-child relationship, responsibility, and respect.
