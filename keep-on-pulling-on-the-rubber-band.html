Rubber bands are pieces of rubber in the shape of a bracelet made to tie things together. Rubber bands come in various sizes, from large to small, from thick to thin.

The raw material for rubber bands is natural rubber, so they are yellow in color. Colorful rubber bands are made by adding dyes. Manufacturers also make rubber bands that are oil-resistant and all-weather resistant.

O-rings look like rubber bands but are made of elastomer and are used as seals. Compared to rubber bands, O-rings are not as stretchy.

Most rubber bands are made from natural rubber, which is the result of processing the latex from the rubber tree. Rubber bands are also made from synthetic rubber, but they are less popular than the elastic natural rubber.

The principle of making rubber bands is very simple. Long cylindrical rubber (tube) is cut into rubber bands according to size.
