What is a logo?

Ans: A logo is a symbol made up of text and images that incorporates a business or a company . A good logo shows what a company does and what the brand values. Logo design is all about creating the perfect ideal brand symbol for a company.

Where do personal logos/class logos used?

Ans: Personal logos and branding are used by creative professionals to add another dimension of identity to their work. They are often used for stationery, website logos, business cards, or for watermarks. Personal logos are the artist's own brand.

What is the importance of logo in today's world?

Ans:  It grabs attention, makes a strong first impression, is the foundation of your brand identity, is memorable, separates you from competition, fosters brand loyalty, and is expected by your audience.
