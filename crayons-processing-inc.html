26. There are 40 crayons in a box.There are twice as many red crayons asblue crayons.There are as many green crayons as red crayons.How many blue crayons are there?​


Answer:

20 blue crayons

Step-by-step explanation:

Let...

x be the red crayonsy be the blue crayons; and z be the green crayons.

Based on the given question, these equations are derived.

2x = yx = z

Now, let's solve for x or red crayons.

[tex]x + y + z = 40 \\ x + 2x + x = 40 \\ 4x = 40 \\ \frac{4x}{4} = \frac{40}{4} \\ x = 10[/tex]

Since, we already know that there are the same number of red and green crayons, we can say that there are also 10 green crayons.

Now, let's find out how many blue crayons are there.

[tex]y = 2x = 2(10) = 20[/tex]

Check:

[tex]red + blue + green = 40 \\ 10 + 20 + 10 = 40 \\ 40 = 40[/tex]

Correct!
