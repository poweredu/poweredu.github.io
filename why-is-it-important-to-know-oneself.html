18. insights and realization about the importance of knowing oneself.​


The Importance of Knowing Oneself

Knowing oneself helps to easily determine your goals and dreams, and how you are going to approach those and make them into fulfillment.  It is a key determinant of who you are going to become in the future.  Your future self will be your drive for your present self.  Thus, it is important to truly know oneself.  Moreover, our wants and desires play an integral role in our daily living.  They lead us on how we are going to thrive in every day.  Through these key areas in life, we get to identify the people who we want to stick around with, and who will help us learn and grow over time.
