Answer:

Correct.

Thelma likes playing the clarinet, marching in the band, and watching football games. You do not have to rewrite the sentence as it already has a parallel structure.

Explanation:

The sentence already has a parallel structure because all three items in the list are expressed in the same form, which is a gerund (verb + ing). This consistency in form creates a parallel structure that makes the sentence easy to understand and gives it a clean, organized look. Additionally, each item in the list is a different activity that Thelma enjoys, and the parallel structure helps to emphasize the similarity of these activities and Thelma's enjoyment of each one. By having parallel structure, the sentence is well-balanced and presents information in a clear and organized manner, making it easier for the reader to understand.
