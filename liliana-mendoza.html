The Elimination Method

Step 1: Multiply each equation by a suitable number so that the two equations have the same leading coefficient. ...

Step 2: Subtract the second equation from the first.

Step 3: Solve this new equation for y.

Step 4: Substitute y = 2 into either Equation 1 or Equation 2 above and solve for x.

To Solve a System of Equations by Elimination

Write both equations in standard form. ...

Make the coefficients of one variable opposites. ...

Add the equations resulting from Step 2 to eliminate one variable.

Solve for the remaining variable.

Low volume pullbacks occur when the price moves towards support levels on lower than average volume. Low volume pullbacks are often a sign of weak longs taking profit, but suggest that the long-term uptrend remains intact. High volume pullbacks suggest that there could be a near-term reversal.

Born: July 30, 1960 (age 61 years), San Fernando

Awards: Cannes Best Director Award

Nominations: Cannes Best Director Award, Grand Jury Prize, Prize of Un Certain Regard, More

Parents: Gervacia Manguerra, Pastor Mendoza

work: profession

He was born and raised in San Fernando, Pampanga. He took advertising arts of the College of Architecture and fine arts at the University of Santo Tomas.

He has directed sixteen films since 2005, also he credited some cinematographer and production designer under his alias. His first frequent collaboration with actor Coco Martin in seven films including Masahista, Summer Heat, Foster Child, Tirador, Serbis, Kinatay, and Captive.

On 25 July 2016, he directed the first State of the Nation Address of President Rodrigo Duterte. Mendoza also directed Duterte's second State of the Nation Address on 24 July 2017.
