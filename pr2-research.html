8. PR2ESSAYexplain the important of review literature related in different type of research. PA HELP!​


ESSAY:

The review of literature is an essential part of any research study because it provides a background and context for the research. It helps to establish the significance of the research and its contribution to the field.

In a review of literature, researchers gather and critically evaluate existing research on a particular topic. This allows them to identify gaps and inconsistencies in the existing knowledge, as well as to identify trends and patterns. By doing this, researchers can build a solid foundation for their own research and demonstrate its relevance and importance.

A review of literature can also help researchers to avoid duplication of effort and to learn from the mistakes of others. By critically evaluating the existing research, researchers can identify which research methods and approaches have been successful in the past, and which have not. This can help them to choose the most appropriate methods and techniques for their own research, and to avoid common pitfalls.

Furthermore, a review of literature can help researchers to identify potential collaborators and to develop new research partnerships. By reading the existing research, researchers can learn about the work of other researchers in their field, and can identify potential areas for collaboration. This can lead to the development of new research partnerships, which can help to advance the field and generate new insights.

In short, the review of literature is an essential part of any research study because it provides a background and context for the research, helps to establish its significance and relevance, and can help researchers to avoid duplication of effort and to develop new research partnerships.

16. PR2why background of the study is an important part of your research?​


Answer:

Sufficient background information helps your reader determine if you have a basic understanding of the research problem being investigated and promotes confidence in the overall quality of your analysis and findings.

So you have carefully written your research paper and probably ran it through your colleagues ten to fifteen times. While there are many elements to a good research article, one of the most important elements for your readers is the background of your study. The background of your study will provide context to the information discussed throughout the research paper. Background information may include both important and relevant studies. This is particularly important if a study either supports or refutes your thesis.

In addition, the background of the study will discuss your problem statement, rationale, and research questions. It links introduction to your research topic and ensures a logical flow of ideas. Thus, it helps readers understand your reasons for conducting the study.
