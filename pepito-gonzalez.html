3. Mrs Gonzalez bought a pine tree that measures 3.50 yards tall. How tall is the pine tree in inches Gonzalez


One yard is three feet. One foot is 12 inches. That means 12*3=36 is how many inches are in a yard. Then you do 3*36= 108. Then, you add one half of 36 for the 0.5 part of 3.5, which is 108+18=126. Therefore, Mrs.Gonzalez's pine tree is 126 inches.

9. 1000 word critique about appasionato by barbara Gonzalez​


Answer:

Appasionato, written by Barbara Gonzalez, is a captivating and powerful story that follows the journey of a young girl as she discovers her passion for music and the courage it takes to pursue it.

The main character is twelve-year-old Sofia, who lives in a small town in Mexico. She is a talented violinist, but her family and the townspeople do not believe that she has a future in music. Despite her parents’ disapproval, Sofia decides to take a chance and apply to a prestigious music school in Mexico City.

The story is told from Sofia’s point of view, and Gonzalez does an excellent job of conveying her emotions and struggles. As Sofia navigates her way through the application process, she faces many obstacles, including financial constraints and the disapproval of her family. Through her determination and hard work, she manages to overcome these obstacles and make it to the music school.

Gonzalez does an excellent job of setting the scene and creating a vivid picture of Sofia’s world. The descriptions of the small town, the music school, and the characters are vivid and engaging. The dialogue is also well-written and realistic, and it helps to further the story and bring the characters to life.

The story is both inspiring and heartbreaking. It is inspiring because it shows that with hard work and dedication, anything is possible. It is heartbreaking because it shows the difficulties that young people face when they are trying to pursue their dreams.

Overall, Appasionato is an engaging and powerful story about a young girl’s journey to pursue her passion for music. Gonzalez does an excellent job of conveying the emotions and struggles of the characters and creating a vivid picture of their world. The story is both inspiring and heartbreaking, and it is sure to leave a lasting impression on readers.
