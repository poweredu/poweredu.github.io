Tabuk, officially the City of Tabuk (Ilocano: Siudad ti Tabuk; Tagalog: Lungsod ng Tabuk), is a 5th class component city and capital of the province of Kalinga, Philippines. According to the 2015 census, it has a population of 110,642 people.[3]

27. the populatio of barangay bulanao centro in tabuk at the beginning of 2019 is,7500.if it increase by 5% every year ,what would be the approximate population at the end of 2028?​


Answer:

The population at the end of 2028 would be 10, 875.

Step-by-step explanation:

5% of 7500 is 375

2028 - 2019 = 9

375 × 9 = 3375

7500 + 3375 = 10, 875
