4. Eddie and Freddie are friends. The sum of their present ages is 48. If Eddie's age in 5 years is twice Freddie's age 8 years ago, how old is Eddie now?


x = Eddie's present age

y = Freddie's present age

the sum of their present age is 48:

                   x + y = 48    (eq.1)

Freddie's age 8 yrs ago:   y - 8

Eddie's age in 5 years:  x + 5   or  2(y - 8)

consider Eddie's age in 5 yrs, equate these two expressions we have,

                                x + 5 = 2(y - 8)

                                x - 2y = -21  (eq.2)

  Use eq. 1 and eq. 2. Multiply eq.1 by 2 then add the two equations to eliminate y:

                               2x + 2y = 96

                           +    x - 2y = -21

                              ___________

                                   3x   =   75

                                     x   =   25

Eddie is 25 yrs old now.
