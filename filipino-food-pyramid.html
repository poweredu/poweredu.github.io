22. How to strengthen the immune system of the Filipino Adolescents following the guide given in the food pyramid?


Answer:To strengthen the immune system of Filipino adolescents following the guidelines of the food pyramid, it is important to focus on a balanced and nutritious diet that includes a variety of foods from all food groups. Some specific steps that can be taken include:Eat a variety of fruits and vegetables: Aim to consume at least 5 servings of fruits and vegetables per day, including a variety of colors and types. These foods are rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants that can help support the immune system.Choose whole grains: Opt for whole grains such as whole wheat, oats, quinoa, and brown rice, rather than refined grains like white bread and pasta. Whole grains are a good source of fiber, which can help improve gut health and support the immune system.Include protein-rich foods: Consume protein-rich foods such as lean meats, poultry, seafood, beans, and nuts. These foods provide the building blocks for immune cells and help support the body's immune response.Limit added sugars: Avoid foods and beverages with added sugars, as these can contribute to inflammation and weaken the immune system. Instead, opt for natural sources of sweetness like fruit.Stay hydrated: Drink plenty of water to help keep the body hydrated and support immune function. Aim for at least 8-10 cups of water per day.By following these guidelines and incorporating a variety of nutrient-dense foods into their diet, Filipino adolescents can help support their immune system and promote overall health and wellness.

The Food Pyramid – A Guide to a Balanced Diet

The Healthy Eating Food Pyramid

Balanced diet is a key to stay healthy. Follow the "Healthy Eating Food Pyramid" guide as you pick your food. Grains should be taken as the major dietary source. Eat more fruit and vegetables. Have a moderate amount of meat, fish, egg, milk and their alternatives. Reduce salt, fat/ oil and sugar. Trim fat from meat before cooking. Choose low-fat cooking methods such as steaming, stewing, simmering, boiling, scalding or cooking with non-stick frying pans. Also reduce the use of frying and deep-frying. These can help us achieve balanced diet and promote health.
