I think the song is about a guy dealing with depression caused by some traumatic event that have happened to him in the past and his lover is trying to help him. He feels that no one could ever help him overcome his problem, no matter how much he wants to seek for help and he also mentions about him being too proud to talk about his suffering. He's afraid that if he opens up to anyone, they would just neglect him.

The message of this song is don't be afraid to face the truth or your problem in life because it won't help us if we keep hiding our true feelings. It's not bad to share your problems and find someone to lean on so that you don't have to deal with the problems alone.

28. if your suffering from body pain which would you prefer take a pain killer medicine or see a therapist for massage? explain


Answer:

See a therapist for massage

Explanation:

Therapeutic massage may relieve pain by way of several mechanisms, including relaxing painful muscles, tendons, and joints; relieving stress and anxiety; and possibly helping to “close the pain gate” by stimulating competing nerve fibers and impeding pain messages to and from the brain. Pain killers side effects in the short-term can include constipation or diarrhea, nausea, and vomiting.
