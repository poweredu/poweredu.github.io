Objectives of philippine folk dance

OBJECTIVES OF PHILIPPINE FOLK DANCES

1. Committing to the values of cultural progress as an innation-building critical element.  

2. Improving the traditional ideals that have been expressed in different folk dances esthetically, spiritually and physically.

3. Participate regularly in folk dancing for its intellectual, spiritual, and andesthetic values.  

4. Disseminating traditional Philippine dances in its pure and authentic form.  

5. To instill the passion for performing folk dances as a cultural heritage and to show the Filipinos  

identity as a people to other nations.
