1. 1.What is Garde Manger?​


Answer:

A garde manger is a cool, well-ventilated area where cold dishes are prepared and other foods are stored under refrigeration. The person in charge of this area is known as the "chef garde manger" or "pantry chef".


2. What is the basic concept of garde manger​


Answer:

A garde manger (pronounced [gaʁd mɑ̃ʒe]; French for "keeper of the food") is a cool, well-ventilated area where cold dishes (such as salads, hors d'œuvres, appetizers, canapés, pâtés, and terrines) are prepared and other foods are stored under refrigeration.


3. 2.What is the rules and responsibilities of Garde Manger?​


Answer:

Chef Garde Manger Duties and Responsibilities: -Oversee the consistency of various preparations within the cold kitchen to ensure quality product and adherence to standard recipes. Prepares all cold food according to recipes, guidelines and standards set by the Executive Chef or hotel standards.
