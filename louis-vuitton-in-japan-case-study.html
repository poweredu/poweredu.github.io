6. What is the impact of louis vuitton products when it comes to innovation and entrepreneurship in the present time?


The impact of Louis Vuitton when it comes to innovation and entrepreneurship in the present time is not only limited to the brand but to the group of companies under LVMH.

The LVMH Group has a strong commitment to innovation that has sustained its success and authenticity over the years. Innovation, a core pillar of LVMH, motivates the Group's houses to pursue excellence and fosters a constant conversation between tradition and contemporary.

This innovation comes with the invention of the Monogram Canvas during the 19th century in order to fight counterfeiting. Aristide Boucicaut, who founded the Le Bon Marché department store and developed modern retail commerce in the 19th century, also introduced fixed prices, labels, periodic sales at specified periods of the year, product returns, and other innovations. Louis Vuitton’s pursuit of greatness is more active than ever.

According to Group Managing Director Toni Belloni, their culture is centered on curiosity, change, opportunity, innovation, and creativity. They want their brands to remain true to their roots while being reinvented to appeal to both current and potential customers.

It can be summarized that the impact of Louis Vuitton has been monumental not only to the fashion world but also to how others conduct business.

8. mabuti ng naidudulot at di mabuting naidudulot ng louis vuitton​


Mabuting naidudulot ng Louis Vuitton

Matibay na produkto at may kalidad kung ikukupara natin sa ibang mga gamit. Mas magtatagal ang mga bagay dito. Pero kahit may kamahalan ito, masasabing sulit naman ang pagkonsumo dito at makakasabay rin sa uso.  

Hindi mabuting naidudulot ng Louis Vuitton

Sa tuwing pagbili ng mga gamit dito ay yumabang na at lumaki ang ulo natin dahil sa pagkakaroon nito. Baka magkaroon ng saloobin na mali kung saan mas mataas ang tingin natin sa sarili. Maituturing na luxury item ito na baka magkaroon ng pagnanasa ang isa na bilhin kahit ipangutang ito para lang makakuha nito. Gayundin, maaari itong maging peke na nabibili sa mahal na halaga at hindi masasabing gawa sa totoong leather.  

Paliwanag:  

Sa pagbili ng mga gamit na may tatak, mahalagang isaalang-alang natin muna ang kalidad at presyo nito. Kapag ang isang gamit ay kilala masasabing mas matibay at tumatagal ito kung ikukumpara sa mga mumurahing mga bagay. Kaya ito ang kapakinabangan kung bibili tayo ng mga may tatak na gamit para sa ating sarili. Pero sa kabilang banda naman, hindi tama kung basta nalang bibilhin ito kung hindi natin pag-iisipan mabuti. At baka maubos ang pera natin kung hindi naman talaga ito kailangan.  

Sa ngayon, makikita natin na may mga bumibili ng Louis Vuitton dahil kilala ito at sikat sa mga social media. Huwag sana tayo magkaroon ng maling saloobin nito na maaaring makaapekto sa ugali natin at pakikitungo sa mga tao na nasa paligid natin.
