To answer the questions above, let's know more about the modal verbs!

Modal Verbs

Modal verbs are words that used to express hypothetical conditions, such as requests, suggestions, capability, and more.

These are the example of modal verbs

can, used for expressing ability or possibilitycould, used for making a request or suggestionmay, used for expressing permission and  possibility in the presentmight, used for expressing possibility in the past and asking permission in formal situationswill, used for making a predictionwould, used for making a request and asking for willingnessmust, used for making a commandshould, used for making or conveying an obligation
