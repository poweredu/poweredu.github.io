9. Give 4 Factors to be considered for proper car of plants and soil to ensure a healthy and bountiful harvest​


Answer:

Proper care of plants and soil is essential for a healthy and bountiful harvest. Here are four factors that should be considered:

Soil preparation: Proper soil preparation is crucial for growing healthy plants. This includes testing the soil pH and nutrient levels, adding organic matter, and ensuring good drainage. Soil preparation also involves removing any rocks or debris that may hinder root growth and aerating the soil to improve its structure.Watering: Adequate watering is important for plant growth, but too much or too little water can be detrimental. Plants should be watered deeply and regularly, but not overwatered, as this can cause root rot and other diseases. The timing of watering should also be considered, as early morning or late afternoon is usually the best time to water.Fertilization: Proper fertilization is important for plant growth and development. Plants require different amounts of nutrients at different stages of growth, and these should be provided in appropriate amounts and at the right time. Organic fertilizers can be used to enrich the soil and promote healthy plant growth.Pest and disease control: Pests and diseases can have a significant impact on plant health and yield. Proper pest and disease control measures should be taken to prevent infestations and treat any problems that arise. This can include using natural pest control methods, such as companion planting or using beneficial insects, as well as using pesticides or fungicides when necessary.
