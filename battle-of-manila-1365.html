10. pagninilay(reaction or opinion)for battle of manila​


Answer:

It’s hard to imagine that a major monthlong battle from World War II — one that devastated a large city, caused more than 100,000 civilian deaths and led to both a historic war crimes trial and a Supreme Court decision — should have escaped scrutiny until now.

But history has somehow overlooked the catastrophic battle for Manila, capital of the Philippines, in the waning months of the war. Like the Rape of Nanking, or the siege of Stalingrad, the tragedy of Manila deserves far greater understanding and reflection today.

James M. Scott remedies that gap with “Rampage: MacArthur, Yamashita, and the Battle of Manila,” the first comprehensive account of one of the darkest chapters of the Pacific War. It is powerful narrative history, one almost too painful to read in places but impossible to put down.

It begins as Gen. Douglas MacArthur, the egotistical military commander of the U.S. colony in the Philippines, was caught woefully unprepared when the war began. Japanese bombers destroyed his planes on the ground and American and Philippine forces were soon overwhelmed. MacArthur famously vowed to return as he was evacuated to Australia.


11. mock battle of manila tagalog summary


MOCK BATTLE OF MANILA


Ang Labanan/Digmaan sa Maynila Bay na kung minsan ay tinatawag na Mock Battle of Manila , ay isang pakikipag-ugnayan sa lupa na naganap sa Maynila noong Agosto 13, 1898, sa pagtatapos ng Digmaang Espanyol-Amerikano, apat na buwan matapos ang mapagpasyang tagumpay ni Commodore Dewey's Asiatic Squadron sa Battle of Manila Bay.


Ang mga manlalaban ay mga pwersang Espanyol na pinamumunuan ng Gobernador-Heneral ng Pilipinas na si Fermin Jáudenes at mga pwersang Amerikano na pinangungunahan ng Brigadier General Army ng Estados Unidos na si Wesley Merritt at ng Navy Commodore George Dewey ng Estados Unidos.


Ang mga pwersang Amerikano ay sinusuportahan ng mga yunit ng Philippine Revolutionary Army, pinamunuan ni Emilio Aguinaldo.


Ang labanan ay tinutukoy bilang "Mock Battle of Manila" dahil ang mga lokal na Espanyol at Amerikanong mga heneral, na legal pa rin sa digmaan, lihim at sama-samang nagplano ng labanan upang ilipat ang kontrol ng sentro ng lungsod mula sa Espanyol hanggang sa Amerikano habang pinapanatili ang Philippine Revolutionary Army, pinangunahan ni Emilio Aguinaldo, mula sa sentro ng lungsod.


Ang labanan ay umalis sa mga pwersang Amerikano sa kontrol ng Intramuros, ang sentro ng Maynila na napalibutan ng mga rebolusyonaryong pwersa ng Pilipinas na lumilikha ng mga kondisyon para sa Labanan ng Maynila ng 1899 at pagsisimula ng Digmaang Pilipino-Amerikano.

The American Asiatic Squadron under Commodore George Dewey engaged and destroyed the Spanish Pacific Squadron under Contraalmirante (Rear admiral) Patricio Montojo. 24. Bakit nadeklara ng Battle of manila bay/Battle of cavite?


Answer:

The Battle of Manila Bay (Filipino: Labanan sa Look ng Maynila; Spanish: Batalla de Bahía de Manila), also known as the Battle of Cavite, took place on 1 May 1898, during the Spanish–American War. The American Asiatic Squadron under Commodore George Dewey engaged and destroyed the Spanish Pacific Squadron under Contraalmirante (Rear admiral) Patricio Montojo. The battle took place in Manila Bay in the Philippines, and was the first major engagement of the Spanish–American War. The battle was one of the most decisive naval battles in history and marked the end of the Spanish colonial period in Philippine history.
