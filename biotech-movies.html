Biotechnology is the use of biological processes, organisms, or systems to manufacture products intended to improve the quality of human life. The earliest biotechnologists were farmers who developed improved species of plants and animals by cross pollenization or cross breeding.

4. What is the function of a vector?? [BIOTECH]​


Answer:

A vector is a DNA molecule used to carry additional genetic material into a cell. A vector that carries foreign DNA is called recombinant DNA. There are four types of vectors including cosmids, viral vectors, plasmids, and artificial chromosomes.

18. define biotech briefly​


Answer:

"Biotechnology”, if we break this word into two, we get Bio+Technology which linguistically means the application of technology in various fields of Biology. Technically, Biotechnology is a branch of science which is an amalgamation of Biology and related topics with present day technology to bring about necessary changes or to bring out the best out of the biological world using tools driven by Technology for the betterment of the society.
