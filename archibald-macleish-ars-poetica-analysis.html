5. sino siya Archibald A. Hill


Answer: Archibald A. Hill

Explanation:

Si Archibald A. Hill ay isang Amerikanong "linguist." Ayon sa kanya, ang wika ay ang kaluluwa ng tao. Ito ang sinulat niya: "I believe it is language and its use which more than anything else is the human spirit and that there is new as well as old truth in Scripture which says 'In the beginning was the Word.'"

27. Explain the quote in relationship to the topic discussed. “One knows that after violent exercise, one breathes heavily for some time: the more violent the exercise, the longer one's respiration is labored”. Archibald Hill​


Answer:

“One knows that after violent exercise, one breathes heavily for some time: the more violent the exercise, the longer one's respiration is labored”. Archibald Hill​

Explanation:

In order to work, our muscles need energy, which is released by chemical processes. During 1910s Archibald Hill outlined these processes by studying muscles from frogs. In opposition to the prevailing view that mechanical movement and chemical processes were parallel sequences, Hill was able to show through measurements of heat generated by the mechanical processes that these were delayed in relation to the movements. The chemical sequence consists of a work phase, which is not dependent on oxygen supply, and a recovery phase when oxygen is required.
