3. Madison is 7 less than four times as old as Paige. The product of their ages is 47 more than the sum of their ages. How old are Madison and Paige? Solution po.​


Answer:

Madison = 13 years old

Paige = 5 years old

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x be the age of Paige

The age of Madison is 4x - 7 (7 less than 4 times as old as Paige)

x (4x - 7) = [x + (4x - 7)] + 47

4x² - 7x = (5x - 7) + 47

4x² - 7x = 5x + 40

4x² - 12x - 40 = 0

x² - 3x - 10 = 0

(x - 5)(x + 2) = 0

x = 5, -2

DIscard -2 and use only 5 then substitute

x = 5 years (Paige's age)

4x - 7 = 4(5) - 7 = 13 years (Madison's age)

30. 2. Andrew starts his soccer practice at 8:35 am. If the practice ends at 2:40 Andrew practice Soccer pm, how long did Andrew practice soccer​


To find out how long Andrew practiced soccer, we need to subtract the starting time from the ending time.

End time = 2:40 pm

Start time = 8:35 am

To convert the start time to pm, we need to add 12 hours since 8:35 am is in the morning.

Start time = 8:35 am + 12 hours = 8 + 12 : 35 = 20:35 pm

Now we can subtract the start time from the end time:

2:40 pm - 8:35 pm = 6 hours

Therefore, Andrew practiced soccer for 6 hours.
