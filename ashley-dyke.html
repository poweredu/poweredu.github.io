28. Ishaan is 42 yearsold and ashley is 12 years old. How many years will it take until ishaan is only 3 times as old as ashley


Solution:

Let x be the number of years;

Ishaan = 42 + x
Ashley = 12 + x

42 + x = 3 (12 + x)
42 + x = 36 + 3x
6 = 2x
x = 3

Ishaan = 42 + x = 42 + 3 = 45
Ashley = 12 + x = 12 + 3 = 15

45 = 3 (15)
45 = 45
