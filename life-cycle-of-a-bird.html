1. how is a life cycle of frog's life cycle the same as a bird's life cycle​


Frog's life cycle and a bird's life cycle​

We can't imagine the similarities between a frog's and a bird's life cycle. Firstly, birds fly, and frogs don't. Secondly, frogs are amphibians, while birds belong to the class Ave. But if we narrow down our perspective to how they're born, we can see that they have a bit of a similarity.

Frogs are not yet welcome to the world once their mothers give birth because they need to hatch from their eggs to start their journey. Same as birds, right? Birds are born through eggs, not like mammals who dwell in the wild as soon as they are born. That's the key similarity between a frog's and a bird's life cycle.

Life cycle of a frog:

egg-tadpole-tadpole with legs-young frog-adult frog

It moves through these stages in a process known as metamorphosis
