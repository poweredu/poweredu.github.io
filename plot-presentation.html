14. 6. What element of a plot where the outcome of the story is presented?​


Explanation:

The element of a plot where the outcome of the story is presented is called the resolution or denouement. It is the point in the story where the conflict is resolved, and the loose ends of the plot are tied up. The resolution follows the climax, which is the point of highest tension in the story. In the resolution, the protagonist usually achieves their goal, and the story's themes are often made clear.
