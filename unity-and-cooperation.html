23. What is introduction of the unity and cooperation in the new normal?​


The introduction of unity and cooperation in the new normal refers to the need for individuals, communities, and organizations to come together and work together in order to effectively navigate and adapt to the current global situation caused by the COVID-19 pandemic. This includes implementing safety measures, following guidelines and protocols, and supporting one another.

Unity and cooperation is crucial in ensuring that everyone's needs are met and that we can work together to mitigate the spread of the virus. This includes being mindful of the safety and well-being of others, and being willing to make sacrifices for the greater good.

In addition, working together to support each other, to share information and resources and to help those who are most vulnerable. This will be essential to strengthen our communities and build resilience in the face of the ongoing pandemic.

Overall, the introduction of unity and cooperation in the new normal is a call to action for everyone to come together, to support one another, and to work together for the common goal of overcoming the challenges posed by the pandemic and moving forward towards a better future.
