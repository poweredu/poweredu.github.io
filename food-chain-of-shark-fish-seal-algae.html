- Sedimentation from erosion

Answer:

THE FOOD CHAIN

Explanation:

The Food Chain or Food Web is the Main composition of the Ecosystem where living and nonliving organisms are connected to one another.

The Process of Food Chain

Is defined as the movement of energy and nutrients from one organism to another. These are being transferred in a form of food.

Here are the  Stages of the Food Chain:

Non-Living Organism such as Plants (Producers)Animals or living organisms that eat the plants (Herbivores)Animals or living organisms that eat the herbivores types of animals. (Carnivores)Animals or living organisms that eat the CarnivoresAnimals at the very top of the food chain are called APEX PREDATORS.

Links Composition of a Food Chain:

Producers- these are the living organisms that produce the energy obtained from the sunlight.Consumers- These are all the animal classifications that belong in the ecosystem. They consume the food available as a source of energy and nutrients to continue their reproductions.Decomposers- also known as the 'Decaying Matter'. These help the aid of bringing the nutrients back to the soil to use again as a source of nutrients.
