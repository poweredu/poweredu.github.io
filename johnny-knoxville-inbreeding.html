Answer:

Inbreeding is the mating of organisms closely related by ancestry. It goes against the biological aim of mating, which is the shuffling of DNA. Human DNA is bundled into 23 pairs of chromosomes, within each chromosome there are hundreds of thousands of genes and what's more, each gene has two copies known as alleles.

7. what is the diffrence between inbreeding and crossbreeding? ​


Answer:

Inbreeding: mating between animals or plants that are closely related.

Crossbreading: mating two purebred individuals but come from different breeds, varieties, or even species.

Difference: inbreeding is the mating of animals that are more closely related than the average in a population, and crossbreeding is the mating of animals that are less related than the average in a population.

18. How to calculate inbreeding coefficient from pedigree?


Answer:

If a child is inbred through more than one line of descent, the total coefficient of inbreeding is the sum of each of the separate coefficients. For example, first cousins are related through two grandparents. Thus, the inbreeding coefficient for the offspring of first cousins is F = (½)5 + (½)5 = (½)4 = 1/16.

27. Johnny is 3 times as old as Mark. 8 years from now, Johnny will be twice as old as Mark, how old is Mark?


Answer:

Mark is 8 years old now.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let Johnny's age = J and Mark's age = M

eq. 1 J = 3M

eq. 2 (J + 8) = 2(M + 8)

Solve for a variable using substitution.

Let's solve for M first.

(J + 8) = 2(M + 8) Substitute J=3M

3M + 8 = 2M + 16 Simplify

M = 8

Now, we can solve for J.

J = 3M

J = 3(8)

J = 24

Mark is 8 years old, while Johnny is 24 years old. Johnny is 3 times as old as Mark. TRUE.

8 years from now, Mark would be 16 years old and Johnny would be 32 years old. Johnny would be twice as old as Mark. TRUE.
