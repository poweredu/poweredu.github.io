14. ano ang talambuhay ni diosdado banatao?


Ano ang talambuhay ni Diosdado Banatao?

Si Diosdado P. Banatao ay pinanganak sa Iguig, Cagayan, Philippines. Si Diosdado P. Banatao ay may istorya na from rags to riches. Noong elementarya siya ay naglalakad lamang siya ng nakayapak para makapasok sa Malabbac Elementary School. Graduate siya ng  BS in Electric Engineering. Naging piloto siya sa Amerika at naging design engineer ng Boeing 747. Noong 1972, sa Stanford University ay nakapagtapos siya ng MS in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science. Siya ay nagdevelop ng unang 10-Mbit Ethernet CMOS with silicon coupler data-link control and transreceiver chip, the first system logic chip set for IBM's PC-XT and the PC-AT, and the local bus concept and the first Windows Graphics accelerator chip for personal computers.

27. Sino po si Diosdado banatao? ​


Answer:

Diosdado P. Banatao (born May 23, 1946 in the small barrio of Malabbac in the town of Iguig, Cagayan, Philippines) is a Filipino entrepreneur and engineer working in the high-tech industry,[2] credited with having developed the first 10-Mbit Ethernet CMOS with silicon coupler data-link control and transceiver chip, the first system logic chip set for IBM's PC-XT and the PC-AT, and the local bus concept and the first Windows Graphics accelerator chip for personal computers.[3] A three-time start-up veteran, he co-founded Mostron, Chips and Technologies, and S3 Graphics.[4] His father, Salvador Banatao, was a rice farmer. His mother, Rosita Banatao, was a housekeeper.[2]

Si Diosdado Banatao ay isang magiting na Pilipinong inhinyero at negosyante na nakapag-ambag ng malaki sa larangan ng teknolohiya sa buong mundo. Sa kanyang buhay at karera, mayroong ilang mahahalagang aral na mapupulot:
