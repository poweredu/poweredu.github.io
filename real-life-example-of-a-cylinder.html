27. Can you give me some examples of Real-life Volume Questions, Necessary dimensions , Formula for Volume for Square pyramid , Cylinder , Cone , Sphere


Answer:

"A reservoir is shaped like a square prism. If the area of its base is 255 square centimeter, how many liters of water will it hold?"

Answer: 3.375 L

Step-by-step explanation:

For square pyramid:

V = s^3

A = s^2

Given:

A = 255 cm^2

Sol'n:

[tex]s^{2} = A\\ \\s = \sqrt{A} \\\\s = \sqrt{255}\\ \\s = 15 cm[/tex]

Thus, volume is:

V = s^3

V = (15 cm)^3

V = 3375 cm^3

note: 1000 cm^3 = 1 L

[tex]V = 3375 cm^{3} *\frac{1 L}{1000 cm^{3} }[/tex]

V = 3.375 L
