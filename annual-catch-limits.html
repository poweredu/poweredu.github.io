21. Anna's annual salary as a housekeeper is Php 42,000.00 .If an annual increase of 10% is given from her initial annual salary, how much would be her annual salary on the sixth year?​


Answer:

25,200 PESOS

Step-by-step explanation:

PRINCIPAL = 42,000 PESOS

RATE = 10% OR 0.1%

TIME = 6 YEARS

42,000 PESOS × 0.1% = 4,200 PESOS

4,200 PESOS × 6 YEARS = 25,200 PESOS
