Sociocultural Context

Sociocultural are the ideas, habits, skills, arts, and tools that characterize a certain group of people at a certain time. Sociocultural is a system of integrated patterns that govern human behavior.

Socio-cultural strength becomes the process of 'thickening' the power of the child's nature which is still vague. Education aims to guide and help children to thicken vague lines so that they can improve their behavior to become fully human.

Socio-cultural forces are considered to have a considerable influence on the formation of social behavior even though their personality and individuality are blurred due to social roles, social structure, lifestyle and self-image.

The implication of implementation in schools in sociocultural-based learning is to create an interactive classroom atmosphere and the student learning process is directed so that the longer students are more and more responsible for their own learning.
