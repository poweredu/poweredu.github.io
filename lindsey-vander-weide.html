17. Lindsey sold stationery to her family and her mother’s friends. She deposited the $125 she earned in a savings account. The account earns 5.18% interest annually. If she does not deposit or withdraw any money from for 18 months, how much will she have in her account?


Answer= 860

Step-by-step explanation:

125(5.18)= 647.5 (12months)

18-12= 4 (months)

12/4= 3

5.18/3= 1.7(4months of interest)

125(1.7)= 212.5(4months of interest in her deposited money)

647.5+212= 860(18months)

btw, annually means every year or year.
