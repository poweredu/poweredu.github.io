7. A footbridge support in a semi-elliptic shape spans a railroad 20m wide. The center of the footbridge is 6m above the center of the railroad. Determine a working equation for the ellipse if the x-axis coincides with the railroad level and the y-axis passes through the center of the footbridge​


The ellipse's center is at (0,0).

[tex]\frac{x^{2}}{a^{2}} + \frac{y^{2}}{b^{2}} = 1[/tex]

The railroad is 20m wide, which means it is 10m on either side of the center. This serves as the major axis (a).

The center of the footbridge is 6m above the railroad, which serves as the minor axis (b).

a = 10, b = 6

standard equation: [tex]\frac{x^{2}}{100} + \frac{y^{2}}{36} = 1[/tex]

general equation:

The Sierra Madre, extending along the Pacific coast from northern to central Luzon, is the longest mountain range in the country. That range and the Cordillera Central merge in north-central Luzon to form the Caraballo Mountains.
