4. Joy is twice as old as jim . If the sum of their ages is 42,how old are they ? Represent the age of joy :_____________​


AGE PROBLEM

[tex]\\[/tex]

PROBLEM:

Joy is twice as old as Jim. If the sum of their ages is 42, how old are they?

Represent the age of Jim: x

Represent the age of Joy: 2x

Equation representing the sum of their ages: x + (2x) = 42

Solve the equation:

Solution:

Let Jim's age be x. The age of Joy depends on Jim's. We know that sum is the result when you add two or more quantities. This means that we need to equate Jim's and Joy's whose sum of their ages is 42.

[tex] \begin{array}{|} \sf{x + (2x) = 42} \\ \\ \sf{3x = 42} \\ \\ \sf{ \frac{3x}{3} = \frac{42}{3}} \end{array} \\ [/tex]

[tex]\:\:\:\:\:\:\:[/tex] x = 14

Jim's age - x

x = 14

[tex]\:[/tex]

Joy's age - 2x

2x

Substitute 14 to it

2(14) = 28

ANSWER:

Age of Joy - 28

Age of Jim - 14

CHECKING:

28 + 14 = 42

Hence, true

26. joy is 5 years younger than anna the sum of their age is 39 how old is joy​


Answer:

Joy is 17 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let:

Anna's age = n

Joy's age = n - 5

Anna's age + Joy's age = 39

Substitute:

Anna's age + Joy's age = 39

                      n + (n - 5) = 39

                        n + n - 5 = 39

                            2n - 5 = 39

                      2n - 5 + 5 = 39 + 5

                                  2n = 44

                                   [tex]\frac{2n}{2} = \frac{44}{2} \\n = 22[/tex]

That means Anna's age is 22, and Joy's age is:

n - 5 = 22 - 5 = 17

Joy's age is 17.
