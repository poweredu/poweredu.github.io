9. My personal commitment towards my goals


Commitment to yourself is a reflection that you are a person who is responsible and cares about yourself.

Here are my commitments:

Taking care of myself

I can start to commit to taking care of myself. Sounds simple, yes, but without realizing it, we often ignore it. For example, when you are physically tired at work, but still force yourself to finish work on the same day even to overtime. Remember, every human being must have a limit.

Commitment to keep getting better

This second point emphasizes more commitment to yourself to be better. For example, I want to maintain my health by committing to exercise every day, I want to get the desired body goals and then commit to only eating healthy foods. In essence, the commitment you make is based on the desire to become a better person.

Caring for the environment

Consciously or not, our earth is not in a good condition. Water, air, and plastic pollution have now become a global problem, you know. Before this problem gets worse, we can start committing to care for the environment.

It's easy, you can start by using environmentally friendly products. Starting from replacing plastic bags with shopping bags, saving electricity and water, to using eco-friendly beauty products.

Commitment to the daily schedule made

To make life easier and more organized, it's better if we dare to commit to the daily schedule that has been made. For example, in a week, note what activities will be done and make a schedule.

Have measurable goals in life

When we are committed to the life goals we want to achieve, it means we have clear goals for the future and are also visionaries and good planners. Do not forget to stay consistent with the commitments that have been made.
