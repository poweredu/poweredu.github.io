29. 1. what is your favourite part in contagion movie? why? 2-5 Enumerate the parts of the movie that are associated with our present situation ​


Answer:

I can provide examples of parts of the movie that relate to our present situation.

2. The first part of the movie that is associated with our present situation is the initial outbreak of the virus in China. This is similar to the current COVID-19 pandemic, which also originated in China.

3. The rapid spread of the virus in Contagion is another similarity to our current situation. The virus in the movie spreads quickly and easily from person to person, just like COVID-19.

4. The panic and fear that the virus causes in the movie is also similar to what many people are experiencing during the COVID-19 pandemic. In Contagion, people hoard supplies and panic-buy, which is similar to what happened during the early days of the pandemic.

5. The efforts to develop a vaccine in the movie are also similar to the current situation. Scientists and medical professionals are working tirelessly to develop a vaccine for COVID-19, just as they did in Contagion.

Overall, the movie Contagion offers a fictionalized account of a pandemic, but it also highlights the importance of preparedness, communication, and cooperation in the face of a global health crisis.
