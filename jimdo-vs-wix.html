15. wix and weebly similarities and differences(venn diagram)​


Wix and Weebly are both website building platforms that allow users to create and design their own websites without the need for coding knowledge. Both platforms offer a variety of tools and features to help users create professional-looking websites.

One key difference between the two platforms is the way in which websites are created. Wix uses a drag-and-drop editor, which allows users to simply drag and drop elements onto their pages to create their website. Weebly, on the other hand, uses a more traditional website editor, where users can add and edit content using pre-designed templates and themes.

Another difference is the range of options and features available on each platform. Wix offers a wider range of design options and features, including more advanced features such as animation and video backgrounds. Weebly, on the other hand, is known for its simplicity and ease of use, making it a good option for users who are new to website building.

Overall, both Wix and Weebly are solid website building platforms that offer users a range of tools and features to help them create professional-looking websites. The main difference between the two is the way in which websites are created and the range of options and features available.
