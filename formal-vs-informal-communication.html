Answer:

Informal language is more casual and spontaneous. It is used when communicating with friends or family either in writing or in conversation.

25. What are the Different of Multicultural Communication VS Cross-cultural Communication VS Intercultural Communication​


Answer:

Multicultural Communication generally involves communication between individuals or groups representing different cultural backgrounds within the same country or society where they coexist. Cross-cultural Communication typically involves communication between individuals or groups from different countries, whereas intercultural communication refers more broadly to communication between people from different cultures or cultural backgrounds and can occur within or across national boundaries. Essentially, multicultural communication emphasizes the coexistence of different cultural groups within a society, cross-cultural communication emphasizes the interaction between people from different countries, and intercultural communication encompasses both of these and any other interactions that involve cultural differences, including those between people within the same society or organization.
