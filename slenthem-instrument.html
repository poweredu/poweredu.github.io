1. description of slenthem​


Answer:

The slenthem (also slentem or gender panembung) is a Javanese metallophone which makes up part of a gamelan orchestra. The slenthem is part of the gendér family. It consists of a set of bronze keys comprising a single octave: there are six keys when playing the slendro scale and seven when playing the pelog.
