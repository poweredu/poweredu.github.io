The United Kingdom, France, Spain, Algeria, Mali, Burkina, Faso, Tongo, and Ghana while in the Southern Hemisphere, passes through Antarctica.  

What is a Prime Meridian?

Prime Meridian line divides the Earth into two divisions. It directly passes through the land of Greenwich in England. It consists of 180 longitudes lines on every side of the Prime Meridian.
