7. what are the similarities of a published book, a published magazine, and a published news article​


Answer:

Book

A book is a collection of pages hand-written or typed that is bound together. Generally, it has either a soft-cover or a hard-cover. The front cover carries the book title, subtitle, and name of the author. If it is thick enough, say more than 200 pages, it would have a spine where the book title, the author’s name and a logo can be printed.

A book can be self-published or through established publishers. If you self-publish, then you are free to do anything you want to get it published, but you must know the whole process of publishing a book. You also bear all costs.

If you want your book to be published by a publishing company, then you have to submit a draft to it. Usually, an author will submit his draft to several publishers. The rejection rate is high, and even if your draft is accepted, your book may be amended in ways that will make you quite uncomfortable.

One great way to publish a book is through Amazon Kindle Direct Publishing. Check it out. All the information is out there provided by this global giant corporation.

Article

An article usually covers a very specific topic, for example: How the United States can reduce gun violence.

An article can be published as a post in a blog, submitted and accepted by a magazine, journal, year book, or a newspaper.

There are also many websites that accept articles, such as seekingalpha.com, .com and articles.org. Once your article is accepted, it will generate backlinks back to your email address or website.

The main purpose of submitting articles is to get your name recognized as an authority in a particular niche or field.

Extra lang to :

Journal

A journal is usually the publication of an established profession such as scientists, doctors, hospitals, university research department and think tank.

It is similar but not the same as magazine for the following reason. A journal is published less frequently, say, quarterly, half yearly or only once a year. It’s purpose is more serious such as medical breakthrough, proposing a new theory and research results. Its circulation is usually limited to people in that niche or specialty.

A magazine is published more frequently, say, weekly and monthly, for general consumption. Its circulation is usually larger than a journal. Because of a magazine’s large circulation, you’ll see companies willing to pay a high price to place their commercial and advertisements there.

Website

A website is a site in the world wide web (Internet) where there is at least one page of information. It can be placed there by a person, a company, a government department, the police, hobby specialist, and so on to proclaim their presence.

The first page usually gives you a lot of information about the person or organization that publishes or manages it. It contains a theme, the subjects they will cover, and ways to contact the person or organization. There is also usually an invitation to subscribe to the website, meaning that articles and other notification will be sent to you, if you are interested.

When posts or articles are regularly published on this website, the website is then called a blog.

You can choose from many website or blogging platforms out there to build your website. The top four platforms are: Wordpress.org, Wix, Wordpress.com, and Blogger.

When people talk about monetizing your talents, this is one way to monetize, and that is, to build a blog or website, and place ads there where you’ll get passive income.

Every website must have a domain name. So you need to buy a domain name, which usually cost less than $10 per year. Then you have to get a web host to handle your traffic to your site. That will cost you around $20 per month.

Did you know that one way to be immortal is to do all of the above?

When you publish a book, an article, or publish blog posts on your website, they live on forever, even if you try to delete them.

I hope the above discussion helps.

15. Ide Questions (if necessary)A. What is a Publisher?B. How to access Publisher?C. What are the parts of Publisher window?​


A. Publisher is a desktop publishing application (program). It is made by Microsoft and it is part of Microsoft Office. Publisher is similar to Word except that it places the emphasis on page layout and design (rather than writing text and proof reading).

B. Step 1

Navigate to the Windows Live website (see Resources) and sign up for a free account. If you already have a Windows Live, MSN or Microsoft Passport, you can use this to log into the Microsoft Office site instead.

Step 2

Go to the Microsoft Office Publisher 2010 Web page (see Resources) and click the "Try Free for 60 Days" button.

Step 3

Sign into the website and confirm your order in the website shopping cart to proceed to the download page.

Step 4

Record the product key provided on the download page for future reference.

Step 5

Click the "Download Now" button to save the Publisher file to your computer.

Step 6

Double-click the file to launch the installation.

Type the product key when the installation prompts you. After the installation is complete, you have 60 days to use the trial of Microsoft Publisher on your computer.

C. The Publisher environment consists of the Title Bar, which also contains the Quick Access toolbar, the Ribbon, the Page Navigation Pane, the Publication Page area, and the Status Bar, which also contains the Page Layout View buttons and the Zoom Slider.
