14. Divine origin meaning


Answer:

The theory of divine rights of the kings also known as the Divine Origin theory is one of the oldest theory of the origin of the state. The supporters of this theory believed that the state doesn't come into being by the people but it is the handiwork of God on the earth. Divine right of kings, in European history, a political doctrine in defense of monarchical absolutism, which asserted that kings derived their authority from God and could not therefore be held accountable for their actions by any earthly authority such as a parliament.
