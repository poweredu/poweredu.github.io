6. What technique that perry welman used ?


Answer:

Perry Welman used a computer, digitally enhanced photos, and Algorithm computation to create "Chaos Theory", "Wires" and his other artworks. This is called Algorithmic Art, a subset of Generative Art.

Explanation:

To understand the art of Perry Welman, let us define first Algorithmic art and Generative art:

Algorithmic art refers to art produced through an algorithm. This means that a sequence of instructions was used to generate an image.Generative Art is art produced by an autonomous system meaning, it has no human interaction.

This simply shows how good Perry Welman is with computing and Algorithm.

Background:

Perry Welman is a Technical Designer for car interior during his early professional career. This would influence his techniques in producing art.

The Wires originated from Perry's project that he started in 1998. He took many photos of electronic cables from household items and automobiles. But it was only in 2007 when his concept was completed.

21. Sino si Commadore Matthew Perry?


Matthew Calbraith Perry[Note 1] (April 10, 1794 – March 4, 1858) was a Commodore of the United States Navy and commanded a number of ships. He served in several wars, most notably in the Mexican–American War and the War of 1812. He played a leading role in the opening of Japan to the West with the Convention of Kanagawa in 1854.

Perry was very concerned with the education of naval officers and helped develop an apprentice system that helped establish the curriculum at the United States Naval Academy. With the advent of the steam engine, he became a leading advocate of modernizing the US Navy and came to be considered The Father of the Steam Navy in the United States.
