17. Angela plans to sell customized bags in an upcoming bazaar. To create one bag, she has to spend ₱412.00 for the materials, and she will sell it for ₱450.00. To participate in the bazaar, she has to pay participation and rental feels totaling ₱5,000. If she was able to create 130 bags, and she believes she can sell them all, should she participate in the bazaar? ​


Answer:

no

Step-by-step explanation:

412(the materials) x 130(the bag) = 53,560(cost)

450(price of the bag) x 130(the bag) = 58,500

58,500-53,560=4,940

not enough for the participation and rental feels

in conclusion, she will lose money
