What is Noun Subject?

-- A noun can be the subject of a verb. The subject tells what a sentence is about.

What is Noun Subject Complement?

--A noun can be a subject complement.

A subject complement follows a linking (being)verb and renames the subject

Example:

Joe is a lover of apples

Joe is the subject (because he's the one that is talking about in the sentence, the linking verb is (is)

and Lover is the subject complement (because it modifies the subject which is Joe)
