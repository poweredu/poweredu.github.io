4. taxonomy A to Z about biology with definition​


Explanation:

Taxonomy is the branch of science that deals with the classification of organisms. It is a system of categorizing living things based on their shared characteristics. In biology, taxonomy classifies organisms into multiple ranks, such as kingdom, class, order, family, genus, species, and more. Taxonomy is important because it helps us understand how organisms are related to each other, and allows us to communicate more effectively about them.
