11. The sum of measures of the interior angles in a polygon takes an array. the sum of the interior angles of a triangle is 180 ,of a quadrilateral is 360,and of a Pentagon is 540 . What is the sum of the interior angles of an icosagon?​


Answer:

there is already a set formula for determining the sum of interior angles in a polygon, and it's (n - 2)*180, wherein n= number of sides.

Step-by-step explanation:

180(n - 2), n = 3

=180(1)

=180[triangle]

180 (n - 8), n = 4

=180(2)

=360[quadrilateral]

and so on...

A nonagon is a polygon with nine sides.

180(n - 2), n = 9

=180(7)

=1260

"The total of the interior angles of a nonagon is 1260".
