Other similarities between ancient art and pre-historic art are that they are made on stones and marble.

The difference between the ancient arts and prehistoric arts are the following:

The Moulding of structures characterizes the Ancient arts, for example, structures of past Pharaohs in Egypt, past Philosophers In Greek, and past Emperor Romans, while Prehistoric arts are characterized by Carving on the wall.
