18. (27 , 9 , 3 , ......) is GS. The order of the term is ......


Geometric Sequence formula

An = A1 (r)ⁿ⁻¹


Solve r (common ratio)

Substitute values

An = 9

A1 = 27

n = 2 (since we use 9 as An)


9 = 27 (r)²⁻¹

Divide both sides by 27

9/27 = r

Reduce 9/27 to lowest term

1/3 = r


Answer: The order of the term is being multiplied by 1/3
