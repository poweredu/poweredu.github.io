23. A race car, traveling at 120 kph, decelerated by 10kph, then accelerated by 5kph, and then decelerated by 24kph. Find its current speed!!,,,give theProblem: Given: Asked: Illustration: Computation: Conclusion:pahelppp​


Problem: Find the current speed of the race car.

Given: The initial speed of the race car is 120 kph. It decelerated by 10 kph, accelerated by 5 kph, and then decelerated by 24 kph.

Asked: What is the current speed of the race car?

Illustration:

Initial speed = 120 kph

1st deceleration = -10 kph

Acceleration = +5 kph

2nd deceleration = -24 kph

Computation:

To find the current speed of the race car, we need to add all the changes in speed and add them to the initial speed.

120 kph - 10 kph + 5 kph - 24 kph = 91 kph

Therefore, the current speed of the race car is 91 kph.

Conclusion:

The current speed of the race car is 91 kph after decelerating by 10 kph, accelerating by 5 kph, and decelerating by 24 kph from its initial speed of 120 kph.
