2. A tent Sq are base of 8 mlf each face of four triangular faces has a hight of 6m, then how much material is used in making the tent?​


1. Find the area of the base of the tent:

Area of base = side length² = 8² = 64 square meters

2. Find the area of each triangular face:

Area of each triangular face = 1/2 x base x height = 1/2 x 8 x 6 = 24 square meters

3. Find the total area of the triangular faces:

Total area of triangular faces = 4 x 24 = 96 square meters

4. Find the total surface area of the tent:

Total surface area of tent = area of base + total area of triangular faces = 64 + 96 = 160 square meters

Therefore, the amount of material used in making the tent is 160 square meters.

18. A tent on a square base has a side of 5m. The height of each triangular face is 4m. Find the amount of canvas used for making the tent.1.What solid figure does the tent resemble?2.What formula is to be used to solve the problem?3.How much canvas is to be used to make the tent?​


Step-by-step explanation:

1. The tent resembles a square pyramid.

2. The formula to find the lateral area of a square pyramid is L = (1/2)Pl, where P is the perimeter of the base and l is the slant height of each triangular face. The total amount of canvas used for the tent would be the lateral area plus the area of the base.

3. The perimeter of the base is 4 x 5 = 20m. To find the slant height, we can use the Pythagorean theorem: h^2 = 4^2 + (5/2)^2 = 25.25. Therefore, the slant height is approximately 5.02m. The lateral area is L = (1/2)(20)(5.02) = 50.2m^2. The area of the base is simply 5^2 = 25m^2. Therefore, the total amount of canvas used is 50.2 + 25 = 75.2m^2.

22. Peter and Eric pitched a tent in a shape of a pyramid. The base of the tent is 3meters wide by 4meters long .the tent had a slanted height of meter.what was the surface area of the tent


Answer:

In the given problem, apply your knowledge in algebraic expression and basic mathematics especially in solving word problems about surface area.

Problem

Peter and Eric pitched a tent that had a shape of a pyramid. The base of the tent is 3 meters wide by 4 meters long. The tent was 5 meters high. What was the surface area of the tent?

Given

Base of tent is 3 m by 4 m

h = 5 m

Solution

Surface area of triangle prism is the sum of all area of faces.

Triangular Face 1 = 1/2BH → 1/2(3)(5) = 7.5 m²

Triangular Face 2 = 1/2BH → 1/2(4)(5) = 10 m²

Rectangular Base = LW → (3)(4) = 12 m²

Surface Area = 2(7.5) + 2(10) + 12

Surface Area = 47 m²

THE CORRECT ANSWER IS 47 m²
