10. what is child welfare own opinion?​


Answer:

Child welfare is a term used to describe a set of government services designed to protect children and encourage family stability.

The goal of child welfare is to promote the well-being, permanency, and safety of children and families by helping families care for their children successfully or, when that is not possible, helping children find permanency with kin or adoptive families.

The resources available to this system are overwhelmed by the number of children and families who experience abuse and neglect. Wattenberg et al. ANSWER:Ang pinakamahalagang paraan upang maitataguyod ang programa ng kabataan ay ang pamamahala ng mga programa sa pamamagitan ng pagtugon sa mga pangunahing pangangailangan ng mga kabataan. Ang mga pangangailangan ay maaaring mag-include ng edukasyon, pampublikong kalusugan, trabaho, pagkakakilanlan, pagpapalakas ng buhay na pangkomunidad, at pagpapalakas ng kapasidad ng mga kabataan upang maging kapaki-pakinabang sa samahan. Ang mga programa ay maaaring maging pampublikong, pribadong, o pandaigdigang, at ang layunin nila ay upang matugunan ang mga pangunahing pangangailangan ng mga kabataan, magbigay ng pagkakataon para sa kanilang pag-unlad, at itaguyod ang isang mas makatarungang tahanan at lipunan para sa lahat ng mga kabataan.

1. Definition of a child

A child is any person under the age of 18.

2. No discrimination

All children have all these rights, no matter who they are, where they live, what language they speak, what their religion is, what they think, what they look like, if they are a boy or girl, if they have a disability, if they are rich or poor, and no matter who their parents or families are or what their parents or families believe or do. When adults make decisions, they should think about how their decisions will affect children. All adults should do what is best for children.
