23. acrostic poem for adolesence​


Answer:

Acrostic of adolescence

All at once the floodgates open;

Doom and storm and strife unleash and wail,

Or so they said: that this here age would be

Lamented and regretted—condemned.

Ephemeral though it was I think

So much of who I am was formed when

Childhood's constraining bonds were broken,

Ending the Herculean tasks I

Never asked for, no more

"Child" or "father's son" or "mother's daughter"; the

Eve of being "me" had come thundering down.
