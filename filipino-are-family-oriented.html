20. In what way is the value orientation of the family reflective of the value orientation of the Filipino people?​


Answer:

Strong family-orientation leads to closer bonds and supportive relationships in all areas of life. It is the quality and nature of family relationships that are of the most importance. Being family-oriented is synonymous with being family-centered or family-focused

The Filipino value system or Filipino values refers to the set of values that a majority of the Filipino have historically held important in their lives. This Philippine values system includes their own unique assemblage of consistent ideologies, moral codes, ethical practices, etiquette and cultural and personal values that are promoted by their society. As with any society though, the values that an individual holds sacred can differ on the basis of religion, upbringing and other factors.

As a general description, the distinct value system of Filipinos is rooted primarily in personal alliance systems, especially those based in kinship, obligation, friendship, religion (particularly Christianity) and commercial relationships.[1]

27. What /who influence the Filipino life style ? What are the cotributions of the Occidentals and Orientals ?In your case, describe their influence with your family? ​


Answer:

The Filipino lifestyle is influenced by a variety of factors, including history, culture, religion, and geography. The country has been colonized by several nations, including Spain, the United States, and Japan, which have all left their mark on the culture.

The Occidentals, specifically the Spanish and Americans, have had a significant impact on the Philippines. The Spanish colonizers introduced Catholicism, which is now the dominant religion in the country, and many Spanish words and customs have become integrated into everyday life. The American period also brought about changes in the education system, language, and government structure.

The Orientals, particularly the Chinese and Malays, have also played a role in shaping Filipino culture. Chinese immigrants have introduced their cuisine and business practices, while Malays have influenced the traditional clothing and music.

In my family, the influence of both the Occidentals and Orientals is evident. We celebrate both traditional Filipino and Western holidays, and we enjoy a variety of cuisines, including Filipino, Chinese, and American. Our religious practices and beliefs are also a blend of Catholicism and traditional Filipino spirituality. Overall, the diversity of cultural influences in the Philippines has created a unique and vibrant way of life.

29. Difference between family of orientation and family of procreation


Answer:

A family of orientation refers to the family into which a person is born. A family of procreation describes one that is formed through marriage. These distinctions have cultural significance related to issues of lineage.
