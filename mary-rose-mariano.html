25. there was a big flower vase with 60 red roses on it .athena mae got 2/15 of the red roses.althea marie got 3/15 and mary grace had 5/15 how many red roses were left​


Answer:

There was a big flower vase with 60 red roses in it. Athena Mae got 2/15 of the red roses. Althea Marie got 3/15 and Mary Grace had 5/15. How many red roses were left​?

20 red roses were left in the big flower vase.

Step-by-step explanation:

A big flower vase has 60 roses.

Athena Mae got 2/15 of the 60 red roses. = Athena Mae got 8 red roses.

Althea Mae got 3/15 of the 60 red roses. = Althea Mae got 12 red roses.

Mary Grace got 5/15 of the 60 red roses. =  Mary Grace got 20 red roses.

20 + 12 + 8 = 40 red roses were taken.

60 - 40 = 20 red roses were left in the big flower vase.
