1. what are the competencies needed to excel in your job?


When it comes to competencies needed to excel in jobs, it could vary depending on the type of job that you are doing. Some jobs would require more competencies, but there are actually some that could be applied to all types of jobs.

Here are some of the key competencies that employers would like their employees would like to have:

1. Analytical

Most of the time people have to think on their feet, especially when it comes to solving a problem when it just suddenly appears. Employees with good analytical thinking would be able to solve the problem even without the help of the boss or higher ups. By having this ingrained to yourself, you will definitely be one of the key players of a company.

2. Interpersonal Skills or Personal Competencies

Someone with great interpersonal skills will be able to work with a team with ease. This is considered one of the soft skills that an employee should have. Most jobs would require a person to interact with other people. By having this skill, a person will be able to show their boss and colleagues that you could an asset to the team or company.

3.  Positive Attitude

Let's all admit it, often times we get drawn to someone with a positive attitude. A positive attitude actually helps pull a team together or even just yourself. By having a positive attitude, you will be able to complete the tasks given to you by looking forward to what you are going to achieve.

These are, well in my opinion some of the most important competencies that a person should have to be able to excel in their jobs.
