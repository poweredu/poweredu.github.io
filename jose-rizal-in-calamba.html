12. Have you seen the house of Jose Rizal in Calamba?Descrive its structural design​


Answer:

The Rizal Shrine's architectural style is Bahay na Bato or House of Stone. It is an updated version of Bahay Kubo. It was popular among the elite or middle class during 19th century. The lower portion is made of stone, while the upper portion consists of hardwood structure with narra floors.

The original color of the house is white but it was repainted to a pale shade of green.

Angle of Depression = 67°

Hypotenuse = 10m

To get the height or the adjacent side, the formula is:

cos ∅ = adjacent/hypotenuse

cos 67° = adjacent/ 10

adjacent=height= 10cos67° = 3.9m
