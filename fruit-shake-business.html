11. In preparing fruit shake consisting of five fruits, how many fruit combinations can be made when five fruits are taken three at a time? ​


✏️COMBINATIONS

[tex]\red{••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••}[/tex]

[tex]\underline{\mathbb{PROBLEM:}}[/tex]

In preparing fruit shake consisting of five fruits, how many fruit combinations can be made when five fruits are taken three at a time?

[tex]\red{••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••}[/tex]

[tex]\underline{\mathbb{ANSWER:}}[/tex]

[tex] \qquad\Large\rm» \:\: \green{10 \: combinations} [/tex]

[tex]\red{••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••}[/tex]

[tex]\underline{\mathbb{SOLUTION:}}[/tex]

» Find the number of combination of 5 fruits taken 3 at a time

[tex]\begin{aligned} & \bold{\color{lightblue}Formula:} \\ & \boxed{\: \rm _nC_r = \frac{n!}{r!(n-r)!} \:} \end{aligned} [/tex]

[tex]\begin{aligned} \rm _5C_3 = \frac{5!}{3!(5 - 3)!} \end{aligned} [/tex]

[tex]\begin{aligned} \rm _5C_3 = \frac{5!}{3! \ 2!} \end{aligned} [/tex]

[tex]\begin{aligned} \rm _5C_3 = \frac{5 \cdot 4 \cdot \cancel{3!}}{ \cancel{3!} \cdot 2} \end{aligned} [/tex]

[tex]\begin{aligned} \rm _5C_3 = \frac{5 \cdot \cancel4^{ \: 2} }{ \cancel 2} \end{aligned} [/tex]

[tex]\rm _5C_3 = 5 \cdot 2[/tex]

[tex]\rm _5C_3 = 10[/tex]

[tex] \therefore [/tex] There are 10 combinations to select 3 fruits from 5 fruits.
