16. how can social dancing and dancw mixer contribute to fitness and community building​


Social Dancing and Dance Mixer can contribute to both fitness and community-building because it is both a form of exercise and an activity that fosters participation from members of the community. As an exercise, dancing in general helps manage weight, lowers blood sugar and reduces stress. As a community-building activity, it helps gather the public and work towards a common goal. Strangers who otherwise do not know each other can engage in social talks and activities.

As a result of social dancing and dance mixer (like zumba in the barangay) people get closer to each other as well as keeping fitness at maximum. They can then help each other in times of calamity or disaster as they no longer consider each other as strangers.
