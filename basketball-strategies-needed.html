2. strategies needed to floorball,rugby and basketball​


Answer:

Floorball

A modern floorball player takes a lot of short spurts in different directions, so they need to be athletic, have good reaction , speed and elasticity skills , and have good eye hand coordination and stick handling skills.

Rugby

Some of the main tactics include using the blindside during a scrum or breakdown, playing in the middle of the field, having the tallest players in specific positions, and making fast plays. One of the main rugby strategies is using the zone system which involves players knowing their exact place on the field.

Basketball

Basically two basic tactics can be distinguished in basketball: The "fast break" and the play-centered game. In fast break the offense attempt to move the ball forward as quickly as possible so that the defense is out of position and outnumbered. The focus here is on speed and quick passes.
