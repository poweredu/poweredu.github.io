ANSWER: C. 703.8 torr

21.2 torr + partial pressure of oxygen = 725 torr
[tex]725 - 21.2 = 703.8[/tex]
The partial pressure of oxygen is 703.8 torr

30. ian and lioness what are their ecological relationship​


Answer:

In biology, symbiosis is the relationship of two living organisms in an ecosystem.

Types of symbiosis:

Mutualism

Commensalism

Predation

Parasitism

Mutualism

This is the relationship between two organisms who benefits from each other.

Ex. Flowering plants and bees.

Commensalism

This is the relationship between two organisms where one benefits while the other is neither harmed nor benefited.

Ex. Orchids on a tree.

Predation

This is the relationship between two organisms where a prey is hunted by a predator to be a food.

Ex. Fox (predator) and rabbit (prey).

Parasitism

This is the type of relationship between two organisms where one benefits (parasite) while the other (host) is harmed.

Ex. Lice (parasite) in the human (host).
